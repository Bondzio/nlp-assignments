TaeTiSeo

Girls' Generation-TTS (also known as Girls' Generation-TaeTiSeo and TaeTiSeo) is a pop girl group from South Korea. It is a sub-group of Girls' Generation. It was formed by S. M. Entertainment in May 2012. It is made up of three Girls' Generation members, Taeyeon, Tiffany and Seohyun. The name TaeTiSeo is a portmanteau of the girls' names: Taeyeon, Tiffany and Seohyun.
