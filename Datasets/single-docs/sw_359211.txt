Amanda Anka

Amanda Katherine Anka-Bateman (born on December 10, 1968 in New York City, USA) is an American movie, television, and voice actress. 

Amanda Katherine Anka was born on December 10, 1968 in New York City. She was born to musician and actor Paul Anka and model Anne de Zogheb, who were married from 1963 until their divorce in 2000. Amanda is the second of five daughters. Her sisters are Alicia Anka (b.1967), Amelia Anka (b.1970), (b.1975) and Alexandra Anka (b.1977). She also has a younger half-
brother, Ethan Anka (b.2004).

Amanda married her boyfriend of two years, actor Jason Bateman (b.1969), on July 3, 2001. They have two daughters together - Francesca Nora Bateman (b.October 28, 2006) and Maple Sylvie Bateman (b.February 10, 2012).
