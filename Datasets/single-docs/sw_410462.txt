Shenzhen &amp; Hong Kong Bi‐City Biennale of Urbanism &amp; Architecture

Shenzhen & Hong Kong Bi‐City Biennale of Urbanism & Architecture is an international biennale held every two years in Hong Kong, China. In 2005, the Chinese government decided to devote the biennale to Chinese architecture and urban planning to promote city of Hong Kong and the area of Shenzhen.
