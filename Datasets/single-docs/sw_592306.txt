František Ševčík

František Ševčík (January 11, 1942 – July 22, 2017) was a Czech ice hockey player. He played for the Czechoslovak national team. He won a silver medal at the 1968 Winter Olympics. He was born in Vilémovice, Bohemia and Moravia, Nazi Germany.

Ševčík died in Prague, Czech Republic on July 22, 2017 from complications of spinal cancer at the age of 75.
