Karen Lewis

Karen Lewis (born June 26, 1953) is an American Chicago Public Schools teacher and labor leader. She is the current president of the Chicago Teachers Union. She is a former King College Prep High School and Lane Technical High School chemistry teacher. She was a teacher for 22 years.

Lewis was considering running for Mayor of Chicago in 2015. In July 2014 poll, Lewis defeated Emanuel in a hypothetical electoral contest by 45-36 . She decided not to run for mayor due to health issues and endorses Chuy Garcia.

On October 9, 2014, Lewis was hospitalized for a "serious illness". No further details about Lewis' health have been revealed. On October 13, a source confirmed that Lewis had been diagnosed with a cancerous brain tumor.
