Hotter Than July

Hotter Than July is the nineteenth album by American recording artist Stevie Wonder. It was released on Motown's Tamla label on September 29, 1980. It was certified platinum, which means it sold more than 1,000,000 copies in the US. It reached number three on the US Billboard chart. This was Stevie Wonder's most successful album in the UK, where it reached number two on the sales charts, and produced four top ten singles.

"Hotter Than July" was nominated for Favorite Soul/R&B Album at the American Music Awards in 1982.

All songs written, produced and arranged by Stevie Wonder except where noted.
