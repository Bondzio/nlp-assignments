Dennis Pitta

Dennis Pitta (born June 29, 1985 in Fresno, California) is an American football tight end for the Baltimore Ravens of the National Football League (NFL). Pitta was drafted out of the Brigham Young University by the Baltimore Ravens with the 114th pick in the fourth round of the 2010 NFL Draft. He won Super Bowl XLVII with the Ravens when they defeated the San Francisco 49ers 34-31.
