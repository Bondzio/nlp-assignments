Aarón Hernán

Aarón Hernán (born November 20, 1930) is an Mexican telenovela and movie actor. Hernán has appeared in over 100 Spanish telenovelas and over 67 movies. He is known for playing priests on telenovelas. He appeared in two American movies, "Apolinar" and "Black Wind".

Hernán was born on November 20, 1930 in Camargo, Chihuahua.
