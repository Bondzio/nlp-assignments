Assassin's Creed: Revelations

Assassin's Creed: Revelations is a 2011 stealth video game made by Ubisoft. It is a sequel to . It is the last game that had Ezio Auditore as a playable character. The game is set in Constantinople (modern day Turkey).

The game got very good reviews, and sold many copies. The game got an 80 out of 100 average from Metacritic. The game sold a million copies in one month. It has sold over 7 million copies overall.
