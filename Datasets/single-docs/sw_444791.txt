Victoria Coren Mitchell

Victoria Coren Mitchell (born 18 August 1972) is a British journalist, television presenter and poker player.

She was born in Hammersmith, London and raised in Cricklewood, London. She gained a first-class degree in English from St John's College, Oxford. She is the daughter of Alan Coren and sister of Giles Coren. 

She presented "Balderdash and Piffle". She presents "Only Connect".

She married David Mitchell on 17 November 2012. The couple's daughter was born in May 2015.
