D'Alto was born on May 5, 1964 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania to astrologer Jackie Stallone (b.1921) and her second husband Anthony Filiti. Toni was a younger half-sister of Sylvester Stallone.

D'Alto was twice divorced and had one son. She died on August 26, 2012 in her home in Santa Monica, California after a longtime battle with cancer, she was 48 years old.
