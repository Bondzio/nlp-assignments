Don't Tell Me

Don't Tell Me is a 2004 song by Avril Lavigne. It is from her album "Under My Skin". On April 27 2004, it was released as the first single off of that album.

Australian CD single




Japan CD single



The song did well on charts, climbing to number twenty-two in the United States, at number ten in Australia, at number six in Spain, at number five in the UK.
