Sue Ane Langdon

Sue Ane Langdon (previously Sue Lookhoff; born 8 March 1936) is a retired American actress. She began her career with singing and theatre. On the television series "Perry Mason", she played Rowena Leach. She also acted in lots of movies. Her character was Opal Ann in the 1970 movie "The Cheyenne Social Club".

Langdon was born in Paterson, New Jersey.
