Gremlins 2: The New Batch (video game)

Gremlins 2: The New Batch is a 1990 video game. This video game was based on the . It was developed for the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) and Game Boy. In the NES game, the player controls the character Gizmo through various levels in a building. Gizmo has weapons. The goal is to reach the Gremlin Control Centre to kill all the gremlins inside.
