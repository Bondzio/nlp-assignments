Tony Shalhoub

Anthony Marcus "Tony" Shalhoub (born October 9, 1953) is an American movie, stage, television, and voice actor. He is known for his roles as Adrian Monk in "Monk", Alexander Minion in the first three "Spy Kids" movie, and as Luigi in "Cars" and in "Cars 2". Shalhoub has also appeared in plays on stage. 

He was born in Green Bay, Wisconsin on October 9, 1953. Shalhoub is married to Brooke Adams since 1992 and they have two children.
