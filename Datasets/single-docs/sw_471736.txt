Simon Says

The game of Simon Says is played by children in many parts of the world. In the game, one player takes on the role of "Simon" and issues instructions to other players. Only instructions prefaced by "Simon says..." are to be followed. Players are eliminated if they follow any instruction that does not begin with "Simon says..." Examples would be instructions such as "Simon says touch your nose" or "Simon says jump on one foot". Players are eliminated until just one is left. The winner of the game is the player who has followed all the instructions correctly.
