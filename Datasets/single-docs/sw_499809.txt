Xavier Bosch i Sancho

Xavier Bosch i Sancho (born July 21, 1967 in Barcelona) is a Catalan writer and journalist. Bosch and Antoni Bassas created the program "Alguna pregunta més?" at Catalunya Ràdio. He received an Ondas award for that program. He worked at "La Vanguardia", "TV3" and "RAC 1" in sports. He was the director of the newspaper "Avui" from 2007 to 2008. In 2010, he founded a newspaper in Catalan, "Diari Ara". He wrote a trilogy whose first book ("Se sabrà tot") received a Sant Jordi Award in 2009. His second and third novels were "Homes d'honor" (2012) and "Eufòria". His last novel, "Algú com tu" (Someone like you, 2015), is winner of the Ramon Llull 2015 award.
