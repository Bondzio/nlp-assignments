Consuelo Moure

Consuelo Moure (1947 – 2 April 2014) was a Spanish-born Colombian actress. She was known for her roles in "Nochebuena", "Pena máxima", "La esquina", "Tres hombres y tres mujeres", "Es mejor ser rico que pobre" and in "Champagne". She was born in Pamplona.

Moure died on 2 April 2014 in Bogotá, Colombia from lung cancer, aged 67.
