Mohabbatein

Mohabbatein is a 2000 Hindi language musical romantic movie directed by Aditya Chopra and produced by Yash Chopra. The film has Amitabh Bachchan, Shahrukh Khan and Aishwarya Rai in the lead with six new debutants playing the supporting roles. The movie went to become superhit at the boxoffice. The movie also won two awards. 



The movie's music were done by Jatin Lalit while lyrics were written by Anand Bakshi. The music of the movie became the most sold album of 2000.
