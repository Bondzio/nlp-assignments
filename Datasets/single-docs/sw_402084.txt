Manuel Angel Martin Perez

Manuel Angel Martin Perez (born January 30, 1980 in Portugos) is a boccia player from Spain. He has a physical disability: He has cerebral palsy and is a BC2 type athlete. He competed at the 2008 Summer Paralympics. He played in the one person BC2 game and he finished thir. He competed at the 2012 Summer Paralympics in London, England.
