Erol Büyükburç

Erol Büyükburç (22 March 1936 - 12 March 2015) was a Turkish pop music composer and singer. In 1961, he composed his best known hit "Little Lucy". He also wrote the lyrics of this melody. He was born in Adana. 

Büyükburç was found dead in his home in İstanbul, Turkey. He was 78 years old. He died of a heart attack.
