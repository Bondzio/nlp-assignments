WWF SmackDown!

WWF SmackDown! is a professional wrestling video game developed by Yuke's and published by THQ. The game was known as Exciting Pro Wrestling in Japan. The Japanese version was published by Yuke's Future Media Creators.

It was released on March 2, 2000 in North America, April 2000 in Europe and on August 8, 2000 in Japan. The game was the first game in the WWF SmackDown! video game series.
