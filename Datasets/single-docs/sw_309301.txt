The Bad Girls Club (season 2)

The Bad Girls Club season 2 is the second season of "The Bad Girls Club". It aired on Oxygen on December 4, 2007. 

Season 2 had an average of 600,000 viewers per episode. The first season had 330,000 viewers per episode. The second season reunion was hosted by Star Jones.
