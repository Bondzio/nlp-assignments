S.L. Benfica

Sport Lisboa e Benfica, simply known as Benfica or SLB, which plays in Portugal and UEFA competitions.

Benfica has won 36 Portuguese Leagues, 29 Portuguese Cups, 7 League Cups, 7 Portuguese Supercups and 2 European Cup/UEFA Champions League titles. They also reached more 5 Champions League finals, 3 Europa League finals and 2 Intercontinental Cup finals.
