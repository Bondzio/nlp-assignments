Eyes Set to Kill

Eyes Set to Kill is an American post-hardcore band from Phoenix, Arizona. 

Sisters Alexia and Anissa Rodriguez started the band with Lindsey Vogt in 2003. In 2005 Caleb Clifton joined the band. In 2006 the band's first extended play was released. It sold 11,000 copies. Vogt left the band in 2007. Alexia took Vogt's place. The band released their first album "Reach" on February 19, 2008.
