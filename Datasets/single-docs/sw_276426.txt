Rick Nash

Rick Nash (born June 16, 1984 in Brampton, Ontario) is a Canadian professional ice hockey right winger for the Columbus Blue Jackets where he is the current team captain. He's currently playing for HC Davos. He was picked by the Blue Jackets with the first overall pick in the 2002 NHL Entry Draft. He played in the OHL with the London Knights.

He was on the Team Canada's men's ice hockey team in the 2010 Winter Olympics where they won the gold medal.
