Kurt Angle

Kurt Steven Angle (born March 9, 1968), is an American retired professional wrestler currently signed to WWE on the Raw brand, where he is the current on-screen general manager.

In 2006 he signed a contract to Total Nonstop Action Wrestling where he is a former TNA World Champion. His TNA contract expired in September 2014.

Angle won a gold medal at the 1996 Summer Olympics, winning with a broken neck. He is the only Olympic gold medalist in professional wrestling history. He wrestled for the WWE and WWF from 1998 to 2006. In February 2017, he announced his retirement from in-ring competition.

Angle returned to WWE on April 3, 2017, as the general manager of Raw.
