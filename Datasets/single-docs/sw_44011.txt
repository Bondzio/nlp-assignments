Automobiles of the 1920s

Automobiles have changed considerably since 1893 due to the more efficent ways for new cars to be developed. The car industry was thriving in the 1920s. There were many new types of cars. In the beginning of the 1920s many of the soldiers returning from World War I bought automobiles. People started to see that having a car would make traveling much easier. Soon almost every American family had a car. Ford was the big car maker but other companies were also big at the time. Ford cars, such as the Ford Model T, were popular because they were cheap and reliable. When new Ford models came out people would always get the newer version.
