Bob Rivers

Bob Rivers (born July 7, 1956) is an American radio actor and personality. He spent almost six years working at WAAF in Worcester, Massachusetts. He has also hosted at other radio stations in Seattle and in Baltimore.

One of the first parodies he produced was "Breakin' Up is Hard on You". That was about the Bell System Breakup. His 1987 album "Twisted Christmas" had the song "Twelve Pains of Christmas".

Rivers was born in Branford, Connecticut.
