Saint-Cyr-l'École

Saint-Cyr-l'École is a commune in the western suburbs of Paris, France. It is 21.4 km (13.3 mi) from the center of Paris.

In 2006, about 16,365 people lived here.

From 1993 to 1996, the main campus of the Service d'exploitation de la formation aéronautique was in the commune.
