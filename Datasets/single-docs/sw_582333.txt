Ray Wilson

Ramon "Ray" Wilson MBE (born 17 December 1934) is an English former footballer. He played at left back. He was a member of the England team that won the 1966 World Cup. In 1971, he managed the Bradford City football team. He was born in Shirebrook, Derbyshire.

Wilson lives in Slaithwaite near Huddersfield with his wife Pat. They have two children. In 2004, Wilson was diagnosed with Alzheimer's disease.
