Mega Man 9

Mega Man 9, known in Japan as , is a platform video game made by Capcom for the Wii's WiiWare service, PlayStation Network, and Xbox Live Arcade. It is the first game in the original "Mega Man" series to be made since the release of "Mega Man 8" for the PlayStation in 1996. It has the same graphics as the NES "Mega Mans".
