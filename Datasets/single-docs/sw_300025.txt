Yulia Takshina

Yulia Eugenyevna Takshina (; July 9, 1980) is a Russian stage and movie actress, former dancer.

Yulia dating with actor Grigory Antipenko since 2005. They have two sons together, Ivan Antipenko (born on June 28, 2007) and Fyodr Antipenko (born on July 3, 2009). Yulia also has step-son, Aleksandr Antipenko.
