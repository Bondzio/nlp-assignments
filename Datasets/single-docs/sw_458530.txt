Clifford Husbands

Sir Clifford Straughn Husbands, GCMG, KA, QC (born 5 August 1926) is a Barbadian politician. He was the Governor-General of Barbados. He held this office from 1996, when he was appointed after the death of Dame Nita Barrow, until he retired on 31 October 2011.
