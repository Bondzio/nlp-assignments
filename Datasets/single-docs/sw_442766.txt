Analeigh Tipton

Analeigh Christian Tipton (born November 9, 1988) is an American figure skater, actress, and fashion model. She became famous after coming third in Cycle 11 of "America's Next Top Model". She has also appeared in the movies "The Green Hornet" (2011), "Crazy, Stupid, Love" (2011), "Damsels in Distress" (2011), and "Warm Bodies" (2013).

Tipton was born in Minneapolis, Minnesota. She is engaged to longtime boyfriend Aaron McManus.
