Úrsula Murayama

Úrsula Murayama (born in Mexico in 1972) is a Mexican actress of Japanese origin who appears in telenovelas (soap operas). Her best known role was that of handmaiden Jacinta in telenovela "Esmeralda" (1997).

Her possible relative was actor Noé Murayama, who died in 1997. He appeared with Úrsula in "Esmeralda".
