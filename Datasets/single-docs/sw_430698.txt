Burlesque (movie)

Burlesque is a 2010 musical movie. It is about a woman losing her waitress job and then moving to Los Angeles. She gets a job dancing in a burlesque show. Cher plays Tess, Christina Aguilera plays Ali and Julianne Hough plays Georgia. The movie was sent to North American theaters just before Thanksgiving 2010. The ratings of the movie were mixed to negative. Its box office performance, however, was close to $90 million.
