I Love It

"I Love It" is a song by Swedish music duo Icona Pop. It first appeared on their album "Icona Pop" but was later released as part of their EP "Iconic" and their album "This Is... Icona Pop". British singer-songwriter Charli XCX sings on the song and co-wrote it. It reached the top ten around the world and reached number one on the UK Singles Chart. It was used as the theme song for the MTV "Jersey Shore" spinoff, "Snooki & JWoww".

On December 14, 2015, the song was certified quadruple-platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America for shipments and streams of over four million copies across the United States.
