Milwaukee

Milwaukee is the largest city in the state of Wisconsin. Milwaukee is also the 27th-largest in the UnitedÂ States. The city is the county seat of Milwaukee County. It is on the southwestern shore of Lake Michigan, and is about 90 miles (145 kilometers) north of Chicago. As of 2009, about 604,133 people lived in Milwaukee. Part of Milwaukee is in Washington County.

The city got its name from the Indian word "millioke", which is thought to mean "the good land" or "gathering place by the water".
