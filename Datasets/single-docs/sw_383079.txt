Big Brother (UK)

Big Brother is a reality television series in the United Kingdom which began in 2000. It is a version of the "Big Brother" franchise. Contestants must complete challenges while living in a house full of cameras. In some series the house is in London; in some series it is in Hertfordshire. In some series, the housemates are celebrities. From 2000-2010, it was shown on Channel 4; from 2011 onwards, it is shown on Channel 5.
