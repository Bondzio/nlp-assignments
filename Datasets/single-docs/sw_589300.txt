Valentín Pimstein

Valentín Pimstein Weiner (August 9, 1925 – June 27, 2017) was a Chilean producer of telenovelas. He was born in Santiago de Chile. He moved to Mexico, where he began to work in producing telenovelas. His most well known works are "María la del Barrio", "Marimar", and "Carrusel".

Pimstein died in Santiago de Chile from respiratory arrest on June 27, 2017 at the age of 91.
