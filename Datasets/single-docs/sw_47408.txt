Carmelo Anthony

Carmelo Kyam Anthony (born May 29, 1984 in Brooklyn, New York) is an American professional basketball small forward. He plays for the Los Angeles Clippers of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Anthony also played for the Denver Nuggets and the New York Knicks from 2003-2011 to 2017 until being traded to in a multi-player deal also to the Los Angeles Clippers.
