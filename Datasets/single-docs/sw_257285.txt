Keith Urban

Keith Lionel Urban (born October 26, 1967 in Whangarei, New Zealand) is a New Zealand-born Australian Grammy Award winning country music singer and songwriter. 

Urban is married to actress Nicole Kidman. They met at G'Day LA, a Hollywood event honoring Australians. The couple have two daughters: Sunday Rose Kidman Urban (born July 7, 2008) and Faith Margaret Kidman Urban (born December 28, 2010).
