Frankie Sandford

Francesca Sandford (born 14 January 1989) is a British pop singer. She was born in Upminster, London. She was a member of S Club 8 (2001-2005). Since 2007, she is a member of girl group The Saturdays.

Sandford had a relationship with Dougie Poynter from 2008-2010. She began a relationship with Wayne Bridge in 2010. The couple's son was born in 2013. The couple married in 2014.

Sandford was a contestant in the 2014 series of Strictly Come Dancing.
