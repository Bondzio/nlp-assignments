Changi Airport

Singapore Changi Airport , or simply Changi Airport, is a large airport in Asia, particularly in the Southeast Asian region, and is the main airport in Singapore. It is located in Changi on a site.

The airport is operated by the Civil Aviation Authority of Singapore (CAAS) and is the home base of Singapore Airlines, Singapore Airlines Cargo, SilkAir, Tiger Airways, Jetstar Asia Airways and Scoot.

In 2015, the airport handled a record of 55,448,964 passengers, a 2.5% increase over the previous year.
