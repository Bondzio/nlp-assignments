America's Next Top Model, Cycle 7

America's Next Top Model, Cycle 7 was the seventh season of the reality television series "America's Next Top Model". It started airing on 20 September 2006. It was the first season to be shown on The CW network.

 Amanda and Michelle Babin are identical twins, however they were judged individually.
