Émile Allegret

Émile Allegret (24 April 1907, Dijon – 22 November 1990, Vaux-sur-Mer) was a French soldier and member of the French Resistance during World War II. He graduated from the "École nationale de l'aviation civile" (French civil aviation university). After the war, he worked as an air navigation engineer.

Émile Allegret was a Commandeur of the Légion d'honneur.
