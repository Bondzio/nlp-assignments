Monikangana Dutta

Moni Kangana Dutta (born September 13) is an Indian supermodel and actress represented by IMG Models.

She has worked for Elle, Vogue, and countless magazines and fashion shows & brands. As for Vogue, "When Vogue called me I couldn't believe it, it's every girl's dream to be on the cover, and there I was with five of the most beautiful girls in the world!!"
