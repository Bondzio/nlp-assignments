Jean-Paul Belmondo

Jean-Paul Belmondo (born 9 April 1933) is a French actor. He is associated with the New Wave of the 1960s.

Belmondo is a "Commandeur" of the Ordre national du Mérite He is also "Commandeur" of the Légion d'honneur.

He received a Career Achievement award from the Los Angeles Film Critics Association Awards.
