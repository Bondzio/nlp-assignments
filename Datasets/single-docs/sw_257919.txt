Dale Jarrett

Dale Arnold Jarrett (born November 26, 1956) is a retired American racecar driver. Jarret used to drive in the NASCAR Sprint cup series. He won the 1999 Sprint cup championship and the 1993 Daytona 500. Jarrett has been called one of NASCAR's 50 greatest drivers. Dale Jarrett retired in 2008 and now does Commentary for ESPN.
