First inauguration of Richard Nixon

The first inauguration of Richard Nixon as the 37th President of the United States was held on January 20, 1969. 

The inauguration was the beginning of the term of Richard Nixon as President and Spiro Agnew as Vice President. 

Chief Justice Earl Warren gave the oath of office to Nixon. Senate Minority Leader Everett Dirksen gave the oath to Spiro Agnew as Vice President.
