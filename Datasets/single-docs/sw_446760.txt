Harvey Pekar

Harvey Lawrence Pekar (; October 8, 1939 â€“ July 12, 2010) was an American comic book writer, music critic and media personality. He is best known for his autobiographical "American Splendor" comic series. 

In 2003, the series inspired a well-received movie version of the same name.

Pekar was born in Cleveland, Ohio. He suffered from prostate cancer and clinical depression. He died in Cleveland Heights, Ohio from an accidental overdose of antidepressants fluoxetine and bupropion, aged 70.
