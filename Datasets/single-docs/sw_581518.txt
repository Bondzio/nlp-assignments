Risa Itō

Risa Itō(Japanese:伊藤 理佐 いとう りさ, Itō Risa, September 6 , 1969 - ) is a cartoonist and animator from Japan. Her main works are: is Hey Pitan!, Higepiyo, Oruchuban Ebichu("Ebichu Minds the House". Oruchuban Ebichu was produced as an animated TV program for Gainax.

She was born Hara town, Suwa District in Nagano Prefecture.
