Richard Karn

Richard Karn (born February 17, 1956) is an American actor and game show host. He is best known for acting as Al Borland in the 1990s ABC sitcom "Home Improvement", and for hosting "Family Feud" during the 2000s. He was born in Seattle, Washington.

Karn became host of "Family Feud" in September 2002. He left in 2006 and was replaced by John O'Hurley. He also hosted GSN's "Bingo America" in 2008. He replaced Patrick Duffy, who hosted the show for the first season.
