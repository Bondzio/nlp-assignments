David Brumbaugh

David Brumbaugh (December 2, 1960 â€“ April 15, 2017) was an American businessman and Republican politician from Oklahoma. 

Brumbaugh was a Representative in the Oklahoma House of Representatives for District 76, located in Broken Arrow, Oklahoma from 2011 until his death in 2017. He was Chairman for the Oklahoma Republican House Caucus which serves as the majority party in the Oklahoma House of Representatives.

Brumbaugh died on April 15, 2017 in Tulsa, Oklahoma, aged 56.
