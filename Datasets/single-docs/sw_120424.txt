Choa Saidanshah

Choa Saidan shah is a village and Union Council of Chakwal District in the Punjab Province of Pakistan. It is at 32°43'0N 72°58'60E. It is a named after a man who was a famous Sufi.Its famous for it spirngs and lakes.Choa Saidan Shah is one of the few villages that have all kinds of facities like Hospitals,Schools,Collages.
There are many famous people like Umar Haseeb who is a famous footballer.
The Awans tribe dominate in the area. Other tribes include Rajputs and Gujar.
