Loud (album)

Loud is the fifth studio album by Rihanna. It was released November 12, 2010. This record produced several hit songs, including number one hits "Only Girl (In the World)", "What's My Name?" and "S&M". The album received mostly positive reviews from the music critics.
The album has sold over 3 milion copies and was 5th on the "Billboard" 200. The album was more upbeat and happy compared to 2009's "Rated R" and features a more dance-pop and R&B sound. Several rap artists made an appearance on the album including Eminem, Drake and Nicki Minaj.
