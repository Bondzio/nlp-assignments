Madiswil

Madiswil is a municipality in the administrative district of Oberaargau in the canton of Berne in Switzerland.

On 1 January 2007 the former municipalities of Gutenburg merged into the municipality of Madiswil and On 1 January 2011 the former municipalities of Kleindietwil and Leimiswil merged into the municipality of Madiswil.
