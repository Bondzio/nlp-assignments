A.C. Maceratese

A.C. Maceratese is a football club which plays in Italy. European football might also be called soccer. A.C. Maceratese plays in the D league. Their stadium is called Stadio Helvia Racina. It can hold 5,846 people. The chairman is named Massimo Paci. He is the most important of everyone else who owns a part of the team. The manager is named Francesco Nocera. He makes most of the important decisions on the team.
