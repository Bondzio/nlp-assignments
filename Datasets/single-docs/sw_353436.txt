Trading Places (song)

"Trading Places" is a song by American singer Usher. It was released on October 17, 2008. It became the fifth and last single from his album "Here I Stand" (2008). Usher wrote the song with The-Dream and Carlos "Los Da Mystro" McKinney. It was produced by McKinney. The song is a slow-tempo song. It is an R&B and hip-hop song. The song talks about men trading roles with women in a relationship.
