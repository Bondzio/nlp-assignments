2008–09 Chicago Blackhawks season

The 2008–09 Chicago Blackhawks season is the team's 82nd season in the National Hockey League (NHL). 

The Blackhawks beat the Calgary Flames in the Conference Quarter-Finals 4 games to 2, and the Vancouver Canucks 4 games to 2 in the Conference Semi-Finals, but lost to the Detroit Red Wings in the Conference Finals 1 game to 4.
