Anton Nossik

Anton Borisovich Nossik (; July 4, 1966 â€“ July 9, 2017) was a Israeli-Russian start-up manager, journalist, social activist and popular Russian blogger (10th place in RuNet according to Yandex). 

Sometimes he is called one of the "fathers of the Russian Internet"or the first Russian-language blogger in the history. He was editor of the largest online news publications Vesti.ru, Lenta.ru, Gazeta.ru and NEWSru.com.

Nossik died from a heart attack at the night of 9 July 2017 near Moscow at the age of 51.
