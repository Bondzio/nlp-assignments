Valéry Giscard d'Estaing

Valéry Marie René Georges Giscard d'Estaing (; born 2 February 1926) is a French centrist politician. He is a member of the Constitutional Council of France. He served as President of France from 1974 until 1981.

He was born in Koblenz, Germany. d'Estaing once made-up a story about him having an affair with Diana, Princess of Wales.
