Yuri Maltsev

Yuri Nicholas Maltsev () is an Austrian school economist and economic historian from Tatarstan. Of Tatar origin, he is Professor of Economics at the Carthage College in Wisconsin and is a Senior Fellow of the Ludwig von Mises Institute in Auburn, Alabama. He has been a consultant for Chase Manhattan Bank and other companies on political and economic changes in Central and Eastern Europe. He also edited "Requiem for Marx".
