Ron Leibman

Ron Leibman (born October 11, 1937) is an American actor and educator. He is known for his role as Dr. Leonard Green in the famous comedy sitcom "Friends". He won an Drama Desk Award in 1993, an Emmy Award in 1979, and an Tony Award in 1993.

Leibman was born on October 11, 1937 in New York City, New York. He studied at Ohio Wesleyan University. Leibman was married to Linda Lavin from 1969 until they divorced in 1981. He has been married to Jessica Walter since 1983. He now lives in New York City, New York.
