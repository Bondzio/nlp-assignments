Kujataa

"Kujataa is a sub-arctic farming landscape located in the southern region of Greenland", says the website of UNESCO.

The first known example of agriculture (or farming) in the Arctic, is at Kujataa.

The oldest trace of the spreading of the Old Norse culture outside Europe, is at Kujataa.

Kujataa got its name on UNESCO's World Heritage List in 2017.
