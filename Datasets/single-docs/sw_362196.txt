Chris Hemsworth

Chris Hemsworth (born August 11, 1983) is an Australian movie, television, and voice actor. He is known for his role as Thor in "Thor", "The Avengers", and "Thor: The Dark World". Hemsworth was born on August 11, 1983 in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia. He has been married to Elsa Pataky since 2010. They have a daughter, Rose Hemsworth.
