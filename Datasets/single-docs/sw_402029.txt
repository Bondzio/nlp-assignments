Ignacio Avila Rodriguez (born January 19, 1979 in Manresa) is a track and field athlete from Spain. He has a disability: he is blind and is T12/B2 type athlete. He raced at the 2000 Summer Paralympics. He finished second in the 4 X 400 meter T11-T13 race. He raced at the 2004 Summer Paralympics. He raced at the 2008 Summer Paralympics. He finished third in the 1,500 meter race. He raced at the 2012 Summer Paralympics.
