Kirby: Planet Robobot

Kirby: Planet Robobot is an action platform video game. It was published by Nintendo. It was developed by HAL Laboratory. It was released for the Nintendo 3DS on April 28, 2016 in Japan and on June 10, 2016 worldwide.

The game was first revealed during a Nintendo Direct on March 3, 2016.

Copy abilities make a return in the series. New copy abilities include "Doctor", "Poison", and "ESP". Kirby can also enter robotic bodies, which can make use of his copy ability perform a stronger attack based on that power.

A multiplayer mode called "Team Kirby Clash" is included. Up to four players can use different copy abilities and powers similar to classes in most RPGs, before working together to defeat bosses.
