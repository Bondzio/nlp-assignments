Kevin McNally

Kevin McNally (born 27 April 1956) mostly known as Kevin R. McNally is an English movie, television, stage, voice, and radio actor who is mostly known for his role as Joshamee Gibbs in all four of the Pirates of the Caribbean movies. McNally has also appeared in many movies such as; "Valkyrie", "Scoop", "Spice World", "Johnny English", "Stalin", "Conspiracy", "Tuesday", and has recently appeared in the 2012 movie "The Raven". McNally was born in Bristol, England. McNally is currently married to Phyllis Logan since 1977, they have a son together.
