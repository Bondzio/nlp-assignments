Antony Booth

Anthony George Booth (born 9 October 1931, later known as Tony and Antony) is a retired English actor. He is best known for his role as Mike Rawlins in the BBC series "Till Death Us Do Part".

Booth is the father-in-law of former Prime Minister of the United Kingdom Tony Blair. Blair is married to Booth's daughter Cherie Booth.

Booth suffered a stroke in 2010. He was diagnosed with Alzheimer's disease in 2004.
