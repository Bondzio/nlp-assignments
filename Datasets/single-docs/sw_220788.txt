Homer's Odyssey

"Homer's Odyssey" is the third full length episode of "The Simpsons", that first started on the Fox network on January 21, 1990. In this episode, Homer becomes a for people's safety in Springfield, and is made a higher rank in his current job as "Nuclear Safety Inspector" for the entire power plant. It was written by Jay Kogen and Wallace Wolodarsky and was the first "Simpsons" script to be completed, although it was the third episode produced.
