Giorgio Albertazzi

Giorgio Albertazzi (20 August 1923 â€“ 28 May 2016) was an Italian actor movie director, screenwriter and singer. 

He was known for his roles in "Little World of Don Camillo", "Concert of Intrigue", "White Nights", "Last Year at Marienbad", "Eva", "Redhead", "I Married You for Fun", "The Assassination of Trotsky", "Five Women for the Killer", "Mark of the Cop", "Once a Year, Every Year", "Li chiamarono... briganti!", "The Rage" and in "Some Say No".

His best known directed movies were "La governante" and "Gradiva". He sang the soundtrack for "Gli angeli del potere".

Albertazzi died on 28 May 2016 in Roccastrada, Italy, aged 92.
