Komla Dumor

Komla Afeke Dumor (3 October 1972 â€“ 18 January 2014) was a Ghanaian journalist. He was born in Accra, Ghana. His grandfather was Philip Gbeho, composer of the Ghanaian national anthem. As of June 2012, Dumor was the main presenter of the BBC World News programme "Focus on Africa".

Dumor died suddenly on 18 January 2014, in London after suffering cardiac arrest.
