Joanne Linville

Joanne Linville (born January 15, 1928) is an American Jewish actress. She acted in a lot of television series and movies. Her television work includes: "Gunsmoke", "Have Gun Will Travel" and "Alfred Hitchcock Presents". Her movies include "The Goddess" and "Gable and Lombard".

Linville was born in Bakersfield, California. She was married to director Mark Rydell from 1962 until they divorced in 1973.
