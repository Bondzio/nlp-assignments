Geoffrey Bayldon

Geoffrey Bayldon (7 January 1924 – 10 May 2017) was a British actor. He is known for having the lead role in "Catweazle" (1970–72). Bayldon denied the role of both the First and Second Doctors in the long-running BBC science fiction television series "Doctor Who". He was born in Leeds, West Riding of Yorkshire.

Bayldon died on 10 May 2017 in London at the age of 93. His partner of many years, fellow actor Alan Rowe, predeceased him in 2000.
