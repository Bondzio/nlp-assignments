Philadelphia Union

Philadelphia Union is a soccer team that plays in Major League Soccer in Chester, Pennsylvania. It was founded in 2008. The team began play in 2010 as a member of the Eastern Conference. They play their home games at the Talen Energy Stadium in Chester, Pennsylvania.

The club's colors are gold and black.

In August 2015, the team announced that they would have a reserve team in Bethlehem, Pennsylvania that will compete in the United Soccer League (USL) starting in 2016. That team would be Bethlehem Steel FC.
