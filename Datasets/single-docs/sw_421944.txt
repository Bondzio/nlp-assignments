Peggy Stewart

Peggy Stewart (born Margaret O'Rourke; June 5, 1923) is an American movie actress. She starred mostly in B-movies during the 1930s and 1940s. Her sister, Patricia O'Rourke, was an Olympic swimmer and was married to World War II hero and B-movie actor Wayne Morris.

Stewart was born on June 5, 1923 in West Palm Peach, Florida. She was married to Don Barry from 1940 until they divorced in 1944. Then she was married to Buck Young from 1953 until his death 2000. Stewart now lives in Los Angeles, California.

Stewart appeared in the 2012 Adam Sandler movie "That's My Boy".
