Michael York

Michael York, OBE (born Michael Hugh Johnson; March 27, 1942) is an English-American television, stage, voice, and movie actor. York has a career that has spanned for 47 years. York has appeared in many movies such as; "Justine", "The Guru", "Murder on Orient Express", "Logan's Run", "Fedora", "True Women", "Austin Powers Movie Series", and in "Transformers: Revenge of the Fallen". 

York was born in Fulmer, Buckinghamshire, England and is a naturalized American citizen. He studied at Bromley Grammar School for Boys and at the University College, Oxford. York has been married to Patricia McCallum since 1965. He has a stepson.

In 2013, York announced he is suffering from amyloidosis and this has affected his voice and face.
