Amo Houghton

Amory "Amo" Houghton, Jr. (born August 7, 1926) is an American politician from the U.S. state of New York. He is a retired member of the United States House of Representatives serving from 1987 through 2005. He was a member of one of upstate New York's most well known families in business, the Houghton family. He was a member of the Republican Party.

Houghton was born in Corning, New York. He studied at Harvard University. He was married to Priscilla Dewey until her death in 2012.
