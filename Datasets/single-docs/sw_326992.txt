Institut supérieur européen de gestion group

Institut supérieur européen de gestion group (ISEG group) is a group of three French higher education schools (ISEG Business school, ISEG Finance school, ISEG Marketing and Communication school) created in 1980.. The group is in seven places, in Paris, Bordeaux, Nantes, Lille, Lyon, Strasbourg and Toulouse (France). ISEG group delivers Bachelor's degree and Master's degree.

ISEG group's main area is entrepreneurship.
