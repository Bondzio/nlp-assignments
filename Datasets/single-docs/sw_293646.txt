Falmouth

Falmouth (meaning mouth of the river Fal) is a town on the south coast of Cornwall. The A39 road links Falmouth with north Devon. In 2001 there were 20,775 people living in Falmouth. Falmouth harbour is a large natural harbour and Carrick Roads are nearby. 

The MP for Falmouth is Sarah Newton, a Tory. The school there is Falmouth School.
