Steve Buscemi

Steven Vincent Buscemi (born December 13, 1957) is an American actor. He was born in Brooklyn, New York City. He is probably best known for his roles as Mr. Pink in "Reservoir Dogs", Carl Showalter in "Fargo", and Seymour in "Ghost World". With a long career, he has worked in several independent movies and commercial movies, just as TV series like "The Sopranos" and "The Simpsons". Buscemi has directed four feature movies. Busemi has been married to Jo Andres since 1987. They have a son, Lucian Buscemi.
