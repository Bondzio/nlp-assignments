Arrondissement of Marmande

The arrondissement of Marmande is an arrondissement of France. It is part of the Lot-et-Garonne "département" in the Nouvelle-Aquitaine region. Its capital, and subpréfecture of the department, is the city of Marmande.

When the Lot-et-Garonne department was created on 17 February 1800, the "arrondissement" of Marmande was part of that original department.

The "arrondissement" is in the northwest of Lot-et-Garonne and is bordered to the north by the Dordogne department, to the east by the "arrondissement" of Villeneuve-sur-Lot, to the southeast by the "arrondissement" of Agen, to the south by the "arrondissement" of Nérac and to the west by the Gironde department.
