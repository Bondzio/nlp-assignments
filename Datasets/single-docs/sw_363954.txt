Kuch Kuch Hota Hai

Kuch Kuch Hota Hai (translation: "Something Happens") is a 1998 Bollywood movie directed by Karan Johar. The movie has Shahrukh Khan, Kajol, Rani Mukherji in the main roles while Salman Khan makes a friendly appearance. The movie has music done by Jatin Lalit while lyrics are written by Sameer. 


The music of the movie became very popular and was the most sold music album of 1998. 

The movie won many awards at the award functions. 

The movie became all time blockbuster at the box office.
