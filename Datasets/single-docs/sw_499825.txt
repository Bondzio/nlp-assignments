Lars Tunbjörk

Lars Tunbjörk (15 February 1956 – 8 April 2015) was a Swedish photographer. He was known for taking pictures of the Swedish landscape and landmarks. He was born in Borås, Sweden.

Tunbjörk died on 8 April 2015. His death was announced on 11 April. No cause of death was given. He was 59 years old.
