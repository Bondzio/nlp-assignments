Nika Futterman

Nika Futterman (born October 25, 1969; New York City, New York) is an American actress, singer, voice artist, and comedienne. She is the voice of Rosie in the "Open Season" series, Stamps in "Back at the Barnyard", one of the dung beetles in "The Wild", Lady Jaye in "", Chum Chum in "Fanboy and Chum Chum", and Lola Caricola in "CatDog".

She did the vocals "Give it to me, baby" in The Offspring hit single "Pretty Fly (for a White Guy)."
