Juno (spacecraft)

Juno is an unmanned NASA New Frontiers spacecraft. It will orbit and explore the planet Jupiter. The spacecraft was launched on August 5, 2011 from the Cape Canaveral Air Force Station. Scott Bolton is leading the mission.

Juno's main goal is to understand the origin and evolution of Jupiter. Juno will also study the planet's atmospheric composition, gravity field, magnetic field, and polar magnetosphere.
