Out of the Woods (song)

"Out of the Woods" is a song co-written and performed by American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift. It is the sixth single from her album, "1989". It was written by Swift and Jack Antonoff from the American alternative band Fun. The two produced the song along with Swedish songwriter and producer Max Martin. The song is the fourth track on the album. It became her sixth top-20 hit in a row on the US "Billboard" Hot 100.

The music video for the song was released on December 31, 2015, live on "Dick Clark's New Year's Rockin' Eve with Ryan Seacrest" on ABC stations in the United States.
