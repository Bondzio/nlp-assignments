Judy Holliday

In "Born Yesterday" (both stage and movie versions), she played Billie Dawn. She received an Academy Award for Best Actress and a Golden Globe Award for Best Actress for this performance. She won a Tony Award for her performance in "Bells are Ringing".

Holliday was born in New York City. She also died there from throat cancer, age 43.
