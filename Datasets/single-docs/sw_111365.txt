Mario Kart: Super Circuit

Mario Kart: Super Circuit is a racing game for the Game Boy Advance released in 2001 and later in 2011 for the Virtual Console on the Nintendo 3DS. 

It has characters from the Mario series of video games. The game contains all the courses from "Super Mario Kart" along with newcoming courses. It can be played single player (modes: Grand Prix, Extra [Super Mario Kart], Quick Run, and Time Trials) multiplayer (modes: Grand Prix, "Link It Up!", V.S., and Battle)
