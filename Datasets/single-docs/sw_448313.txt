Peter Scoones

Peter Scoones (27 October 1937 â€“ 20 April 2014) was an Emmy Award-winning British underwater cameraman. Scoones is known for his vivid work on the oceans. His work inspired many people to dive and many divers to take up underwater photography. Scoones dived since the 1950s and continued so until his death in 2014. 

Scoones died on 20 April 2014 at the age of 76. He is survived by wife, son, and daughter.
