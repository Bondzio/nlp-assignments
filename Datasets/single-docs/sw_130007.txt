Tarot

The Тarot (often Тarot cards) are a set of seventy-eight cards, comprising twenty-two Тrump cards, and four suits of fourteen cards each with four Face cards. In the English speaking world, Тarot cards are mostly used for divination. Tarot cards are also used in many European countries for playing trick taking card games.
