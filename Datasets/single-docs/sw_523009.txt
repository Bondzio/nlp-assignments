Varduhi Varderesyan

Varduhi Varderesyan (; 19 March 1928 â€“ 24 November, 2015) was an Romanian-born Armenian actress. She was born in Bucharest, Romania. In 2002 she was declared an honorary citizen of Yerevan. She was known for her roles in "Looking for the Addressee", "Northern Rainbow", "Hayrik", and in "The Journey".

Varderesyan died in Yerenvan, Armenia at the age of 87.
