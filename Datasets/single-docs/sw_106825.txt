Sam Allardyce

Sam Allardyce (born: 19 October 1954) is an English football manager. He is the manager of Crystal Palace. He has also been the manager of Blackpool, Notts County, Bolton Wanderers, Newcastle United, West Ham United and the England National Football Team. He used to play football, and played for Bolton Wanderers, Sunderland, Millwall, Tampa Bay Rowdies, Coventry City, Huddersfield Town, Preston North End and West Bromwich Albion.
