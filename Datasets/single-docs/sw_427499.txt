No Ordinary Love

"No Ordinary Love" is a 1992 ballad. It is from the album "Love Deluxe" by singer Sade. It was in the 1993 movie "Indecent Proposal". The song hit #26 in Ireland, United Kingdom and Scotland. In June 1993, the song became a hit across the world. Sade won a Grammy Award for this ballad in early 1994. The full ballad is seven minutes and 21 seconds in length. The single edit is five minutes and 22 seconds.
