Martin Luther, Heretic (1983 movie)

Martin Luther, Heretic was a movie made in 1983, which was the year of the 500th birthday of Martin Luther. The movie starred Jonathan Pryce as Martin Luther and Maurice Denham as Johann von Staupitz.

The movie begins in the year 1506, while Luther was a Monk. The movie ends in the year 1522, when Luther returns from his exile at the Wartburg Castle in 1522 to Wittenberg.
