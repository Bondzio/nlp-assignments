Cars Toons: Mater's Tall Tales

Cars Toons: Mater's Tall Tales is an American animated short television series by Pixar, featuring the characters Mater and Lightning McQueen from the movie "Cars". The series' first broadcast was on October 27, 2008, on Toon Disney, and Disney Channel. The show was then moved to Disney Junior in 14 February 2011.


A video game based on the show was released on 19 October 2010 for the Wii.
