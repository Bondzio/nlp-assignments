Sid Haig

Sid Haig (born July 14, 1939) is an American actor. He is known for starring in two Rob Zombie movies "House of 1000 Corpses" and "The Devil's Rejects".

Haig was born on July 14, 1939 in Fresno, California. He studied at the Pasadena Playhouse. Haig has been married to Susan L. Oberg since 2007.
