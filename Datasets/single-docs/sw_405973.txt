Paris-Saint-Lazare railway station

The Paris-Saint-Lazare railway station is one of the most important railway stations in Paris. It is in the eighth arrondissement of Paris. It was built between 1842 and 1853. It serves the major cities of Normandy, but also the western suburbs and northwest of Paris.

It is served by metro lines 3, 9, 12, 13, and 14.
