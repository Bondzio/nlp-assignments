Jorge Mendoza

Jorge Mendoza Parrado (born June 15, 1972 in Barcelona) is a goalball athlete and track and field athlete from Spain. He has a disability: he is blind and is a type B1 goalball player. He played goalball at the 1996 Summer Paralympics. His team was third. He was as the 2000 Summer Paralympics in Sydney, Australia. He threw the discus and javelin.
