Sex and the City

Sex and the City is an American television romantic sitcom created by Darren Star and produced by HBO. It broadcast from 1998 until 2004. 

The original run of the show had a total of 94 episodes. Throughout its six-year run, the show received help from various producers, writers and directors.

The series starred Sarah Jessica Parker, Kim Cattrall, Kristin Davis, and Cynthia Nixon. It is set in New York City.

The series soon became into two feature movies, "Sex and the City" (2008) and "Sex and the City 2" (2010).
