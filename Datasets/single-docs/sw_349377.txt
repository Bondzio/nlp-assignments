Franck Goldnadel

Franck Goldnadel, born the 14th of August 1969, is a French aerospace engineer. Since the 1st of March 2011, he is head of Paris-Charles de Gaulle Airport.

He graduated from the French Civil Aviation University (Promotion 1990). He has worked for 15 years in air transport before being nominated head of Paris-Charles de Gaulle Airport.
