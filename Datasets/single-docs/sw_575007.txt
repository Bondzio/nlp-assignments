Stephen Kearney

Stephen Peter Kearney (born 11 June 1972) is a New Zealand professional rugby league coach and former player. He is the head coach of National Rugby League (NRL) team, the New Zealand Warriors. Before this, he was the head coach of the New Zealand national team and the Parramatta Eels. He also played for the Warriors and Melbourne Storm in the 1990s and 2000s.
