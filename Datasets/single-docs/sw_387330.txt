Metroid: Other M

Metroid: Other M is an action-adventure video game published by Team Ninja and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo Wii console. It is the latest game in the "Metroid" series. It was first announced at E3 2009. It was released on August 30, 2010 in North America, on September 2, 2010 in Japan, and on September 3, 2010 in Europe.

"Metroid: Other M" plays in both first person and third-person. It takes place between "Super Metroid" and "Metroid Fusion".
