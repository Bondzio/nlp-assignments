Tik Tok

"Tik Tok" (styled "TiK ToK") is the debut solo single by American singer-songwriter Kesha. It is from her debut studio album, "Animal". The song topped charts worldwide, including countries like the UK, Canada, Australia, and the US, where it was the best-performing song of 2010. It is Kesha's second single after the number-one single "Right Round" by rapper Flo Rida; her vocals are not credited on this song.
