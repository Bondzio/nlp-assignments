Colleges and universities in Kansas

This is a list of colleges and universities in the U.S. state of Kansas.

The Kansas Board of Regents runs six state universities, as well as 19 community colleges, five technical colleges, six technical schools and a municipal university. The Board also lets private and out-of-state institutions to operate in Kansas with a Certificate of Approval that is renewed every year. These schools teach business trade, technical, or industrial occupations that lead to a certificate, diploma, or academic degree.
