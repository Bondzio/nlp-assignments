Alexander Dennis Enviro200

The Alexander Dennis Enviro200 (previously known as TransBus Enviro200) is a single decker low-floor midibus. It was built by Alexander Dennis in three generations starting in 2003. The bus was first built by TransBus International until the company was sold to Alexander Dennis.

The first generation TransBus Enviro200 was launched in 2003.

The second generation Enviro200 was launched in August 2006.

The third generation Enviro200, known as the Enviro200 MMC (short for Major Model Change), was launched in May 2015.
