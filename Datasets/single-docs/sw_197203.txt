Med Hondo

Med Hondo (born Mohamed Abid Hondo, 1936) is a Mauritanian movie director, producer, screenwriter, actor and voice actor. He moved to France in 1959 and began to work in movies during the 1960s. In 1967, critics praised for the first movie he directed, "Soleil O". It won a Golden Leopard Award at the Locarno International Film Festival in Italy. When American movies are dubbed into French, Hondo often plays the French version of a character. For example, he played the French version of Eddie Murphy's character Donkey in "Shrek".
