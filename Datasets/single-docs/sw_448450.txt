Blöndulón

Blöndulón is one of Iceland's largest lakes. It was created in between the years of 1984 and 1991. It has a greatest depth of and is nearly above sea level.

It is found near the Kjölur highland road in the Highlands of Iceland. It was made as a reservoir for the Blönduvirkjun power plant. The lake is often used for salmon fishing. Blöndulón has been the sight of erosion.
