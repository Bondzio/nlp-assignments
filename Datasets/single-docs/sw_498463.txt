Wajid Khan

Wajid Khan (b. March 10, 1981) is an Indian painter, nail artist. He is the first ever artist to acquire the patent for Nail Art. Wajid was named in the Guinness Book of World Records, Golden Book of World Records, Limca Book of Records, Asia Book of Records and India Book of Records. His nail portraits include Mary (mother of Jesus), Jesus, Dhirubhai Ambani and Mahatma Gandhi.
