Ángel Di Maria

Angel di Maria (born 14 February 1988) is an Argentine professional footballer. He plays for french league Ligue 1 club Paris Saint-Germain F.C. and the Argentina national team. He can play as either a winger or attacking midfielder.

After beginning his career with Rosario Central, Di María moved to Europe in 2007 to play for Benfica of Portugal. He earned a €25 million move to Real Madrid three years later. After winning the Champions League with Real Madrid, he signed for Manchester United in 2014 for a national record £59.7 million. 
In summer 2015, he signed a 4 year contract with Paris Saint-Germain F.C. for €63 million. 

A full international for Argentina since 2008, Di María has earned over 50 caps for his country. He scored the goal that won the country first place at the 2008 Olympics, and has also represented them at two World Cups and the 2011 Copa América.
