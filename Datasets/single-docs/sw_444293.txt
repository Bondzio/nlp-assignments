Salman Khan

Salman Khan (pronunciation : ; born Abdul Rashid Salim Salman Khan on 27 December 1965) is an Indian actor, producer, television presenter, and philanthropist. Khan mostly appears in Hindi movies. 

Khan was sentenced to a five-year imprisonment on 6 May 2015 from running over 5 people, killing 1, with his car. On 8 May 2015, Khan was granted bail.

Salman Khan was born in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India. His parents are veteran his first wife Sushila Charak (later adopted the name Salma Khan) and Salim Khan.
