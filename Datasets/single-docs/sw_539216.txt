Lola Novaković

Zorana "Lola" Novaković (25 April 1935 – 3 April 2016) was a Serbian singer. She was popular during the 1960s and the 1970s. She was born in Belgrade, Yugoslavia. 

She was known for having represented FPR Yugoslavia at Eurovision Song Contest in 1962 where she finished fourth.

In 1962, she starred in "Šeki snima, pazi se", a full-length comedy movie.

She died a few weeks before her 81st birthday on 3 April 2016.
