P. J. Byrne

Paul Jeffrey "P. J." Byrne (born December 15, 1974) is an American movie and television actor. Byrne is known for his roles in movies such as in "Horrible Bosses", "Final Destination 5", "The Campaign" and "The Wolf of Wall Street". He is known for his television roles such as his role in "The Game" and his starring role in "Intelligence".
