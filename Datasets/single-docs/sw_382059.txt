The Lion King's Timon and Pumbaa

The Lion King's Timon and Pumbaa is an animated Disney television series. It is based off The Lion King movie. The series was shown from September 16, 1995 to 1999. It had a total of 86 episodes.


Five VHS cassettes contaning 34 episodes of the series were released in Chile.
