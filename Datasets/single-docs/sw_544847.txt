Corgémont

Corgémont is a municipality in the administrative district of Jura bernois in the canton of Bern in Switzerland. It is located in the French-speaking part of the canton. Corgémont is in the Jura mountains.

Corgémont has a population of 1,650 as of 2014. There are 689 families living in Corgémont. Males make up 50.1% of the population while females make up 49.9%. The average age is 41.6 years old.
