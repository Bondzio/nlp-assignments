Lord Brocktree

Lord Brocktree is a fantasy book written by Brian Jacques in 2000. It is the thirteenth book in the "Redwall" series. Lord Brocktree was also translated into French.

A badger Lord named Brocktree is a strong badger with a mighty sword. With the help of friends Lord Brocktree takes back the mountain fortress of Salamandastron from Ungatt Trunn the Wildcat Warlord and his vermin horde.
