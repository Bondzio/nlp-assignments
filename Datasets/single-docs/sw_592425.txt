Yahya Abdul-Mateen II

Yahya Abdul-Mateen II (born 1987) is an American actor. He is best known for his role Cadillac in the television series "The Get Down". He also starred in "Baywatch" (2017). He will appear in "The Greatest Showman" and DC's "Aquaman" as Black Manta.

Abdul-Mateen was born in New Orleans, Louisiana. Before acting, Abdul-Mateen was an architect.
