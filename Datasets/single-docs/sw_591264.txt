Français fondamental

Français fondamental is a simplified version of French language with a view to teaching French to foreigners and the populations of the French Union, while France wanted to improve the diffusion of its language in the world. A series of surveys conducted in the 1950s and 1960s show that a limited number of words are used in the usual way, both orally and in writing and in all circumstances, As well as a limited number of grammatical rules necessary for the functioning of the language.
