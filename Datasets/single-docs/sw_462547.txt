City Slickers

City Slickers is a 1991 western-comedy-drama movie. It stars Billy Crystal, Daniel Stern, Bruno Kurby, Patricia Wetting, Jeffrey Tambor, Phill Lewis, and Jack Palance. For his performance as Curly Washburn, Palance won an Academy Award for his role.

This movie got mostly positive review from critics. It was released on June 7, 1991.
