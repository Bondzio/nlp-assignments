Vampires Suck

Vampires Suck is a 2010 American parody movie directed and written by Jason Friedberg and Aaron Seltzer. The movie is a parody of the vampire movie genre but is mostly a parody of Stephenie Meyers series, "Twilight". The movie was be released in North America on August 8, 2010, August 26, 2010 in Australia and October 15, 2010 in the United Kingdom. "Vampires Suck" achieved $4 million at the box office.
