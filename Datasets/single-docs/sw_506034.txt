Daria

Daria was an animated series. It was a spin-off of Beavis and Butt-head. It centered around teenager Daria Morgendorffer. It was set in a fictional universe in Lawndale. The show started airing on January 1, 1997. The last episode was shown on January 21, 2002. It's been recently announced that the show may be revived.
