Preston Wamsley

Preston Maki Wamsley (born April 29, 1986) is an American television actor.

Wamsley is best known for his recurring role as "Ronnie Wilson"" on the 1990s sitcom, "The Hughleys". Wamsley has also made appearances on other sitcoms including "My Wife and Kids", "The Nanny", and "Beyond Belief: Fact or Fiction".
