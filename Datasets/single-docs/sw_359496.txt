Julianna Margulies

Julianna Margulies (born June 8 1966) is an Emmy and Golden Globe award winning actress. She has received more Screen Actors Guild Awards than any other actress or actor. She played Carol Hathaway in the long-running series "ER" (1994 to 2002) and Alicia in the CBS legal drama "The Good Wife". She also performed in sitcoms and theatrical movies. She was raised in New York.
