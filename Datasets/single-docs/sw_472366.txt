Armageddon (2000)

Armageddon (2000) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by World Wrestling Federation (WWF). It was held on December 10, 2000 at Birmingham-Jefferson Civic Center in Birmingham, Alabama. It was the second Armageddon event held by the WWF. It was the only WWF pay-per-view event to be held in the state of Alabama.

The main event was a six-man Hell in a Cell for Kurt Angle's WWF Championship. Kurt Angle defeated Stone Cold Steve Austin, Triple H, The Undertaker, Rikishi, and The Rock to retain his championship.
