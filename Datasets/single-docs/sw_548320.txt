Kazimieras Uoka

Kazimieras Uoka (March 4, 1951 â€“ July 16, 2016) was a Lithuanian politician. He was known for being a signatory of the 1990 Act of the Re-Establishment of the State of Lithuania. 

He was born in Kaunas. Uoka graduated from Vilnius University in 1976. He was a leader of the labor movement in the early years of the independence movement.

Uoka died on July 16, 2016 in Vilnius, aged 65.
