Acetonitrile

Acetonitrile (also known as ACN) is a kind of chemical compound with formula CHCN. It is a clear liquid and is the simplest molecule in a group called nitriles. Acetonitrile is made when plastics are being made so is quite cheap. Chemists use it as a solvent for chemical reactions and in cromatography.

Acetonitrile is flammable and can hurt our eyes.
