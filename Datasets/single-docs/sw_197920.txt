Bragging Rights (2009)

Bragging Rights (2009) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view (PPV) show made by World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE), that took place on October 25, 2009 at the Mellon Arena in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. It was the first Bragging Rights event held by the WWE and was created to replace Cyber Sunday in the late October slot of WWE's pay-per-view calendar.
