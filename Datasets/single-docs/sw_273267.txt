Potassium periodate

Potassium periodate is a chemical compound. Its chemical formula is KIO. It has potassium and periodate ions in it. 

Potassium periodate is a white solid. It is an oxidizing agent. It is similar to potassium perchlorate. It does not dissolve good in water. It makes potassium iodate and oxygen when heated. 

It is made by reacting potassium iodide with a strong oxidizing agent.

It is used to find out how much potassium is in something because it is one of the few potassium compounds that do not dissolve easily in water.
