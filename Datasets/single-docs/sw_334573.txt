Michel Bernard

Michel Bernard, born the 1st of March 1943 at Bourges, graduates from "École Polytechnique" (promotion 1964) and "École nationale de l'aviation civile" (promotion 1967), was the head of the "Agence nationale pour l'emploi" from 1995 to 2005 and the President of Air Inter airline from November 1993 until march 1995.
