Johann Andreas Wagner

Johann Andreas Wagner (1797 – 1861) was a German palaeontologist, zoologist and archaeologist. 

Wagner was a professor at the University of Munich. He was also the of the Zoologische Staatssammlung (the German State Zoology Collection).
He was the author of "Die Geographische Verbreitung der Säugethiere Dargestellt" (1844-46).
