Per Lillo-Stenberg

Per Lillo-Stenberg (21 June 1928 â€“ 27 March 2014) was a Norwegian actor. His best known roles are in the movies "Olsenbanden gir seg aldri!" (1981) and "Olsenbandens siste stikk" (1999). He was born in Oslo. He and his wife Mette Lange-Nielsen had a son, Lars Lillo-Stenberg, a musician for deLillos.

Lillo-Stenberg died after a short illness on 27 March 2014 in Levanger. He was 85 years old.
