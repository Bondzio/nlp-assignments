Choi Soo-young

Choi Soo-young (; Hanja: ; Japanese: チェ・スヨン; born February 10, 1990) is a South Korean singer-songwriter, actress and TV presenter. She is a former member of the Korean-Japanese duo group "Route θ" and a current member of the South Korean girl group Girls' Generation. Sooyoung is the only Asian woman in "Complex's" Hottest '90s Babies.
