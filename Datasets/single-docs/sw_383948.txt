The Legend of Zelda: Collector's Edition

The Legend of Zelda: Collector's Edition is a 2002 compilation video game for the Nintendo GameCube game console. It includes four games from the "The Legend of Zelda" series. The games included are "The Legend of Zelda", "", "", and "". It also includes a 20-minute demo of "" and two "The Legend of Zelda"-related videos. It was originally released as a pre-order bonus for "The Legend of Zelda: The Wind Waker" in Japan and North America. It was later released in a GameCube bundle in North America and Europe in 2003.
