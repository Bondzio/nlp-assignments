Tony Haygarth

Anthony "Tony" Haygarth (4 February 1945 â€“ 10 March 2017) was an English television, movie and theatre actor. He was known for his roles in "Chicken Run" and "Fakers". In 2008, he played Alfred Doolittle in the Old Vic's Production of "Pygmalion". In November 2008 he joined the cast of "Emmerdale" as Mick Naylor. 

Haygarth died from Alzheimer's disease on 10 March 2017 in Royal Tunbridge Wells, Kent, aged 72.
