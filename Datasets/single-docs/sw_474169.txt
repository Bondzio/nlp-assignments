Gerhard Louis De Geer

Gerhard Louis De Geer, born 27 November 1854, dead 25 February 1935, was a Swedish politician. He served in the first chamber of the "Swedish parliament" between 1901-1914. He was also the governor of Kristianstad County betwween 1905-1923. Also, he served as the Prime Minister of Sweden between 1920-1921.
