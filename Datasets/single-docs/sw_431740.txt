Leopard danio

The leopard danio ("Brachydanio frankei") is a freshwater fish. They are native to India.

The Leopard danio is an active fish that likes to live in shoals (groups). They are peaceful fish and are often kept in community fish tanks. They grow to about 6 cm and like to live in a water temperature of about 18–24°C.

Leopard danios are omnivores and will eat bloodworms, brine shrimp and flake foods.
