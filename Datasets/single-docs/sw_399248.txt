Bob Gunton

Robert Patrick "Bob" Gunton, Jr. (born November 15, 1945) is an American actor. He is known for his role as Warden Samuel Norton in the 1994 movie "The Shawshank Redemption".

Gunton was born on November 15, 1945 in Santa Monica, California. He studied at Mater Dei High School and at University of California, Irvine. Gunton was married to Annie McGreevey from 1980 until they divorced in 2006. Then he was married to Carey Pitts since 2006. He has one child with McGreevey.
