Dhadkan

Dhadkan () is a Bollywood romantic drama movie directed by Dharmesh Darshan. The movie has Akshay Kumar, Shilpa Shetty, Sunil Shetty and Mahima Chaudhry in the main roles. The movie's music was done by Nadeem-Shravan.


The music of the movie went to become very popular of that year. It was also the second most sold Bollywood music album.
