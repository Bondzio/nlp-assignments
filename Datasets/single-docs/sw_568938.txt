Richard DeVos

Richard Marvin DeVos Sr. (born March 4, 1926) is an American businessman. He is known for being the co-founder of Amway along with Jay Van Andel (company restructured as Alticor in 2000), and owner of the Orlando Magic NBA basketball team. 

In 2012, "Forbes" magazine listed him as the 60th wealthiest person in the United States, and the 205th richest in the world, with an estimated net worth of $5.1 billion. At one point, he was one of the 10 wealthiest Americans.

DeVos was born in Grand Rapids, Michigan. He studied at Calvin College. He served in the military in World War II in the United States Army Air Corps. He is married to Helen June DeVos. Together, they have four children. His son, Dick DeVos, is married to Betsy DeVos the 11th and current United States Secretary of Education.
