Judith Godrèche

Judith Godrèche (also Judith Goldreich; born March 23, 1972) is a French actress and author. She has acted in over thirty movies. In "The Art of Love", she played Amèlie. In "Stoker" (2013), she played Doctor Jacquin.

Godrèche was born in Paris, the daughter of two psychotherapists. Her paternal grandparents were Holocaust survivors from Poland, who changed their name from Goldreich. Godrèche left school at age 15, settling into a long-term relationship with older director Benoît Jacquot.
