Mafia III

Mafia III is an open world third-person action-adventure video game. It was developed by Hangar 13. It was published by 2K Games. It was announced on July 29, 2015 and revealed at Gamescom on August 5, 2015. It was released on Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One on 7 October 2016.

The game is set in 1968 in New Orleans, Louisiana in the United States. The protagonist is Lincoln Clay, a mixed race orphan and Vietnam War veteran. Vito Scaletta, "Mafia II" protagonist, returns.
