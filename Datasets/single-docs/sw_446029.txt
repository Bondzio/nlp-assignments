Paul Lüönd

Paul Lüönd (15 April 1950 – 3 April 2014) was a Swiss musician and politician. He formed a group with his older two brothers called "Mosibuäbe". Lüönd was born on 15 April 1950. He grew up in the Mosi on Ingenbohl. 

He died on 3 April 2014 from a long illness in Switzerland, aged 63.
