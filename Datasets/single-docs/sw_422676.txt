Wycombe Abbey

Wycombe Abbey is a private secondary boarding school for girls in High Wycombe, England. It has the best girls' boarding school exam results in the United Kingdom. 37% of students go to Oxford or Cambridge universities. It was started in 1896 by Dame Frances Gove.
