Inside Out (2015 movie)

Inside Out is a 2015 American computer animated dramedy adventure movie. It was produced by Pixar Animation Studios for Walt Disney Pictures, being the 15th movie in the Pixar series. The movie is directed by one of Pixar's leading talents Pete Docter. The film was shown at the Cannes Film Festival on May 18, 2015 and was released on June 19, 2015.

Riley Anderson is an 11-year-old girl. She moves from Minnesota to San Francisco with her family. This affects a number of emotions in her head. The five emotion characters are Joy, Anger, Disgust, Fear and Sadness. These emotions all help with her thoughts. They help each other with Riley's mind. Each of the characters has power over an island of Riley's life, which represent different parts of her personality. After a series of events that change different things, she learns to accept her new life.
