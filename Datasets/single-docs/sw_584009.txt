Nick Galifianakis (politician)

Nick Galifianakis (born July 22, 1928) is an American politician. He was the former Democratic U.S. Congressman from North Carolina. He served as a representative from 1967 through 1973. 

Galifianakis was born in Durham, North Carolina. He studied at Duke University.

Galifianakis is the uncle of comedian Zach Galifianakis.
