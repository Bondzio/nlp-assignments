Keith Loneker

Keith Joseph Loneker (June 21, 1971June 22, 2017) was an American actor and American football player. He was born in Roselle Park, New Jersey. For much of his short career, his large football-lineman build garnered him roles largely as thugs or football players. He played for the St. Louis Rams from 1993 through 1995 and later for the Atlanta Falcons in 1996. His first movie was director Steven Soderbergh's "Out of Sight" (1998) and later the Neil LaBute thriller, "Lakeview Terrace" (2008).

Loneker died on June 22, 2017 in Lawrence, Kansas from cancer, aged 46.
