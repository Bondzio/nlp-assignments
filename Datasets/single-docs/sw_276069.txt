Matt Calvert

Matt Calvert (born December 24, 1989 in Brandon, Manitoba) is a Canadian professional ice hockey player. Drafted in the 5th Round (127th overall) by the Columbus Blue Jackets during the 2008 NHL Entry Draft he currently plays with the Springfield Falcons of the AHL. He is the only player in WHL history to score three short-handed goals in one period in the playoffs.
