Galícia Esporte Clube

Galícia Esporte Clube is a football club which plays in Salvador da Bahía, Brazil.

Bahia League Championship (Campeonato Baiano)

"Champion": 1937, 1941, 1942, 1943 and 1968.

"Runner-up": 1935, 1936, 1938, 1939, 1940, 1967, 1980, 1982 and 1995.

Bahia League Championship Second Level (Campeonato Baiano Segunda Divisão)

"Champion": 1985, 1988 and 2013.

"Runner-up": 2007.

Northeast Zone of the North-Northeast Cup (Copa Norte-Nordeste)

"Runner-up": 1969.
