Mitsubishi Grandis

The Mitsubishi Grandis is a automobile produced by Mitsubishi Motors. It replaced the Mitsubishi Space Wagon in 2003. It was sold in Japan, Asia, Europe, Oceania, Mexico, Honduras, Jamaica, and South America.

It won the Best MPV award at the "Bangkok International Motor Show" from 2005 to 2010. 

Production ended in 2011.
