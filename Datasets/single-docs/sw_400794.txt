Vito Corleone

Vito Andolini Corleone (born Vito Andolini in Corleone, Province of Palermo, Sicily, Italy) is a fictional character in Mario Puzo's novel "The Godfather" and in Francis Ford Coppola's first two movies, where he was portrayed by Marlon Brando in "The Godfather" and, as a younger man, by Robert De Niro in "The Godfather Part II".
