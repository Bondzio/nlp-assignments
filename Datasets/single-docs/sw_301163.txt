I Gotta Feeling

"I Gotta Feeling" is a single released in 2009 by The Black Eyed Peas under their fifth studio album "The E.N.D.". The song debuted at number 2 on the Billboard Hot 100 in the United States, and later hit number 1 on the U.S. charts. It stayed number one for fourteen weeks. It won the Grammy for Best Pop Performance by a Duo or Group with Vocals at the 2009 Grammy Awards.
