House 2

Dom-2 ("House-2") is a Russian reality television show created by TNT channel. In the show, contestants are building a house and trying to find someone to love in the process. Couples then compete for the house itself.

First episode of "Dom-2" was aired on May 11, 2004. It remains the most highly-rated, profitable and long-running reality show on television in Russia. "Dom-2" broadcasts on the TNT channel at 11 pm every day. It is hosted by Kseniya Sobchak, Kseniya Borodina and Vassia Zhopin.

After more than 3,000 episodes, as on August, 2012, it is the longest running reality show in the world.
