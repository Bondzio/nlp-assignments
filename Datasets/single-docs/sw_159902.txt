Juvigny-sur-Seulles

Juvigny-sur-Seulles is a commune. It is found in the region Basse-Normandie in the Calvados department in the northwest of France.

The commune existed before the French Revolution. Before, it was simply "Juvigny". The name only became Juvigny-sur-Seulles in 1938.

The commune is a few kilometres away from the D-Day landings beaches. It is also near Caen and Bayeux.

In 1850, the inhabitants built a more modern bridge.
