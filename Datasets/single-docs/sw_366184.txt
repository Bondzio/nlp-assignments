Wendy Crewson

Wendy Jane Crewson (born May 9, 1956) is a Canadian movie, television, voice, and stage actress who is known for his role as "The Santa Clause", "The Santa Clause 2", "", "Better Than Chocolate", and in "Air Force Air". He won an Gemini Award.

Crewson was born on May 9, 1956 in Hamilton, Ontario, Canada. She studied at Queen's University and at Webber Douglas Academy of Dramatic Art. Crewson was married to Michael Murphy from 1988 until they divorced in 2009. They had two children.
