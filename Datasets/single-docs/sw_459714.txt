Night at the Museum: Secret of the Tomb

Night at the Museum: Secret of the Tomb is an upcoming 2014 American comedy movie directed by Shawn Levy and written by Robert Ben Garant, David Guion, Michael Handelman and Thomas Lennon. 

It is the sequel to the 2009 movie "". The movie stars Ben Stiller, Robin Williams, Rebel Wilson, Ben Kingsley, Dan Stevens, Owen Wilson, Skyler Gisondo, Steve Coogan and Ricky Gervais. The movie is scheduled to be released on December 19, 2014, by 20th Century Fox. It is the third and final installment of the trilogy.

This is the last movie of both Mickey Rooney and Robin Williams before their deaths in 2014.
