Stuntman: Ignition

Stuntman: Ignition is a racing/action video game that was published by THQ and was developed by Paradigm Entertainment. It was released for the PlayStation 2, PlayStation 3 and the Xbox 360 systems. Stuntman: Ignition was the sequel to the 2002 video game, Stuntman. The game received positive reviews, GameSpot gave it a 7.5 out of 10 and IGN gave it a 7.1 out of 10.
