The Streets

The Streets were a rap/garage band whose lead singer was Mike Skinner, a British rapper. Born in Birmingham, Skinner has often been criticised for using a "mockney" accent during interviews and in many songs.

They released their first album, "Original Pirate Material", in 2002, The album's singles were "Has It Come To This", "Let's Push Things Forward", "Don't Mug Yourself", "The Irony Of It All" and "Weak Become Heroes.
