Caroline Wozniacki

Caroline Wozniacki (born 11 July 1990) is a Danish professional tennis player. As of September 2015, she is the number five ranked female tennis player in the world. She used to be world number one. In her career, she has won 23 singles tournaments on the Women's Tennis Association (WTA) Tour. Her best performances in a Grand Slam event were reaching the finals of the 2009 and 2014 U.S. Open. She lost to Kim Clijsters and Serena Williams.

Wozniacki was born in Odense to Polish Catholic immigrant parents.
