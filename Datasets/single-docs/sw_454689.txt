Ken Howard

Kenneth Joseph "Ken" Howard, Jr. (March 28, 1944 â€“ March 23, 2016) was an American actor. He was best known for his roles as Thomas Jefferson in "1776" and as basketball coach and former Chicago Bulls player Ken Reeves in the television show "The White Shadow". He was elected president of the Screen Actors Guild in September 2009 and elected to a second term in September 2011.

Howard died in Valencia, California on March 23, 2016 at the age of 71.
