Atlanta United FC

Atlanta United Football Club is an American soccer team from Atlanta, Georgia that plays in Major League Soccer as a member of the Western Conference since the 2017 season. The team was founded on April 16, 2014, and it is owned by the owner of the Atlanta Falcons of the NFL, Arthur Blank. The team's manager is Gerardo "Tata" Martino. The team's colors are black, red and gold. The team plays their home games at Bobby Dodd Stadium and will later move to Mercedes-Benz Stadium, which is currently under construction and expected to be completed during late 2017.
