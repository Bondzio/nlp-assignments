Lisa's Pony

"Lisa's Pony" is the eighth episode of the third season of "The Simpsons". It first aired on the Fox network in the United States on November 7, 1991. In this episode, Homer Lisa by forgetting to buy the right reed for her saxophone. desperate to win back his daughter's love, Homer gives Lisa the one thing she has always wanted, a pony named "Princess." Homer struggles with two jobs to cover the cost of and feeding the pony. Lisa, upon seeing what Homer must go through to pay for the pony, decides to give it away.
