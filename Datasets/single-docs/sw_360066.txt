Gangnam Style

"Gangnam Style" () is a song by South Korean musician, PSY. The music video has been watched over 1.5 billion times on YouTube , making it the site's most watched video ever.
It is the first video in the website's history that more than 1 billion people have seen. It has made people make many videos like it but in other locations, named "parodies". Barack Obama, David Cameron, and Ban Ki-moon tried the song's dance moves.
