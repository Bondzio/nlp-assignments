Enrique Pinti

Enrique Pinti (born October 7, 1939) is a retired-Argentine comedian and actor. His career began in 1957.

Pinti was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina.

He is known for his roles in "Juan que reía" (1976), "Esperando la carroza" (1985), "Flop" (1990), "Perdido por perdido" (1993), "Tango" (1998), and in "Arregui, la noticia del día" (2001).

He now lives in Buenos Aires. Pinti is openly gay.
