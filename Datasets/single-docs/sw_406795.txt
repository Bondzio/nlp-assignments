Joan Caulfield

Joan Caulfield (June 1, 1922 - June 18, 1991) was an American former model and actress. She started acting during the early to mid 1940s. She was in the 1947 movie "The Unsuspected". She appeared mostly on television in her later life, including "Murder, She Wrote". Caulfield was born in New Jersey. She later moved to Los Angeles. She died from cancer at age sixty-nine.
