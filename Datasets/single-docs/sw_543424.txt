Echternach (canton)

Echternach () is a canton in the east of Luxembourg. The capital is Echternach.

With an area of , and a population (2016) of 18,007, the Echternach canton has a population density of inhabitants/km².

It is bordered on the north by the German district of Eifelkreis Bitburg-Prüm (part of the "Land" of Rhineland-Palatinate), to the east by the district of Trier-Saarburg (also part of Rhineland-Palatinate), to the south by the Grevenmacher canton and to the west by the Diekirch and the Mersch cantons

Part of this canton is a natural region called the Little Switzerland.

The Echternach canton has 8 communes (2016 population).
