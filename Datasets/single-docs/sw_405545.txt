Milo Đukanović

Milo Đukanović (; born 15 February 1962) is a Montenegrin politician. He has been the country's Prime Minister since 2012. Đukanović has previously served as Prime Minister three times, from 1991 to 1998, from 2003 to 2006 and from 2008 to 2010. He also served as President from 1998 to 2002. Đukanović is the leader of the Democratic Party of Socialists of Montenegro.
