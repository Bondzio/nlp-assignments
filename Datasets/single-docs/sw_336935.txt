Kareena Kapoor

Kareena Kapoor (; born on 21 September 1980), is a famous Bollywood actress. She has been many genres, from romantic dramas to comedies, independent movies to major Bollywood movies.

She is the sister of Bollywood actress Karisma Kapoor. She is the daughter of Bollywood Actors Randhir Kapoor and Babita. Often informally referred to as 'Bebo', she is the granddaughter of actor and filmmaker Raj Kapoor and niece of actor Rishi Kapoor.

She is the partner of Bollywood Actor Saif Ali Khan. They are referred to India's Brangelina (with the name 'Saifeena') by the press.
