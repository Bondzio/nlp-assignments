Dave Chappelle

David Khari Webber "Dave" Chappelle (born August 24, 1973) is an American comedian, screenwriter, television and movie producer, movie, voice, and television actor, and artists. He is known for his comedy works and for having his own show, "Chappelle's Show". He is also known for his role as Thurgood Jenkins in "Half Baked".

Chappelle was born on August 24, 1973 in Washington, D.C.. He has been married to Elaine Chappelle since 2001. They have 3 children.
