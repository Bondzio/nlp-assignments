Gabriel Agbonlahor

Gabriel Agbonlahor (born 13 October 1986) is an English footballer. He plays for Aston Villa. He is of Nigerian and Scottish descent.

Gabriel Agbonlahor's professional football career started in 2005. He played his first game for Watford on loan from Aston Villa. This means that he was still an Aston Villa player, but he was allowed to play for Watford to get experience. He then played for Sheffield Wednesday, again on loan. He played his first English Premier League game in 2006 for Aston Villa.

Gabriel Agbonlahor played 16 games for the England Under 21 team. He has also played for the England national football team.
