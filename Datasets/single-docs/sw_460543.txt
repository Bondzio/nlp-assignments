Jason Momoa

Joseph Jason Namakaeha Momoa (born August 1, 1979) is an American actor, model, director, writer, and producer. He is known for his television roles as Ronon Dex on the television series "" and as Khal Drogo in the HBO fantasy television series "Game of Thrones". 

He also starred in the movie "Conan the Barbarian".

In 2014, Momoa's "Road to Paloma" was released. It was his first movie that he directed. He played the comic book hero, Aquaman, in the 2016 movie "" and will play the character again in the 2017 movie "Justice League" and his own movie in "Aquaman" (2018).
