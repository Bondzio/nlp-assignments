Ellen Vogel

Ellen Marie Elze Anthing Vogel (January 26, 1922 – August 5, 2015) was a Dutch television, movie, and stage actress.

Vogel was born on January 26, 1922 in The Hague, Netherlands. She was raised in Amsterdam. 

Vogel married Hans Jürgen Tobi in 1942. They divorced in 1949. She was in a relationship with Fons Rademakers from 1950 to 1955. Vogel then married James Münninghoff in 1976. Their marriage lasted until his death in 2012. Vogel had a son with Hans, Peter Paul. 

Vogel died in Amsterdam at the age of 93.
