Pac-Man and the Ghostly Adventures

Pac-Man and the Ghostly Adventures is a Japanese/Canadian/American computer animated television series. It is produced by Bandico Namco Games, Arad Productions Inc. and 41 Entertainment. In the United States, the series first aired on June 15, 2013 on Disney XD. In Canada, it was first shown on March 17, 2014 on Disney XD. In Japan, it was first shown on April 5, 2014 on Tokyo MX and BS11.

On the planet Pac-World, Pac-Man and his friends Cylindria and Spiral help to protect Pacopolis from the threat of Ghosts
