Carshalton Beeches railway station

Carshalton Beeches station opened on the 1st October 1906. The train operator on this station is Southern (train operating company). This station is in fare zone 5.

There are two platforms on this station. Platform 1 is for northbound trains towards Victoria Station only. Platform 2 is for southbound trains towards, Sutton (Surrey), Epsom and Epsom Downs.
