List of India Test cricketers

This is a list of Test cricketers who have played for India. A Test match is an international cricket match between two of the leading cricketing nations. The list is arranged in the order in which each player won his Test cap. Where more than one player won his first Test cap in the same Test match, those players are listed alphabetically by surname.

A total of 31 players have been the captain of the Indian Test team so far. Mahendra Singh Dhoni has the highest win rate (49.99%), followed by Sourav Ganguly (42.85%). Sourav Ganguly captained the team for most number of test matches (49). Iftikhar Ali Khan Pataudi is the youngest captain for India in test matches.
