Shah Azizur Rahman

Shah Azizur Rahman (; 1925 - 1988) was a Bangladeshi politician who served as the Prime Minister of Bangladesh. However, he was the subject of considerable controversy for his collaboration with the Pakistan Army against the struggle to establish Bangladesh.

He served as Prime Minister under President Zia. 
He was a veteran parliamentarian and was famous for his razor sharp tongue. He was fluent in several languages.
