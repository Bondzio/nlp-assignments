Design of a Decade: 1986–1996

Design of a Decade: 1986–1996 is the first greatest hits album by American recording artist Janet Jackson. It was released on October 10, 1995 by A&M. It features 14 of Jackson's hits from her three previous albums; "Control" (1986), "Janet Jackson's Rhythm Nation 1814" (1989), "janet." (1993) as well as two newly recorded singles "Runaway" and "Twenty Foreplay".
