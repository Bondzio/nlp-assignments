Journalism

Journalism is a type of writing. The term is used to describe the activities of newspapers, news shows on television, news shows on radio, and news magazine. 

There are many different news and information jobs in the field of journalism, such as the jobs of newspaper reporter, television news anchor, writer, editor, sub-editor, illustrator, and photographer. Most people use journalism to express themselves and put their feelings into what they are writing.

People who work in journalism jobs are called journalists.
