John Parrott

John Parrott (born 11 May 1964 in Liverpool) is an English former professional snooker player. He was twice in the finals of World Snooker Championship. In 1989, he lost to Steve Davis, in 1991 he defeated Jimmy White.

Parrott started his professional career in 1989, in the season 2010/11 he lost his place in the world rankings. Now he is snooker commentator.
