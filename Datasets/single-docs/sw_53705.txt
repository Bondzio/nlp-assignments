Großlangheim

Großlangheim is a small village in Bavaria, Germany. About 1,600 people live in Großlangheim. Big events in Großlangheim are the Weinfest (wineparty) and the Panik-Party in the Winehill. Panik-Party is the biggest open air party in Unterfranken. Over 5,000 people came to this event in 2006 which is organized by teenagers from Großlangheim.
