Eoghan Quigg

Eoghan Quigg (12 July 1992) is a pop singer based in the United Kingdom and Ireland. He has been active since 2008 when he was a contestent on "The X Factor" where he finished in third place. In Ireland he has had a number 1 album called "Eoghan Quigg". Quigg was going to be signed by Simon Cowell, CEO of Syco Records, but was instead signed to RCA.
