Boris Pustyntsev

Boris Pavlovich Pustyntsev (; 2 June 1935 – 4 March 2014) was a Russian human rights activist. He was best known as chairman of the charitable organisation St. Petersburg NGO.

Pustyntsev was born in Vladivostok, Primorsky Krai, then Soviet Union. His father was Pavel Pustyntsev (1910–1977).

Pustyntsev died after a long illness on 4 March 2014 in Saint Petersburg. He was 78.
