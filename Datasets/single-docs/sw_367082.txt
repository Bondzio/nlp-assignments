Maamme

Maamme (Finnish) or "" (Swedish) is the title of Finland's national anthem. There is no law on an official national anthem in Finland, but Maamme is firmly established by convention. The anthem was composed by German-born composer Fredrik Pacius in 1848 and first performed 13 May 1848. and the lyrics where written by Finnish poet Johan Ludvig Runeberg in Swedish. In 1889 it was translated to Finnish by Paavo Cajander.
