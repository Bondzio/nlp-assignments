Denny Miller

Denny Scott Miller (born Dennis Linn Miller; April 25, 1934 â€“ September 9, 2014) was an American actor. He was best best known for his role as Tarzan in the movie "Tarzan, the Ape Man" (1959). He also appeared in many television series such as "Wagon Train", "Gilligan's Island", "Magnum, P.I.", "The Brady Bunch" and "Gunsmoke".

Miller was born in Bloomington, Indiana. He was married to Kit Smythe until their divorce. He was later married to Nancy Miller until his death.

Miller died from Lou Gehrig's disease on September 9, 2014 in Las Vegas, Nevada. He was 80. He was also diagnosed with the disease in January 2014.
