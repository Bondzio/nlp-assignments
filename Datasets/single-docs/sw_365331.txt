Isla Fisher

Isla Fisher (born 3 February 1976) is a British-Australian actress who has been in many movies and television shows. She was born in Muscat, Oman to Scottish parents. She moved to Perth, Western Australia when she was six. She starred in the movie "Wedding Crashers".

Fisher became engaged to Sacha Baron Cohen in 2004. The couple married in 2010. They have two daughters and a son.
