Heath Slater

Heath Miller (born July 15, 1983 in Pineville, West Virginia) is an American professional wrestler. He currently works with the WWE where he wrestles under the ring name Heath Slater. He was a member of the stables 3MB and The Nexus.

Slater has won the WWE Tag Team Championship three times with Justin Gabriel. He also won a Slammy Award in 2010 for the debut of The Nexus. Slater's finishing move is the "Smash Hit" which is a spinning lifting DDT.
