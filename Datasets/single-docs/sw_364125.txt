Hirosaki, Aomori

Hirosaki is a castle town of the Tsugaru clan. The town grew up around Hirosaki Castle, which was built in 1601.

Hirosaki uses the swastika ("manji" ) as official symbol. This came from the flag emblem of Tsugaru clan in Edo period.

Hirosaki is located in southwest Aomori Prefecture. It is south of Mount Iwaki; and it borders Akita Prefecture.
