F.C. Steaua Bucureşti

F.C. Steaua Bucureşti is a football team from Bucharest, Romania. It was founded on June 7, 1947 as "ASA Bucureşti" ("ASA Bucharest"). It has won the most championships in Romania, 23, and it is the only team from Romania to win a UEFA Champions League title. Its chairman is George Becali (usually "Gigi" Becali).
