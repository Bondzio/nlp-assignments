Chiriku Hachiman-gū

, also known as , is a Japanese Shinto shrine in Miyaki, Saga Prefecture. 

"Chiriku Hachiman-gū" was the chief Shinto shrine ("ichinomiya") of Hizen Province. It is a chief shrine of Saga Prefecture and Nagasaki Prefecture.

In 1940, the shrine was recognized in the system of State Shinto. It was among the ranked, nationally significant shrines or which included 50 sanctuaries.
