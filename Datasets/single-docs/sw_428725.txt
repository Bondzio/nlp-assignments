Chlöe Howl

Chlöe Howl (born March 4 1995) is an English singer. On 4 March 2013, her first extended play "Rumor" was released for free download. In June 2013, she released the video for her first single "No Strings". She released her second extended play "No Strings" on August 23. "No Strings" was played in the movie "Kick-Ass 2".

Howl was nominated for BBC's Sound of 2014 prize on 3 December 2013.

On 5 December 2013, was nominated for the 2014 BRITs Critics' Choice Award.

On 9 December 2013, her second single "Paper Heart" was released.
