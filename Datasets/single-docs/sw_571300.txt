Rod J. Rosenstein

Rod J. Rosenstein (born January 13, 1965) is an American lawyer and politician. He is the 37th and current United States Deputy Attorney General since April 26, 2017. He was the United States Attorney for the United States District Court for the District of Maryland and a former nominee to the United States Court of Appeals for the Fourth Circuit.

Then President-Elect Donald Trump nominated Rosenstein to serve as Deputy Attorney General for the United States Department of Justice on January 13, 2017. He was confirmed by the Senate on April 25, 2017 by a vote of 94-6.
