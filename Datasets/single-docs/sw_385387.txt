Greta Scacchi

Greta Scacchi (born 18 February 1960) is an Italian-Australian actress. She acts mostly in movies, although sometimes in television programs. She starred in lots of movies, which include "White Mischief" and "Presumed Innocent". She has acted in many other movies as well.

Scacchi was born Greta Gracco in Milan to an English mother and Italian father. Since 1995, she is an Australian citizen, and is still an Italian citizen.
