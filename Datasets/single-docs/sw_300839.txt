El Salvador at the Olympics

El Salvador has been in nine Summer Olympic Games. 

The International Olympic Committee's official abbreviation for El Salvador is ESA. In 1964, it was SAL for ""salvador"".

There have been no teams from El Salvador at the Olympic Winter Games. As of 2010, El Salvador does not have a medal. 

The "Comité Olímpico de El Salvador" was formed in 1949 and recognized by the IOC in 1962.
