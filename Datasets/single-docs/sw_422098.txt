Philip Baker Hall

Philip Baker Hall (born September 10, 1931) is an American actor. He is known for his roles in "Midnight Run", "Say Anything...", "Air Force One", "The Rock", "The Truman Show", "The Talented Mr. Ripley", "Bruce Almighty", "Rush Hour", "You Kill Me", "In Good Company", "Dogville", "The Amityville Horror", "The Matador", "The Sum of All Fears", "The Zodiac", and "Zodiac".

Hall was born on September 10, 1931 in Toledo, Ohio. He studied at the University of Toledo. Hall was married twice, both ended in divorce. Hall had two children.
