Crash course

Crash Course (also known as A Mother's Fight for Justice) is a 2001 American made-for-tv drama movie, Inspired by a true story.

A mother is angry at the boy who hit her son while driving drunk, because he never apologizes.

She tries to fight for revenge against the will of her husband and son. After he served in prison, the son decided to stop the fight, he came to say that he was never allowed to say that he is sorry.
