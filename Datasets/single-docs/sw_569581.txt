Ni Yulan

Ni Yulan is a Chinese business lawyer. In 2016, she received the International Women of Courage Award.

Yulan fought for property rights for people who lost their homes because of building for the 2008 Beijing Olympics. She went to prison many times. In prison she was beaten and lost the use of her legs. She applied for a passport to travel to the U.S. to receive the award, but the Chinese government refused.
