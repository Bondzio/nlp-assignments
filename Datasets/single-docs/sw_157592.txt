Gary Neville

Gary Neville (born 18 February 1975) is an English retired footballer and current coach of Spanish team Valencia CF. Neville played for English club Manchester United during his whole career, and was captain of the club. He has also played for the England national team. His brother Phil Neville is also a footballer.
