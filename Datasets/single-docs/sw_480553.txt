Liberty Heights

Liberty Heights is a 1999 American comedy-drama movie. It was directed by Barry Levinson and was produced by both Barry Levinson and Paula Weinstein. "Liberty Heights" was released on November 17, 1999. The movie was written by Levinson and it is semi-autobiographical account of his time growing up in Baltimore during the 1950s.

The movie gained positive reviews from critics and holds a 87% rating on Rotten Tomatoes.
