Nicholas Sand

Nicholas Sand (May 10, 1941 â€“ April 24, 2017) was an American chemist. He was a cult figure known in the psychedelic community for his work as a clandestine chemist from 1966 through 1996 for the Brotherhood of Eternal Love. He was born in New York City.

Sand was part of the League for Spiritual Discovery at the Millbrook estate in New York, has been credited as the "first underground chemist on record to have synthesized DMT" and was known for creating and selling large amounts of LSD.

Sand died in his sleep at his home in California of a suspected heart attack on April 24, 2017 at the age of 75.
