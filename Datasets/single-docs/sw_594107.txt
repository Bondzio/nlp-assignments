Who You Are

Who You Are is the debut album by English singer-songwriter Jessie J, released on 28 February 2011 by Lava Records. The album was originally due to be released a month later but due to Jessie J's popularity the album got brought forward. The album was written over a five year period.

"Who You Are" received mainly positive reviews from music critics.

On 6 March 2011, "Who You Are" debuted at number two on the UK Albums Chart, behind Adele's "21". It sold 105,000 copies in its first week. In August 2013 the album was certified quadruple Platinum by the British Phonographic Industry (BPI) for selling 1,200,000 copies in the United Kingdom.
