Lahore Qalandars

Lahore Qalandars (Urdu/) is one of the five teams which plays in the Pakistan Super League (PSL). The team represents the city of Lahore and was founded in 2015. It is owned by Qatar Lubricants Company Limited (QALCO). Azhar Ali is the captain of Lahore Qalandars, while the head coach is Paddy Upton.
