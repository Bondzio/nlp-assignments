Lalah Hathaway

Eulaulah Donyll Hathaway (also known as Lalah Hathaway; born December 16, 1968) is an American contemporary R&B and jazz singer and musician. She has had several hit singles. They include "Let Me Love You" (a 1994 song) and "Baby Don't Cry" in 1990.

Lalah Hathaway is the daughter of late singer-songwriter Donny Hathaway.

Hathaway was born in Chicago.
