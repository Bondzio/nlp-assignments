Annie Oliv

Annie Oliv(born 1988 in Jönköping, Sweden) is a Swedish beauty queen, cello player and personal assistant to a disabled person.

On September 8, 2007, Oliv won the "Miss Sweden World" title. She later represented her country at Miss World 2007. She placed 4th in the competition. She won the title "Miss World Europe 2007". Annie Oliv played cello during Melodifestivalen 2008 in Mickey Huskics entry in semifinal number three on February 23.
