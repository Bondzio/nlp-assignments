Eurovision Song Contest 2015

The Eurovision Song Contest 2015 was the 60th edition of the contest and took place in Vienna, Austria, after Conchita Wurst won for Austria in Copenhagen, Denmark in 2014.

A total of 40 countries participated in that year, while Australia participated for the first time ever as a special-guest due the 60th anniversery.

Ukraine quit because of politics, while Cyprus, Czech Republic and Serbia returned to the contest.

The first semi-final took place on Tuesday, the 19th May 2015. 16 countries participated and 10 qualified for the final (fed marked).

The "Big-Five" members Austria, France and Spain voted in this semi-final.
The second semi-final took place on Thursday, the 21th May 2015. 17 countries participated and 10 qualified for the final (fed marked).

The "Big-Five" members Germany, Italy and the United Kingdom voted in this semi-final.

The Grand Final took place on Saturday, the 23th May 2015. Sweden won with 365 points, while Austria and Germany finished both last with zero points.
