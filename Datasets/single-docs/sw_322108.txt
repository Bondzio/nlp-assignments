Georgia Engel

Georgia Bright Engel (born July 28, 1948) is an American actress. She is the voice of Bobbie in the "Open Season" franchise. She was also on "The Mary Tyler Moore Show" and on "Everybody Loves Raymond". She had two Emmy nominations for both shows.

She played a good witch in the 2007 recurring role of Esmeralda on the now-defunct NBC soap opera "Passions".
