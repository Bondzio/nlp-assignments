JoBeth Williams

Margaret JoBeth Williams (born December 6, 1948) is an American movie, television, stage, voice actress, and movie director. She is known for her roles in "Poltergeist", "The Big Year", "The Big Chill", "", "Kramer vs. Kramer", and in "Stop! Or My Mom Will Shoot".

Williams was born on December 6, 1948 in Houston, Texas. Williams studied at Jones High School and at Brown University. She married John Pasquin in 1982. They have two children.
