Kristen Bell

Kristen Anne Bell (born July 18, 1980) is an American television, voice, stage, and movie actress. She appeared in "Forgetting Sarah Marshall", "Veronica Mars", "When in Rome", "Spartan", "Gossip Girl", "Polish Wedding", and in the 2012 comedy "Hit and Run".

She was born in Huntington Woods, Michigan. She and Dax Shepard have been partners since 2007. In November 2012, the couple announced that they were expecting a child. Their daughter, Lincoln Bell Shepard was born on March 28, 2013.
