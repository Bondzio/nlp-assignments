Rules of Engagement (TV series)

Rules of Engagement is an American sitcom set in New York City. It was shown from February 5, 2007 - May 20, 2013. It was shown on CBS. It has five main characters. Patrick Warburton and Megyn Price play married couple Jeff and Audrey. Oliver Hudson and Bianca Kajlich play engaged couple Adam and Jen. David Spade plays womanizing narcissistic single man Russell. Adhir Kalyan plays Timmy, Russell's office assistant.
