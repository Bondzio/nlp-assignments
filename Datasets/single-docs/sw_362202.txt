Paul Bettany

Paul Bettany (born May 27, 1971) is an English movie, voice, television, and stage actor. He voiced J.A.R.V.I.S. in the movies "Iron Man", "Iron Man 2" and "The Avengers". He will also voice the character in "Iron Man 3". Bettany was born in Shepherd's Bush, London, England. He has been married to Jennifer Connelly since 2003. They have three children.
