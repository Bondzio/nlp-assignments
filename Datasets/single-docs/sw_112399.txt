Simpsons Roasting on an Open Fire

"Simpsons Roasting on an Open Fire" is an episode of "The Simpsons" from the first season. It was the first full length episode of "The Simpsons" to be aired. It was first aired on December 17, 1989. Bart gets a tattoo from a shop in the Springfield Mall, which Marge has to use all of the Christmas money to have it removed.
