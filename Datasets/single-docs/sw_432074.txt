Cicely Tyson

Cicely Tyson (born 19 December 1933) is an American actress. She played Sipsey in the comedic-drama movie "Fried Green Tomatoes" (1991), and played Mamma Kay in the movie "The Haunting in Georgia" (2013). She also played Carrie Grace Battle in the television series "Sweet Justice".

Tyson was born in New York City.
