Ronnie O'Sullivan

Ronnie O'Sullivan (born 5 December 1975) is an English professional snooker player. He was born in Wordsley, West Midlands. He grew up in Chigwell, Essex and still lives there. He was World Champion in 2001, 2004, 2008, 2012 and 2013.

O'Sullivan's first century break made at age 10.

He has won the UK Championship five times. This was in 1993, 1997, 2001, 2007, and 2014. He has won the Masters seven times, in 1995, 2005, 2007, 2009, 2014, 2016 and 2017.
