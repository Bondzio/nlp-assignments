Tangerang

Tangerang is a city in Banten, Indonesia. It is located 25 km west of Jakarta. It is the sixth largest city in the nation. It has an area of 164.54 km² and an population of 1,797,715 (2010), increasing to 2,001,925 as at 2014 It is thus the eighth . The Soekarno-Hatta International Airport which serves Greater Jakarta is located in the city.
