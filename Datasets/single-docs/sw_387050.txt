Pablo Zibes

Pablo Zibes (born in 1971 in Buenos Aires) is an Argentinian actor and mime. He has lived in Germany since 1995.
He trained as an actor at The Municipal School of Performing Arts ( or EMAD) in Buenos Aires in 1989-1991. After that, he became a professional mime. He improved his mime skills through courses in Argentina and Europe "Scuola Teatro Dimitri", Switzerland. He worked as a mime and street performer in Europe and Asia
. He performs mostly for festivals, events, conventions and television. He does walk-around entertainment as different characters, like a robot or wind-up toy soldier.
