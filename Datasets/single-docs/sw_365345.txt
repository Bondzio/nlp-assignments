Ron Perlman

Ronald N. "Ron" Perlman (born April 13, 1950) is an American television, stage, movie and voice actor. He is known for his role as Hellboy in "Hellboy" and in "". He won a Golden Globe Award in 1988 for his role as Vincent in "Beauty and the Beast".

Perlman was born on April 13, 1950 in New York City, New York. He studied at the University of Minnesota. He has been married to Opal Perlamn since 1980. They have two children.
