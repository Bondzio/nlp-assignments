Jameela Jamil

Jameela Jamil (born 25 February 1986) is a British television and radio presenter. She hosts "The Official Chart" on BBC Radio 1. 

Jamil was born in Hampstead, London to an Indian father and an English mother.

In 2012 the first collection by 'JAM by Jameela Jamil' was released on Very.co.uk.
