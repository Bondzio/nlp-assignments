Crash Bandicoot 2: N-Tranced

Crash Bandicoot 2: N-Tranced, released in Japan as is a video game published by Universal Interactive Studios (Konami for Japan). It was developed by Vicarious Visions for the Game Boy Advance. It was released in North America on January 7, 2003, in Europe on March 14, 2003 and in Japan on December 4, 2003.
