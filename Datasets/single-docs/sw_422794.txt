Brian Doyle-Murray

Brian Doyle-Murray (born Brian Murray; October 31, 1945) is an American comedian, screenwriter, actor and voice actor. He is the older brother of actor/comedian Bill Murray and the two have acted together in some movies. Murray appeared on "Caddyshack", "Scrooged", "Ghostbusters II", "The Razor's Edge" and "Groundhog Day".
