Brooks Orpik

Richard Brooks Orpik (born September 26, 1980) is an American professional ice hockey defenseman. He currently plays for the Washington Capitals where he is an alternate captain. He has also played for the Pittsburgh Penguins.

Before playing in the NHL, Orpik played 3 years of college hockey at Boston College with the Eagles.
