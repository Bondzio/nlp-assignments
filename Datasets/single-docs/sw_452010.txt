Rollin' (Limp Bizkit song)

"Rollin' (Air Raid Vehicle)" is the third single off American nu metal band Limp Bizkit's third studio album "Chocolate Starfish and the Hot Dog Flavored Water". The song was released on October 10, 2000. 

There was a remix version of the song titled "Rollin' (Urban Assault Vehicle)" which featured rappers DMX, Redman, and Method Man. It was also produced by Swizz Beatz. The remix was ranked #4 on VH1's list of 50 Most Awesomely Bad Songs.

The song was used as the theme song for American professional wrestler The Undertaker from December 2000 to May 2002. Limp Bizkit performed the song live during his entrance for WrestleMania XIX. The song was featured in the 2001 video game NHL Hitz 2002 and the remix was featured in the 2001 movie The Fast and the Furious.
