Rodrigo García

Rodrigo García (born 24 August 1959) is a Colombian-born television and movie director and screenwriter. He is the son of Nobel Prize-winning author Gabriel García Márquez. 

He directed movies and television series such as "Nine Lives" and "Albert Nobbs", and episodes of several HBO series, "Six Feet Under", "Carnivàle", and "Big Love".
