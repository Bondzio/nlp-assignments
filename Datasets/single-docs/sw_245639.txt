Rik van den Boog

Rik van den Boog (born 1 September 1959 in Amsterdam) is the managing director of Dutch Football club AFC Ajax since October 2008.

On 22 May 2010 several media reported that stock holder of The Entertainment Group, Unico Glorie, has reported Rik van den Boog to police for Fraud and Theft. Unico Glorie says that Van den Boog made a payment of Euro 2.000.000 without permission.
