Cathay Pacific

Cathay Pacific Limited is an airline from Hong Kong. It transports passengers and cargo to over 120 cities world-wide. Its main base or hub is at Hong Kong International Airport. It is one of the six airlines in the world to get a five star rating from Skytrax. It won "Airline of the Year" award from Skytrax in 2003, 2005 and 2009. Its sister airline is Dragonair which serves destinations in mainland China and Asia.
