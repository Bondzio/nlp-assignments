David Barrallo Fuertes

David Barrallo Fuertes (born June 4, 1973 in Barcelona) is a track and field athlete from Spain. He has a physical disability and is T44 type athlete. He raced at the 2000 Summer Paralympics. He finished second in the 4 X 400 meter T42-T46 race. He finished fourth in the 200 meter race. He finished fifth in the 400 meter race.
