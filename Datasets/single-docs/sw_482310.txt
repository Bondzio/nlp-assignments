Still Alice

Still Alice is a drama movie from 2014 based on the novel of the same name. It is about a linguistics professor diagnosed with early-onset Alzheimer's disease. Julianne Moore plays Alice. Kristen Stewart plays Lydia. Kate Bosworth plays Anna.

"Still Alice" was released on December 5, 2014 in the United States. Julianne Moore won an Academy Award for Best Actress.
