Abby Elliott

Abigail "Abby" Elliott (born June 16, 1987) is an American actress and comedian. She does impressions of celebrities on television. She played Jeanette Peterson in "How I Met Your Mother". She also played Taylor in the 2014 remake movie "Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles".

Elliott was born in New York City. Her father, Chris Elliott, was on "Saturday Night Live" in 1994-1995. Her grandfather is comedian Bob Elliott.
