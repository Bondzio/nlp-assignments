Jessica Simpson

Jessica Simpson (born July 10, 1980) is an American singer and actress. She is the daughter of Tina and Joe Simpson and older sister of singer and actress Ashlee Simpson. 

As a singer, she has 7 "Billboard" Top 40 hits, 5 gold albums and 2 platinum albums.

She has starred in the movies "The Dukes of Hazzard" (2005) and "Employee of the Month" (2006)

She has a cosmetics line called "Dessert Treats" and "Dessert Beauty". 

Jessica Simpson was born in Abilene, Texas. She is the daughter of Tina Ann and Joseph Truett. She has a sister whose name is Ashlee Simpson who is also a singer.
At first, Simpson sang at a local church. At age twelve, she auditioned for The Mickey Mouse Club, but was not selected.

Simpson was married to musician Nick Lachey from 2002 until they divorced in 2006.
