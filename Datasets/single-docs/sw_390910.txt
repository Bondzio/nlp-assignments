Max Wright

George Edward Maxwell "Max" Wright (born August 2, 1943) is an retired-American actor, best known for his role as Willie Tanner on the television comedy series "ALF".

Wright was born on August 2, 1943 in Detroit, Michigan. He was married to Linda Wright since 1965. They have two children, Ben and Daisy. Wright now lives in Los Angeles, California. 

In 1995, he was diagnosed with lymphoma. He is in remission. In January 2000, Wright was arrested for drunken driving in Los Angeles, California. Wright had another DUI arrest in 2003.
