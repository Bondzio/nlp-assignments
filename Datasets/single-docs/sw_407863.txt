Rise of the Planet of the Apes

Rise of the Planet of the Apes is a 2011 American science fiction movie. It stars James Franco, John Lithgow, Brian Cox, Tom Felton, and Andy Serkis. It was released on August 5, 2011 to positive reviews. A sequel to the film "Dawn of the Planet of the Apes" was released on July 11, 2014. A third film is scheduled for release in July 14, 2017.

Roger Ebert gave the movie 3 out of 4 stars. He liked how Andy Serkis played Caesar by saying it was a "wonderfully executed character" and "one never knows exactly where the human ends and the effects begin, but Serkis or Caesar gives the best performance in the movie."
