Manuel Uribe Ángel

Manuel María Uribe Angel (4 September 1822 – 16 June 1904) was a Colombian physician, geographer and politician. He has been called the ""father of medicine of Antioquia"". He was known outside the realm of medicine for his studies and works on the geography and history of Colombia. 

He also served as President of the then Sovereign State of Antioquia and as Senator of Colombia.
