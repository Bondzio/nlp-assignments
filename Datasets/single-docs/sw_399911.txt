Jacky Cheung

Jacky Cheung (born 10 July 1961) is a Hong Kong singer-songwriter and actor. Along with, Aaron Kwok, Andy Lau and Leon Lai, he is one of the Cantopop (四大天王). As of May 2003, he has sold more than 25 million records.
