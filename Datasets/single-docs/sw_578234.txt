More Life

More Life (also known as More Life: A Playlist by October Firm) is a playlist by Canadian rapper Drake. It was released on March 18, 2017, by Young Money Entertainment and Cash Money Records. Drake stated that the collection of songs is a playlist and not an album.

The first single, "Fake Love", was released on October 23, 2016, the day before Drake's 30th birthday. It reached the top ten around the world.

"Passionfruit" is set to be issued as the second single from "More Life" on March 28, 2017.
