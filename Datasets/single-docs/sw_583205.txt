Buhe (politician)

Buhe or Bu He (; March 1926-May 5, 2017), also known as Yun Shuguang, was a Chinese politician. The eldest son of the powerful Inner Mongolia leader Ulanhu. Buhe served as Chairman of Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region between 1982 and 1993. Between 1993 and 2003 he was Vice Chairman of the National People's Congress.

Buhe was a member of the 12th and the 13th Central Committees of the CPC. He died on 5 May 2017 at age of 91.
