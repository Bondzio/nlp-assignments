Yang Hyong-sop

Yang Hyong-sop (born 1 October 1925)
is a North Korean politician. He was elected chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly in 1983, and served until 1998. He is currently vice president of the Presidium of the SPA.

Yang was born in Hamhung. He attended Moscow State University and Kim Il-sung University. He is married to Kim Shin-sook, a cousin of Kim Il-Sung.
