Saw II

Saw II is a 2005 Canadian-American horror film. Saw II is a sequel to 2004's "Saw". It is the second installment in the seven-part Saw franchise. It was released October 28, 2005. The movie was directed and co-written by Darren Lynn Bousman. It was Co-written with series creator Leigh Whannell. Saw II stars Donnie Wahlberg, Franky G, Dina Meyer, Erik Knudsen, and Tobin Bell. Rotten Tomatoes gave it an audience score of 59%.
