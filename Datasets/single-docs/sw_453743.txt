Captain Toad: Treasure Tracker

Captain Toad: Treasure Tracker is a puzzle platformer video game. It was developed by Nintendo EAD Tokyo. It was published by Nintendo. It is a spin-off of the "Super Mario" series. It is an expansion of the minigame that was featured in the 2013 game "Super Mario 3D World".

The game was released in Japan on November 13, 2014. It will be released on December 5, 2014 in North America. It will be released on January 2, 2015 in Europe. It will be released on January 3, 2015 in Australia.
