Bellegarde-sur-Valserine

Bellegarde-sur-Valserine is a commune. It is found in the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes region in the Ain department in the east of France. In 2010, 11,790 people lived in the commune. The inhabitants are the "Bellegardiens". The local economy is mostly based around commerce. Monuments in the commune are numerous. There are many castles including the "Châteaux de Musinens", "Mussel", "Arlod", "Vanchy" and "Crêts". There are also many churches including the "Saint-Vicent-de-Paul", "Notre-Dame-de-l'Assomption", "Saint-Nicolas d'Arlod" and "Saint-Claude de Vanchy" churches. Famous people from Bellegarde-sur-Valserine include Robert Barrier, a politician, David Venditti, a French rugby player, Younès Kaboul, a French football player, Nelly Moenne Loccoz, a French snowboarder and Yves Amoureux, an author and movie producer.
