Baku Stock Exchange

The Baku Stock Exchange, BSE () is the main stock exchange of Azerbaijan which is situated in the capital Baku. The Baku Stock Exchange was established in 2000 and it began its operations the same year on February 15 with the first transaction. The stock exchange is owned by its own members, who are shareholders. The current head of the stock exchange is Emin Aliyev, who is the Chairperson of the Board of directors.
