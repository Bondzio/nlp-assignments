Alan Kupperberg (May 18, 1953 â€“ July 16, 2015) was an American comic artist. He was known for working in both comic books and newspaper strips. He began writing and drawing for Marvel Comics in 1974. Kupperberg has done illustrations for Archie Comics, "National Lampoon", "Cracked" magazine, "Spy", and McClannahan Books.

Kupperberg died in New York City from thyroid cancer, aged 62.
