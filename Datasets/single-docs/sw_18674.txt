Wichita, Kansas

Wichita is the largest city in the U.S. State of Kansas.
Wichita is the 49th largest city in the United States with about 354,865 people in the year 2005. It is in Sedgwick County, which is in the south-central part of the state.

Many parts of airplanes are made in Wichita. Also, Wichita State University is in the city.
