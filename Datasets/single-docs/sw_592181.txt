Sarah Huckabee Sanders

Sarah Elizabeth Huckabee Sanders (born August 13, 1982) is an American political aide. Sanders is the 31st and current White House Press Secretary since July 26, 2017. She served as Deputy White House Press Secretary from January through July 2017. 

Sanders is the daughter of former governor of Arkansas and presidential candidate Mike Huckabee, and former Arkansas first lady Janet Huckabee.
