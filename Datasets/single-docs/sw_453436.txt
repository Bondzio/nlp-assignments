National Anthem of the Republic of China

The National Anthem of the Republic of China is the national anthem of the Republic of China. It is about how the Three Principles of the People can be used to achieve the dreams and hopes of the Chinese people.

On July 16, 1924, Sun Yat-sen wrote the words of the song for his speech at the opening ceremony of the Whampoa Military Academy.
