John de Lancie

Jonathan "John" de Lancie, Jr. (born March 20, 1948) is an American stand-up comedian, actor, singer, voice artist, and comedian. He is best known for his roles as Q in "Star Trek" and Discord in "".

De Lancie was born Jonathan de Lancie, Jr. in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania on March 20, 1948 to John and Andrea de Lancie.

De Lancie attended Kent State University and the Juilliard School. De Lancie has appeared in several television series. These include "L.A. Law", "Days of Our Lives", "", "The Practice" and "Picket Fences". He has also appeared in "Stargate SG-1", "Breaking Bad" and "".
