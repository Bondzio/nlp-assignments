Tú Robaste Mi Corazón

"Tu Robaste Mi Corazón" () is a duet by Mexican-American singer Selena and Mexican singer Emilio Navaira. "Tu Robaste Mi Corazon" was the third and last single from "Selena Live!" (1993). In "Siempre Selena" (1996), the male parts of the song was performed by Pete Astudillo. The song peaked at number five on the Hot Latin Tracks, number six on the Latin Pop Airplay and number eight on the Latin Regional Mexican Airplay charts.
