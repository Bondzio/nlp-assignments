Rick Moranis

Frederick Allan "Rick" Moranis (born April 18, 1953) is a semi-retired Canadian-American actor, comedian, screenwriter and songwriter. He was nominated for a Grammy Award two times. He was known for his role in the movie "Honey, I Shrunk the Kids". He is also known for appearing in "Ghostbusters", "The Flintstones", "Spaceballs", and in "Little Shop of Horrors".
