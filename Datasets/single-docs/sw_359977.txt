Cesar Romero

Cesar Julio Romero, Jr. (February 15, 1907 â€“ January 1, 1994) was an American stage, movie and television actor. He was in movies, radio works, and television programs for almost sixty years. His roles included playing lovers, historical people in costume dramas, characters in light comedies, and the Joker in the "Batman" 1966 TV series.

Romero was born on February 15, 1907 in New York City. He was raised in Bradley Beach, New Jersey. He never married. He was a homosexual. Romero died on January 1, 1994 in Santa Monica, California from complications of bronchitis and pneumonia. He was 86 years old.
