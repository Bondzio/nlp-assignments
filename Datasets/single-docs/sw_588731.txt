Gunter Gabriel

Gunter Gabriel (born Günter Caspelherr; 11 June 1942 – 22 June 2017) was a German singer, musician and composer. Gabriel became famous in Germany as singer of Schlager songs. He published "Wer einmal tief im Keller saß. Erinnerungen eines Rebellen" with Oliver Flesch in 2009. 

Gabriel died on 22 June 2017 in Hannover from complications of a fall at the age of 75.
