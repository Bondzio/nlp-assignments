Bob Costas

Robert Quinlan "Bob" Costas (born March 22, 1952 in Queens, New York City, New York) is a Sports commentator. He currently works with NBC Sports and MLB Network. Costas has hosted nine Olympic Games. He has won 26 Emmy Awards. He used to work with HBO hosting "Inside the NFL", "Costas Now", and "On the Record with Bob Costas". He called games for the American Basketball Association's Spirits of St. Louis, Missouri Tigers men's basketball, Chicago Bulls telecast on WGN-TV, and NFL and National Basketball Association games for CBS.
