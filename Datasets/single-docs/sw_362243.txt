Sylvia Sidney

Sophia Kosow (August 8, 1910 – July 1, 1999) was an American movie, television, voice, and stage actress who is known for her role in the crime-drama movies. She is also known for playing Florence Norris in "Mars Attacks!" and as Juno in "Beetlejuice". Sidney has won a Golden Globe Award and a Saturn Award.

Sidney was born in The Bronx, New York on August 8, 1910. Sidney has been married to Bennett Cerf from 1935 until they divorced in 193. Then she married Luther Adler from 1938 until they divorced in 1946. They had a son named Jacob (1939–1987). She was married to Carlton Alsop from 1947 until they divorced in 1951.

Sidney died on July 1, 1999 in New York City, New York from throat cancer. She was 88 years old.
