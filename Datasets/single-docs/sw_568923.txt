Dmytro Grabovskyy

Dmytro Grabovskyy (, 30 September 1985 â€“ 23 January 2017) was a Ukrainian-Israeli professional road bicycle racer. He was born in Simferopol, Ukraine. He last competed for UCI Continental team ISD-Lampre. He was second in the time trial for juniors at the 2005 World Championship Cycling in Madrid and he won the road race. 

Grabovsky became an Israeli citizen in 2014.

He died from a heart attack in 23 January 2017, aged 31.
