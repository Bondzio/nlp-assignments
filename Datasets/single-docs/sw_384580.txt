John B. Anderson

John Bayard Anderson (born February 15, 1922) is an retired American-Republican politician from Illinois. He ran for president in 1980, but lost to Ronald Reagan as the main Republican candidate. He was a politician since the 1960s. He would later lose as a candidate as an independent.

Anderson was born on February 15, 1922 in Rockford, Illinois to a Swedish-American family. He studied at the University of Illinois, at the University of Illinois College of Law, and at the Harvard Law School. Anderson was married to Keke Machakos since 1953.
