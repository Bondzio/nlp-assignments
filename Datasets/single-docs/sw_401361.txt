Ayelet Zurer

Ayelet Zurer (born 28 June 1969) is an Israeli actress. She is known for her roles in "Angels & Demons", "Fugitive Pieces", and in "Man of Steel".

Zurer was born on 28 June 1969 in Tel Aviv, Israel to a Jewish family. She has been married to Gilad Londovski since 2003. They had one child.
