Shailene Woodley

Shailene Woodley (born November 15, 1991) is an American television and movie actress. She has starred in many sitcoms. She worked with George Clooney in "The Descendants", a drama movie in 2011. She had work on Divergent as Tris Prior and in The Fault in our Stars as Hazel Grace.

She was born in San Bernardino County, California. She was raised in Simi Valley, Caliofrnia.
