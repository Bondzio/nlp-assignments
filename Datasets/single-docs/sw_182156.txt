Giovanni Trapattoni

Giovanni Trapattoni (born 17 March 1939) is an Italian former football player who is now manager (head coach) of the Republic of Ireland national team. He has played for Italy national team. He is considered to be one of the most successful coaches of Italys Seria A.

Trapattoni had a successful career as a player with A. C. Milan in the 1960s and early 1970s. Playing as a defender and defensive midfielder. He played for the Italian national team
