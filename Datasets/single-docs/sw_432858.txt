Ezra Miller

Ezra Matthew Miller (30, September 1992) is an American actor. He played Patrick in the 2012 movie "The Perks of Being a Wallflower". For the role he won the Boston Society of Film Critics Award for Best Supporting Actor. He will play Leon in the 2014 movie "Madame Bovary". He has said that he is queer, not gay.

He appeared in the 2016 movie "" as The Flash. He will reprise his role in the upcoming "Suicide Squad" and the 2017 movie "Justice League". He portrayed Credence Barebone in the 2016 movie "Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them".
