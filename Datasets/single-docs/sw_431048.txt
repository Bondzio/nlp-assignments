Elinor Donahue

Mary Eleanor Donahue (born April 19, 1937), best known as Elinor Donahue, is an American actress. She was known for her role in the 1950s sitcom, "Father Knows Best". She was born in Tacoma, Washington

Donahue was married for nearly 30 years to producer Harry Ackerman. They met each other on the set of "Father Knows Best". She and Ackerman had three children together: Peter, James, and Christopher Ackerman.
