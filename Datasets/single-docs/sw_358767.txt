Rachael MacFarlane

Rachael Ann MacFarlane (born March 21, 1976) is an American voice actress best known for her voice roles in "Codename: Kids Next Door" as supreme leader Numbuh 362 and the FOX television animated series "Family Guy" and "American Dad!", both created by her brother Seth MacFarlane. She was born in Kent, Connecticut. She has been married to Spencer Laudiero since 2008. They have one daughter.
