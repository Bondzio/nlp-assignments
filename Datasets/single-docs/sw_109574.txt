Province of Ravenna

The province of Ravenna () is a province in the Emilia-Romagna region in northeastern Italy, on the Adriatic Sea. Its capital is the city of Ravenna.

The province was created in 1859.

The province of Ravenna is in the eastern part of the Emilia-Romagna region along the Adriatic Sea.
