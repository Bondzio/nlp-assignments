Edie Adams

Edith Elizabeth Enke, also known as Edie Adams (April 16, 1927 â€“ October 15, 2008), was an American business woman, singer, actress and comedienne. She won several Tony and Emmy Awards for her acting. She played Eileen Sherwood in the Broadway play "Wonderful Town". She also played Miss Olsen in the movie "The Apartment".

Adams was born in Kingston, Pennsylvania. She died from pneumonia and cancer at age eighty-one in Los Angeles.
