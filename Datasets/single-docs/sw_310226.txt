Dave Semenko

David John Semenko (July 12, 1957 â€“ June 29, 2017) was a Canadian professional ice hockey centre that played 2 seasons in the WHA and 10 seasons in the National Hockey League (NHL) for the Edmonton Oilers, Hartford Whalers and Toronto Maple Leafs organizations. He won back-to-back Stanley Cups in 1984 and 1985 with the Edmonton Oilers. Fans nicknamed Semenko "Cementhead" even though his official team nickname was always "Sammy".

Semenko died on June 29, 2017, 13 days before his 60th birthday after a short battle with liver and pancreatic cancer.
