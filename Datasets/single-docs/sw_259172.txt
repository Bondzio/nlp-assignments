Love, Pain &amp; the Whole Crazy Thing

Love, Pain & the Whole Crazy Thing is the fifth studio album by New Zealand-born Australian country music singer Keith Urban. It was released on November 7, 2006. The producers of the album was Dan Huff and Keith Urban. The album is certified double Multi-Platinum by the RIAA and Platinum by the RIAA. The album sold over 2,000,000 units in the United States and over 100,000 in Canada.
