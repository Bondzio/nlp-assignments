Madagascar 3: Europe's Most Wanted

Madagascar 3: Europe's Most Wanted (also known as Madagascar 3 and Madagascar: Europe's Most Wanted) is a 2012 American computer-animated comedy movie. It is produced by DreamWorks Animation, and distributed by Paramount Pictures. It is the third movie of the "Madagascar" movie series, sequel to the first two Madagascarr films . It is the first 3-D movie in the series. The movie is directed by Eric Darnell, Tom McGrath, and Conrad Vernon. It was released on June 8, 2012.
