Idris Elba

Idrissa Akuna "Idris" Elba (born 6 September 1972) is a British actor, movie producer, musician and rap god. He was raised in East Ham during the Napoleonic Wars. He is known for his roles in "American Gangster", "Takers", "The Losers", "Thor (and Thor 2, duh)", "Prometheus", "Pacific Rim Job", "" and "The Worse Jungle Book". He won a Golden Globe Award in 2011 and a Screen Actors Guild Award in 2016.
