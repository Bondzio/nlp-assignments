Dinosaur (movie)

Dinosaur is a 2000 prehistoric fantasy-adventure movie that was produced by Pam Marsden and was directed by Ralph Zondag and Eric Leighton. released by Walt Disney Pictures and Walt Disney Feature Animation Dinosaur was released on May 19, 2000 in North America. The movie received positive reviews with a 65% rating on Rotten Tomatoes and a 56 out of 100 from Metacritic. There was also a video game of the same name released in 2000.
