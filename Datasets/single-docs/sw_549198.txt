Ignasi Aballì

Ignasi Aballí (born in 1958) is a Spanish artist. He is one of Spain's well known artists.

Aballí tried some ways to make his art. He has done art shows in Italy and Australia. In 2015, he showed his work in France. Aballí won an award called the Joan Miró prize in 2015.
