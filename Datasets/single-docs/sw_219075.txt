Home Alone 3

Home Alone 3 is the third film in the Home Alone series. It is an American criminal comedy movie. The movie has a different household and villains than the first two films. The director was Raja Gosnell, the editor of both originl films and the star was Alex D. Linz. It was released on December 12, 1997 by 20th Century Fox. The film grossed $79,082,515 worldwide.

In this film the boy, Alex, is sick at home with the chicken pox. He must defend his home against four burglars.

A made-for-television sequel, "Home Alone 4: Taking Back the House", followed in 2002 and Home Alone 5 followed in 2012.
