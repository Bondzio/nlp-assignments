Fred Willard

Frederic "Fred" Willard (born September 18, 1939) is an American actor, comedian, voice actor and writer. His best known movie roles include "This Is Spinal Tap" (1984), "Waiting for Guffman" (1997), "American Wedding" (2003), "A Mighty Wind" (2003), "" (2004), "Harold & Kumar Go to White Castle" (2004), "WALL-E" (2008), "Youth in Revolt" (2009) and "" (2013). He had also appeared in television series such as "Everybody Loves Raymond", "The Simpsons" and "Modern Family".

Willard was born in Shaker Heights, Ohio. He has been married to Mary Lovell since 1968. They have a daughter, Hope. In July 2012, he was arrested after "engaging in a lewd act" in an adult movie theater.
