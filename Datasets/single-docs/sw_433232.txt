Bronies: The Extremely Unexpected Adult Fans of My Little Pony

Bronies: The Extremely Unexpected Adult Fans of My Little Pony is a 2012 documentary film about bronies. These are adult fans of the 2010 animated television series, "". The film was released on DVD and Blu-ray on February 17, 2013. Laurent Malaquais directed the film. The creator of "Friendship Is Magic" executive-produced it.
