T-Pain

Faheem Rasheed Najm (born September 30, 1983), better known by his stage name T-Pain, is an American singer-songwriter, rapper, record producer, and actor. He is signed to Young Money Entertainment. His debut album, "Rappa Ternt Sanga", was released in 2005. In 2007, T-Pain released his second studio album "Epiphany". This album became number one on the "Billboard" 200. His third studio album,"Thr33 Ringz", was released in 2008. T-Pain has two Grammy Awards. T-Pain is the founder of the record label Nappy Boy Entertainment. It was created in 2005. 

As a singer, T-Pain is well known for using Auto-Tune to affect how his voice sounds.
