Lamborghini Countach

The Lamborghini Countach is a sport car that was built by Lamborghini from 1974 to 1990. The Countach is named after the type of fighting bull. The Countach was replaced by the Lamborghini Diablo. The designer of the Countach was Gruppo Bertone. The production of the Countach ended in 1990. Top Gear named the Countach #1 on their list of "100 Sexiest Supercars of All Time".
