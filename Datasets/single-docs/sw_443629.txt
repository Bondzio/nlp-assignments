Bert I. Gordon

Bert I. Gordon (born September 24, 1922) is an American movie maker. He made many movies that are science fiction. He made his first movie when he was 32 in 1955, called "King Dinosaur". In the movie, a group of astronauts go to another planet and find lots of strange creatures.

In an interview with Shout! Factory, he explained he always wanted to make movies ever since one of his relatives gave him a video camera.

Gordon had a daughter, Susan. She was a child actor. She even starred in Bert's movie, "Tormented".
