Lars von Trier

Lars von Trier (b. 30 April 1956) is a Danish movie director. He has won many prizes. These include the Palme d'Or, the Grand Prix, and the Prix du Jury at the Cannes Film Festival. Some of his movies are controversial, such as "Antichrist". His newest film, Nymphomaniac, was released on December 25, 2013 in Denmark.
