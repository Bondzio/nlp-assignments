Mike Enzi

Mike Enzi (born February 1, 1944) is the senior United States Senator from Wyoming. He is a member of the U.S. Republican Party and has been in Senate since January 3, 1997.

Enzi was born on February 1, 1944 in Bremerton, Washington. He studied at George Washington University and at University of Denver. He is married to Diana Buckley. They have three children.
