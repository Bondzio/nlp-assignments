R. Lee Ermey

Ronald Lee Ermey (born March 24, 1944) is an American actor and voice actor. Ermey served with the United States Marine Corps in Vietnam and was retired in 1971. He was the voice of Sarge in "Toy Story", "Toy Story 2", and "Toy Story 3". Ermey was born on March 24, 1944 in Emporia, Kansas. Ermey has been married to Nila Ermey since 1975; they have 4 children. Ermey retired as a Staff Sergeant but was later given the honorary rank of Gunnery Sergeant.
