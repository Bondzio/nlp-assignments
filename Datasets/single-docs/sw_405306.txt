George Becwar

George Jerome Becwar (September 16, 1917 - July 9, 1970) was an actor. He was born in Berwyn, Illinois. He played Prof. Strowski in Ed Wood's "Bride of the Monster" (1955). He appeared in 1950s-early 1960s television series like "Sky King", "Fury", "Sea Hunt", and "The Rebel". He died in Santa Monica, California.
