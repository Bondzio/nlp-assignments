Toyota Highlander

The Toyota Highlander is a medium-sized crossover sport utility vehicle sold by Toyota. The Highlander is on the Camry platform and shares the Camry's parts including the engine. The Highlander was redesigned in 2008 and Toyota sells the Highlander in Australia and Japan as the Kluger. it has a v6 or v8 engine. The Highlander's competitors are the Nissan Pathfinder, Chevrolet Traverse, Hyundai Santa Fe and the Ford Explorer.
