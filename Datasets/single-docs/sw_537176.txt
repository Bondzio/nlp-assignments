Daan Myngheer

Daan Myngheer (13 April 1993 – 28 March 2016) was a Belgian cyclist. In 2015, he was a member of the Verandas Willems cycling team and in 2016 he was a member of the Roubaix–Lille Métropole. He was born in Roeselare, Belgium.

Myngheer died in a hospital in Ajaccio, France after suffering a heart attack during the Criterium International, aged 22.
