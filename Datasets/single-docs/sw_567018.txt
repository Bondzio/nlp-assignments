Cem Özer

Cem Özer (born October 5, 1959, Göksun, Kahramanmaraş), Turkish theater, cinema and serial player, server and writer.

The artist who created the Istanbul University Faculty of Law, later worked in communities such as the Dostlar Theater. He was recognized on television by the presentation of the program. He was a lucky competitor in the Dene competition. He has appeared in several motion picture films and series. Fashion designer Esin Maraşlıoğlu and actress Nurgül Yeşilçay were married.
