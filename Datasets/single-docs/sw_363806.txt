Ben Mendelsohn

Paul Benjamin "Ben" Mendelsohn (born 3 April 1969) is an Australian movie, television, stage, and voice actor who is known for his roles as Andrew "Pope" Cody in "Animal Kingdom", Russell in "Killing Them Softly", John Daggett in "The Dark Knight Rises" and as Orson Krennic in "Rogue One". 

Mendelsohn was born on 3 April 1969 in Melbourne, Australia He was married to Emma Forrest since 2012.
