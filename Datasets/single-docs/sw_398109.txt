Sandy Lam

Sandy Lam, () also known as Lam Yik Lin, is a singer of Cantopop music. She sings in Cantonese, Mandarin, English, and Japanese.



On January 2017, joined Hunan Television "Singer 2017", as the 1st round competitors, on April 15, 2017 won the competitions.
