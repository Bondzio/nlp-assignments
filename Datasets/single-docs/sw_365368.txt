Steve Forrest

Steve Forrest (September 29, 1925 â€“ May 18, 2013) was an American movie, television, stage, and voice actor. He was known for his role as Lt. Hondo Harrelson in "S.W.A.T.".

Forrest was born William Forrest Andrews in Huntsville, Texas on September 29, 1925. He has been married to Christine Carilas from 1948 until his death in 2013. They had three children. 

Forrest died in Thousand Oaks, California on May 18, 2013 from natural causes, aged 87. He was later buried at Pierce Brothers Valley Oaks Memorial Park Westlake, Los Angeles County, California.
