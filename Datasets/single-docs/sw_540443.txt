Outram Park MRT Station

Outram Park MRT Station (EW16/NE3/TE17) is an MRT Station in Singapore. It is an interchange station on the East West MRT Line and North East MRT Line. In 2021 it will also be an interchange for the [Thomson-East Coast MRT Line]].
