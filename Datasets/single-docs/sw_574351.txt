Moshe Katsav

Moshe Katsav, Hebrew: משה קצב , (born December 5, 1945 in Yazd, Iran) is a former Israeli politician of Likud. 

He was mayor of Kiryat Malakhi, a Member of Knesset and a (deputy) government minister. He was also president of Israel from 2000 to 2007. 

From 2011 to 2016 he was in prison because of a conviction of sexual offences (like rape). Katsav denied the accusations.
