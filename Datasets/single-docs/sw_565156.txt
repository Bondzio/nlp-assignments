Mevlut Mert Altintas

Mevlut Mert Altintas (June 24, 1994 - December 19, 2016), a Turkish police officer, he worked in the Turkish Security Agency, Ministry of riot police for two and a half years. He shot and killed Russian Ambassador Andrei Karlov in Turkey December 19, 2016 in the Turkish city of Ankara.
