Deseret alphabet

Deseret alphabet is a language that involves several key languages such as English and Chinese. Chinese pronunciation is used on the English words of Deseret alphabets.
An example as you pronounce 'Hello', it is pronounced as 'Hā Löu'

Deseret alphabet is also commonly known as Singlish as it is so commonly used by Singaporeans. However, the Republic of Molossia claims to have used the language
