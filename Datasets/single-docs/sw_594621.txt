Sapa'u Ruperake Petaia

Sapa'u Ruperake Petaia (born 11 April 1951) is a Samoan poet and writer. 

He was born in Samoa, and studied at the University of South Pacific in Fiji. He is working as a civil servant manager in Samoa.

A collection of his poems called "Blue Rain" was published in 1980 (among them the poem "Kidnapped"). Another poem called "Patches of the Rainbow" was published in 1992.

In his works he is dealing with the colonial and Western effects on Samoa.
