Andy Samberg

Andrew David "Andy" Samberg (born August 18, 1978) is an American actor, stand-up comedian, singer, writer and the lead member of the comedy group, The Lonely Island. He is known for his works in "Saturday Night Live", "Hot Rod" and "Brooklyn Nine-Nine".

He is influenced by Steve Martin, Mel Brooks and Adam Sandler. He married Joanna Newsom in September 2013.
