Léon Degrelle

Léon Joseph Marie Ignace Degrelle (June 15, 1906 in Bouillon - March 31, 1994 in Malaga) was a Belgian politician and collaborator.

Degrelle is the founder of the fascist party of Rex in 1936.

During World War II he erected the Walloon Legion, which fought as a Waffen-SS unit at the Eastern Front.

After the war he managed to escape to Spain, where he died in 1994, aged 87.
