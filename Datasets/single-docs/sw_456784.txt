Vincent C. Gray

Vincent Condol Gray (born November 8, 1942) is an American politician. He served as the seventh Mayor of Washington, D.C. from 2011 to 2015. Gray served as Chairman of the Council of the District of Columbia, and as Councilmember for Ward 7. In the 1990s he also served as director of the DC Department of Human Services.

On April 1, 2014, he was defeated in the Democratic primary by Muriel Bowser.
