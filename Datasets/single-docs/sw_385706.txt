Martha Reeves

Martha Rose Reeves (born July 18, 1941) is an American musician and former politician. She was a councilwoman in Detroit, Michigan. She was associated with R&B group Martha and the Vandellas during the 1960s. Together, their hits included "Dancing in the Street" and "Heat Wave". She had some solo songs, also.

Reeves was a Detroit Councilwoman between 2005 and 2009. She was born in Alabama. She was raised in Detroit.
