Gary Veneruzzo

Gary Raymond Veneruzzo (born June 28, 1943 in Fort William, Ontario) is a retired professional ice hockey player who played 7 games in the National Hockey League and 348 games in the World Hockey Association. He would with the St. Louis Blues, Los Angeles Sharks, Michigan Stags, Baltimore Blades, Cincinnati Stingers, Phoenix Roadrunners, and San Diego Mariners.
