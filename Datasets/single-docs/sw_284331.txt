Faye Dunaway

Dorothy Faye Dunaway (born January 14, 1941 in Bascom, Florida, USA) is an American actress. She has starred in "Bonnie and Clyde", "Chinatown", and the 1968 and 1999 film versions of "The Thomas Crown Affair". She won an Academy Award for Best Actress for her role in "Network".
