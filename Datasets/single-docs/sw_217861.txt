Tech N9ne

Aaron Dontez Yates (born November 8, 1971 in Kansas City, Missouri) is an American rapper better known by his stage name Tech N9ne. 

He has been rapping since 1985 and has made 13 studio albums. He got his stage name from rapper Black Walt because of his fast rhyming style.

He is mainly a hip-hop artist but also raps in the horrorcore genre of music.
