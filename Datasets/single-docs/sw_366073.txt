Nicolas Roeg

Nicolas Jack Roeg (born 25 August 1929) is an English movie director and cinematographer who is known for his works in "Don't Look Now", "The Man Who Fell to Earth", "Walkabout", "Bad Timing", and in "Performance".

Roeg was born on 25 August 1929 in London, England. Roeg was married to Susan Stephen from 1957 until they divorced in 1977. He was married to actress Theresa Russell from 1982 until they divorced. He has been married to Harriet Harper since 2004.
