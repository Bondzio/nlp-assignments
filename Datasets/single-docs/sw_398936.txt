María Begoña Redal Giraldos

Maria Begoña Redal Giraldos (born August 3, 1975 in Navarra) is a goalball player from Spain. She is blind and is a type B2 goalball player. She played goalball at the 1996 Summer Paralympics. Her team was third. She played goalball at the 2000 Summer Paralympics in Sydney, Australia. Her team was second.
