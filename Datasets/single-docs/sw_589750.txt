Wülflingen

Wülflingen is a district in the city of Winterthur in Switzerland. It is District number 6.

The district has these quarters: Weinberg, Oberfeld, Lindenplatz, Niederfeld, Neuburg, Hardau, Härti and Taggenberg.

Until 1922 it was a municipality on its own. Then it became part of Winterthur.
