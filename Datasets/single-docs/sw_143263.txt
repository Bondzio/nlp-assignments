Imran Khan

Imran Ahmad Khan Niazi () (born 25 November 1952) is a retired Pakistani cricketer who played international cricket for two decades in the late 20th century. He became a politician in the mid-1990s. Currently, besides running his own political party, Khan is also a charity worker and cricket commentator.
He made his political party Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaaf(PTI). He made cancer hospital after his mother died because of this disease.
