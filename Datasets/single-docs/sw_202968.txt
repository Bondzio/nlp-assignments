Donau-Ries

Donau-Ries ("Danube-Ries") is a rural district in Swabia in southwest Bavaria, Germany. Neighbor districts are:Ansbach (district), Weißenburg-Gunzenhausen, Eichstätt (district), Neuburg-Schrobenhausen, Aichach-Friedberg, Augsburg (district), Dillingen (district) and in Baden-Württemberg: Heidenheim (district) and Ostalbkreis); Donauwörth is the capital of the Donau-Ries.
