Jennifer Tilly

Jennifer Tilly (born Jennifer Elizabeth Chan, 16 September 1958) is an American actress. She was born in Harbor City, Los Angeles, California. Her father was Chinese American; her mother was of Irish, Finnish and Native American ancestry. Tilly's sister is actress Meg Tilly (born 1960). Tilly's acting career began in 1983. She acted in "Bound", "Liar Liar" and two movies in the "Child's Play" series. She plays Bonnie Swanson in television series "Family Guy".
