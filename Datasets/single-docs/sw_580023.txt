Bill Page

Bill Page (born September 11, 1925) is an American reed player, band leader, and entrepreneur. He was best known for his work in the Lawrence Welk Band. 

Page was born in Chicago, Illinois. He studied at Wilbur Wright College.

Page left the Lawrence Welk Show in 1965 and went on to perform with Barry Manilow, Ted Mack, Frank Gorshin, Judy Garland, and on "The Tonight Show" starring Johnny Carson.
