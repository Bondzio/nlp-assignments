Yuki Kato

Yuki Anggraini Kato (born 2 April 1995 in Malang, East Java) is an Indonesian actress. Yuki Kato became known after starring in the soap opera "Primata Cantik". In addition to being the main character in the soap opera series "Heart" and "My Love", Yuki she also starred in "Jodoh Arietta" as the sister of Raffi Ahmad's character.
