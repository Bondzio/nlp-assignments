Pam Tillis

Pamela Yvonne "Pam" Tillis (born July 24, 1957) is an American country music singer and actress. She began her career in 1981. In 1991, she had a song in the Top 40 on the "Billboard" Hot Country Songs for the first time. Her father is country musician Mel Tillis.

Tillis has released many albums. Two of them are "Put Yourself in My Place" and "Sweetheart's Dance".

Tillis was born in Florida. She was raised in Nashville, Tennessee. Her father is country music singer Mel Tillis.
