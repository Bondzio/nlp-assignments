NXT Tag Team Championship

The NXT Tag Team Championship is a professional wrestling championship in the WWE. The title is a NXT-only championship.

The title was introduced by special guest Shawn Michaels on the January 23, 2013 edition of NXT. He announced that there would be a tournament held to crown the inaugural champions.

The current champions are #DIY (Johnny Gargano and Tommaso Ciampa). They are in their first reign, both as a team and individually.
