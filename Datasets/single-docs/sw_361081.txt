Yun Jeung

Yun Jeung ( Hanja 尹拯, 1629-1714), also known as Yung Chŭng, was a Korean scholar, writer, politician, and Neo-Confucian philosopher of the Joseon period. The name he used for writing was Myungjae. 

Yun was the son of Yun Seon-geo.

Yun is part of the Silhak school.

Yun was a disciple of Kim Jip, Song Siyeol. He was a political rival of Kwon Sang-ha.
