Peter Howell (actor)

Peter Howell (1919 – 20 April 2015) was an English actor. He was known for his appearances on "Emergency – Ward 10", "The Avengers", "The Prisoner", "Doctor Who", "The Lord of the Rings", and "Yes Minister".

Howell was born in London. He died after a short illness on 20 April 2015 in Northwood, aged 95/96.
