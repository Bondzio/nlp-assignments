Khloé Kardashian

Khloé Alexandra Kardashian (born June 27, 1984) is an American television personality. She is the sister of Kim and Kourtney Kardashian. She was married to Los Angeles Lakers basketball player Lamar Odom from 2009 to 2016. Kardashian is the daughter of Kris and Robert Kardashian Sr., who is known for representing O. J. Simpson during his infamous 1995 trial. She has one brother, Rob Kardashian Jr. She has had her own reality television series, "Kourtney and Khloé take Miami" and "Khloé & Lamar". She and her sisters own the fashion store DASH and formerly owned the children's store Smooch, both run by her family.

In 2017, calling herself the "fat, funny sister" during her childhood, Kardashian introduced the new E! TV series "Revenge Body with Khloé Kardashian", focused on helping contestants lose weight and improve physically and mentally over 12 weeks before making a reveal of their new body. The show was picked up for a second season.
