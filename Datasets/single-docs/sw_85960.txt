Contone

Contone is a municipality of the district Locarno in the canton of Ticino in Switzerland. On 25 April 2010 the former municipalities of Contone, Caviano, Gerra Gambarogno, Indemini, Magadino, Piazzogna, San Nazzaro, Sant'Abbondio and Vira Gambarogno merged into the new municipality of Gambarogno.
