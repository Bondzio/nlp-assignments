Lollipop (Candyman)

"Lollipop (Candyman)" is a pop song from the group Aqua. It is from their first album, "Aquarium". The song was released in 1997. This song was said to be one of the group's favorite songs. It was not as successful as their song "Barbie Girl".

The longest version is 7 minutes and 28 seconds long.
