Dami Im

Dami Im (, born 17 October 1988) is a South Korean-born Australian singer. She is best known for winning the fifth season of "The X Factor" in 2013. Her first album was an ARIA number one. She has three studio albums, two extended plays, nine singles, two album appearances, and four music videos. She represented Australia in the 2016 Eurovision Song Contest, coming second with "Sound of Silence".

Im was born in Incheon. She grew up in Brisbane, Queensland. She is married and lives in Daisy Hill, a suburb of Logan City, Queensland. She is a Christian.
