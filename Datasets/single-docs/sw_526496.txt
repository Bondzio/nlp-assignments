Stuck in the Middle (TV series)

Stuck in the Middle is an upcoming Disney Channel Original Series. It was created by Alison Brown and executive producer Linda Videtti Figueiredo. The series stars Jenna Ortega as Harley Diaz.. The series is going to air on February 14, 2016.

The series is about a family of 9, seen through the point of view of Harley Diaz, a 12 year old inventor who just so happens to be the middle kid in a large family,


The Disney Channel series "Stuck In The Middle" started airing on February 14, 2016. The Series premiered on March 11, 2016. Production began on November 30, 2015.
