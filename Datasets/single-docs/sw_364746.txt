Resident Evil 6

Resident Evil 6 is a survival horror video game made by Capcom for the PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360 game consoles. It's the next game in the "Resident Evil" video game series for the PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360. The demo for Resident Evil 6 received bad reviews from IGN. The actual game itself received mixed reviews from reviewers such as GameSpot, IGN and G4.
