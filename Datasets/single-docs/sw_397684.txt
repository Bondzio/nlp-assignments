Constantino Romero

Constantino Romero García (May 29, 1947 – May 12, 2013) was a Spanish TV and radio host and actor. 

He was known for dubbing in Spanish as Darth Vader in "Star Wars", as James T. Kirk in "Star Trek", as James Bond in several "James Bond" movies, as Harry Callahan in "Dirty Harry", and as the Terminator in "Terminator".

Romero was born on May 29, 1947 in Alcalá de Henares, Spain. He was raised in Chinchilla de Monte-Aragón. Romero retired in 2012 after 47 years of acting. He was married to Joana Vila until his death. Romero died on May 12, 2013 from ALS in his home in Barcelona, Spain, aged 65.
