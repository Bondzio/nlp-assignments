Ana Johnsson

Anna Lovisa Johnsson better known by her stage name Ana Johnsson () (born October 4, 1977 in Gothenburg, Sweden) is a Swedish singer and songwriter.

Johnsson currently lives in Stockholm, Sweden, and is engaged. Johnsson gave birth to her first child, a boy, on June 1, 2011. Johnsson names Foo Fighters, Red Hot Chili Peppers, Creed, Erykah Badu and Jane's Addiction as her favorite bands.
