Vailly-sur-Aisne

Vailly-sur-Aisne is a commune. It is in the Picardie region in the Aisne department in the north of France. In 2012, 2,055 people lived there.

In July 1429, Charles VII of France along with Joan of Arc and after his coronation in Reims, stopped in Vailly where he spent the night there.
