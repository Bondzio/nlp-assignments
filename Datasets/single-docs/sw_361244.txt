Gary Cole

Gary Michael Cole (born September 20, 1956) is an American television and movie actor. 

Cole was born in Park Ridge, Illinois on September 20, 1956. He graduated at Illinois State University.

Cole began his acting career on stage with the Steppenwolf Theatre Company in 1985. In television, Cole had starring roles in the series "Midnight Caller", "American Gothic", and "Crusade". In movies, Cole appeared in supporting roles in "The Brady Bunch Movie", "Office Space", "", and "". He provided the voice of Mayor Jones in "Scooby-Doo! Mystery Incorporated". 

He has been married to Teddi Siddall since 1992. They have a daughter.
