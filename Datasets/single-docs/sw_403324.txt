Marcos Manuel Llados

Marcos Manuel Llados (born March 26, 1962 in Madrid) is a ski athlete from Spain. He has a disability: he cannot see. He is a B2 type skier. He ski raced at the 1992 Winter Paralympics. He was the number three skier in the giant slalom race. He ski raced at the 1994 Winter Paralympics. He was the number three skier in the super giant race.
