Judith Anderson

Dame Judith Anderson, AC, DBE (10 February 1897 â€“ 3 January 1992) was an Australian actress. She lived in the United States and performed on stage, television and movies. She received two Emmy Awards and a Tony Award for her acting. Her Hollywood movies included the Academy Award-nominated movie "Rebecca", "Laura" in 1944 and "The Diary of a Chambermaid" in 1946. Anderson was born in Adelaide, South Australia, although she later relocated to Santa Barbara, California. Anderson died of pneumonia at 94.
