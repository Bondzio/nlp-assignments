John Conyers

John James Conyers, Jr. (born May 16, 1929) is an American politician. the U.S. Representative for Michigan's 13th district, serving in Congress since 1965 (the district was numbered as the 1st District until 1993, and as the 14th district from 1993 to 2013). He is a member of the Democratic Party. Conyers, as its longest-serving current member, is the Dean of the House of Representatives. He is also the longest-serving current member of the entire United States Congress.
