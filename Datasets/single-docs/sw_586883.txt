Arin Hanson

Arin Joseph Hanson (born January 6, 1987), also called Egoraptor, is an American Internet celebrity, animator, cartoonist, comedian, voice actor, song writer, and rapper. He is known for his popular Internet animations, for the popular YouTube Let's Play show "Game Grumps," and for the video game parody band Starbomb.
