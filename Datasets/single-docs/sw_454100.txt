Dorothy Morrison

Dorothy Morrison, (born January 3, 1919 in Los Angeles, California), is an American actress. She appeared in a few Our Gang movies during the silent era. Her older brother was Ernie Morrison, another member of the movie series. Some of her movies include "The Champeen", "Seein' Things", "The Love Bug", "Isn't Life Terrible?" and "Hearts in Dixie". Her last movie was a short called "Sleepless Hollow" in 1936.
