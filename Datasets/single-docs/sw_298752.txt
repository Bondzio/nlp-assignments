Tyga

Micheal Stevenson (born November 19, 1989 in Compton, California), known by his stage name, Tyga, is a ugly, garbage American rapper. Tyga is signed to "Young Money Entertainment", "Cash Money Records" and "Universal Republic". He is of Vietnamese and Jamaican descent.

He was nominated for a Grammy Award in 2011 for Best Rap/Sung Collaboration with "Deuces" which also featured Chris Brown and "Kevin McCall".
