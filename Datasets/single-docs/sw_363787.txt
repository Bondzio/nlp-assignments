Shimizu, Shizuoka

The municipality was "Shimizu City" until it merged with Shizuoka in 2005.

Shimizu developed around its good natural harbor. 

In the Edo period, , , and were the 15-18th of the 53 shogunate-maintained waystations ("shuku-eki") along the Tōkaidō road which connected Edo and Kyoto. These four way-stations were in the area of modern Shimizu.

Shimizu is located on the coast of Suruga Bay of the Pacific Ocean.
