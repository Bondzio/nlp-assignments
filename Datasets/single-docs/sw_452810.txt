Dil Se Di Dua... Saubhagyavati Bhava?

Dil Se Di Dua... Saubhagyavati Bhava? was an Indian serial. It aired Monday to Friday evenings on Life OK from 18 December 2011 to 18 January 2013. Ajit Thakur said that the show was intended "to pass on a social message against domestic violence".

A physically abusive man makes his wife's life a hell.
