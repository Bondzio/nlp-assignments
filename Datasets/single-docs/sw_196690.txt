Sujoy Ghosh

Sujoy Ghosh is an Indian movie director. He mas made several movies including "Home Delivery" (2005) and "Jhannkaar Beats" (2003). A song from "Jhannkaar Beats", "Tu Aashiqui Hai", won a National Award.

Sujoy Ghosh was born in Calcutta in 1966. His family moved to London when he was 13. He attended Manchester University.
