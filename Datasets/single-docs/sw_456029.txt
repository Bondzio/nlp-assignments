Emilio Álvarez Montalván

Emilio Álvarez Montalván (31 July 1919 – 2 July 2014) was a Nicaraguan diplomat and politician. Her served as a the Foreign Minister of the Republic of Nicaragua from 1996 until 2001. He was a ophthalmologist, medical doctor and writer.

Álvarez was born in Managua. He was married to Carmen Guerra. They had six children.

Álvarez died from a heart attack on 2 July 2014, aged 94.
