Dale Earnhardt, Jr.

Ralph Dale Earnhardt, Jr. (born October 10, 1974) is an American racecar driver. Earnhardt drives the #88 AMP Energy/National Guard Chevrolet Impala SS in the NASCAR Sprint cup series for Hendrick Motorsports. Earnhardt, Jr. won the 2004 Daytona 500. Earnhardt is the son of the seven time Sprint cup champion Dale Earnhardt.
