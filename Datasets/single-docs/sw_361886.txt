Alfred Molina

Alfred Molina (born Alfredo Molina, May 24, 1953) is an English movie, television, stage, and voice actor. He is known for his roles as Diego Rivera in "Frida", Satipo in "Raiders of the Lost Ark", Dr. Octopus in "Spider-Man 2", and as Maxim Horvath in "The Sorcerer's Apprentice".

Molina was born on May 24, 1953 in Paddington, London, England to a Spanish mother and Italian father. He grew up in Notting Hill, London. He studied at Guildhall School of Music and Drama. Molina has been married to Jill Gascoine since 1986. He has one daughter from another relationship.
