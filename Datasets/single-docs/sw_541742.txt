Joe Temperley

Joe Temperley (20 September 1929 â€“ 11 May 2016) was a Scottish saxophonist. He has performed with many instruments but is most associated with the baritone saxophone and bass clarinet. He was born in Lochgelly, Scotland. Temperley played with The Thad Jones/Mel Lewis Orchestra, but most famously with Jazz at Lincoln Center Orchestra.

Temperley died in Manhattan, New York from kidney failure after suffering from cancer on 11 May 2016, aged 86.
