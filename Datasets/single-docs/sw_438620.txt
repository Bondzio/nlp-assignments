James Condon

James Thomas Condon (27 September 1923 â€“ 14 February 2014) was an Australian actor. He made many television appearances on shows such as: "The Story of Peter Grey", "Homicide", "Matlock Police", "Number 96", "Bellamy", "The Young Doctors", "Carson's Law", "Sons and Daughters", "Prisoner", "The Flying Doctors", "Neighbours", "Blue Heelers" and "Something in the Air". He was born on 27 September 1923 in Fremantle, Western Australia. He was of Irish ancestry.

Condon died on 14 February 2014, at the age of 90. He was outlived by his four daughters: Elizabeth, Susan, Katherine and Mary-Jane. His wife, actress Anne Haddy, died in 1999.
