Anyang, Gyeonggi

Anyang is a city in South Korea. It is in Gyeonggi province. Anyang is located south east of Seoul. The city has developed along Anyang Stream, which is 32.2 km long. The city is divided into two halves; the Manan district and Dongan districts. Dongan district has about 356,566 people and Manan district has about 216,714 people. Anyang is famous for Anyang yuwonji and the Anyang art park. It has seven mountains. Mt. Suri is the most famous one. 

It has produced many celebrities like Rain, Kim Jong Kook, Kang Min, Niel.
