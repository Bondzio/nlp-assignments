Márta Antal-Rudas

Márta Antal-Rudas (14 February 1937 – 6 June 2017) was a Hungarian javelin thrower. She competed at the 1960, 1964 and 1968 Olympics and finished in ninth, second and fourth place. She was born in Debrecen, Hungary.

Antal-Rudas died on 6 June 2017 in Budapest, Hungary at the age of 80.
