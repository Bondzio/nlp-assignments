Patricia Routledge

Patricia Routledge (born 17 February 1929) is an English retired character comedy actress and singer. She has received several awards. She received a Tony Award in 1968 and an Olivier Award in 1988, both for Best Actress in a Musical. She played the character Hyacinth Bucket in the British sitcom "Keeping Up Appearances". She also performed on stage, including the characters Hippolyta in "A Midsummer Night's Dream" and the title character for "Little Mary Sunshine".

Routledge was born in Tranmere, Cheshire, England.
