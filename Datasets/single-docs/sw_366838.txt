Jake LaMotta

Giacobbe "Jake" LaMotta (born July 10, 1921), nicknamed "The Bronx Bull" and "The Raging Bull," is an American retired professional boxer and former World Middleweight Champion. He was played by Robert De Niro in the 1980 movie "Raging Bull".

LaMotta is an Italian-American. He was born on July 10, 1921 in The Bronx, New York City. He has been married six times. He has four daughters.
