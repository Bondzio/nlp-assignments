Paolo Sorrentino

Paolo Sorrentino (born 31 May 1970) is an Italian director and screenwriter. His movies include "One Man Up" (2001), "The Consequences of Love" (2004), "The Family Friend" (2006), "Il Divo" (2008), "This Must Be the Place" (2011) and "The Great Beauty" (2013).

Sorrentino was born in Naples.
