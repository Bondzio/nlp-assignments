Ol' Dirty Bastard

Russell Tyrone Jones (November 15, 1968 â€“ November 13, 2004) was an American rapper and music producer. He was better known by his stage name Ol' Dirty Bastard. He is probably best known for being one of the founding members of the rap group Wu-Tang Clan. He also had a solo career and released two studio albums during that time.

Jones was African American. He had 13 children. He had problems with drug addiction. He also frequently broke the law; his convictions included attempted assault and cocaine possession. He was shot by robbers in 1998.

Jones died on November 13, 2004 due to an accidental drug overdose of a mixture of cocaine and prescription drug Tramadol.
