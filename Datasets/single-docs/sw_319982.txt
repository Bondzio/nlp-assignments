Kieran Ault-Connell

Kieran Ault-Connell (born 30 July 1981) is an Australian athlete with cerebral palsy. He was born in Melbourne. At the 2000 Summer Paralympics, he won two gold medals in the men's 4 x 100m relay T38 and men's 4 x 400m relay T38 races. At the 2004 Summer Paralympics, he won a silver medal in the Men's Javelin F37 event.
