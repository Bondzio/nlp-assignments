Guy Clark

Guy Charles Clark (November 6, 1941 â€“ May 17, 2016) was a Grammy Award winning American country and folk singer, musician, songwriter, recording artist, and performer. He has released more than twenty albums.

His songs have been recorded by other artists including Jerry Jeff Walker, Jimmy Buffett, Lyle Lovett, Ricky Skaggs, Steve Wariner, and Rodney Crowell. 

Clark won the 2014 Grammy Award for Best Folk Album: "My Favorite Picture Of You".

Clark died in Nashville, Tennessee from lymphoma on May 17, 2016, aged 74.
