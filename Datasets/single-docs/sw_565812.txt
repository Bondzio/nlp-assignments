Flåm, Norway

Flåm is a village in Norway. It is in the municipality of Aurland in Sogn og Fjordane county.

The closest airport is Sogndal Airport, Haukåsen, which is about 70 kilometres from Flåm. The European route E16 highway between Oslo and Bergen runs through Flåm.

In 2014, the population was reported to be around 350.
