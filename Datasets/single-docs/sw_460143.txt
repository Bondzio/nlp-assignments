Chicago (That Toddlin' Town)

Chicago" is a popular song. It was written by Fred Fisher and was published in 1922. The song has been recorded by many artists, but the best-known version is by Frank Sinatra. One line mentions that evangelist, Billy Sunday could not shut down Chicago.

The song was featured in H.C. Potter's 1939 movie, "The Story of Vernon and Irene Castle".
