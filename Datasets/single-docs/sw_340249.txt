Blaenavon Industrial Landscape

Blaenavon Industrial Landscape is the UNESCO World Heritage Site in Blaenavon, Wales.

The site includes coal and ore mines, quarries, a primitive railway system, furnaces, workers' homes, and the social infrastructure of the community.
