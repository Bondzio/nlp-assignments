Milap Chand Jain

Milap Chand Jain (21 July 1929 â€“ 29 April 2015) was an Indian judge and politician. He served as the Chief Justice of the Rajasthan High Court. He also served as the acting Governor of Rajasthan from 3 February to 14 February 1990. He was also the chairman of the "Jain Commission" set up by the government to inquire into Rajiv Gandhi's assassination.

Jain was born in Jodhpur, Rajasthan. He had three children; a son and two daughters.

Jain died after a short illness on 29 April 2015 in Jaipur, aged 86.
