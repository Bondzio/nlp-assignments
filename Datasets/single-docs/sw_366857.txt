Juno Temple

Juno Violet Temple (born July 21, 1989) is an English movie, television, stage, and voice actress who is known for her roles in "Atonement", "Dirty Girl", "St Trinian's", "Killer Joe" and in "The Dark Knight Rises".

Temple was born on July 21, 1989 in London, England to Amanda Pirie and to Julien Temple. She studied at Enmore Primary School, Bedales School, and at King's College.
