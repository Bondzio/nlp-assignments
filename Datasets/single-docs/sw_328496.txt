Bellver Castle

Bellver Castle ("bellver" in Castilian means "good view") is a castle that was built by order of King Jaime II for use as a residence of the kings of Mallorca.
The castle is built at above sea level.
Today it belongs to the City Council of Palma de Mallorca and it is the Museum of the History of the city of Palma. It is open to the public.
Uses: 

Bellver Castle was one of 100 finalists for the 12 Treasures of Spain in 2007.
