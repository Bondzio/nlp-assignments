Bauma

Bauma is a municipality in the district of Pfäffikon in the canton of Zurich in Switzerland.

On 1 January 2015 the former municipalities of Sternenberg merged into the municipality of Bauma.
Bauma, Sternenberg, Saland, Dillhaus, Dürstelen, Neuthal, Wolfensberg, Wellenau, Lipperschwändi and Schindlet.
