Joe Fleishaker

Joseph Isaac Fleishaker (1954 â€“ May 23, 2016) was an American character actor. He was born in Brooklyn. He was best known for his appearances in low-budget cult and horror movies such as those made by Troma Entertainment. He appeared in "Troma's War", "Sgt. Kabukiman N.Y.P.D." and "Tromeo and Juliet". He also appeared in several skits on "Late Show with David Letterman".

Fleishaker died of a heart attack at a hospital in The Bronx, New York on May 23, 2016, aged 62.
