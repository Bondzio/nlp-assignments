Rowland Evans

Rowland Evans (April 28, 1921-March 23,2001) was a conservative "Chicago Sun-Times" columnist and co-host of the Cable News Network's (CNN) "Evans & Novak", later "Evans, Novak, Hunt, & Shields". He was also a political analyst for CNN, along with his partner on "Evans and Novak" and at the "Chicago Sun Times", Robert Novak.
