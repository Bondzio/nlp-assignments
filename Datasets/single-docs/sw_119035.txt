Moorslede

Moorslede is a municipality in the Belgian province of West Flanders. Moorslede comprises the towns of Dadizele and Moorslede itself.

In 2007, 10697 people lived there.

It is at 50° 53 North, 03° 03 East.

Constant Lievens (1856-1893), the apostle of the Chota Nagpur, was born in Moorslede.

The 1950 World Cycling Championshipwas held in Moorslede and was won by Briek Schotte.
