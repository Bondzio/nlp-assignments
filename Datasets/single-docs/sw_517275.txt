Emilia Clarke

Emilia Clarke (born May 1, 1987) is an English actress. She played Sarah Connor in "Terminator Genisys". She also played Holly Golightly in the stage play of "Breakfast at Tiffany's". She was in "Game of Thrones". Clarke is best known for her role as Daenerys Targaryen in the HBO series "Game of Thrones". For this role she received an Emmy Award nomination for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Drama Series in 2013. She was nominated again for the same role in 2015. Emilia Clarke was born in London, England.
