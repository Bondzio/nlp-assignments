The Chainsmokers

The Chainsmokers are an American pop and electronic dance music duo. They are made up with Drew Taggart and Alex Pall.

The duo released their first studio album, "Bouquet", in October 2015. Their song, "Roses", reached the Top 10 on "Billboard" Hot 100. In 2016, "Closer", with vocals from Halsey, became their first #1 single on the Hot 100 chart.

The Chainsmokers are from New York City.
