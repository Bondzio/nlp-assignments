Frankie Bridge

Francesca "Frankie" Bridge (n√©e Sandford; born 14 January 1989) is an English singer-songwriter. 

She was previously a member of S Club Juniors and is now a member of girl group The Saturdays, signed to the Fascination and Polydor labels. 

The group has released sixteen singles so far, of which thirteen have reached the Top 10 on the UK Singles Chart. There was one UK number-one single, "What About Us". In addition, the group has had five Top 10 albums on the UK Albums Chart, which were all certified by the British Phonographic Industry. 

Bridge has had nineteen UK Top 10 Singles and six UK Top 10 Albums.
