Joey King

Joey Lynn King (born July 30, 1999) is an American teen movie, television, voice actress, model,and singer who is known for her roles as Ramona Quimby in "Ramona and Beezus". King also appeared in "The Dark Knight Rises", "Oz the Great and Powerful", "Family Weekend", "White House Down", and in "The Conjuring". She has won a Young Artist Award. Her career began in 2002.
