FAMAS

FAMAS or sometime FA-MAS ( or "Assault rifle of the Saint-Ã‰tienne weapon factory") is a bullpup-styled assault rifle designed and manufactured in France. It's the main assault rifle of the French Army, French Air Force, and French Navy since 1980. It was also sold for export.
