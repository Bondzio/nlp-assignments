Agnieszka Radwańska

Agnieszka Radwańska (born 6 March 1989 in Kraków) is a Polish tennis player. She is currently the world number three women's tennis player. She is also the highest ranking Polish tennis player.

In 2007, Radwańska became the first Polish player to win a WTA singles title. In 2012 she lost the final of the Wimbledon Championships to Serena Williams. She won WTA Finals in Singapour in 2015.
