Big Eyes

Big Eyes is a 2014 American biographical drama movie directed by Tim Burton and produced by Burton and Scott Alexander and Larry Karaszewski. 

It is based on the careers of American artists Margaret and her husband Walter Keane. It is set in San Francisco, California during the 1950s. They are played by Amy Adams and Christoph Waltz. It also stars Jason Schwartzman and Terence Stamp. The music was composed by Danny Elfman.

It was released to very positive reviews on December 25, 2014.
