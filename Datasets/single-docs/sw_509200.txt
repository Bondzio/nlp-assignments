David Perdue

David Perdue (born December 10, 1949) is the junior United States Senator from Georgia. He is a member of the U.S. Republican Party and has been in Senate since January 3, 2015.

Perdue was born on December 10, 1949 in Macon, Georgia. He graduated from the Georgia Institute of Technology. He is married to Bonnie Dunn Perdue. They have two children.
