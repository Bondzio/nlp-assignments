Que te perdone Dios

Que te perdone Dios Also known as Que te perdone Dios... Yo no is a Mexican telenovela produced by Angelli Nesma Medina for Televisa. It is the remake of the telenovela "Abrázame muy fuerte" produced in 2000. The production of the telenovela began on November 15, 2014.

Zuria Vega, Mark Tacher and Rebecca Jones star as the protagonists, while Sergio Goyri, Sabine Moussier and Altaír Jarabo star as the antagonists. With the special participation of María Sorté and René Strickler.


As of February 16, 2015, Canal de las Estrellas is broadcasting "Que te perdone Dios" weeknights at 6:15pm, replacing "Yo no creo en los hombres".
