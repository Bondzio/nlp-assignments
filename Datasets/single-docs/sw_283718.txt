Daniel (name)

Daniel is a Hebrew given name for males and a surname. It means, "GOD IS MY JUDGE".

There are many nicknames for "Daniel": "Dan", "Danny, Dani" (English) and "Danya" (Russia). For females, nicknames include "Danielle", "Daniela", "Dani", and "Danitza". "Daniel" as a surname developed into Daniels, meaning "son of Daniel".
