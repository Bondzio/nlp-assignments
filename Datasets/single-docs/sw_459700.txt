Sam Smith

Samuel Frederick "Sam" Smith (born May 19, 1992) is a British singer-songwriter. He became famous in October 2012 when the song "Latch" by Disclosure came out. In 2014 Smith had a single, "Stay with Me", which became a major international success. His 2015 song "Writing's on the Wall" was recorded for the 2015 James Bond movie "Spectre" and won a Golden Globe Award and an Academy Award for Best Original Song.

Smith was born in London. He came out as gay in May 2014.
