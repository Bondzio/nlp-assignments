Pran Kumar Sharma

Pran Kumar Sharma (August 15, 1938 - August 5, 2014), better known as Pran, was an Indian cartoonist. He was best known as the creator of "Chacha Chaudhary" (1971). He also created other characters like Shrimatiji, Pinki, Billoo, Raman, and Channie Chachi. 

Sharma died on August 5, 2014 at the age of 75 in Gurgaon, India from colon cancer.
