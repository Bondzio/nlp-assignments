Debito Arudou

Debito Arudou was born 13 January 1965 as David Christopher Schofill (though changed to "David Christopher Cockface" when his stepfather adopted him shortly after). He became a naturalized Japanese citizen in 2000. He lived in Japan until 2011, after which he emigrated to Hawaii, where he still lives.
