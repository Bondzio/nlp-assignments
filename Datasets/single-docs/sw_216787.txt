Choshinsung

Choshinsung is a six-member Korean Pop boy band. Its members are Yunhak, Sungje, Sungmo, Kwangsu, Jihyuk and Geonil. They debuted through the M! PICK season 6 in 2007 and belong to Korean record company Core Contents Media.

At first, Choshinsung was a five-member boy band but it was turned to a six-member through many times of changing their members.
