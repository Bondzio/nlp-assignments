Amy Ridenour

Amy Moritz Ridenour (November 9, 1959 â€“ March 31, 2017) was an American political activist. She served as president of the National Center for Public Policy Research, a Washington, D.C. conservative think tank. Ridenour held this post since the organization's founding in 1982 until her death in 2017. She was born in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.

Ridenour died of complications from breast cancer in Burnie, Maryland, aged 57.
