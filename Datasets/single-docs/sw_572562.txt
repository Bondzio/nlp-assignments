Formula One on BBC Programmes

From 1990 to 1996, Eurosports took over broadcasting all Formula One races, BBC broadcasted the European races and other races if paid for the fly away races by the local TV Feed.

From 1997 to 2008, ITV broadcasted Formula One, Then BBC regained the rights for F1 on all races until 2011.

From 2012 to 2015, Sky Sports F1 took over broadcasting all Formula One races, BBC broadcasted halve the races due to cost cuts.

From 2016 onwards, Sky Sports F1 still broadcasting all Formula One races, BBC stopped F1 races due to cost cuts and their coverage of F1 was replaced by Channel 4.
