Bill Sykes (chaplain)

Revd William ("Bill") George David Sykes MA (1939 â€“ 17 January 2015) was an English college fellow, chaplain, and book author. Sykes was born in Yorkshire, England.

Sykes wrote books and reflective thoughts on religious ideas, most notably "Visions of Faith: An Anthology of Reflections". His portrait was painted by the artist Daphne Todd.

Sykes died on 17 January 2015 at the John Radcliffe Hospital in Oxford, England, aged 75.
