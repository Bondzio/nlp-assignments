Where Did the Feeling Go?

"Where Did The Feeling Go?" is a song by Australian soft rock duo "Air Supply". In 1989, American singer Selena had done a cover version of the song. The song was not released until after her murder. The single was released on the in 1997. Selena's version peaked at number 88 on the Canadian Hot 100 chart and number 26 on the Adult Top 40 chart.
