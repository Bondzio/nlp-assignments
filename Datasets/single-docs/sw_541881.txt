Jean François de Noailles, Marquis of Noailles

Jean François de Noailles, Marquis of Noailles 28 August 1658 - 23 June 1692) was a French nobleman and was known by his title of "Marquis of Noailles" ("marquis de Noailles") He was a member of the influential House of Noailles. He was also a ""Maréchal de camp"" 

Noailles married Marguerite Thérèse Rouillé in 1687 and had two daughters.
