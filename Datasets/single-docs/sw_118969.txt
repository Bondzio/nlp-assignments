Leuven

Leuven is a municipality in the Belgian province of Flemish Brabant.

In 2007, 91,942 people lived there.

It is at 50° 52 North, 04° 42 East.

Leuven hosts Belgium's biggest university - Katholieke Universiteit Leuven
So, Leuven is very young populated city , and there were more than 45000 students in Leuven in 2012.

Leuven also has a long history. The town hall in the city centre dates from the Middle Ages.
