Gloria O'Connor

Gloria Noble (April 15, 1929 â€“ June 4, 2013) mostly known as Gloria O'Connor was an American actress. She was known for her role in "The Small Town Girl". She was also in "The Affairs of Dobie Gillis", "Skirts Ahoy!" and "Latin Lovers". Her sister, Tichi Wilkerson Kassel, was the editor and publisher of the Hollywood Reporter and founding president of Women In Film. 

Gloria was born on April 15, 1929 in Los Angeles, California. She was raised in Los Angeles, California and Mexico. Gloria met Donald in the MGM commissary while she was having lunch with Liz Taylor. She was married to Donald O'Connor from 1956 until his death in 2003. They had three children, Alicia, Fred, and Kevin. And four grandchildren, Caitlin, Calli, Jackson and Jessi. And Donald's Daughter, Donna. O'Connor died on June 4, 2013 from natural causes in her Thousand Oaks, California home, aged 84.
