Jean-Luc Bilodeau

Jean-Luc Bilodeau (born November 4, 1990) is a Canadian actor. He has been acting since 2004. He is best known for his role as Josh Trager in the ABC Family channel program "Kyle XY". Bilodeau also appeared in many movies and television shows. These include, "Ill Fated", "Trick 'r Treat", "16 Wishes", "No Ordinary Family" and "Best Player". Bilodeau appeared in Emmalyn Estrada's music video "Don't Make Me Let You Go". He played Jeremy in the movie "LOL". He currently stars in the ABC Family sitcom, "Baby Daddy".
