Pour Some Sugar on Me

"Pour Some Sugar on Me" is a 1987 heavy metal single by Def Leppard. The song has sexual themes in it. It reached #2 on "Billboard" Hot 100 in July of 1988.

The song was from Def Leppard's studio album "Hysteria". 

There are several versions of the song. The extended version is five minutes and 35 seconds long.
