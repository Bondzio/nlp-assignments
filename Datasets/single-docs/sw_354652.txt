Christopher Plummer

Arthur Christopher Orme Plummer (born December 13, 1929) is a Canadian stage, voice, television and movie actor. He was born in Toronto, Ontario.

Plummer's first role was in the 1958 movie "Stage Struck". He is known for his roles in "The Sound of Music", "The Night of the Generals", "The Return of the Pink Panther", and "The Man Who Would be King". Plummer is the oldest actor to win an Academy Award. He won at the age of 82.

He is the father of actress Amanda Plummer.
