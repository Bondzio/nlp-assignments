Nelson Villagra

Nelson Villagra (born 9 August 1937) is a Chilean actor, author and director. He is thought to be the most successful actors in Chilean cinema. His career began in 1967. Villagra is known for his roles in "Cantata de Chile", "The Last Supper", "Amnesia", "Jackal of Nahueltoro", and "Tierra del fuego".
