Halloween 4: The Return of Michael Myers

Halloween 4: The Return of Michael Myers is an 1988 horror movie. It is the fourth movie in the "Halloween" movie series. The movie was about Michael Myers returning home to kill his niece Jamie Lloyd (Danielle Harris), the daughter of Laurie Strode. Dr. Loomis (Donald Pleasence) tries to stop him. The movie was released on October 21, 1988.
