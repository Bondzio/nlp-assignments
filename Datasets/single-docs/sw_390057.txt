Horrible Bosses

Horrible Bosses is a 2011 American black comedy movie. It was directed by Seth Gordon. The story is about sexual harassment, corrupt bosses and plots to murder. Jason Bateman, Jason Sudeikis, Charlie Day, Kevin Spacey, Jennifer Aniston, Colin Farrell, Jamie Foxx, Donald Sutherland, and Bob Newhart star in the movie. The movie was released in June-July 2011. It received positive reviews. It made less than $210 million worldwide. "Horrible Bosses" is 98 minutes long. A sequel, "Horrible Bosses 2", was released on November 28, 2014.
