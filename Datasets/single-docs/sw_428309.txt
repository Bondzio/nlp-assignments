Ma Ying-jeou

Ma Ying-jeou () is a Taiwanese politican. He was the President of the Republic of China from 20 May 2008 to 20 May 2016.

Ma Ying-jeou was born in Kwong Wah Hospital in Kowloon, British Hong Kong on 13 July 1950. He studied in National Taiwan University, New York University and Harvard University.
