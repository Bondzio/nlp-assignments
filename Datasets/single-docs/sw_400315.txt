Rosalia Lazaro Calleja

Rosalia Lazaro Calleja (born January 25, 1975 in Madrid) is a track and field athlete from Spain. She has a disability: She is blind and a T12/P12/F12/B2 type athlete. She was at the 1996 Summer Paralympics and competed in the long jump and pentathlon. She finished second in the long jump and 9 in the pentathlon. She was at the 2000 Summer Paralympics and competed in the long jump. She finished first. She was at the 2004 Summer Paralympics and competed in the long jump and finished second. She was at the 2008 Summer Paralympics and competed in the long jump 100 meter race.
