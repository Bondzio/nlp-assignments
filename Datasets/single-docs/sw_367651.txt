Zulfikar Ghose

Zulfikar Ghose (born March 1935) is a writer and novelist of Pakistani origin, settled and teaching in Texas, USA. Among his better known works are "The Murder of Aziz Khan" (1967), "The Incredible Brazilian" (trilogy, 1972-78), "Don Bueno" (1983) and "The Triple Mirror of the Self" (1992).
