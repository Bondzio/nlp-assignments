Jessica Walter

Jessica Walter (b. January 31, 1941 near Brooklyn, New York) is an American actress. She played Lucille Bluth in the television sitcom "Arrested Development". She also played Tabitha Wilson in the series "90210".

Walter received an Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actress in a Miniseries or TV Movie. She won that award for playing Amy Prentiss in the police drama television series of the same name.

Walter was born in Brooklyn, New York.
