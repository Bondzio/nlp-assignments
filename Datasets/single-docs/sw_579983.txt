Atanase Sciotnic

Atanase Sciotnic (1 March 1942 â€“ 5 April 2017) was a Romanian sprint canoeist. He was a member of Dinamo Bucharest. He was born in Tulcea County, Romania.

Sciotnic took part in the two-man and four man events at most major competitions between 1963 and 1974 and won two Olympic and nine world championships medals, including four gold medals.

Sciotnic died on 5 April 2017 in Bucharest at the age of 75.
