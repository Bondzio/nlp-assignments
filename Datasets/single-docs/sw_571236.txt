Brendan Rodgers

Brendan Rodgers (born 26 January 1973) is a Northern Irish football manager and former player. He is the manager of Scottish Premiership club Celtic. He played for Ballymena United and Reading before retiring at the age of 20 due to injury. He has also managed Watford, Reading, Swansea City and famously Liverpool from 2012 to 2015.

Rodgers was born in Carnlough. His son, Anton (born 1993), is a footballer.
