Bill Hader

William "Bill" Hader (born June 7, 1978 in Tulsa, Oklahoma) is an American movie, television, stage, voice actor, comedian, producer and writer. 

He is best known for his roles in "Saturday Night Live", "Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs", "Superbad", "Hot Rod", "Tropic Thunder", "Adventureland", "Paul", "Forgetting Sarah Marshall", "", "Inside Out" and "".

He is married to Maggie Carey in 2006 and they had two children.
