Deliver Us from Evil

Deliver Us from Evil is a 2014 crime and horror movie. It is about a New York City police officer who gets pulled into a case that is demon-related. Eric Bana plays the officer, Ralph Sarchie. Olivia Munn plays his wife.

The movie is based on a 2001 book called "Beware the Night". Screen Gems distributes the movie.

The movie was released on July 2, 2014.
