Ice Cube

O'Shea Jackson (born June 15, 1969), better known by his stage name Ice Cube, is an American rapper, actor, screenwriter, and producer. He was a member of the legendary rap group N.W.A., who is best known for popularizing the gangsta rap genre with "Straight Outta Compton" (1988). After leaving N.W.A. in 1989, he began doing solo work.
