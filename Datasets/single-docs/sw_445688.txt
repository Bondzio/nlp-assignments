George Bookasta

George Bookasta (July 14, 1917 â€“ March 26, 2014) was an American child actor. He appeared in many movies some with Charlie Chaplin, Humphrey Bogart, Don Ameche, Douglas Fairbanks, and Gary Cooper. 

Bookasta appeared in "It Had to Happen", "The Great O'Malley", "Forty Little Mothers", "That Night in Rio", "The Chocolate Soldier", and in "The Red Danube". 

Bookasta was born in Kansas City, Missouri. His parents were Indian actors. He studied at Hollywood High School. Bookasta died on March 26, 2014 in Saratoga Springs, New York, aged 96.
