Dmytro Hnatyuk

Dmytro Hnatyuk (; 28 March 1925 â€“ 29 April 2016) was a Ukrainian baritone opera singer and a former member of the Ukrainian Parliament. He was a singer at the Kiev Opera and Ballet Theatre appearing as a soloist in many songs. Hnatyuk was elected the head of the Musical Society of Ukraine.

Hnatyuk died on 29 April 2016, aged 91. He was buried at Kiev's Baikove Cemetery.
