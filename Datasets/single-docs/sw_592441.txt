One Belt, One Road

The project One Belt One Road (OBOR, Chinese: 一帶 一路 / 一带 一路, pinyin: Yidai Yilu) is trying to build infrastructure for transportation, from China to other countries on other continents and in Asia; the continents, outside of Asia, that the project is interested in, are Europe and Africa. Projektet startede i 2013.

"The new silk road" is a part of OBOR.

A High-speed rail in Thailand is part of OBOR; the Bangkok-Phitsanulok High-Speed Railway is under construction (as of 2017).
