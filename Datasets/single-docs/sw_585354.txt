Zbigniew Wodecki

Zbigniew Wodecki (6 May 1950 – 22 May 2017) was a Polish singer, musician, composer, actor and TV presenter. He was born in Kraków, Poland. 

Wodecki was mostly known for songs “Chałupy Welcome To”, “Lubię wracać tam, gdzie byłem”, “Zacznij od Bacha” or the Polish versions of the "Maya the Honey Bee", The Gummi Bears and the "" soundtracks. He was one of the judges on the Polish version of the "Dancing with the Stars" show, "", during its first twelve seasons.

Wodecki died at a hospital in Warsaw, Poland on 22 May 2017 from complications of a stroke and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease at the age of 67.
