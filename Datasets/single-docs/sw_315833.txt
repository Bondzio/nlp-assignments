Eva Marie Saint

Eva Marie Saint (born 4 July 1924, Newark, New Jersey) is an American movie and stage actress. She has performed in many movies, television programs and stage acts.

Saint has been married to producer/director Jeffrey Hayden since October 28, 1951. They have two children, Darrell (born April 1, 1955) and Laurette (born July 19, 1958), and three grandchildren. Hayden died on December 24, 2016.
