Shere Hite

Shere Hite (born November 2, 1942) is a German feminist. She is known for her research on women's orgasms. "The Hite Report: A Nationwide Study of Female Sexuality" was published in 1976. The report is Hite's most famous work. 

Hite was born in Saint Joseph, Missouri. She received her masters degree in History from the University of Florida in 1967. She then moved to New York City.

In 1985 Hite married Friedrich Horicke, a German concert pianist. The couple divorced in 1999.
