Lemonade (Beyoncé album)

Lemonade is the sixth studio album by American recording artist Beyoncé. It was released on April 23, 2016, by Parkwood Entertainment. "Lemonade" is Beyoncé's second "visual album", following her self titled 2013 album. It is also a concept album.

"Lemonade" received generally positive reviews from music critics.
