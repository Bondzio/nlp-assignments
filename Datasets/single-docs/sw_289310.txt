Daniel Cleary

Daniel Michael Cleary (born December 18, 1978) is a Canadian ice hockey left and right winger. He currently plays for the Detroit Red Wings. He has also played for the Chicago Blackhawks for 2 seasons, the Edmonton Oilers for 4 seasons and the Phoenix Coyotes for 1 season. He was drafted with the 13th overall pick in the 1997 NHL Draft by the Chicago Blackhawks. He also played with the Belleville Bulls of the OHL.
