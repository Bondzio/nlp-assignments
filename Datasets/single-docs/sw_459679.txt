Fancy (song)

"Fancy" is a song by recording artist Iggy Azalea. It features English singer-songwriter Charli XCX, who also sings in the song. This song is from her first studio album, "The New Classic". It was released on February 17, 2014 as the fourth single from the album.

"Fancy" is Azalea's most successful song as of August 2014. The song hit #1 on the "Billboard" Hot 100 in the United States and on the Canadian Hot 100. "Fancy" was also successful in several other countries.

In the US, "Fancy" was ranked #4 on the Year-End "Billboard" Hot 100 of 2014. It was the second-best performing song by a woman of the year, behind Katy Perry's "Dark Horse".
