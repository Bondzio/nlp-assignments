Chainsaw

A chainsaw (or chain saw) is a portable mechanical saw, typically powered by a two-stroke engine. It is used for cutting trees, to fell snags, to cut firebreaks in wildland fires, and to harvest firewood. Special chainsaws are used for cutting concrete.
