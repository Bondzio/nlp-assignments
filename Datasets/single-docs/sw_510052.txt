Dorney Park

Dorney Park is a theme park in Allentown, Pennsylvania.

Halloween Haunt is an annual event that is held from September to October. The park hires around 700 people for this event. It was originally introduced in 1998 as Halloweekends, but was rebranded Halloween Haunt in 2008.

Halloween Haunt currently features 12 attractions including eight mazes and four scare zones.
