Gerra (Gambarogno)

Gerra (Gambarogno) is a municipality of the district Locarno in the canton of Ticino in Switzerland. On 25 April 2010 the former municipalities of Gerra (Gambarogno), Caviano, Contone, Indemini, Magadino, Piazzogna, San Nazzaro, Sant'Abbondio and Vira merged into the new municipality of Gambarogno.
