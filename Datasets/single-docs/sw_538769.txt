Grand Theft Auto VI

Grand Theft Auto VI is a confirmed video game in the Grand Theft Auto Series (Rockstar Games), set to be released sometime between 2016-2020. The game has been predicted to feature one of the following locations in its map: The entire United States, Bogota (Capital of Columbia), Las Vegas Nevada, or the previous Grand Theft Auto games' maps combined into one enormous setting. Rumors have it that the game's story mode may feature a female protagonist. Actors who are rumored to have been cast as voices for the game include Eva Mendes and Ryan Gosling.
