Max and Ruby

Max and Ruby is an Canadian-American children's television series created by Rosemary Wells. The series aired originally on Treehouse TV in Canada and Nick Jr. in the United States. It is aimed at a preschool children. The series was created by children's book author / illustrator Rosemary Wells. It is based on the fictional world of her "Max and Ruby" books. The episodes show stories about three year old Max, an anthropomorphised toddler bunny, and his big sister, seven year old Ruby, a patient young lady. The show has a good message for its audience by showing Max and Ruby playing together and using respect and love in solving their problems.


Max gets into trouble in almost every episode, but Ruby gives him a helping hand.
