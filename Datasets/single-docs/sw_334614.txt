Dirk Bogarde

Sir Dirk Bogarde (28 March 1921 - 8 May 1999) was a British actor and writer.

Bogarde was born in West Hampstead, London. He starred in many movies, including "Victim", "The Servant", "Darling", "Accident" and "Death in Venice".

Bogarde wrote novels, autobiographies and memoirs.

Bogarde was a closeted homosexual who lived with his partner Anthony Forwood (1915-1988) for nearly forty years until Forwood's death. Bogarde died of a heart attack in Chelsea, London.
