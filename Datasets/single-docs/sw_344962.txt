Plausible Prejudices: Essays on American Writing

Plausible Prejudices: Essays on American Writing is a book of essays about writing. The author is Joseph Epstein. 

"Kirkus Reviews" and "Los Angeles Times" wrote about the book. The "Chicago Tribune" also had good things to say about the book. It said that Epstein's essays of literary criticism are his most interesting works and that they were definitely worth preserving together in a collection.
