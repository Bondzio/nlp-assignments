Juan Carlos Maccarone

Juan Carlos Maccarone (19 October 1940 – 29 March 2015) was an Argentine Roman Catholic retired bishop. In 1996, he became bishop in Roman Catholic Diocese of Chascomús. On 18 February 1999, Maccarone was nominated as bishop in Roman Catholic Diocese of Santiago del Estero.

In 2005, a video was published, which showed Maccarone having sex together with a 23-year-old man.
