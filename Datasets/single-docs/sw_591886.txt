He was born in Toronto, Ontario. He was known for his role as Judge Alan Ridenour in a recurring role on the television drama "", a role he also played in "" and "Law & Order". He appeared in 95 episodes of "Cagney & Lacey" as Sergeant Ronald Coleman.

Atkin was also known for voicing Bowser Koopa in several Mario Bros. television series in the late 1980s and 1990s.

Atkin died on July 17, 2017 in Toronto of lung cancer at the age of 74.
