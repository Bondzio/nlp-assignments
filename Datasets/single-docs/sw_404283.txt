The Shaggy Dog

The Shaggy Dog is a 1959 Walt Disney movie. It stars Fred MacMurray, Tommy Kirk, Kevin Corcoran, and Annette Funicello. It is based on the story, "The Hound of Florence" by Felix Salten. It was Disney's first live-action movie. A sequel was made in 1976 called "The Shaggy D.A." which stars Dean Jones, Tim Conway, and Suzanne Pleshette.
