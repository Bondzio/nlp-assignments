Zabardast Khan

Zabardast Khan (died 1788) was a Suleman Khel Ghilzai Pathan warrior in history, who fought at the Third Battle of Panipat as part of the Afghan forces of Ahmad Shah Durrani.

He is not to be confused with another historical person Zabardust Khan (nick-named 'Suba Khan'), a Hindkowan Tanoli, who served as an administrator in the upper Hazara District also under the Durranis.
