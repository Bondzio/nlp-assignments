Kanta-Häme

Kanta-Häme is a region in Finland. Kanta-Häme has many features that are similar to the Finnish Lakelands. Neighbouring regions are Uusimaa, Päijänne Tavastia, Pirkanmaa and Southwest Finland.

Torronsuo National Park and Liesjärvi National Park are both located in Kanta-Häme, near Tammela.
