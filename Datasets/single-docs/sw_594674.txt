D. J. Fontana

Dominic Joseph "D. J." Fontana (born March 15, 1931) is an American musician. He was born in Shreveport, Louisiana. He is best known as the drummer for Elvis Presley for 14 years. He played on over 460 RCA cuts with Elvis. He also played for The Blue Moon Boys and Sonny Burgess. 

On April 4, 2009, Fontana was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in Cleveland in the sidemen category.
