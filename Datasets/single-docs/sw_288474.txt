Ellen Page

Ellen Philpotts-Page (born February 21, 1987), who goes by Ellen Page, is a Canadian actress. She was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Actress for her role in "Juno", in which she played a pregnant teenager. She has also starred in movies including "Hard Candy", "Whip It", and "Inception".

Page is from Halifax, Nova Scotia.

She was nominated for the Orange Rising Star Award in 2008.

On February 14, 2014, Page came out as gay.
