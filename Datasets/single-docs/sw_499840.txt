André Mba Obame

André Mba Obame (15 June 1957 – 12 April 2015) was a Gabonese politician. After serving as an adviser to President Omar Bongo in the 1980s, he was a minister in the government of Gabon from 1990 to 1991 and again from 1997 to 2009.

Obame died in Yaoundé, Cameroon, on 12 April 2015 from a long-illness, aged 57.
