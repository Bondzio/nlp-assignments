Bob St. Clair

Robert Bruce "Bob" St. Clair (February 18, 1931 â€“ April 20, 2015), nicknamed "The Geek", was an American football player and politician. He played for the NFL team San Francisco 49ers from 1953 to 1963. He also served as mayor of Daly City, California from 1958 to 1961.

St. Clair was born in San Francisco, California. He was of Yaqui Native American ancestry.

St. Clair died after a short illness on April 20, 2015 in Santa Rosa, California. He was 74.
