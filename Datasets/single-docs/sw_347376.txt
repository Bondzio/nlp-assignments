Los Temerarios

Los Temerarios are a Mexican romantic music group. They were started in 1978 by brothers Adolfo Angel and Gustavo Angel and their cousin Fernando Angel. During their early years they were known as "Conjunto La Brisa".

"Los Temerarios" have recorded more than 20 albums and been honored with multiple awards and award nominations. In 1991, they earned 5 Billboard awards nominations in the Latin categories.
