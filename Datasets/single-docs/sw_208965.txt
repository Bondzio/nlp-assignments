Ichiro Suzuki

Ichiro Suzuki (鈴木 一朗, Suzuki Ichirō) (born October 22, 1973 in Toyoyama, Nishikasugai, Aichi Prefecture, Japan), is a Japanese baseball player playing as right fielder for the New York Yankees.

He is the first MLB player to enter the Japanese Baseball Hall of Fame and has won a Gold Glove award in each of his first ten years in the major leagues.
