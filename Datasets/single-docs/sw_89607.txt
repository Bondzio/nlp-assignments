San Nazzaro, Switzerland

San Nazzaro is a municipality of the district Locarno in the canton of Ticino in Switzerland.

On 25 April 2010 the former municipalities of San Nazzaro, Caviano, Contone, Gerra (Gambarogno), Indemini, Magadino, Piazzogna, Sant'Abbondio and Vira (Gambarogno) merged into the new municipality of Gambarogno.
