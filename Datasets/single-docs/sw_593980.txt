Francisco Núñez Olivera

Francisco Núñez Olivera (born 13 December 1904), age , is the oldest man in the world. He was born at Extremadura, and joined the army at 19 to fight in the Rif War. 

Núñez Olivera had his kidney removed when he was 90 and a cataract operation aged 98, and is a veteran of the Spanish Civil War. He was nicknamed "Marchena" after Pepe Marchena, due to his resemblance to the singer. With the death of Israeli supercentenarian Yisrael Kristal on 11 August 2017, Núñez became the worlds oldest living man.
