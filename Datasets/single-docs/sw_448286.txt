Tito Vilanova

Francesc "Tito" Vilanova i Bayó (17 September 1968 – 25 April 2014) was a Spanish footballer. He played as a central midfielder for, and later managed, FC Barcelona. He retired in 2013 because of his ill health. He was born in Bellcaire d'Empordà, Catalonia.

Vilanova died on 25 April 2014 due to complications from throat cancer in a hospital in Barcelona, aged 45.
