Juan Manuel Tenuta (23 January 1924 â€“ 5 November 2013) was a Uruguayan actor. He was known for his roles in "Waiting for the Hearse", "I Don't Want to Talk About It" and "Night of the Pencils". 

Tenuta was born on 23 January 1924 Fray Bentos, Uruguay. He was married to actress Adela Gleijer until his death in 2013. He was father of the actress Andrea Tenuta. Tenuta died on 5 November 2013 in Buenos Aires, Argentina from a stroke, aged 89.
