Fujisawa, Kanagawa

Fujisawa is a Japanese city in Kanagawa Prefecture on the island of Honshū. 

In the Edo period, "Fujisawa-shuku" was 6th of the 53 shogunate-maintained waystations ("shuku-eki") along the Tōkaidō road which connected Edo and Kyoto. 

The city expanded around Yugyō-ji after 1325.
