2015 Baltimore riots

The 2015 Baltimore riots were riots that began on April 25, 2015 in Baltimore, Maryland. The riots happened in response to the death of an African American man named Freddie Gray. Gray was 25 years old. He lived in Baltimore. He had been arrested for having a switchblade. He was in good health when he was arrested. Later, he had injuries to his spine and larynx. He fell into a coma on April 12. He died the following week.

Over 200 people were arrested. A state of emergency was declared in Baltimore. The city was later put under curfew.
