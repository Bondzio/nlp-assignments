Elizabeth Arden

Florence Nightingale Graham (born 31 December 1884-died 18 October 1966), better known by her acting name of Elizabeth Arden, was a Canadian-American business woman who founded what is now known as Elizabeth Arden, Incorporated. She built a cosmetics network in the United States. At the peak of her career, she became one of the wealthiest women in the world. She was born next to Woodbridge in the Province of Ontario.
