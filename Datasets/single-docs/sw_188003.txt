Ding Liren

Ding Liren (born 1992) is a young chess player from China. He won the Chinese Chess Championship in 2009. On 6 June 2009, at the age of 16, he became the youngest ever person to win the national title. In October 2009, he became China's 30th Grandmaster.

In April 2011, Ding won the Chinese Championship for a second time.
