IONIS Education Group

IONIS Education Group is the leading private higher education group in France. The group was created in 1980 and has more than 20,000 students and 62,000 alumni in 2015 currently working in business, IT, aviation, energy, transportation, biology, management, finance, marketing, communication, and design. 22 schools are members of the group.

It also has an independant non-profit Foundation.
