Murugan

Murugan, also known as Kartikeya, () is the Tamil god of war and victory. Muruga is the main god of worship in Tamil Nadu, South India, Sri Lanka, Mauritius and many other places where Tamilans live. He is a god with six faces. The god Murugan has six shrines in Tamil Nadu, which are known as Arupadaiveedu. In Tamil Nadu, Murugan has continued to be popular with all classes of society right since the Sangam age.
