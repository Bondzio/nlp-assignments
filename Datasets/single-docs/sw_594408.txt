Andrzej Blumenfeld

Andrzej Stanisław Blumenfeld (12 August 1951 – 14 August 2017) was a Polish actor and voice dubber. He was born in Zabrze, Poland. His career began in 1973. He was known for Polish-dubbing from American movies. His best known work are "The Pianist", "Delivery Man", and "The Witcher".

Blumenfeld died on 14 August 2017 in Warsaw, Poland of cancer at the age of 66.
