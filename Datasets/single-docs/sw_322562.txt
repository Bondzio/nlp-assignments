Kung Fu Panda: Secrets of the Masters

Kung Fu Panda: Secrets of the Masters (also shortened to Secrets of the Masters) is a 2011 computer-animated short movie from DreamWorks Animation. It was released on December 13, 2011 with the "Kung Fu Panda 2" DVD and Blu-ray.

"Secrets of the Masters" won in 2012 an Annie Award for Best Animated Special Production.
