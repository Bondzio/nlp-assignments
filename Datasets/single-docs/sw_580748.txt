Alstom Metropolis C751A

Alstom Metropolis C751A is the first generation of CBTC rolling stock used on the Singapore MRT system. The trains operate on the North East Line.

The trains began operation on 20 June 2003. 21 six-carriage trains were built in France, by Alstom. The C751As are the first rolling stock in Singapore to be operated without a driver.
