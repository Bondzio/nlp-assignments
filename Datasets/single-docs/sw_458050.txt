Bel Kaufman

Bella "Bel" Kaufman (May 10, 1911 - July 25, 2014) was an Ukrainian-American teacher and author. She was best known for writing the 1965 bestselling novel "Up the Down Staircase".

Kaufman was born in Berlin, Germany to Ukrainian parents. She was raised in Odessa and Kiev, Ukraine.

Kaufman died at home in New York City on July 25, 2014, aged 103.
