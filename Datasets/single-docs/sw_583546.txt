Inkoniq

Inkoniq IT solutions is a private limited company based in Bangalore, India. The company provides design and development services in the space of web, mobile and IoT. 

Inkoniq was established in 2011, with a goal of helping corporations for mobile app design and development with focused UX/ UI designs. The company was founded by Rajesh Kumar with two co-founders Prachod Prasad and Hareesh P.
