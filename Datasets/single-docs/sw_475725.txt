Frida Appelgren

Frida Erika Appelgren, earlier married "Muranius", born 5 April 1981 in Malmö, Sweden, is a Swedish music artist. She scored a mid-2007 hit with the song "Dunka mig gul och blå". 

Together with Headline she participated at Melodifestivalen 2008 with the song "Upp o hoppa". The song ended up 10th in the finals.
