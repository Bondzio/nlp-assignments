Nina Gordon

Nina Rachel Shapiro Gordon (born November 14, 1967) is an American singer, songwriter and musician. She is best known for co-founding alternative rock band Veruca Salt with friend Louise Post in 1993. She left the group in 1998 and started a solo career, releasing two albums. In 2013, Gordon returned to Veruca Salt.

Gordon is married to musician Jeff Russo. They have two children together.
