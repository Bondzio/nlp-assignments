Miloš Krasić

Miloš Krasić (born 1 November 1984) is a Serbian professional football winger. He plays for Fenerbahçe SK and the Serbian national team.

Fenerbahçe completed the transfer of Krasić for €7 million on 3 August, 2012. Krasić signed a four-year contract worth €2.3 million per-season.

Krasić has been an important part of Serbia and Montenegro under-21 squad on the last two European U-21 championships, as well as at the 2004 Summer Olympics.
