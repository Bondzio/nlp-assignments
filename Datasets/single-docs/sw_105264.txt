Adam Sandler

Adam Sandler (born September 9, 1966) is an American actor, comedian, musician, screenwriter, and movie producer. His family is Jewish and descends from Russian and Israeli immigrants on both sides. He is best known for his comedic roles, such as in the movies "Billy Madison", "Happy Gilmore", "The Wedding Singer", "The Waterboy", "Big Daddy", "Little Nicky", "Spanglish", "I Now Pronounce You Chuck and Larry" and "You Don't Mess with the Zohan". Sandler was born in New York City and grew up in New Hampshire.
