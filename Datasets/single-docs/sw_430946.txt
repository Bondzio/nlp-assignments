Zekes

Zekes was a dansband from Skara, Sweden. It was formed in January 2009. The band participated at Dansbandskampen in 2009, where they reached the final. On 27 December 2009, the song "I ett fönster" entered Svensktoppen, the Swedish record chart.

In June 2012, SR Skaraborg announced that the band would split up after its summer tour of 2012. Their final appearance was at Matfestivalen in Skövde in August that year.
