Mo Williams

Maurice "Mo" Williams is currently a professional basketball that plays for the Los Angeles Clippers. He is a point guard, who wears the jersey number #2. He was born in Jackson, Mississippi. He was born on December 19, 1982. He formerly played for the Utah Jazz from 2003 to 04, the Milwaukee Bucks from 2004 to 08 and the Cleveland Cavaliers from 2008 to 2011.
