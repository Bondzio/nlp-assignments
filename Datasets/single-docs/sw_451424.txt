Cinnamon Girl

"Cinnamon Girl" is a song first performed by Neil Young in 1969. It first appeared on the album "Everybody Knows This is Nowhere". The song was released in 1970. It reached #55 on the "Billboard" Hot 100.

The lyrics are about Young daydreaming for a girl to love, singing he waits "between shows" for her.

The song was also performed by Hole in 1991.

The Smashing Pumpkins recorded it in 2012.
