Şebnem Sönmez

Şebnem Sönmez (born June 1968 in Istanbul) is a Turkish theatre and cinema actress.

She started to develope her experience of playing in theatre in Istanbul Pendik Youth Teatre while she was a student in high school. She took part in the theatre plays in groups such as Kartal Art Teatre, Taner Barlas Mim Teatre. After completing and having her diploma from Istanbul State Conservatory, she worked in Dormen Teatre. Then she took part in TV Serials and also continued her theatre experience in Besiktas Cultural Center. She taught teatre in kindergartens, primary, high schools. While she was teaching in a school as a grade teacher, she found a theatre group named as "Yaz Tiyatrosu" (Summer Teatre) in her school and she continued her works uninterrupted.
