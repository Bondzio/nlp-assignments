Lada

Lada is a brand of car from the AvtoVAZ company in Tolyatti, Samara Oblast, Russia. The car was first sold as an export under the Zhiguli name in the domestic Soviet market after June 1966. They are most popular in Russia and Europe.

In April 2012, AvtoVAZ announced that they had ended model 2107 called the Lada Riva / Lada Nova after more than 40 years.
