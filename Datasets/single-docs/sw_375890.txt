Ville Nieminen

Ville Nieminen (born 6 April 1977 in Tampere, Finland) is a Finnish professional ice hockey player that currently plays for Tappara of the SM-liiga. 

Nieminen has also played in the NHL for the Colorado Avalanche, Pittsburgh Penguins, Chicago Blackhawks, Calgary Flames, New York Rangers, San Jose Sharks and St. Louis Blues.
