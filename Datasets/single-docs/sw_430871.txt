The Octonauts

The Octonauts is a American-British television series is an Disney Junior from February 14, 2011 until November 25, 2014. It is produced by Silvergate Media for the BBC channel Cbeebies. Its first episode ran on 10 October 2010. The show is broadcast in the United States through Disney Junior. It was first shown there in 2012.

It is like "Star Trek" and "Thunderbirds" along with the adventures of Jacques Cousteau.
