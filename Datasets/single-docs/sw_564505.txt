Lester L. Wolff

Lester Lionel Wolff (born January 4, 1919) is an American politician. He is a former Democratic member of the United States House of Representatives from New York. He served as a representative from 1973 through 1981.

He now serves as president of the International Trade and Development Agency. In 2014, Wolff received the Congressional Gold Medal, the highest civilian award in the United States.

Wolff is also the chair of the Touro College Pacific Community Institute, the author of numerous books on foreign policy, and the host of weekly PBS show "Ask Congress".
