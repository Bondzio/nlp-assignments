Room on the 3rd Floor

Room on the 3rd Floor is McFly's first album. It was released in 2004. It started at number one in the UK Albums Chart. This broke the world record for the youngest band with a Number One album. The Beatles used to hold the record. It reached Number One after selling 61,589 copies in the first week. It is certified 2x Platinum in the UK for sales of over 800,000 copies.
