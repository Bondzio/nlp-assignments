Hook (movie)

Hook is a 1991 American fantasy-comedy movie directed by Steven Spielberg. It was produced by Frank Marshall, Kathleen Kennedy, and Gerald R. Molen. It was written by James V. Hart and Malia Scotch Marmo. It was composed by John Williams. It is based on the book of the same name.

It stars Robin Williams, Dustin Hoffman, Julia Roberts, Gwyneth Paltrow, Maggie Smith, and Bob Hoskins. It was released to positive reviews on December 11, 1991.
