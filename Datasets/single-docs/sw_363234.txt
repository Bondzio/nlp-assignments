Phir Bhi Dil Hai Hindustani

Phir Bhi Dil Hai Hindustani is a 2000 Bollywood movie directed by Aziz Mirza. The movie stars Shahrukh Khan and Juhi Chawla in main roles. The movie's music were done by Jatin Lalit. 

The movie is about rivalry between two reporters, Ajay Bakhshi (Shahrukh Khan) and Rai Banerjee (Juhi Chawla). Later, they reunite to fight against corruption in the country.
