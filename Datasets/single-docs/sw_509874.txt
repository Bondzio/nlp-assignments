Eva de Goede

Eva de Goede (born 23 March 1989 in Zeist, Netherlands) is a Dutch field hockey midfielder. She currently plays for the Netherlands women's national field hockey team. With the Netherlands, she won gold medals in the 2008 and 2012 Summer Olympics, as well as in various other tournaments.

Along with her teammate, Ellen Hoog, de Goede has modeled with "Sports Illustrated".
