SEAT Ibiza

SEAT Ibiza is a car produced by SEAT since 1984. It is currently in its Fourth Generation. The first generation appeared in 1984 while the second generation was the first based on the Volkswagen Polo and arrived in 1993 while it got a facelift in 1999. It entered its third generation in 2002 while the present fourth generation has been on sale since 2008.
