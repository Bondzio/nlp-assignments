Screaming Bloody Murder

Screaming Bloody Murder is the fifth studio album by Canadian punk rock band, Sum 41. "Screaming Bloody Murder" was released on March 25, 2011. 

The album featured the self-titled single "Screaming Bloody Murder", "Baby, You Don't Wanna Know" and "Blood in My Eyes". "Blood in My Eyes" was nominated for a Grammy Award for Best Hard Rock/Metal Performance at the 2011 Grammy Awards.
