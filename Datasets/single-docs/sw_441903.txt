Dara Ó Briain

Dara Ó Briain (; born 4 February 1972) is an Irish comedian and television presenter. He is best known for hosting panel shows such as "The Panel" (2003–11), "Mock the Week" (2005–present), "" (2012–present) and "" (2006–present). He has also been on "Don't Feed the Gondolas" (1997–2001), "Three Men in a Boat" (2006–11), "Have I Got News for You" (1990–present), "QI" (2003–present), "Stargazing Live" (2011–present), "Dara Ó Briain's Science Club" (2012–13) and "" (2012–14).

Ó Briain was born in Bray, County Wicklow. He has two children with his wife, Susan, a surgeon. They live in London, England.
