The Hobbit: The Battle of the Five Armies

The Hobbit: The Battle of the Five Armies is a 2014 action, fantasy and adventure movie. It is about the dwarves and Bilbo having accidentally released the dragon Smaug. Bard the Bowman breaks out of prison and fights Smaug. Evangeline Lilly plays Tauriel. Cate Blanchett plays Galadriel.

The movie took in over $939 million overall. It got very positive reviews from critics.
