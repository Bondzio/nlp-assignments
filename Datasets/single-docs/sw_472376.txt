Somethin' Stupid

"Somethin' Stupid" is a ballad written by C. Carson Parks. It was recorded in 1966 by Parks and his wife Gaile Foote. The song is best known for being recorded by Frank Sinatra and his daughter Nancy. The Sinatras' version spent four weeks at the #1 spot on the "Billboard" Hot 100. 

The song was also recorded by Tammy Wynette, The Lennon Sisters, Marvin Gaye, Robbie Williams and Nicole Kidman and many others.
