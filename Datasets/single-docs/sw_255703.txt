Wolfgang Petersen

Wolfgang Petersen (born 14 March 1941) is a German movie director and screenwriter. His work includes "The NeverEnding Story", "Enemy Mine", "Outbreak", "In the Line of Fire", "Air Force One", "Das Boot", "The Perfect Storm", "Troy", and "Poseidon". 

Petersen was born in Emden, Lower Saxony, Germany on 14 March 1941. 

One of his first efforts was the 1977 "Die Konsequenz"

He is currently working on a live-action adaptation of the 2006 anime movie Paprika.
