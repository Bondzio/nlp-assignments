Val Jellay

Valerie "Val" Muriel Jellay (25 September 1927 – 6 May 2017) was an Australian actress, soubrette, singer, dancer and author. She also appeared in "Prisoner" as Mabel Morgan, and in "Neighbours" as Connie O'Rourke. She appeared in many guest roles in Australian soap operas.

Jellay died in Sydney from pneumonia on 6 May 2017 at the age of 89.
