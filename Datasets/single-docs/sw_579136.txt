It is intended to be the first installment in a planned duology, as well as being the second adaptation following Tommy Lee Wallace's "It" (1990) miniseries. 

The movie tells the story of seven children in Derry, Maine who are terrorized by the eponymous being, only to face their own personal demons in the process. 

"It" is scheduled to be released on September 8, 2017 in the United States.
