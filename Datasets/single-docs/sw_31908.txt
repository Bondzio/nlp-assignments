Petr Čech

Petr Čech (born 20 May 1982) is a Czech football player. He currently plays for Chelsea in English Football Premiership. Before playing for Chelsea, he played in Ligue 1. He became a player for Chelsea in June 2004.

Čech is thought to be one of the best goalkeeper in the Premier League. He had the record of the least losses in his first season playing for Chelsea. That season he helped Chelsea to win the title of Premier League. He also helped Chelsea to win the same title the next year. 

In June of 2015, Čech agreed to sign for Arsenal, ending his 11 year career at Chelsea Football Club.
