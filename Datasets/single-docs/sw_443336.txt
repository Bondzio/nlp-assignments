Cocteau Twins

Cocteau Twins were a Scottish rock band. Robin Guthrie and Will Heggie started the band in 1979 in Grangemouth. The band was named after the song "The Cocteau Twins" by Johnny and the Self-Abusers. The band's first album "Garlands" was released by 4AD records in 1982. In 1996 the band released their last album "Milk and Kisses". In 1997 the band broke up.
