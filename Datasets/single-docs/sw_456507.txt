Lethal Injection (album)

Lethal Injection is a 1993 gangsta rap studio album from Ice Cube. Similar to his earlier albums, "Lethal Injection" was a strong hit. It debuted at #5 on the "Billboard" 200. It was criticized for what some saw as toning down of political content. 

Several singles, including "You Know How We Do It", became popular.

"Lethal Injection" was released on 7 December 1993.
