Mandello del Lario

Mandello del Lario is an Italian town and "comune" in the province of Lecco, in Lombardy. It is on the Como Lake. 

Since 1921, Mandello del Lario has been home to the Italian motorcycle maker Moto Guzzi. Guzzi is owned by Piaggio & Co. SpA. Each year since 2001, the commune has hosted GMG (aka Giornata Mondiale Guzzi or Worldwide Guzzi Days).

The Grigna massif is in Mandello's territory.
