List of civil parishes in Cumbria

This is a list of civil parishes in the ceremonial county of Cumbria, England.

The whole of the borough is parished.
The former Barrow in Furness County Borough is unparished.

The former Carlisle County Borough is unparished.

The former Whitehaven Municipal Borough is unparished.

The former Penrith Urban District is unparished.

The whole of the district is parished.
