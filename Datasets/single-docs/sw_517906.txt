Spacetoon

Spacetoon is an Arabic television channel launched in the United Arab Emirates in 2000, broadcasting to the Arab world. The channel airs Arabic television shows such as , Fairly OddParents, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, Kids Incorporated (similar to Kidsongs), Spongebob Squarepants, The Electric Company, Rugrats, All Grown Up!, Hi Hi Puffy Ami Yumi, The Smurfs, , Fraggle Rock, Transformers, Barney & Friends, Code Lyoko Hey Arnold! and Recess.
