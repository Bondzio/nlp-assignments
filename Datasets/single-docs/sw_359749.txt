Frank Langella

Frank A. Langella, Jr. (born January 1, 1938) is an American stage and movie actor. He has won a Tony Award for Best Leading Actor in a Play for his role as Richard Nixon in the play "Frost/Nixon" (2006), and later received an Academy Award nomination for Best Actor in a Leading Role for the same role in the movie version, "Frost/Nixon" (2008). Langella is also known for his other movie appearances such as in; "Superman Returns", "The Caller","The Beast", "House of D", "The Box", "Wall Street: Money Never Sleeps", "Unknown", and has recently appeared in the 2012 movie "Robot & Frank".

Langella was born in Bayonne, New Jersey on January 1, 1938, and was educated at Syracuse University. Langella was married to Ruth Weil from 1977 until they divorced in 1996. He was partners with Whoopi Goldberg from 1996 until they separated in 2001. He had 2 children with Ruth Weil.
