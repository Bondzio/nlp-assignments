Wii Party U

Wii Party U is a party video game for the Wii U game console. It is the sequel to "Wii Party". It was released in North America and Europe on October 25, 2013, in Australia on October 26, 2013, and in Japan on October 31, 2013.

"Wii Party U" is a party video game which takes elements from the "Mario Party" series. It has over 80 minigames. Players are able to play minigames with the Wii U GamePad.
