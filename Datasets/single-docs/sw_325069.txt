King Kong Bundy

Christopher Alan "Chris" Pallies (born November 7, 1957 in Atlantic City, New Jersey) better known by his ring name, King Kong Bundy, is an American stand-up comedian, actor and professional wrestler who is best known for wrestling with World Wrestling Federation. He won a Slammy Award in 1987 for the Bobby "The Brain" Heenan Scholarship Award. While he was signed to the WWF, Bundy had numerous feuds with Andre the Giant, Hulk Hogan and The Undertaker.
