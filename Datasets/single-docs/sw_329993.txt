Greatest Hits: My Prerogative

Greatest Hits: My Prerogative is the first greatest hits album by Britney Spears. 
It was released on November 1, 2004. The album has all of her hit songs to that point. It also includes a cover of Bobby Brown's "My Prerogative" and two songs from the "In The Zone " recording sessions which did not make it on to that album. Singles released in this album include "My Prerogative" and, outside of the United States, "Do Somethin'".
