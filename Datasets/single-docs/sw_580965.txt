Chabelo

Xavier López Rodríguez (born February 17, 1035), better known as "Chabelo", is a Mexican actor and television host.

He has been working on television for over sixty years. "Chabelo" has been in more than thirty motion pictures and recorded more than thirty musical albums. He also has produced many shows like "La Cuchufleta" and "La Güereja Quiere Más". 

His most famous TV show was "En Familia con Chabelo", which was broadcast every Sunday morning on Televisa. This show was made up of contests with people from the audience, gifts and games and was mainly for children. The program ended on Sunday, December 20, 2015 after 47 years.
