Valeria Kudryavtseva

Valeria Lvovna Kudryavtseva (born on 19 May 1971) is a Russian television presenter, actress, singer and dancer.

Valeria was married to musician Sergei Linyuk since 1990 until 1992. They has one child, son Zhan Linyuk (b.1990).

She was married to Matvei Morozov since 2004 until 2007.

She in relationship with musician Sergei Lazarev since 2008. She was pregnant from Sergei, but she had a miscarriage in July 2011.
