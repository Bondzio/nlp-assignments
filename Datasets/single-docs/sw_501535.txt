Princess Charlotte of Cambridge

Princess Charlotte of Cambridge (Charlotte Elizabeth Diana; born 2 May 2015) is the daughter of Prince William, Duke of Cambridge, and Catherine, Duchess of Cambridge, the granddaughter of Charles, Prince of Wales, and the great-granddaughter of Queen Elizabeth II. She was born at St Mary's Hospital in Paddington, London. Catherine's pregnancy was announced on 8 September 2014 by Clarence House. From birth, she is the fourth in line to succeed the Queen, Elizabeth II, after her grandfather, father, and older brother, Prince George of Cambridge (born 2013).
