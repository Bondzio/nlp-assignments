Adriana Sivieri

Adriana Sivieri (born 21 June 1918) is a retired Argentine-born Italian movie actress. She also worked in radio and as a voice actor, dubbing foreign movies for release in Italy. She was known for her roles in "Vanity" (1947), "Bitter Rice" (1949) and in "At the Edge of the City" (1953).

Sivieri was born in Buenos Aires.
