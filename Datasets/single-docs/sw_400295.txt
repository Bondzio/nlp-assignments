Fernando Gómez Doblas

Fernando Gómez Doblas (born October 16, 1965 in Málaga) is a track and field athlete from Spain. He has a disability: He has cerebral palsy and a T36 type athlete. He raced at the 1996 Summer Paralympics in Atlanta, Georgia. He finished second in the 400 meter T34-T35 race, the 200 meter T34-T35 race and the 100 meter T35 race. He raced at the 2000 Summer Paralympics in Sydney, Australia. He did not finish 1, 2 or 3 in any race he raced in at the 2000 Games.
