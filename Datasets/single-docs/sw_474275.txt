Beji Caid Essebsi

Mohamed Beji Caid Essebsi (or el-Sebsi, , ""; born 29 November 1926) is a Tunisian lawyer and politician. He is currently the President of Tunisia. He was elected in the first presidential election that followed the Tunisian Revolution. From 27 February 2011 to 24 December 2011, he was the Prime Minister of Tunisia.
