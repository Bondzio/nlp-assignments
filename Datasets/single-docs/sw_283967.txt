True Jackson, VP

True Jackson, VP is a 2008 television series sitcom. The series stars Keke Palmer, Ashley Argota, Matt Shively, Greg Proops, Danielle Bisutti, Robbie Amell, Ron Butler, and Koplemen. The series ran from November 8, 2008 and is set to end in 2011 with 5 episodes left to air. It was created by Andy Gordon. 


Keke Palmer, Ashley Argota, and Matt Shively have appeared in every episode. Danielle Bisutti was absent for one epiode. Ron Butler was absent for eleven episodes. Greg Proops and Robbie Amell were absent for eighteen episodes. Even though he's not a main cast member, Dan Koplemen has only been absent for one episode.
