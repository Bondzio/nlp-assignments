Jose Antonio Sanchez Medina (born August 2, 1970 in Madrid) is a track and field athlete from Spain. He has a disability: He is blind and a T12/B2 type athlete. He raced at the 1996 Summer Paralympics. He finished first in the 4 X 400 meter T11-T13 race, the 1,500 meter T12 race and the 800 meter T12 race. He raced at the 2000 Summer Paralympics in Sydney, Australia.
