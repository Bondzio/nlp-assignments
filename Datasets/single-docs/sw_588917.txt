Tonny van der Linden

Anthonie "Tonny" van der Linden (19 November 1932 â€“ 23 June 2017) was a Dutch footballer. He played as a forward. He played for Eredivisie clubs DOS and Elinkwijk. In 1958, he won the Eredivisie title with DOS. Between 1957 and 1963, he capped 24 times for the Netherlands national team, scoring 17 goals. He was born in Zuilen, Netherlands.

Van der Linden died on 23 June 2017 in Vianen, Netherlands at the age of 84.
