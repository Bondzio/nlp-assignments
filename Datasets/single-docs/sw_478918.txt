Alexis Tsipras

Alexis Tsipras (; born 28 July 1974) is a Greek politician. He became Prime Minister of Greece on 26 January 2015. On 20 August 2015, seven months into his term as Prime Minister, Tsipras announced his resignation, and called for an election, to take place the following month. He was elected as Prime Minister once again in September 2015.

Tsipras is a member of the Syriza party, and is a democratic socialist. A major issue of his term has been dealing with the Greek debt crisis.
