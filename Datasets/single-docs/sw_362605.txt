Bob Lamey

Bob Lamey (born December 23, 1938) is an American sportscaster. He is the radio voice of the Indianapolis Colts. He has been with the franchise since they moved to Indianapolis from Baltimore except for the 1992-1994 seasons. During that time, he was replaced by Joe McConnell. He was also the voice of the Indiana Pacers of the NBA. He also called ice hockey games for the Indianapolis Checkers and Indianapolis Racers as well as the voice of the Carolina Cougars of the original American Basketball Association and served as a turn reporter for the Indianapolis Motor Speedway Radio Network.
