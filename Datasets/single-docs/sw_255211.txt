Gregory Helms

Gregory Shane Helms (born July 12, 1974 in Smithfield, North Carolina), is an American professional wrestler. Helms is best known for working with World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) where he worked under the ring names The Hurricane and Gregory Helms. He was also known for working with World Championship Wrestling (WCW) where he wrestled under the ring name "Sugar" Shane Helms.

He is a one-time WWF European Champion, two-time WWE Cruiserweight Champion, two-time WWE World Tag Team Champion - with Kane and with Rosey and one-time WWF Hardcore Champion.
