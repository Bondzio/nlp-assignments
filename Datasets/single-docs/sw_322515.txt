Arkady Ter-Tadevosyan

Arkady Ter-Tadevosyan (; also known by the troops under his command as Komandos (); May 22, 1939 in Tbilisi, Georgian SSR) was a military leader of the Armenian forces during the Nagorno-Karabakh War. Ter-Tadevosyan is best known as the commander of the operation to capture the town of Shushi in May 1992.

Ter-Tadevosyan was born in Tbilisi, Georgian SSR. He attended a military academy in Saint Petersburg.
