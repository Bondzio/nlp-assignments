Buffalo, New York

Buffalo is a city in the U.S. state of New York. Buffalo is the second-biggest city in New York State. 

There are about 280,000 people in the city of Buffalo. There are more than a million people in the surrounding area. 

Buffalo is on the eastern side of Lake Erie near the Niagara River. It is close to Niagara Falls, and to Canada. 

Buffalo has an NFL team called the Buffalo Bills (who play in the suburb of Orchard Park, NY) and an NHL team called the Buffalo Sabres.
