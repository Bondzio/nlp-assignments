Liza Minnelli

Liza May Minnelli (born March 12, 1946 in Los Angeles, California) is an American actress and singer, who has won the Oscar and the Tony Award, and also many others. She is the daughter of famous actress and singer Judy Garland and her second husband, movie director Vincente Minnelli. She acted and sang in many famous movies, including "Cabaret".
