Channing Tatum

Channing Matthew Tatum (born on April 26, 1980 in Cullman, Alabama) is an American movie producer and actor who stars in "Coach Carter", "Dear John", "21 Jump Street", its sequel "22 Jump Street", "White House Down", "The Lego Movie", "Foxcatcher", "The Hateful Eight", and in "Hail, Caesar!". 

In 2006, Tatum met actress Jenna Dewan. They became engaged in 2008. They married on July 11, 2009. Their daughter was born in 2013.
