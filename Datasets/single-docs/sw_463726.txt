Asma Jahangir

Asma Jilani Jahangir (born 27 January 1952 in Lahore) is a leading Pakistani lawyer. She is an advocate of the Supreme Court of Pakistan, President Supreme Court Bar Association of Pakistan and a human rights activist who works both in Pakistan and internationally to prevent the persecution of religious minorities, women, and exploitation of children. 

She has won several international awards for her activism including the Four Freedoms Award in 2010 for promoting Religious Freedom.
