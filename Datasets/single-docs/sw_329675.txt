Kitakyushu Municipal Museum of Art

The museum acquires, conserves, researches, communicates and exhibits a collection of 6,000 artworks.

The museum shows the work of Japanese artists, like Hokusai's woodblock print "The Great Wave off Kanagawa" by Hokusai. 

The collection of Western art includes a portrait of Edouard Manet and his wife painted by Edgar Degas in 1869.

The current building was designed by Arata Isozaki. It was built in 1974. 

The municipal art collection was established in the 19th century.
