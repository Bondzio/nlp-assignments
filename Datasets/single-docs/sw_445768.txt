V. K. Murthy

V. K. Murthy (; 26 November 1923 â€“ 7 April 2014) was an Indian cinematographer. During his career, he won two Filmfare Awards in the best cinematographer category. His best known movies are "Pyaasa" (1957), "Kaagaz Ke Phool" (1959) and "Sahib Bibi Aur Ghulam" (1962). For each of those movies, he won a Filmfare Award. He was born in Mysore, Kingdom of Mysore and lived in Mumbai for most of his life.

Murthy died on 7 April 2014 at his home in Bangalore, India. He was 91 years old.
