Masashi Kishimoto

Masashi Kishimoto (, "Kishimoto Masashi") is a Japanese manga artist. He is best known for creating the hugely popular manga series known as "Naruto". He has been drawing from a very early age. Two of his former assistants, Osamu Kajisa ("Tattoo Hearts") and Yuuichi Itakura ("Hand's"), have also had success following their work on "Naruto".

Kishimoto was born 8 November 1974 in the Katsuta county of Okayama, Japan. He has a twin-brother named Seishi Kishimoto. They started watching "Dragon Ball" and "Kunnikuman".
