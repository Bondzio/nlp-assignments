Lars Andreas Larssen

Lars Andreas Larssen (27 March 1935 â€“ 29 January 2014) was a Norwegian stage, movie and television actor. His career began in 1957 when he starred in the movie "Nine Lives". In 1959, Larssen starred in a bigger role in the movie "The Master and His Servants". 

Larssen was born on 27 March 1935 in Hadsel, Norway. He studied acting at the Folketeatret. Larssen was diagnosed with Alzheimer's disease in 2003 and was forced to retire due to his illness. He lived in Oslo during his last years with his wife Monna Tandberg. Larssen died on 29 January 2014 in Oslo from the disease, aged 78.
