Arrondissement of Villeneuve-sur-Lot

The arrondissement of Villeneuve-sur-Lot is an arrondissement of France. It is part of the Lot-et-Garonne "département" in the Nouvelle-Aquitaine region. Its capital, and subpréfecture of the department, is the city of Villeneuve-sur-Lot.

When the Lot-et-Garonne department was created on 17 February 1800, the "arrondissement" of Villeneuve-sur-Lot was part of that original department.

The "arrondissement" is bordered to the north by the Dordogne department, to the east by the Lot department, to the southeast by the Tarn-et-Garonne department, to the south by the "arrondissement" of Agen and to the west by the "arrondissement" of Marmande.
