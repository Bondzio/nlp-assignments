Charlie Daniels

Charles Edward "Charlie" Daniels (born October 28, 1936) is an American musician. He plays a fiddle and country rock music. His best known song is "The Devil Went Down to Georgia".

Daniels was born in Leland, North Carolina. Daniels has been married to Hazel Daniels since 1963. They have a son, Charlie Daniels, Jr., and three grandchildren. He is a stroke survivor.
