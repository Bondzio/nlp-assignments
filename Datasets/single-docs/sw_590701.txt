Heinz Schneiter

Heinz Schneiter (12 April 1935 â€“ 6 July 2017) was a Swiss football player and manager. He was born in Thun, Switzerland.

Schneiter got 44 caps and 3 goals for Switzerland, playing all three games at the 1962 World Cup as well as in Switzerland's 0-5 loss to West Germany at the 1966 World Cup. He scored against West Germany in the 1962 World Cup.

Schneiter coached FC Thun and BSC Young Boys.

Schneiter died on 6 July 2017 in Thun of coronary artery disease at the age of 82.
