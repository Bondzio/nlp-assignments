Carshalton railway station

On 1st October 1868, Carshalton station opened. The two main trains that run in this station is Southern (train operating company) and Thameslink. This station is in fare zone 5.

There are two station in this station. Platform 1 is for trains towards Central London and Bedfordshire. This includes trains towards Victoria station, St Albans and Luton. Platform 2 is for southbound trains. This includes trains to Sutton (Surrey) and Epsom.
