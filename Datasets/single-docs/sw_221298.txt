Bart Gets Hit by a Car

"Bart Gets Hit by a Car" is the tenth episode of "The Simpsons" second season. The episode first started on January 10, 1991.

This episode has the first appearance of "Dr. Nick Riviera", "Lionel Hutz", and the "Blue-Haired Lawyer". Also, guest star Phil Hartman and the show's then-script Doris Grau made their first episode in the show.
