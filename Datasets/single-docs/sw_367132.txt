Taufiq Rafat

Taufiq Rafat (1927-1998) was a famous Pakistani poet and writer born in Sialkot, Pakistan.He was pioneer of English language poetry/writing in Pakistan. 

His work was included in several early anthologies. His own best known collection of poems was "Arrival of the Monsoon" (1984). He also translated several works of various Punjabi writers into English. 

Rafat inspired and mentored many younger poets in the 1980s and 1990s.
