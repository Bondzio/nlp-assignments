Fuller House (TV series)

Fuller House is a television sitcom. This is the sequel to "Full House", which aired on ABC from 1987 to 1995. The show looks at Donna Jo "D.J." Tanner, now a widowed mother of three sons.

Most of the original "Full House" series cast members have returned to play the same characters in "Fuller House". They have made guest appearances or have returned to their original acting roles. Candace Cameron Bure plays D.J. Tanner. Jodie Sweetin plays Stephanie. Andrea Barber plays Kimmy.

The series began on February 26, 2016 on Netflix. On March 2, 2016, a second season was ordered.
