Amy Stiller

Amy Stiller (born August 9, 1961) is an American actress, stand-up comedian and Bikram Yoga instructor. 

She was born in New York, New York. She is the daughter of the comedians Jerry Stiller and Anne Meara, and the older sister of actor Ben Stiller. 

She is married to voice actor Rodger Bumpass who does the voice of Squidward in "Spongebob Squarepants".
