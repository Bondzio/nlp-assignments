All Through the Night

"All Through the Night" is a song by Cyndi Lauper. It is off her first album "She's so Unusual". This was the only single by Lauper released worldwide which did not have a music video. It peaked at #5 on the "Billboard" Hot 100. It became her fourth top five song in the United States.

The song received mostly positive reviews from critics. It was released in September 1984 in the United States.
