Elspet Gray

Elspeth Jean Gray, Baroness Rix (Elspet Jean MacGregor, April 12, 1929 â€“ February 18, 2013) was a Scottish actress and activist. She was known for her roles as Mrs. Palmer in "Solo" and as Lady Collingford in "Catweazel".

Gray was born on April 12, 1929 in Inverness, Inverness-shire, Scotland. She was married to Brian Rix from 1949 until her death in 2013. They had four children, one died. Gray died on February 18, 2013 from unknown causes, aged 83.
