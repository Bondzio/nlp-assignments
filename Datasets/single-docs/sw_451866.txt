Eva Hart

Eva Miriam Hart (January 31, 1905 â€“ February 14, 1996) was an RMS "Titanic" survivor. She was seven years old when she and her parents, Benjamin and Esther, boarded "Titanic". Hart was sleeping when the "Titanic" hit an iceberg at 11:40 PM.

Hart and her mother were picked up by RMS "Carpathia".

Hart was born in Ilford, Essex, England. She died in Chadwell Heath, London, two weeks after her 91st birthday.
