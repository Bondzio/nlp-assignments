Peter Abbay

Peter Abbay (born May 3, 1966) is an American actor. He is rumored to play the mysterious unseen Banker on NBC's hit game show, "Deal or No Deal". He also appeared on several television series, such as "House" as a cab driver, "Punk'd" as a Mob Boss, and "Another World" as Logan. He recently acted in the independent movie "Manhattan Minute".
