Ken Jeong

Kendrick Kang-Joh “Ken” Jeong (born July 13, 1969) or Dr. Ken is an American comedian, actor, and physician. He is known for his roles as Leslie Chow in "The Hangover", "The Hangover Part II", and in "The Hangover Part III". He also starred as Ben Chang in "Community". 

Jeong was born in Detroit, Michigan on July 13, 1969. He studied at Duke University and at University of North Carolina School of Medicine. Jeong is married to Tran Ho. They have two daughters.
