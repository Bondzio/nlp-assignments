Sheba Medical Center

The Sheba Medical Center is a hospital in Israel. It is located at Tel Hashomer region in Ramat Gan.

The medical center was established by the American Army in 1941. It operated until 1943. In 1944 the hospital was opened again by the British Army. In 1948 it was turned into the Israeli Army medical center until 1953. Today it is one of the biggest hospitals in Israel. It does surgery and treatments for patients from other countries.
