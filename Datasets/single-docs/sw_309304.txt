The Bad Girls Club (season 1)

The Bad Girls Club (season 1) was the first season of "The Bad Girls Club". It aired on Oxygen on December 5, 2006.

The first season had on average of 300,000 viewers per episode. This record was later beat in the second season. The show became the "most watched" show on Oxygen history until season two aired. Kerry Harick became a successful country singer. Some of her songs appeared on "Billboard". This is the first season to have a lot of replacements. The reunion show was taped inside the house.
