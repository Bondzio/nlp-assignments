Robert Hughes (critic)

Robert Studley Forrest Hughes, AO (28 July 19386 August 2012) was an Australian-born art critic, writer and maker of television documentaries. 

Hughes was born in Sydney, Australia in 1938. Hughes died on August 6, 2012 in The Bronx, New York, aged 74.

In 1970, he became the art critic for "Time" magazine.
