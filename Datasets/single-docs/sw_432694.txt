Ron Reagan

Ronald Prescott "Ron" Reagan (born May 20, 1958 in Los Angeles, California) is an American political commentator. He presents his commentary on the radio. He currently works for MSNBC as a political analysist. Reagan is a Democrat and an atheist despite being the son of Republican President Ronald Reagan. His mother is Nancy Reagan. He is often incorrectly called Ronald Reagan Jr. or Ronald Reagan II. In 2011 he wrote a book on his father called My Father at 100.
