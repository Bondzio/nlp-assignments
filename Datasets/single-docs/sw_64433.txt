Queen Silvia of Sweden

Queen Silvia of Sweden (born Silvia Renate Sommerlath) (born 23 December 1943) and of half German, half Brazilian descent is the Queen consort of King Carl XVI Gustaf, Sweden's monarch, and the mother of the heir apparent to the throne, Crown Princess Victoria. Their other two children are

She is styled "Her Majesty The Queen" and is in Sweden most often referred to as "Queen Silvia". 

She also make efforts for charitable organizations, human rights and sign language and opposes child sexual abuse and child pornography.
