Yin and yang

Yin and yang is a Chinese symbol for balance and harmony, and the opposite forces of nature. 

The symbol has two colors, White and Black which symbolize nature's balance of forces such as Good and Evil, Up and Down, Light and Dark, Male and Female, Life and Death, etc. Basically, this symbol represents the positive and negative forces in the Universe. This symbol has been used in Asia for a long time, and has become used in the rest of the world. How two opposite things are somehow one. It could also mean the good in the bad and the bad in the good.
