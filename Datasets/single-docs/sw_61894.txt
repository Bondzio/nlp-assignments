Multimedia

Multimedia that uses several forms of information content (e.g. text, audio, graphics, animation, video) to inform or entertain the audience (User). "Multimedia" also means the use of electronic media to store and experience multimedia content.
Multimedia means that computer info can be represented through audio, graphics, image, video and animation in addition to traditional media (text and graphics).

Multimedia Making it Work, by Tay Vaughan, Published by Osborne McGraw Hill 1993 ISBN 0078-82552-0
