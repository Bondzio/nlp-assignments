Bon Appétit (song)

"Bon Appétit" is a song by American singer Katy Perry. It is from her fifth studio album, "Witness". The song features hip-hop group Migos. It entered the "Billboard" Hot 100 at number 76 and has reached number 59 so far on that chart.

The video shows Perry being prepared by chefs as a main dish and features Migos.
