Iftar

Iftar (), refers to the evening meal for breaking (fasting) during the Islamic month of Ramadan.
Muslims fast in the holy month of Ramadan.
At the time of Maghrib they eat and drink.

Dua for iftar is
اللَّهُمَّ اِنِّى لَكَ صُمْتُ وَبِكَ امنْتُ [وَعَلَيْكَ تَوَكَّلْتُ] وَعَلَى رِزْقِكَ اَفْطَرْتُ.O Allah! I fasted for You and I believe in You [and I put my trust in You] ...
