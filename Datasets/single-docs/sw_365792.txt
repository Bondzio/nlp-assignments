Richard Hamilton (basketball)

Richard "Rip" Hamilton (born February 14, 1978) is an American professional basketball player who is currently a free agent. His career began in 1999 when he played for the Washington Wizards until 2002. Then he played for the Detroit Pistons from 2002 until 2011. He most recently played for the Chicago Bulls.

Hamilton was born on February 14, 1978 in Coatsville, Pennsylvania. He began to play basketball in the Coatsville area.
