Barry Pepper

Barry Robert Pepper (born April 4, 1970) is a Canadian movie, television and voice actor. He is known for his roles as Dean Stanton in the 1999 movie "The Green Mile" and as Robert F. Kennedy in the 2011 mini-series "The Kennedys". He won an Emmy Award in 2011 for his role as Robert F. Kennedy.

Pepper was born in Campbell River, British Columbia, Canada. Pepper has been married to Cindy Pepper since 1997. They have a child.
