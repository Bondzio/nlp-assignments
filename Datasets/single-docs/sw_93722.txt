Shock Value (Timbaland album)

Shock Value is the second solo studio album by record producer and rapper Timbaland. It is his fifth album overall. The album has a long list of guest artists, among them Fall Out Boy, Justin Timberlake, The Hives, Keri Hilson, Nelly Furtado, Missy Elliott, 50 Cent, Dr. Dre, OneRepublic, Elton John, Magoo and Nicole Scherzinger.

The album debuted at #5 on the U.S. "Billboard" 200, with 138,000 copies sold. It debuted at number ten in the U.K. and on the week of July 22 went to number five. The album then reached number two in the UK and number thirteen in France.

The album was certified Platinum in Canada (100,000 units) by the CRIA in August 2007. On the Australian ARIA Albums Chart, "Shock Value" was certified 3x Platinum with sales of 210,000+ copies. 

In BBC Radio 1's end of year chart for 2007, "Shock Value" was the ninth most bought album in the UK.
