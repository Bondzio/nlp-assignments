Don Lusk

Donald Lusk, (born October 28, 1913) is an American retired animator and director. He worked for The Walt Disney Company.

Lusk is known for his works in "Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs", "Pinocchio", "Fantasia", "Bambi", "Song of the South", "Cinderella", "Alice in Wonderland", "Peter Pan", "Lady and the Tramp" and "Sleeping Beauty".
