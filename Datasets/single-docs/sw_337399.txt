Legally Blonde

Legally Blonde is a 2001 American comedy movie based on a novel with the same name. "Legally Blonde" stars Reese Witherspoon and Jennifer Coolidge and was released on July 13, 2001. The movie got a lot of good reviews and was nominated for a Golden Globe Award for Best Motion Picture: Musical or Comedy and is 29th on Bravo's 2007 list of "100 Funniest Movies".
