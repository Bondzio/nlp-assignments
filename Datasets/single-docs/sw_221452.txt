Old Money

"Old Money" is the seventeenth episode of The Simpsons second season. It first started on the Fox network in the United States on March 28, 1991. In this episode, Grampa's new girlfriend at the "Retirement Castle" dies and leaves him with $106,000. He heads for a casino to spend the money, but is stopped by Homer, so he decides to spend the money on the retirement home instead.
