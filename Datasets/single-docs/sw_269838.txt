Martin Truex, Jr.

Martin Lee Truex Jr. (born June 29, 1980 in Mayetta, New Jersey) is an American racecar driver. Truex Jr. drives the No. 78 Toyota Camry for Furniture Row Racing in the NASCAR Sprint Cup series. Truex, Jr. won the 2004 and 2005 Nationwide Series. Truex, Jr.'s brother, Ryan Truex, Jr. and his cousin Curtis are also racecar driver.
