Foot-pound force

The foot-pound force (symbol: ft·lb), or simply foot-pound (symbol: ft·lb), is a unit of energy and also a unit of torque. By definition it is one foot times one pound-force.

As a unit of energy, one foot-pound is the energy it takes to push with one pound-force one pound for a distance of one foot.
