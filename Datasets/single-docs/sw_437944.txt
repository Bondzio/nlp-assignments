Team Dresch

Team Dresch is an American punk band. Donna Dresch started the band in 1993. In 1995 they released their first album "Personal Best". In 1996 they released their second album "Captain My Captain". After this Kaia Wilson and Melissa York left the band to start another band called The Butchies.

The band broke up in 1998. In 2004 they got back together. In May 2010 the band played two shows at Ladyfest Brazil.

All of the members have been openly lesbian since the band started.
