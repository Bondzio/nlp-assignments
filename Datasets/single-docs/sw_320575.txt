Han Myung-hoi

Han Myung-hoi (한명회, 1415 - 1487) was a Korean Joseon Dynasty politician, writer, soldier. Father-in-law of King Yejong of Joseon (the 8th King of Joseon Dynasty) and Seongjong of Joseon (9th King of Joseon Dynasty and nephew of Yejong of Joseon).

His daughter, Queen Jangsun, married King Yejong of Joseon. Another daughter, Queen Gonghye, married King Seongjong of joseon.
