1. FC Cologne

1. FC Köln is a football club that is from Cologne, Germany. The 1 KC KOLN was created in 1948. It was a connection of two football clubs in Cologne (Kölner BC 01 und SpVgg Sülz 07).
In the present day, 1.FC Köln plays in 2. Bundesliga. The club's main rival is Borussia Mönchengladbach, though they have a friendship with F.C. St. Pauli (a club in Hamburg), Borussia Dortmund and the F.C. Liverpool.
