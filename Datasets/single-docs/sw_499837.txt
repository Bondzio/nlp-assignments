Sheila Kitzinger

Sheila Helena Elizabeth Kitzinger MBE (29 March 1929 - 11 April 2015) was a British natural childbirth activist and author on childbirth and pregnancy. She was known for being an expert on natural childbirth. She was born in was born in Taunton, Somerset.

Kitzinger died in Oxfordshire, England after a short illness, aged 86.
