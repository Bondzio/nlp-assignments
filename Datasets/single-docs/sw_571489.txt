Morgan Spurlock

Morgan Valentine Spurlock (born November 7, 1970) is an American documentary filmmaker, humorist, television producer, screenwriter and political activist. He is best known for the directing and starring in the documentary "Super Size Me" (2004). The movie earned him an Academy Award for Best Documentary Feature nomination.
