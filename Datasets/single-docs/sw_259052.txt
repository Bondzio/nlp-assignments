Bugatti Veyron

The Bugatti Veyron is a 2-door Coupé that is built by the Bugatti brand. The Veyron can also be bought as a roadster. The Veyron is in its 1st generation. The car is built in France. The Sport Series of the Veyron is currently the fastest production car in the world with a top speed of 431.072 km/h (267.856 mph), though the new Koenigsegg Agera aims to beat the record.

The car costs about £1.6 million to buy. The tyres cost over £20,000 for a set.

The car has a monocoque construction. The car has two V8 engines joined together to make a W16 engine.
