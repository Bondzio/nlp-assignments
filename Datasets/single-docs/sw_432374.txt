Lê Đức Anh

Lê Đức Anh (born 1 December 1920) is an Vietnamese general and politician. He was President of Vietnam from 1992 until 1997. He became President after leading the Vietnamese forces in Cambodia throughout the 1980s. He was regarded as a conservative. He supported maintaining strict party control over local policies.

He was born in Phú Lộc District, Thừa Thiên–Huế Province on 1 December 1920.
