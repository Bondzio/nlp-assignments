East Ayrshire

East Ayrshire () is one of 32 council area of Scotland. It borders North Ayrshire, East Renfrewshire, South Lanarkshire, South Ayrshire and Dumfries and Galloway. With South Ayrshire and the mainland areas of North Ayrshire, it formed the county of Ayrshire.

The area was formed in 1996, from the former Kilmarnock and Loudoun and Cumnock and Doon Valley districts.

Kilmarnock is the largest town. Other towns are New Cumnock and Cumnock.

The HQ for East Ayrshire Council is on London Road Kilmarnock
