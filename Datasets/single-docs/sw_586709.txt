Valentines (EP)

Valentines is the second EP by American singer-songwriter Mariah Carey, released by Columbia Records in the United States on January 1, 2000. It is a limited edition CD and the first Carey record in the U.S. to feature "Theme from "Mahogany" (Do You Know Where You're Going To)", a Diana Ross cover that was released as a bonus track on non-U.S. copies of both Carey's compilation album "#1's" (1998) and seventh studio album "Rainbow" (1999).
