John Ritter

John Ritter (September 17, 1948 - September 11, 2003) was an American television and movie actor. He was featured in the 1970s television series "The Waltons". He performed in several situation comedies on the television, including "Three's Company". Ritter was born in Burbank, California and died there on September 11, 2003 of Aortic dissection caused by heart disease. He also did the voice for Clifford the Big Red Dog.

Ritter's parents were Tex Ritter and Dorothy Fay. Ritter's sons Jason Ritter and Tyler Ritter are actors.
