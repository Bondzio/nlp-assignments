National High School of Chemical and Technological Engineers

The National High School of Chemical and Technological Engineers "(French: École nationale supérieure des ingénieurs en arts chimiques et technologiques)" is a renowned graduate engineering school in France. It is in a campus of the University of Toulouse.

Academic activities and industrial applied research are performed mainly in French and English languages. Students from a dozen of nationalities participate to the different curricula at ENSIACET.

Most of the 1000 graduate engineer students at ENSIACET live in dedicated residential buildings nearby research labs and metro public transports on a campus that is shared with students from University of Toulouse.
