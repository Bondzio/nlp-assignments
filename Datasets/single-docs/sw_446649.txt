Jos Chabert

Jozef P. A. "Jos" Chabert (16 March 1933 â€“ 9 April 2014) was a Belgian politician for Christen-Democratisch en Vlaams (Christian Democratic and Flemish; CD&V). He was a Minister of State from 24 November 2009 until his death. Before this, he was a member of the Parliament of the Brussels-Capital Region. He was born in Etterbeek, Brussels-Capital Region.

From 1965 to 1976 he was an alderman of Meise.

Chabert died on 9 April 2014 in Brussels, aged 81.
