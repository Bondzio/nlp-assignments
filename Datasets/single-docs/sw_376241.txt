Liberal Democratic Party (Japan)

The Liberal Democratic Party (Japanese:自由民主党 "Jiyū-Minshutō"; or just Jiminto) is one of the main political parties in Japan. It has ruled Japan for most of the time since World War II. The party is conservative. The current leader is Shinzo Abe.

The party wants a close relationship with the United States, and wants fast export-based economic growth. They also want to undergo more privatization. The party is social conservative, and is against the legalization of same-sex marriage. Jiminto is also a patriotic party.

In 2012, the Liberal Democratic Party had around 800,000 members.
