Francisco Boedo Vazquez

Francisco Boedo Vazquez (born October 29, 1959 in A Coruña) is a judo athlete from Spain. He has a disability: he is blind and is a B2 type athlete. He competed at the 1996 Summer Paralympics in judo. He finished second in the 86 kg group. He competed at the 2000 Summer Paralympics in judo. He finished 7 in the up to 81 kg Open group.
