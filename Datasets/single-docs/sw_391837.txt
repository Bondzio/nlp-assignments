Shlomo Yanai

Shlomo Yanai (born May 18, 1952 in Holon) is a retired Israeli military officer and businessman. He held the rank of Major General. He was the CEO of Teva Pharmaceutical Industries (TEVA) from 1 March 2007 to May 2012. He was offered the directorship of Mossad by Benjamin Netanyahu but turned it down. 

Shlomo Yanai was born in Holon in 1952, and was raised in Tel Aviv.
