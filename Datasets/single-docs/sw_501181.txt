Lennie Mayne

Leonard Edward "Lennie" Mayne (8 November 1927 â€“ 20 May 1977) was an Australian television director. He was best known for directed four "Doctor Who" serials, "The Curse of Peladon" (1972), "The Three Doctors" (1972), "The Monster of Peladon" (1974) and "The Hand of Fear" (1976). He also worked on television series such as "Warship" and "The Onedin Line". He was married to actress Frances Pidgeon.

Mayne died after drowning in a boating accident on the English Channel. The accident happened when the dinghy he was using, with two other yachtsmen, was capsized by a wave. He was 49.
