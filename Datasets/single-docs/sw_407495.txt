Miller's Crossing

Miller's Crossing is a 1990 mystery crime drama thriller movie. Marcia Gay Harden plays Verna Bernbaum. Frances McDormand plays a secretary. The subject in the movie is Mafia related issues and crimes. The movie was released in September 1990 in the United States. The budget was over $14 million. "Miller's Crossing" earned back only $5 million. However, the movie was critically praised.
