Arrondissement of Mirande

The arrondissement of Mirande is an arrondissement of France. It is part of the Gers "département" in the Occitanie region. Its capital is the city of Mirande.

When the "arrondissements" were created on 17 February 1800, Mirande was one of them in the Gers department.

It is the southernmost and the smallest of the "arrondissements" of the department, with an area of . It is also the "arrondissement" with fewer people living there, with 37,945 inhabitants and a population density of inhabitants/km².

The "arrondissement" is bordered to the north by the Auch and Condom "arrondissements", to the southeast by the Haute-Garonne department, to the south by the Hautes-Pyrénées and the Pyrénées-Atlantiques departments, and to the west by the Landes department.
