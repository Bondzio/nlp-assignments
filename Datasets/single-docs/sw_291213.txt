List of Rugrats episodes

The following is a list of episodes from the American animated series "Rugrats". The first episode aired on Sunday March 16, 1997. The first three seasons aired between 1997 and 1999. The series returned with two Jewish holiday specials in March 1998 and October 1998. From 1999 to 2005, the series began airing regular episodes again. "Rugrats" and Nickelodeon aired the Tenth Anniversary special "All Growed Up" on September 18, 2001, recognizing the day the series officially began as one of the original three Nicktoons. "Rugrats" ended on Tuesday June 28, 2005, along with fellow Nicktoon "Hey Arnold!". As of December 2010, "Rugrats" reruns still air sometimes on Nickelodeon's main channel as well as Nicktoons. There are 170 episodes, three movies, and two direct-to-DVD specials as of October 6, 2007.
