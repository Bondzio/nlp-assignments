Susie Figgis

Susan Figgis (born in Kenya) is a British casting director. She has cast many celebrities in Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone, "Charlie and the Chocolate Factory", the "Pirates of the Caribbean" movie series, "Alice in Wonderland", "The Full Monty", "Sleepy Hollow", and "Gandhi". She has worked with Richard Attenborough and Tim Burton. 

She is married. They have one child. Figgis now lives in London.
