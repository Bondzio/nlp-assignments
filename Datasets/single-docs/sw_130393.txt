Music of the Sun

Music of the Sun is the first studio album released by Barbadian recording artist Rihanna. The album was released in August 2005 by Def Jam and SRP with the singles "Pon de Replay" and "If It's Lovin' that You Want. The album features a wide range types of music, including dancehall, reggae and R&B and features guest appearances from Vybz Kartel, Kardinal Offishall, J-Status and Elephant Man.
