Josenid

Josenid (born December 3, 1998; Panama), is a child-genre singer from Panama. Josenid started singing when she was 9, while she was participating in a contest called "Canta Conmigo" in Panama. Josenid placed 3rd. After the festival Josenid collaborated with Makano and she recorded the song "Su Nombre en mi Cuaderno" (Your Name In My Notebook), and her first single "Amor de colegio" (grade school Love) in 2010.
