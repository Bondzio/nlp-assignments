Monte Markham

Monte Markham (born June 21, 1935) is an American movie, television, stage, radio, voice actor, movie producer, and movie director. He is known for his roles in; "One Is a Lonely Number", "Hot Pursuit", "Jack Holborn", and in "Guns of the Magnificent Seven".

Markham was born on June 21, 1935 in Manatee County, Florida. Markham has been married to Klaire Keevil Hester since 1961.
