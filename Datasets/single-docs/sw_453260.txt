Billy Bathgate (movie)

Billy Bathgate is a 1991 American crime movie. It is based on the novel of the same name. Dustin Hoffman plays Billy Bathgate. Nicole Kidman plays Drew Preston. Steve Buscemi plays Irwin. Bruce Willis plays Bo. This movie is about gangsters in 1930s in The Bronx.

The movie got mixed reviews by critics. It did not make much money. It was released in November 1991.
