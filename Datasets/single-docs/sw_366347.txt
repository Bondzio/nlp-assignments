David Spade

David Wayne Spade (born July 22, 1964) is an American movie, television, stage, radio, voice actor, comedian, screenwriter, and television personality. He is known for his roles as Dennis Finch in "Just Shoot Me!", as Marcus Higgins in "Grown-Ups" and as Russell Dunbar in "Rules of Engagement".

Spade was born on July 22, 1964 in Birmingham, Michigan. Spade moved to Scottsdale, Arizona at age four. He studied at Arizona State University. Spade has a daughter named Harper. The mother is Jillian Grace, a Playboy Playmate.
