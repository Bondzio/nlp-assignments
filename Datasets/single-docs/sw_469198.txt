Myles Munroe

Myles Munroe, (20 April 1954 - 9 November 2014) was a Bahamian Evangelical Christian evangelist and ordained Pentecostal minister. He founded and led the Bahamas Faith Ministries International (BFMI) and Myles Munroe International (MMI). Munroe also wrote many books about his faith. 

Munroe died with his wife, daughter, and 6 others in a private plane crash on 9 November 2014, aged 60.
