Xenophilius Lovegood

Xenophilius Lovegood is a fictional character of the Harry Potter series. He is the father of Luna Lovegood (friend of Harry on the story), and the writer of "The Quibbler", a magazine in which he writes the things that just he believes. He and Luna believe Harry when he says Voldemort has returned. Lovegood was played by Rhys Ifans in "Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows".
