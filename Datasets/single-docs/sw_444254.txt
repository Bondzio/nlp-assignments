7 Year Bitch

7 Year Bitch was an American punk rock band from Seattle, Washington. 

In 1990 the band was started by Selene Vigil, Stefanie Sargent, Elizabeth Davis and Valerie Agnew as Barbie's Dream Car. After releasing their first single they were signed with C/Z Records.
