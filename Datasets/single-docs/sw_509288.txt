Arrondissement of Limoux

The arrondissement of Limoux is an arrondissement of France. It is part of the Aude "département" in the Occitanie region. Its capital, and subprefecture of the department, is the city of Limoux.

When the Aude department was created on 17 February 1800, the "arrondissement" of Limoux was part of that original department.
