Agoura High School

Agoura High School is a public high school in Agoura Hills, California, United States. It is the largest school in the Las Virgenes Unified School District, which covers the western section of Los Angeles County. In "Newsweek"'s 2007 poll of the best high schools in the United States, Agoura High School ranked 1173th. The current principal of the school is Brian Mercer.
