Balad

Balad, Hebrew: בל״ד , a Hebrew acronym of "Brit Le'umit Demokratit", Hebrew: ברית לאומית דמוקרטית ,
the Arab version: "at-Tajamuʿ al-Waṭanī ad-Dīmūqraṭī", ‎‎Arab: التجمع الوطني الديمقراطي ,
(the Arab version is shortened to "Tajammua", Arabic: بلد , meaning "Country / Nation"), 
both meaning "National Democratic Assembly", is an Israeli political party. 

Its ideology is social democratic, Arab nationalist, pan-Arabic, secular, and anti-Zionist.

Political leader is Jamal Zahalka.

Balad was founded in 1995.

Number of seats in the 20th Knesset: 3 (0).
Balad is cooperating with Hadash, Ra'am and Ta'al as HaReshima HaMeshutefet (Hebrew) or al-Qa'imah al-Mushtarakah (Arab) (both meaning "The Joint List"). Together they have 13 seats.
