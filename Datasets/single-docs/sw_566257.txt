Mads Mikkelsen

Mads Dittmann Mikkelsen (born 22 November 1965) is a Danish actor. He began his acting career in 1996. He is best known for his roles in the movies "King Arthur" (2004), "Casino Royale" (2006), "Coco Chanel & Igor Stravinsky" (2009), "The Hunt" (2012) and "Doctor Strange" and "Rogue One" (both 2016). He also appeared as Hannibal Lecter in the NBC television series "Hannibal" (2013–15).

Mikkelsen was born in Østerbro, Copenhagen. His older brother, Lars, is also an actor. He married Hanne Jacobsen in 2000. They have two children.
