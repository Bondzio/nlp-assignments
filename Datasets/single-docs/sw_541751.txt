Muhammad Abdul Wahhab

Haji Muhammad Abdul Wahhab (, "Ḥājī Muḥammad ‘Abdul-Wahhāb"; born January 1, 1923) is an Islam preacher and the "Ameer" of Tablighi Jamaat in Pakistan. He played an important role in the Tablighi Jamaat movement. 

Wahhab was born in Delhi, India. He studied at Islamia College in Lahore. 

In October 2013 it was reported that the name of Haji Abdul Wahhab was suggested to head a Loya Jirga in preparation for peace talks with the Pakistani Taliban.
