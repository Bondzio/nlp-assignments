Saint-Didier-sur-Chalaronne

Saint-Didier-sur-Chalaronne is a commune. It is found in the region Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes in the Ain department in the east of France.

The Chalaronne (a long river) flows westward through the southern part of the commune. It then flows into the Saône, which makes up part of the commune's south-western border.
