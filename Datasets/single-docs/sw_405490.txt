Sweden men's national ice hockey team

The Sweden men's national ice hockey team () represents the country of Sweden in men's ice hockey. The team started in 1920. It won the world championships in 1953, 1957, 1962, 1987, 1991, 1992, 1998, 2006, 2013 and 2017. They also became Olympic champions in 1994 and 2006.
