PK machine gun

The PK is a machine gun designed in the Soviet Union and is still being used currently by the Russian Army. The PK went in produtcion in 1961 and entered in 1969 to replace the Degtyaryov and the SG-43. The PK was designed by the AK-47 inventor named Mikhail Kalashnikov. The Russian factory produced over 1,000,000 PK machine guns since the 1960s and today.
