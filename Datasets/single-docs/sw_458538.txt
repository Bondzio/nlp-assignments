Kylie Jenner

Kylie Kristen Jenner (born August 10, 1997) is an American reality television personality. She is known for appearing on the E! reality TV show "Keeping Up with the Kardashians". She is a daughter of Caitlyn Jenner and Kris Jenner. She is also the sister of Kendall Jenner. Kylie Jenner is currently 19 years old and is dating Tyga. Kylie Jenner is the youngest of 7 children including Kourtney Kardashian, Kim Kardashian, Khloe Kardashian, Brody Jenner, Rob Kardashian, Kendall Jenner, and finally Kylie Jenner. Kylie Jenner has already purchased 4 of her own houses at the age of 19!
