WWE United Kingdom Championship

The WWE United Kingdom Championship is a wrestling title owned by WWE. It was created in December 15, 2016 and a 16-man tournament with British wrestlers was make on January 14 and 15, 2017 to crown the champion. Tyler Bate beat Pete Dunne in the final to become the first and current champion.
