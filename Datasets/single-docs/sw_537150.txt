Jim Harrison

James "Jim" Harrison (December 11, 1937 â€“ March 26, 2016) was an American author. He was known for his poetry, fiction, reviews, essays about the outdoors, and writings about food. He was best known for his 1979 novella "Legends of the Fall". He has been called "a force of nature", and his work has been compared to that of William Faulkner and Ernest Hemingway.

Harrison died of a heart attack on March 26, 2016, aged 78.
