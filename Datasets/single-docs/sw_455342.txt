Hannover Hauptbahnhof

Hannover Hauptbahnhof (German: for "Hanover main station") is the main railway station of Hanover, Germany. It is the most important transportation service in the city. Each day, 250,000 people use the station.

It is served by local, regional and long-distance trains. It has 12 tracks on 6 platforms.
The statue in front of the main building is showing king Ernest Augustus I of Hanover riding a horse.
