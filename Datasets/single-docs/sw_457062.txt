RoboCop 3

RoboCop 3 is a 1993 American science fiction film action movie. It is the third movie in the RoboCop franchise. It's a sequel to "RoboCop" and "RoboCop 2". Robert John Bruke replaces Peter Weller as the cyborg RoboCop. A remake of the first film of the same name released in February 2014. The film picks off where the second film left off.
