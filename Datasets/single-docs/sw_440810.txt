Alfonso Cuarón

Alfonso Cuarón Orozco (; born 28 November 1961) is a Mexican movie director, screenwriter, producer and editor. He is best known for his movies "A Little Princess" (1995), "Y Tu Mamá También" (2001), "Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban" (2004), "Children of Men" (2006), and "Gravity" (2013).

Alfonso Cuarón was born in Mexico City. He has been married twice. He is now in a relationship with Sheherazade Goldsmith.
