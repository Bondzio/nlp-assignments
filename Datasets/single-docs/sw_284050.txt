Rango (2011 movie)

Rango is a 2011 American computer-animated action comedy movie that was produced by Gore Verbinski, Graham King and John B. Carls and was directed by Gore Verbinski. "Rango" was released on March 4, 2011 in North America. "Rango" received positive reviews with a 88% rating on Rotten Tomatoes and a 75 out of 100 from Metacritic. It won the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature.
