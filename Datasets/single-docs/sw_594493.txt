Steve King (businessman)

Steve King (born July 4, 1941) is an American businessman and Republican Party activist. He is the founder of King Capital LLC, an equity investment and real estate company. He is a former FBI agent. 

In 1988, he campaigned unsuccessfully for the Republican U.S. Senate nomination in Wisconsin. 

In July 2017, President Donald Trump nominated King to be the next United States Ambassador to the Czech Republic.
