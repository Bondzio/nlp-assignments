Donkey Kong Country: Tropical Freeze

Donkey Kong Country: Tropical Freeze is a platform video game for the Wii U game console. It was developed by Retro Studios and published by Nintendo. It is the seventh game in the "Donkey Kong Country" series. It is the sequel to "Donkey Kong Country Returns". It was released on February 21, 2014.

The story is about a group of Viking animals that invade Donkey Kong Island. Players are able to play as Donkey Kong, Diddy Kong, Dixie Kong, and Cranky Kong. This game is the first game to have Dixie Kong as a playable character since "". It has two-player multiplayer.

The music for this game was composed by David Wise. David Wise wrote the music for the original Super Nintendo "Donkey Kong Country" games a very long time ago. He was not involved in "Donkey Kong Country Returns", which was released in 2010.
