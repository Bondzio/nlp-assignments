Margie Hines

Margie Hines (Margret Hines; October 1, 1909 â€“ February 4, 2011) was an American movie, stage, voice, and television actress. She is known for her voice role as Olive Oyl in "Popeye". She was born in New York City, New York on October 1, 1909. She was married to Jack Mercer from 1939 until they divorced in 1942. 

Hines died on February 4, 2011 from natural causes in Albany, New York, she was 101 years old.
