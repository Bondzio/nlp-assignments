Scott Robinson

Scott Robinson (born 12 March 1992 in Edinburgh) is a Scottish professional football player currently playing for Heart of Midlothian in the Scottish Premier League.

He is the youngest player in SPL history. He is also the youngest player to have ever played football for Hearts, having made his first play in a 1-0 win over Inverness Caledonian Thistle on 26 April 2008. He was 16 years, 1 month and 14 days old.
