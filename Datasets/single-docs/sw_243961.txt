Lights Out

"Lights Out'" is a pop song by English singer Rick Astley. It was his first mainstream single in England for almost 17 years. The song was performed for the first time on Peter Kay's 2010 Tour, that took place at the MEN Arena, Manchester on 27 April 2010. Astley was a special guest and performed a medley of his old hits before his new song.

"Lights Out" was released on 7 June 2010 and was first broadcast on the Ken Bruce show on BBC Radio 2.
