La Isla Bonita

"La Isla Bonita" is a 1986 Latin and pop song performed by Madonna. The song is about a beautiful island. 

"La Isla Bonita" achieved popularity all across the world. It reached #1 in the United Kingdom. It also hit #4 on the "Billboard" Hot 100.

This song was released in February of 1987 off her album "True Blue".
