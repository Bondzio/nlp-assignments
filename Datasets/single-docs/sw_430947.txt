Rolandz

Rolandz is a dansband from Sweden. It was established in 2008 by Robert Gustafsson. They first became successful during a Dansbandskampen break appearance in 2008. In 2009, the band participated at "Allsång på Skansen" on 7 July 2009.

The singer and leader of Rolandz is the character Roland Järverup, whom Robert Gustafsson played in the 1999 movie "Screwed in Tallinn" by Killinggänget. The idea to create Rolandz using the Roland Järverup character was developed later.
