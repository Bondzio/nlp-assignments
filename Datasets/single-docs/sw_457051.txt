RoboCop 2

RoboCop 2 is a 1990 American science fiction action movie. It was directed by Irvin Kershner and starred Peter Weller. It is a sequel to the 1987 movie "RoboCop". The movie is set in the near future in Detroit, Michigan. A sequel to this movie, "Robocop 3", was released in 1993. A remake of the 1987 movie was released in February 2014.
