Spyro: Year of the Dragon

Spyro: Year of the Dragon (also called Spyro 3: Year of the Dragon or Spyro 3) is a platform video game that was published by Sony Computer Entertainment. It was released for the PlayStation video game system on October 25, 2000 in North America, on October 23, 2000 in the United Kingdom and on November 10, 2000 in the PAL region.

The game got a 8.4 out of 10 from GameSpot and a 9.1 out of 10 from IGN. 

It is the third game in the "Spyro" video game series and also the last "Spyro" game that was made by Insomniac Games.
