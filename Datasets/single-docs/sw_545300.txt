Arkadiusz Milik

Arkadiusz "Arek" Milik (born on 28 February 1994 in Tychy, Poland) is a Polish association football forward who currently plays for the Dutch club AFC Ajax and the Poland national football team. He has also played for Rozwój Katowice, Górnik Zabrze, Bayer 04 Leverkusen and FC Augsburg.
