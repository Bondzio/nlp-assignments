Darhal Tehsil

Darhal Tehsil is a town and notified constituency of Rajouri District Indian State of Jammu & Kashmir.

Darhal is a Tehsil in Rajauri District of Jammu & Kashmir State, India. 
It is at 1671 m elevation (altitude). Urdu is the local language. People also speak Gojri and Dogri.

It is cold in summer. Darhal summer highest day temperature is in between 19 ° C to 43° C. Average temperatures of January is 11 ° C , February is 13 ° C , March is 19 ° C , April is 24 ° C , May is 30 ° C .
