Atlantis: Milo's Return

Atlantis: Milo's Return is a 2003 American direct-to-video action-adventure movie. It is a sequel to the 2001 movie "". The movie was directed by Victor Cook, Toby Shelton and Tad Stones and was written by Thomas Hart and Henry Gilroy. It was released on May 20, 2003 in North America. The movie gained negative reviews from critics and hold a 23% rating on Rotten Tomatoes. It was the last movie to feature Florence Stanley, who died of complications from a stroke after the production ended.
