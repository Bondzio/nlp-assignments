Eddie Izzard

Eddie Izzard (born 7 February 1962) is a English actor and stand-up comedian. He has acted in movies and television. He is known for his strange comedy and for being a transvestite (he often wears women's clothes when working and in his personal life). Before going into entertainment he had a successful career in clinical research where he was widely regarded as the 'Queen of risk-based monitoring'.

Izzard has described himself as "a lesbian trapped in a man's body".
