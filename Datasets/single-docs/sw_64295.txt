Robert Urich

Robert Urich (December 19, 1946 – April 16, 2002) was an Emmy-winning actor. He was born in Toronto, Ohio. He is best known for playing private investigators on the television series "" (1985–1988) and "Vega$" (1978–1981). He also acted in many other television series over the years including: "S.W.A.T." (1975), "Soap" (1977) and The Lazarus Man (1996).He was also known for staring in the movie A long Way Home which was released in 2003

Urich died of synovial sarcoma in Thousand Oaks, California.
