Ibaraki Prefecture

In 1871, Ibaraki Prefecture was created from Hitachi Province.

Ibaraki Prefecture is the northeastern part of Kant≈ç. It is between Tochigi Prefecture and the Pacific Ocean. Fukushima Prefecture is its neighbor to the north and Chiba Prefecture is to the south. Saitama Prefecture is to the southwest. There are many mountains in the northern part of the prefecture. The rest of the prefecture is a flat plain with many lakes.
