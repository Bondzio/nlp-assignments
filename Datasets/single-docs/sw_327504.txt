Qadar Yar

Qadar Yar (1802â€“1892), was a Muslim Jatt of the Sandhu clan. He was born in the village Machhike, in Gujranwala District in Punjab (in modern-day Pakistan) during the rule of the Sikh Empire. He was the court poet of the Lahore Sikh "Darbar", during the reign of Maharaja Ranjit Singh. He was an important poet of Punjabi language and his famous epic "Puran Bhagat" is ranked amongst the great Punjabi classics.
