June Angela

June Angela (born June Angela Profanato, August 1959) is an American actress, tap dancer and singer. She was 10 years old in season 1 (1969-1970) of "Sesame Street" (where she cameo appeared) and 12-17 and 49 years old in seasons 1-7 (1971-1977, 2006 and 2009) of "The Electric Company".
