Stanley Matthews

Sir Stanley Matthews CBE (born 1 February 1915 - 23 February 2000) was an English footballer. He was born in Stoke-on-Trent. Matthews played football for Stoke City and Blackpool. He was knighted in 1965 and died in 2000.
