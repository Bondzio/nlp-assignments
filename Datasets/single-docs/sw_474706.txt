Any Given Sunday

Any Given Sunday is a 1999 American drama movie. It was directed by Oliver Stone and was produced by Richard Donner, Oliver Stone, Dan Halsted, Lauren Shuler Donner, and Clayton Townsend. "Any Given Sunday" was released on December 22, 1999. The movie is based on the Pat Toomay novel, "On Any Given Sunday".

The movie gained mixed reviews from critics and holds a 50% rating on Rotten Tomatoes.
