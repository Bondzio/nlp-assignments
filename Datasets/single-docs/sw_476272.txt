Gina Raimondo

Gina Marie Raimondo (born May 17, 1971) is an American politician, businesswoman, and venture capitalist. She is the 75th and current Governor of the State of Rhode Island. Raimondo, a member of the Democratic Party, is the first woman to serve as Governor of Rhode Island. She has served as the General Treasurer for the State of Rhode Island from 2011 through 2015.
