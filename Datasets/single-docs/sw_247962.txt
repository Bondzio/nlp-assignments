Croissant

The croissant is a type of bread. It is shaped like a crescent moon. It is usually a breakfast food. It is also called a crescent. It can also be called a crescent roll, although a crescent roll may be smaller. It is not a quick bread. It is made of a variant of puff pastry. 

Breads shaped like crescents have been around since medieval times. Croissants used to be high-class food. Now they can be bought frozen which can be cooked with only a little work.
