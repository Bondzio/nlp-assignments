Jan Skopeček

Jan Skopeček (born 19 September 1925) is a Czech actor and playwright. He was born in Litoměřice, Czechoslovakia. Skopeček began his acting career in 1949, appearing in a number of plays. He was married to actress Věra Tichánková. The two remained together until Tichánková's death in January 2014.
