Antonov Serial Production Plant

Antonov is an aircraft manufacturer. It makes some of the largest aircraft in history. The Antonov 225 cargo plane is 276 feet long with a wingspan of 275 feet. It has six engines. It is 37 feet longer than the Airbus A380. This plane is only used for cargo though. It is larger than the Spruce Goose in length but the Spruce Goose's wingspan is 9.5m longer. Antonov also makes passenger planes like the An-148 and the An-140. Antonov was established on May 31, 1946.
