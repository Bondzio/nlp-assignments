Antonio Banderas

José Antonio Domínguez Bandera (born August 10, 1960), better known as Antonio Banderas, is a popular Spanish movie television, actor and singer who has acted in several important Hollywood movies including "", "Assassins", "Interview with the Vampire", "Mariachi" sequels, "Philadelphia", "The Mask of Zorro", and the "Shrek" sequels.

Banderas and his wife Melanie Griffith said in June 2014 that they are divorcing. The divorce was finalized in 2015.
