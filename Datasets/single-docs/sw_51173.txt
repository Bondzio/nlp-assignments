Digimon

Digimon is a Japanese anime television series. It is about digital creatures called Digimon(= Digital Monsters). Digimon also has movies, manga, toys, video games and card games about these creatures. Digimon was created by the Bandai company. The Digimon are often used for evil by the main villain, depending on what season of Digimon.
