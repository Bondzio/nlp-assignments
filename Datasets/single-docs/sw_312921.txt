Zell Miller

Zell Miller (born February 24, 1932) is a former United States Senator from Georgia. Miller was Lieutenant Governor from 1975 to 1991, 79th Governor of Georgia from 1991 to 1999, and as United States Senator from 2000 to 2005.

He wrote a book "". The book spent nine weeks in the New York Times Best Seller list for hardback non-fiction.
