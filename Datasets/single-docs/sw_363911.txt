Johnny Lewis

Jonathan Kendrick "Johnny" Lewis (October 29, 1983 â€“ September 26, 2012), or Johnny K. Lewis, was an American movie and television actor. He is known for his role as Kip "Half-Sack" Epps in "Sons of Anarchy". Lewis was born on October 29, 1983 in Los Angeles, California. 

On the morning of September 26, 2012, at the age of 28, Lewis was found dead in Los Angeles, California, after falling into the ground. Lewis is suspected of killing an 81 year old woman, his landlady, And his death was ruled an injured fall.
