The Flash

The Flash is the name of several popular comic book characters from the DC Comics universe. All versions of the Flash have the ability to travel at a very high speed and defy the laws of physics. 

Jay Garrick was the first character to take on the alter-ego "The Flash". Barry Allen is currently the youngest Flash. He calls himself "Kid Flash". Other characters who have been the Flash are Barry Allen and Wally West. Ezra Miller played the Flash in "" and will again in "Justice League" (2017).
