Rooney Mara

Patricia Rooney Mara (born April 17, 1985) is an American movie and television actress. Mara first acted in 2005. She has acted in movies including "A Nightmare on Elm Street" (the remake of the 1984 horror movie) and "The Social Network". Mara became famous as Lisbeth Salander, the title character in the 2011 version of the movie "The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo". In 2013, she starred in the movie "Side Effects".

Mara is also known for her charity work. She oversees the charity Uweza Foundation, which supports programs for children and families in the Kibera slum in Nairobi, Kenya.

Rooney is the younger sister of actress Kate Mara.
