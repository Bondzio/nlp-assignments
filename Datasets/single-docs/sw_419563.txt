The Chew

The Chew is an American television talk and cooking show. It airs on ABC. It started on September 26, 2011. It replaced the soap opera "All My Children". The show's name was inspired by talk show "The View". It focuses on food-related and lifestyle topics. It is hosted by Mario Batali, Michael Symon, Carla Hall, Clinton Kelly, and Daphne Oz.

On April 11, 2012, ABC announced that the show would air for a second season.
