The Slim Shady LP

The Slim Shady LP is the second studio album and the major-label debut by American rapper, Eminem. It was released on February 23, 1999 and was produced by Dr. Dre, Eminem, Mark & Jeff Bass, and Mel-Man. The album got critical and commercial success. It debuted at number two on the "Billboard" 200 charts. Two singles from the album were released, "Just Don't Give a Fuck" and "My Name Is". It earned Eminem a Grammy Award for Best Rap Album and was included in "Rolling Stone Magazine"'s list of the 500 greatest albums of all time.
