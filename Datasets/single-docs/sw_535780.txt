Nancy Olson

Nancy Olson (born April 14, 1928) is an American actress. She got an Academy Award nomination for Best Supporting Actress for her role as Betty Schaefer in "Sunset Boulevard" (1950). She played Betsy Carlisle in "The Absent-Minded Professor". She played Mrs. Abbott in "Airport 1975" (1974). She has mostly retired from acting.

Olson was born in Milwaukee, Wisconsin.
