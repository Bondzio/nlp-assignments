Davis Cleveland

Davis Cleveland (born February 5, 2002 in Houston, Texas) is an American television actor, rapper and singer. He is best known for his role as Flynn Jones on the Disney Channel original series "Shake It Up".

Davis Cleveland was born February 5, 2002 in Houston, Texas. Cleveland currently lives in Los Angeles. He enjoys studying martial arts and playing guitar. Other hobbies are video games, skateboarding and rollerblading. In March 2011, Cleveland told Tanya Rivero on "Good Morning America" of his experience working on "Shake It Up". He said, "It's the best experience ever. It's like Disney World every morning." He also helps to raise money to help find a cure for cystic fibrosis through hosting cystic fibrosis walks.
