Planes: Fire &amp; Rescue

Planes: Fire & Rescue is a movie that was released on July 18, 2014. It is a sequel to the 2013 film "Planes", a spin-off of Pixar's "Cars" franchise. The movie was produced by DisneyToon Studios.
This movie stars Dane Cook, Julie Bowen, Ed Harris, Wes Studi, Jerry Stiller, Anne Meara, Fred Willard, Brad Garrett, Dale Dye, Barry Corbin, and Hal Holbrook.
