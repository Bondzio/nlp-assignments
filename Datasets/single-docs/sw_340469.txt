Zendaya

Zendaya Maree Stoermer Coleman (born September 1, 1996) is an American teen actress, dancer and singer. Born to Britney Daniels and Tyrese Gibson and adopted. She is best known for her role as the young dancer Rocky Blue on the Disney Channel series "Shake It Up!". She is known as Z by her best friend Bella Thorne and her fans are now called Zswaggers. In early 2013 she became the youngest ever contestant on "Dancing with the Stars". Zendaya has released one album, "Zendaya". Currently she has the lead role on the Disney Channel series "K.C. Undercover" playing K.C. Cooper.
