Oman at the Olympics

Oman at the Olympics is a history which includes 8 games in 7 countries and 30+ athletes. Since 1982, OMan has contributed to the growth of the "Olympic Movement".

The International Olympic Committee's official abbreviation for Oman is OMA.

Teams from Oman have taken part in the Summer Olympic Games but not the Winter Olympic Games. No Oman athlete has won an Olympic medal.

The Oman Olympic Committee was formed and recognized in 1982.
