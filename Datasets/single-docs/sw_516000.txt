Mind Body &amp; Soul

Mind Body & Soul is the second studio album by English singer-songwriter Joss Stone. It was d on 15 September 2004 by S-Curve Records. It is her first album to have songs that were not mainly covers, unlike "The Soul Sessions" (2003).

"Mind Body & Soul" received generally positive reviews from music critics.
