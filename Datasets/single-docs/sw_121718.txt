Boulevard of Broken Dreams (Green Day song)

"Boulevard of Broken Dreams" is a song by the American punk rock band Green Day, from their seventh studio album, "American Idiot".

The song depicts a solemn walk of a lonely Jesus of Suburbia (The protagonist of the album) down a road where he walks alone. The song is considered by many to be the best song ever written by Green Day, and it has received many awards.
