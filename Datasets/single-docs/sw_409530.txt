Agatha

Agatha is a female given name. It comes from the Ancient Greek word "agathos", meaning "good". The name last ranked in the Top 1000 girls names in the United States in the 1930s.

Saint Agatha of Sicily was a 3rd-century saint. There was a famous crime writer named Agatha Christie. There have been several tropical cyclones in the Eastern Pacific Region named "Agatha". Tropical Storm Agatha in 2010 caused over $1 billion in damage and over three hundred deaths across Central America.

There are different versions of the name, for example Agata.
