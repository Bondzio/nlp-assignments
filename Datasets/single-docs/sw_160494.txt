Yukihiko Sato

Yukihiko Sato (born 11 May 1976) is a Japanese football player. He plays for V-Varen Nagasaki. In 2009 there was a story in the newspapers saying that Australia's Newcastle United Jets were interested in getting the midfielder to play for them.
