Robert Forster

Robert Wallace Forster, Jr. (born July 13, 1941) is an American actor. He is best known for his role as Max Cherry in Quentin Tarantino's "Jackie Brown" (1997), which got him an Academy Award nomination for Best Supporting Actor. He is also known for his roles as John Cassellis in Haskell Wexler's "Medium Cool" (1969).

Forst was born in Rochester, New York.
