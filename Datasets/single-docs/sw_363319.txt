Will Arnett

William Emerson "Will" Arnett (born May 4, 1970) is a Canadian - American movie, television, stage and voice actor who is known for his comedic roles as George Oscar Bluth, Jr in "Arrested Development", Devon Banks in "30 Rock", Mr. Perkins in "Despicable Me", as The Missing Link in "Monsters vs. Aliens", and as Batman in "The Lego Movie" and "The Lego Batman Movie".

He was born on May 4, 1970 in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. He was married to Penelope Ann Miller from 1994 until they divorced in 1995. In 2003, Arnett married to Amy Poehler. They have two children. In September 2012, Arnett and Poehler separated.
