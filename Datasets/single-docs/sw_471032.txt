Marcel Breuer

Marcel Lajos Breuer (pronounced broy-ər ;22 May 1902 – 1 July 1981), was a Hungarian-American modernist, architect and furniture designer. He was born in Pécs. One of the masters of modernism, Breuer used the ideas he had developed in the carpentry shop at the Bauhaus. His personal architecture made him one of the world’s most popular architects. He died in New York City.
