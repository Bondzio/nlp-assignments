Tevin Campbell

Tevin Campbell (November 12, 1976) is an American R&B singer-songwriter. He has put out many hit singles. His first hit song was "Tommorow (A Better You, Better Me)" with Quincy Jones. It was number one on the "Billboard" Hip Hop and R&B chart in June 1990. He released "Round and Round" in November 1990. Other songs followed over the next two years. He was born in Texas.
