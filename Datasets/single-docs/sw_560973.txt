Conor McGregor

Conor Anthony McGregor (born 14 July 1988) is an Irish professional mixed martial artist. He is signed to the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC). He is the UFC Featherweight Champion and UFC Lightweight Champion at the moment. He has fought as a featherweight, lightweight, and welterweight. He started his career in 2008.

McGregor was born in Dublin. His parents are Tony and Margaret McGregor. He started kickboxing at the age of 12. He has been in a relationship with Dee Devlin since 2008. Their first child is due to be born in May 2017.
