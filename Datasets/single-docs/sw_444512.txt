Candy (1968 movie)

Candy is a 1968 sex comedy movie. The movie is a series of pornographic stories related to high school students. 

Ewa Aulin plays Candy. Ringo Starr plays Emanuel. Walter Matthau and Marlon Brando are also in the movie.

"Candy" was released in December 1968. The reviews were mostly positive. Its box office intake was good.
