Jerry Van Dyke

Jerry Van Dyke (born July 27, 1931) is an American comedian and television actor. He performed in many television sitcoms and Big Lots commercials. His comedic acting goes back to the 1960s. His brother is actor Dick Van Dyke.

Van Dyke was born in Danville, Illinois, in 1931. His parents were Loren (nickname “Cookie”) and Hazel (nee McCord) Van Dyke. He is of Dutch ancestry on his father's side and of English and Scottish ancestry on his mother's side. His mother is a "Mayflower" descendant.
