Nemesis (mythology)

Nemesis (in Greek, ) is the Greek goddess of giving people what they deserve (revenge and retribution against arrogance) and Balance of luck. Nemesis also has wings and is the daughter of Chiron. She has a balance or scale, which she uses to make sure that every person gets the same amount of good and luck.
Nemesis often is seen with another goddess named Aids. Aides is the goddess of shame. She is similar to Nemesis.
