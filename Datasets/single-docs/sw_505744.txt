Killing Reagan

Killing Reagan: A Violent Assault That Changed a Presidency is a book written by Bill O'Reilly and Martin Dugard about the attempted assassination of U.S President Ronald Reagan. 

The book is the fifth in the "Killing" series, following "Killing Lincoln", "Killing Kennedy", "Killing Jesus", and "Killing Patton". The book was released on September 22, 2015.

On September 26, 2015, about a week after the novel's release National Geographic announced that a television movie version is in the works without a specific release date or cast. In May 2016, it was announced that Tim Matheson and Cynthia Nixon would play Ronald and Nancy Reagan respectively in the movie. Filming will begin in late May and will be released sometime in the fall of 2016.
