Münsingen

Münsingen () is a town and municipality in the administrative district of Bern-Mittelland in the canton of Berne in Switzerland.

On 1 January 2013 the former municipalities of Trimstein merged into the municipality of Münsingen and On 1 January 2017 the former municipalities of Tägertschi merged into the municipality of Münsingen.

The municipality lies on the River Aare between the cities of Bern and Thun.
