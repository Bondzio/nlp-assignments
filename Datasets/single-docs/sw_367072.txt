Khattar

Khattar are a large tribe of the Punjab, scattered over both India and Pakistan. In India they are classified as Jatt and Khatri; whereas in Pakistan they are generally classified as Jatt or Rajput. The Khattars in Pakistan are almost all Muslims now whereas those in India are Muslims, Hindus and Sikhs.

Some famous people of the Khattar tribe in history include Nawab Muhammad Hayat Khan and Sir Sikandar Hayat Khan. A famous Hindu Khattar today is Mr Manohar Lal Khattar, who is Chief Minister in the province of Haryana, India.
