Deep Throat (movie)

Deep Throat is a 1972 American pornographic film. In it Linda Lovelace plays herself. She was paid $1250 for acting in the movie. The movie was directed by Gerard Damiano. In 1974 "Deep Throat Part II" was released. The movie was made in New York. A documentary about "Deep Throat" called "Inside Deep Throat" was released in 2004.
