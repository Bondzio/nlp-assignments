Is It the Beat?

"Is It The Beat?" is a song by American singer Selena. The dance-pop song peaked at number four on the US Hot Dance Club Songs. It was also the Spanish version of "Enamorada de Ti". Selena had recorded several versions for this song. She had performed the freestyle version at live concerts in 1989-1991. The single was not released until after Selena's murder. It was included in the movie soundtrack in 1997. It was released with dance-club remixes.
