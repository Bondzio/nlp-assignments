Australia's Next Top Model, Cycle 7

Australia's Next Top Model, Cycle 7 is the seventh season of "Australia's Next Top Model". It began airing on August 2011. This cycle includes 16 contestants. The winner was 17-year-old Montana Cox from Melbourne.
