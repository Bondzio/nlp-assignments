Tishrei

Tishrei (), or "Tishri," is a month of the Hebrew calendar. In the Hebrew Bible, it is called "Ethanim" (), or simply "the seventh month". (The counting starts at Nisan, the spring month containing Passover.) The name Tishrei was brought back by the Jews from their Babylonian exile.
