Chaz Bono

Chaz Salvatore Bono (born Chastity Sun Bono; March 4, 1969) is an American actor and is a spokesperson for the Gay & Lesbian Alliance Against Defamation. He danced on ABC's "Dancing With the Stars".

In 1995 Chaz, who was called Chastity at the time, told The Advocate that he was a lesbian.
In 2008 Bono started gender transition to male. Bono is no longer a lesbian. He is a heterosexual man.

He is the son of Sonny and Cher Bono. He is a female-to-male transsexual. A documentary on Bono's experience, "Becoming Chaz", was at the 2011 Sundance Film Festival. It was later on the OWN: Oprah Winfrey Network.
