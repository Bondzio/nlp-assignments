Self Portrait (Van Gogh)

"A self-drawn (or self-taken) picture of an artist is also called self portrait"

Self Portait is an oil painting by Vincent van Gogh. It measures 57.2 x 43.8 cm (22 1/2 x 17 1/4 in.) It was painted in 1889. It hangs in the National Gallery of Art, Washington DC. It is one of Van Gogh's last self portraits.
