Cary Grant

Cary Grant (January 18, 1904 - November 29, 1986), born as Archibald Alexander Leach in Bristol, United Kingdom, was an actor in the United States.. He starred in many movies, including 1959's "North by Northwest". 

Grant became an American citizen in 1942. At that time, he legally changed his name to "Cary Grant."

Grant died of a cerebral hemorrhage (a bleed in his brain) in Davenport, Iowa, United States. He was cremated and his ashes were scattered.
