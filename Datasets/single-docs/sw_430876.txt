Richard Drew

Richard Drew is an Associated Press photo-journalist. He is best known for his photograph "The Falling Man", which shows a man falling from the World Trade Center towers during the September 11 attacks. A British documentary about his photograph, "The Falling Man", aired on Channel 4 on March 16, 2006.

Drew was one of the press photographers at the assassination of Robert F. Kennedy. He has worked as an Associated Press photographer for over 40 years.
