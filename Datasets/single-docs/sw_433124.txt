Arnold Klein

Arnold William Klein (born February 27, 1945) is an American dermatologist. In 1983 Michael Jackson became one of Klein's patients. He is a member of the Advisory Board of the AIDS Services Foundation Orange County. He has published over 150 scientific papers.

Klein was born in Michigan. His father was a Jewish Orthodox rabbi. In 1971 he got his doctorate in medicine from the University of Pennsylvania School of Medicine.

Klein is gay.
