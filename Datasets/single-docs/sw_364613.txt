Stacy Keibler

Stacy Keibler (born October 14, 1979 in Rosedale, Maryland) is an American actor, model and retired professional wrestler. She was best known for her time with World Wrestling Entertainment where she worked from 2001 to 2006. She also worked with World Championship Wrestling from 1999 to 2001. Keibler is also known for her long legs and was known as "The Legs of WCW" and "The Legs of WWE" when she was a professional wrestler. When she competed on the second season of Dancing with the Stars she was nicknamed "The Weapon of Mass Seduction" because of her long legs. She married Future Ads CEO Jared Pobre on March 8, 2014 at Mexico.
