Money Train

Money Train is a 1995 American comedy crime thriller movie. In it are Woody Harrelson, Robert Blake, Jennifer Lopez and Wesley Snipes. Harrelson, Snipes and Lopez play transit cops who are foster brothers. Robert Blake plays as an arrogant officer named Patterson. "Money Train" not only received substandard and negative reviews, it was considered a box office bomb. It was released November 22, 1995.
