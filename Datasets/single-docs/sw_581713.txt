Wiesław Gołas

Wiesław Gołas (born 9 October 1930 in Kielce) is a Polish actor and former Armia Krajowa's soldier. 

Gołas was cast in a role of Tomasz Czereśniak in a widely popular TV World War II series "Czterej pancerni i pies". He has appeared in a number of movies including "Ogniomistrz Kaleń" (1961) and "Dzięcioł" (1970).
