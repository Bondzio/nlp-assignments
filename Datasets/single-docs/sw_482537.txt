Ibrahim Biogradlić

Ibrahim "Ibro" Biogradlić (8 March 1931 – 20 February 2015) was a Bosnian footballer. He was born in Sarajevo, Kingdom of Yugoslavia. He played at 1956 Summer Olympics for SFR Yugoslavia, winning a silver medal. He was capped 646 times for FK Sarajevo, and is the club's record holder with the most appearances. 

He died on 20 February 2015 in Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina after a long illness, aged 83.
