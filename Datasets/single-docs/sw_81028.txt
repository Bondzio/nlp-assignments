Bilten

Bilten is a municipality of the canton of Glarus in Switzerland. On 1 January 2011 the former municipalities of Bilten, Filzbach, Mollis, Mühlehorn, Näfels, Niederurnen, Oberurnen and Obstalden merged into the new municipality of Glarus Nord.
