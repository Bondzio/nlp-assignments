Kenya at the Olympics

Kenya at the Olympics is a history which began in 1956. 

The International Olympic Committee's official abbreviation for Kenya is KEN.

Teams from Kenya have been in every Summer Olympic Games since then, except for the boycotted 1976 and 1980 Games. Kenya participated in the Winter Olympic Games in 1998, 2002 and 2006. Kenyan athletes have won a total of 75 medals, all in athletics and boxing.

The National Olympic Committee for Kenya is the "National Olympic Committee Kenya", and was founded in 1955.
