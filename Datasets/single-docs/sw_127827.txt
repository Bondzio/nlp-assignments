Ric Flair

Richard Morgan Fliehr (born February 25, 1949, in Minneapolis, Minnesota) better known by his ring name, Ric Flair, is a semi-retired American professional wrestler that is currently signed to Total Nonstop Action Wrestling.
He wrestled for 36 years. His last WWE match was against Shawn Michaels at WrestleMania XXIV. He is a two time WWE Hall of Famer.

His nickname is ""The Nature Boy" Ric Flair".
