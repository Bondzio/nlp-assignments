Tiny Toon Adventures

Tiny Toon Adventures, also known as Steven Spielberg Presents Tiny Toon Adventures, is an American animated television series. It aired from September 14, 1990 to May 28, 1995. It aired on CBS in 1990, in syndication from 1990 to 1992, and on Fox Kids from 1992 to 1995. It follows a group of young cartoon characters who attend Acme Looniversity in the town of Acme Acres to become the next generation of "Looney Tunes" characters.
