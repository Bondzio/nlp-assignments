Jaime Nebot

Jaime José Nebot Saadi (born October 22, 1946) is an Ecuadorian politician. He serves as mayor of Guayaquil, which is Ecuador's largest city. Although he is a member of the Social Christian Party, he has distanced himself from it and now is more on running the city. Nebot twice ran unsuccessfully for President of Ecuador. He is considered as the protégé of former president León Febres-Cordero.
