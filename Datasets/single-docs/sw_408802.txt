She starred as Patricia "Patty" Russell in the sitcom "My Sister Sam" in the late 1980s. The sitcom aired for two seasons. She played Stephanie in "The End of Innocence".

Schaeffer was born in Eugene, Oregon. She was shot dead in Los Angeles, California, by an obsessed fan who had stalked her for almost three years.
