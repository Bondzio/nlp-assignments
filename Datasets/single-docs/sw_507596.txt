K.C. Undercover

K.C. Undercover is an American sitcom, created by Corinne Marshall. Rob Lotterstein is the executive producer. The series stars Zendaya as K.C. Cooper. K.C. Undercover was released on January 1, 2015 on Watch Disney Channel. It was followed by the television premiere on January 18. The first season has 27 episodes. 

On May 15, 2015, TVLine reported that the show will have a second season..

In Washington D.C., there is K.C. Cooper, a high school math genius. Her parents, Craig & Kira, are secretly undercover spies. She becomes an undercover spy too. Her brother, Ernie, tried to help because he feels left out. Eventually he joins the team. Ernie's desire led Craig & Kira to get a robot child named Judy. Each episode centers on K.C. and her family. They have family issues but go on missions to save the country.
