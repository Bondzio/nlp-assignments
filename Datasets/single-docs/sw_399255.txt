Gerry Bednob

Gerry Bednob (born May 18, 1950) is an American actor and comedian of Indo Trinidadian heritage.

Gerry currently appears as Bling Bling Shelton in "Free Radio" on Vh1. He had a role in the 2010 movie "Furry Vengeance", with Brendan Fraser and Brooke Shields, playing Mr. Gupta. He is also the uncle of Michelle Jhagroo. He also appeared in "The 40-Year-Old Virgin".
