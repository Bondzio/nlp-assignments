Tomislav Nikolić

Tomislav Nikolić (born 15 February 1952) is a Serbian politician, a founder and former leader of Serbian Progressive Party. He served as President of Serbia from 2012 to 2017.

In May 2007, he served briefly as the President of the National Assembly of Serbia. In 2008, he founded the Serbian Progressive Party (Српска напредна странка, "SNS"), the biggest political party in Serbia.
