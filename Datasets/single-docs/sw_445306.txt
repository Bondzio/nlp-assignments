Tiffini Hale

Tiffini Hale (born in 1975) is a former singer. She appeared on "Kidsongs: Cars, Boats, Trains or Planes" and "A Day at the Circus" in 1986-1987. She appeared on Disney's "Mickey Mouse Club" television show - seasons 1-4, 5 and 7 (1989-1991, 1992 and 1984-1995). She was a member of the band The Party in 1990-1993.
