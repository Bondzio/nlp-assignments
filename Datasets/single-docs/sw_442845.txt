Nick Lachey

Nicholas Scott "Nick" Lachey ( ; born November 9, 1973) is an American singer-songwriter, actor, producer, and television personality. He was best known as the lead singer of the multi-platinum selling boy band 98 Degrees.

Lachey was born in Harlan, Kentucky and was raised in Cincinnati, Ohio. He is of Dutch, English, French, German, Irish, Scottish and Welsh descent. He was married to singer Jessica Simpson from 2002 to 2006. He married model Vanessa Minnillo in 2011. Their son, Camden John Lachey, was born on September 12, 2012.
