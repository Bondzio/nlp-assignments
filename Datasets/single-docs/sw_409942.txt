Stacy

Stacy (also Stacey) is a mostly female given name, but is used sometimes on males. Several celebrities use this name. They include Stacy Ferguson, Stacey Dash and Stacy London. Fictional people include Stacey Colbert, a character in the television show "Ned & Stacey". The meanings of this name are disputed. For girls, "Stacy" means "resurrection" in Greek.
