F. Lee Bailey

Francis Lee Bailey Jr., commonly referred to as F. Lee Bailey, (born June 10, 1933) is an American former attorney. Bailey was born in Waltham, Massachusetts. He worked in Massachusetts and Florida.

He was a criminal defense attorney. He served as the lawyer in the re-trial of physician Sam Sheppard. He was also the supervisory attorney over attorney Mark J. Kadish in the court martial of Captain Ernest Medina for the My Lai Massacre. He was one of the lawyers for the defense in the O. J. Simpson murder case. In 2014 he was denied a law license by the Maine State Bar Association and the Maine Supreme Judicial Court.
