Mikkel Kessler

Mikkel Kessler (born March 1, 1979 in Copenhagen) is a Danish professional boxer. His nickname is "The Viking Warrior". Kessler won the WBA Super Middleweight regular title on June 21, 2008, beating Dimitri Sartison by a 12th round knockout.
