Lo Bosworth

Lauren Ogilvie "Lo" Bosworth (born September 29, 1986) is a American television personality and writer. She was born and raised in Laguna Beach, California. There she attended Laguna Beach High School.

In 2004, Bosworth became famous when she appeared on the reality series "Laguna Beach: The Real Orange County". She appeared later in the series spin-off "The Hills".
