Newport, Shropshire

Newport is a market town in the ceremonial county of Shropshire, England. It is in the borough of Telford and Wrekin. It is located about north of Telford and about west of Stafford, on the Shropshire/Staffordshire border. The 2001 census recorded 10,814 people living in the town's parish. This makes it the second-largest town in Telford and Wrekin and the fifth-largest in the ceremonial county of Shropshire. The population in 2008 was between 12,444 and 15,632.
