Yip Man

Yip Man (18931972), also known as Ip Man, was the first teacher to teach openly the martial art Wing Chun. He started teaching in Hong Kong. His students spread Wing Chun around the world. Later, Ip Man was the teacher of Bruce Lee.

After Ip Man died, the teacher Duncan Leung taught Wing Chun in Hong Kong from 1945 to 1969.
Nowadays, Wing Chun is taught around the world. Many children do Wing Chun.
