Koç Holding

Koç Holding, the largest Turkish industrial conglomerate and Turkey's largest group of companies. Koç Holding was founded in 1926 by Vehbi Koç. Its headquarters in Nakkaştepe, Istanbul.

Koç Holding is the only Turkish company to be listed in Fortune Global 500 list.

Currently, Koç Holding’s Chairman is Mustafa Koç.
