Peter Mark Richman

Peter Mark Richman (born Marvin Jack Richman, April 16, 1927) is an American movie, television, stage, voice, and radio actor who is known for his roles in "", "", "Friendly Persuasion", "Agent for H.A.R.M", and in "Dark Intruder". His career began in 1949.

Richman was born on April 16, 1927 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania to Yetta Dora Peck and to Benjamin Richman.Richman studied at the Actors Studio. He was married to Helen Richman since 1953. They have five children.
