Aad van Dulst

Arie-Jan "Aad" van Dulst (May 9, 1929 in Rotterdam - March 5, 2001 in Hoofddorp) was a Dutch politician of the Christian Historical Union (CHU) and its successor the Christian Democratic Appeal (CDA).

He was a councillor in Hilversum and The Hague, and mayor of Tietjerksteradeel and Haarlemmermeer.

Van Dulst studied theology at Utrecht University.

He worked as a journalist for regional newspapers, at NRCV radio, and as a director.
