Michelle Creber

Michelle Nicole Creber (born September 7, 1999 in Vancouver, British Columbia) is a Canadian voice actor, dancer, singer and voice artist who is best known for voicing "Apple Bloom" as well as the singing voice for "Sweetie Belle" in . She released her first solo album titled "Timeless: Songs of a Century" on August 28, 2012. The album features popular musicians from the brony community. She also hosts her own Internet radio show called Saturday Night Songs, on the brony media network "Everfree Network".
