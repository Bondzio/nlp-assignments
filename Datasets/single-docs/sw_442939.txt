Alexei Navalny

Alexei Anatolievich Navalny (born 4 June 1976) is a Russian lawyer, politician, and activist. He became known through his LiveJournal blog. In 2012, "The Wall Street Journal" described him as "the man Vladimir Putin fears most." In September 2013 Navalny came second in the Moscow mayoral election. Navalny is a Russian Opposition Coordination Council member. He is a Yale World Fellow.
