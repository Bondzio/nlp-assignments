Mercedes-Benz C-Class

Mercedes-Benz C-Class is a car made by Mercedes-Benz since 1993. It is currently in its third generation. The C-CLASS is one of the most popular Mercedes-Benz.it is a competiter to the BMW 3-Series and audi A4. The interier is full of soft-touch leather and plastic. It is no longer the cheapest mercedes you can get in the U.S. The CLA 250 takes that place so for 2015 Mercedes was able to to up the quality a bit. it slots in between the E- class and the CLA 250.
