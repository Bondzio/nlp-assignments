Abdul-Karim Mousavi Ardebili

Abdul-Karim Mousavi Ardebili (; Januray 28, 1926 - November 23, 2016) was an Iranian politician and Muslim leader. He served as the head of judiciary system of Iran for about 8 years after terror of Dr. Beheshti until death of Khomeini. He was a marja of the Twelver branch of Shia Islam.

He was born in Ardabil. He died in Tehran on 23 November 2016.
