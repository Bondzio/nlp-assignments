Bazargan

Bazargan (; also Romanized as Bāzargān, Bāzarqān, and Bāzergān) is a city in Iran. It is the capital of Bazargan District, in Maku County, West Azerbaijan Province. The city is the most important Iranian ground border for importing and exporting. At the 2006 census, its population was 9,047, in 2,126 families.
