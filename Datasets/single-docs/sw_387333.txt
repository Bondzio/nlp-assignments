Metroid Prime: Trilogy

Metroid Prime: Trilogy is a compilation video game for the Nintendo Wii console. It was released on August 24, 2009 in North America, on September 4, 2009 in Europe, and on October 15, 2009 in Australia. It includes the games "Metroid Prime", "", and "". In this game, "Metroid Prime" and "Metroid Prime 2: Echoes" have the same control system used in "Metroid Prime 3: Corruption". They also have shorter load times and better graphics.
