Alia Bhatt

Alia Bhatt (born on 15 March 1993) is an British film Actress who appears in Bollywood films. She is regarded as one of the most successful Bollywood Actress. She started her career through a Karan Johar's romantic-drama-comedy Student of the Year alongside Siddarth Malhotra and Varun Dhawan in 2012 for which she gained a Filmfare award for Best Debut Female nomination.
