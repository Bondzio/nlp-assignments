Szczecin-Goleniów &quot;Solidarność&quot; Airport

Szczecin-Goleniów "Solidarność" Airport (Polish: "Port Lotniczy Szczecin-Goleniów im. NSZZ "Solidarność"") is an airport near Goleniów, Szczecin, Police and Świnoujście in Poland. The airport has good train connections to Szczecin and Kołobrzeg.


Charter flights operates to Egypt (Hurghada and Sharm el-Sheikh), Turkey and Tunesia.
