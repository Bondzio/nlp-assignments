Arrondissement of Moulins

The arrondissement of Moulins is an arrondissement of France. It is part of the Allier "département". Its capital, and of the department, is the city of Moulins.

On 4 March 1790, with the establishment of the department of Allier, Moulins was one of the districts of the department. With the establishment of the "arrondissements" on 17 February 1800, Moulins was one of the original "arrondissements".

The "arrondissement" of Moulins is bordered to the north by the Nièvre (Burgundy) department, to the east by the Saône-et-Loire (Burgundy) department, to the south by the "arrondissement" of Vichy, to the west by the "arrondissement" of Montluçon and to the northwest by the Cher (Centre) department.
