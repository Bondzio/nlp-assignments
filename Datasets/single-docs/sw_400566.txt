Aitzol Arzallus Lizeaga

Aitzole Arzallus Lizeaga (born December 19, 1978 in San Sebastian) is a 7-per-team football player from Spain. He has a disability: he has cerebral palsy and is a C7 type player. He played 7-per-team football at the 1996 Summer Paralympics. His team finished third after they played the United States and, won 2-1. He played 7-per-team football at the 2000 Summer Paralympics. His team finished 6.
