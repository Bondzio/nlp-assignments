Desmond Wilkinson Llewelyn (September 12, 1914 â€“ December 19, 1999) was an Welsh movie, television, stage, radio, and voice actor who is known for his role as Q in all of the James Bond movies from 1963 through 1999.

Llewelyn was born on September 12, 1914 in Newport, Wales. He studied at Radley College. Llewelyn was married to Pamela Mary Pantlin from 1938 until his death in 1999. Llewelyn died on December 19, 1999 in Firle, East Sussex, England due to being injured in a road accident, aged 85.
