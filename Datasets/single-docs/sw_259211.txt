Jessie Flower

Jessie Flower (born August 18, 1994 in Indiana) is an American voice actress. She is most well known as the voice of Toph in the animated television series "". She also voiced the character Meng in the season one episode "". She voices Peggy in "Random! Cartoons" episode Tiffany. Jessie has been in several movies, including "Meet the Robinsons", "Over the Hedge", "The Ant Bully", "Finding Nemo", "The Emperor's New School", and "Brother Bear 2".
