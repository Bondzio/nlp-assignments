Chim Chim Cher-ee

"Chim Chim Cher-ee" is a song from "Mary Poppins", the 1964 musical movie. It was sung by Dick Van Dyke and Julie Andrews. It won the 1964 Academy Award for the Best Original Song.

The song was inspired by one of the drawings of a chimney sweep. It was covered and redone through other musicians.
