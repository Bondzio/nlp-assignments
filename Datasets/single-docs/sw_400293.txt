Alfonso Fidalgo Lopez (born June 11, 1969 in Cembranos) is a track and field athlete from Spain. He has a disability: He is blind and a F11/T11/B1 type athlete. He was at the 1996 Summer Paralympics. He finished first in the F11 shot put and first in the F11 discus. He was at the 2000 Summer Paralympics. He finished second in the F11 shot put and first in the F11 discus
