Scar (The Lion King)

Scar is a fictional character and the main antagonist in Walt Disney Pictures' animated movie "The Lion King". He is the younger brother of Mufasa, the great uncle of Kion, Kopa, and Kiara, and the uncle of Simba. Scar was voiced by Jeremy Irons with singing vocals by Jim Cummings. His supervising animator was Andreas Deja. Scar is one of four Disney characters to be nominated for "Best Villain" in the MTV Movie Awards (the other three being the White Witch, Captain Barbossa and Davy Jones), and is also the only Disney animated character to have achieved this.
