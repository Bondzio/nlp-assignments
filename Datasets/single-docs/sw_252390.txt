Summer of 4 Ft. 2

"Summer of 4 Ft. 2" is the season finale of "The Simpsons" 7th season. It originally aired on May 19, 1996. In this episode, Ned Flanders has to do Jury duty, and therefore can't go to his beach house. He allows the Simpsons to use it for 2 weeks. Lisa, realizing that she is unpopular in Springfield, decides to try to make friends in this new place. After getting a new wardrobe, she meets some new kids and befriends them by not being herself. Bart, jealous of Lisa's new popularity, shows her new friends that Lisa is not cool. Lisa is furious at Bart, but gets happier when her friends accept her for who she is.
