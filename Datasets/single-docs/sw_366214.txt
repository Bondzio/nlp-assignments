Catherine O'Hara

Catherine Anne O'Hara (born March 4, 1954) is a Canadian-American movie, television, stage, voice actress, writer and comedian. She is well known for her roles in "SCTV", and her roles in movies such as in "After Hours", "Beetlejuice", "Home Alone", and "The Nightmare Before Christmas". She has won an Emmy Award.

O'Hara was born on March 4, 1954 in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. O'Hara studied at Burnhamthorpe Collegiate Institute. She has been married to Jo Welch since 1992. They have two children.
