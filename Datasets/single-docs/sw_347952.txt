Jean-Michel Vernhes

Jean-Michel Vernhes, born in 1950 at Mazamet, is a French aerospace engineer and businessman. He is the current President of French Airports ("Union des aéroports français") and of Toulouse-Blagnac Airport.

Graduate from the French Civil Aviation University (1971), Jean-Michel Vernhes starts his career in 1975 at the air traffic control center of Bordeaux. He has done a career of 20 years in air navigation. He is nominated President of Toulouse-Blagnac Airport in 1999.

The 18th of May 2011, in addition, he become President of the French Airports ("Union des aéroports français").
