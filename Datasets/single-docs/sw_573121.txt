Yigal Amir

Yigal Amir, Hebrew: יגאל עמיר‎‎ , (born May 23, 1970 in Herzliya) is an Israeli murderer. 

Amir is undergoing a life sentence because of murdering Prime Minister Yitzhak Rabin on November 4, 1995.

His motivation was political. He was furious about the Oslo Accords and murdered Rabin because of his peace efforts with the Palestinians.

Amir is an Orthodox Jew and has a Jewish-Yemenite background. During the murder he studied law and computer science at Bar-Ilan University and also Jewish law.
