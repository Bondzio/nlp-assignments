New Taipei City

New Taipei City () is a city in northern Taiwan. It circles Taipei City. It is south of Keelung, north of Taoyuan County and west of Yilan. It is a part of the Republic of China and the largest city in Taiwan.


New Taipei City controls 29 districts (區). It has 1,017 villages (里). The villages are divided into 21,683 neighborhoods (鄰).
