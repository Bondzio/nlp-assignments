Ian Brennan

Ian Brennan is a television writer, actor, and producer. Brennan is known mostly for creating the FOX television series, "Glee". He has written "all of the episodes" along with two other colleagues. Brennan's first written television series was "Growing" in 2006. He has also been in several theater performances since 1999.

He was nominated for two 2010 Writers Guild of America Awards for "Glee".
