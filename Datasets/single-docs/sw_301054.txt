Ksenia Sobchak

Ksenia Anatolyevna Sobchak (; born on November 5, 1981) is a Russian television presenter, actress and singer. Since 2004 she has been the presenter of the reality show "House 2". She is the only child of politicians Anatoly Sobchak (1937-2000) and Lyudmila Narusova (b.1951).

Ksenia was never married and has no children.

She lives in Moscow (Russia).
