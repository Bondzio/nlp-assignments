Diamonds (song)

"Diamonds" is a song by Barbadian singer Rihanna. It was on her seventh studio album, "Unapologetic". It was the first song off of the album. It was Rihanna's twelfth number one single on the "Billboard Hot 100" chart.

Several rappers have rapped or made a remix to the song. They include Kanye West, Flo Rida and Eve.
