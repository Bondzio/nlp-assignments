Arrondissement of Vichy

The arrondissement of Vichy is an arrondissement of France. It is part of the Allier "département". Its capital is the city of Vichy.

When the department of Allier was created on 4 March 1790, Vichy was not one of ist districts. With the establishment of the "arrondissements" on 17 February 1800, Vichy was part of the "arrondissements" of "Lapalisse".

On 24 August 1941, the capital of the "arrondissement" of "Lapalisse" was moved from the city of Lapalisse to the city of Vichy that was then the capital of the French State.

The "arrondissement" of Vichy is in the southeast of the Allier department and is bordered to the west and north by the "arrondissement" of Moulins, to the east by the Saône-et-Loire (Burgundy) department, to the southeast by the Loire (Rhône-Alpes) department, to the south by the Puy-de-Dôme department and to the southwest by the "arrondissement" of Montluçon.
