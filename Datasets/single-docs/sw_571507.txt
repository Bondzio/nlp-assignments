Radboud University Nijmegen

Radboud University Nijmegen (Dutch: "Radboud Universiteit Nijmegen"), shortened to RU, is a Roman Catholic university in Nijmegen, a city in the province of Gelderland, the Netherlands.

Radboud University Nijmegen was established in 1923.

It has about 20,000 students.

Its focus is on all sorts of sciences.
