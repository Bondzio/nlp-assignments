Nontron

Nontron () is a commune and a sub-prefecture of the Dordogne department in the Nouvelle-Aquitaine region, southwestern France. It is the capital of the northernmost "arrondissement" of the Dordogne department.

The "Nontronite" is a mineral (a silicate mineral) rich in iron named after Nontron.
