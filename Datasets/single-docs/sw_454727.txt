Julie Bishop (actress)

Jacqueline Wells (later Julie Bishop (August 30, 1914 â€“ August 30, 2001) was an American actress. She appeared in over 80 movies between 1923 and 1957. She also acted on television. She began her career as a child actress. She played Myrtle Reed in the movie "Lady Gangster". She played a stewardess in "Princess O'Rourke".

Bishop also acted on stage.

The actress was born in Denver, Colorado. She died in Mendocino, California on her 87th birthday from pneumonia.
