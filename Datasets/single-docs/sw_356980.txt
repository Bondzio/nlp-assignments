Soleil Moon Frye

Soleil Moon Frye (born August 6, 1976) is an American television actress. Her best-known roles are that of Punky Brewster in the same-named sitcom which lasted from 1984 to 1988 and Roxie in the television sitcom "Sabrina, the Teenage Witch" (starting in 1996 and ending in 2003). Frye was also in other things on television.

She began acting at age 6 on television.
