Harry Melling (actor)

Harry Edward Melling (born 13 March 1989) is an English actor. He is best known for playing Dudley Dursley in the Harry Potter films. 

Melling was born in London. He became a member of the Millfield Theatre Youth Drama Group when he turned 9.

Melling appeared in five of the Harry Potter films as Harry Potter's cousin Dudley Dursley. After the Harry Potter films ended Melling went to Lamda drama school.
