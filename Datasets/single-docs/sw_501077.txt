Macbeth (character)

Macbeth is a main character and antagonist in the play "Macbeth" by William Shakespeare. He was the Thane of Cawdor. He would eventually kill the then-King of Scotland Duncan so that he can become king. His wife, Lady Macbeth makes him kill the king. He then eventually kills his friend Banquo and Macduff's family. He is eventually killed by Macduff.
