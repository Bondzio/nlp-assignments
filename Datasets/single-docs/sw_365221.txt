Arthur O'Connell

Arthur O'Connell (March 29, 1908 â€“ May 18, 1981) was an American stage, movie, television, voice, and radio actor. He was known for his roles in "Citizen Kane", "Operation Petticoat", "Picnic", "April Love", "Bus Stop", and in "Anatomy of Murder". 

O'Connell was born on March 29, 1908 in New York City, New York. He was married to Ann Hall Dunlop until they divorced in 1973. O'Connell died on May 18, 1981 from Alzheimer's disease in his Los Angeles, California home, aged 73.
