Wang Xiaobo

Wang Xiaobo (; pinyin: Wáng Xiǎobō; May 13, 1952–April 11, 1997) is a famous writer in mainland China. His most famous and successful Literary works is "Golden Time".

Xiaobo was born in Beijing, China. In 1972, he became a teacher in the Shandong province. In 1978, he started studying at the Renmin University of China. Xiaobo's most famous novel was published in 1994. It was named "Golden Time".
