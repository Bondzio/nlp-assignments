Mir Hazar Khan Khoso

Justice (R) Mir Hazar Khan Khoso (Urdu, ) (born 30 September 1929), was the caretaker Prime Minister of Pakistan, from 25 March 2013 to 5 June 2013. 

Khoso is a retired Judge who served as the Chief Justice of the Federal Shariat Court and served as the interim Prime Minister ahead of the general elections scheduled in May 2013.
