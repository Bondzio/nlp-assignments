WWE Cruiserweight Championship

The WWE Cruiserweight Championship is a wrestling title owned by WWE and can be defended in "Raw" and "205 Live". Only wreslters under can dispute it. The first champion was T.J. Perkins and the current is Neville.

In 2016, WWE created the Cruiserweight Classic, a 32-man tournament for cruiserweights. When T.J. Perkins beat Gran Metalik in the final, Triple H gave him the new WWE Cruiserweight Championship for the new cruiserweight division on the Raw brand.

After the "205 Live" was created, the Cruiserweight Championship also could be defended in this program.
