Hyrule Warriors

Hyrule Warriors is a video game for the Wii U console. It is a crossover between "The Legend of Zelda" and "Dynasty Warriors" series. It was developed by Tecmo Koei and Nintendo. It is a spin-off game in "The Legend of Zelda" series. It was released on August 14, 2014.

The game plays like the "Dynasty Warriors" series, using the setting and characters from "The Legend of Zelda". The player controls Link to take on a large number of enemies from "The Legend of Zelda" series. The player can use weapons such as a sword and bombs, as well as Link's spin attack. Like in other "The Legend of Zelda" games, the player can get new items by finding and opening chests.
