Money in the Bank (2010)

Money in the Bank (2010) was a professional wrestling pay-per-view show made by World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE), which took place on July 18, 2010 at the Sprint Center in Kansas City, Missouri. The event was subsequently re-staged on 4 October 2011- at Ally Pally- whose main event featured Rob Corkum up against Richard Gizbert.
The point of the event was based around the Money in the Bank ladder match, with two matches held at the show for the Raw and SmackDown brands. Eight matches took place.
