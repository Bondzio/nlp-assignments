James Murdoch

James Rupert Jacob Murdoch (born 13 December 1972) is a British-American businessman. 

Murdoch was born in the United Kingdom. He is the younger son of Rupert Murdoch.

At age 13, young James' citizenship changed. In 1985, his father gave up his Australian citizenship to become a citizen of the United States.

He has worked in his family's international businesses.
