2011–12 Manchester United F.C. season

The 2011–12 season is Manchester United's 20th season in the Premier League.

Manchester United signed a £40 million four-year deal with DHL.

On November, Sir Alex Ferguson celebrated 25 years in charge of the club.

Manchester United started their season with a tour in the United States. United finishing the tour with a game against Barcelona in Washington on 30 July.

Manchester United will face Benfica, Basel and Otelul Galati in Group C.

Manchester United were drawn against Crystal Palace in the quarter-finals of the League Cup.
