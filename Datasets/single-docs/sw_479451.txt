Will McBride (photographer)

Will McBride (January 10, 1931 â€“ January 29, 2015) was an American photographer in reportage, art photography and book illustration, as well as, a painter and sculptor. He was known for his works that were against censorship in the United States by taking picture of people naked. McBride was born in St. Louis, Missouri and raised in Chicago, Illinois. His entire career was based in Berlin.

McBride died in his home in Berlin, Germany, aged 84.
