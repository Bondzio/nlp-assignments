Kobe Airport

The airport is on an artificial island just off the coast of Kobe. It is south of Sannomiya Station, which is the main train station in the city.
The city government of Kobe proposed an airport in the harbor next to Port Island. Early planning was published in 1982.

The airport opened on February 16, 2006. The airport handled its first international aircraft flight in September 2006. It is officially classified as a 3rd class airport.
