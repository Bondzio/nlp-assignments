Malappuram

Malappuram is a city in the southern Indian state of Kerala. It is 50 km southeast of Kozhikode, 90 km northwest of Palakkad and 140 km northwest of Coimbatore.Malappuram is the head quarters of the district.In earlier times, Malappuram was the head quarters of European and British troops and later it became the head quarters of the Malabar Special Police (M.S.P).The municipality of Malappuram city has started kottakkunnu amusement park, a water theme park in the valley of kottakkunnu.Malppuram is well known for its greenery, making it a tourist spot.
