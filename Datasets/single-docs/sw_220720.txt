The Marvelous Misadventures of Flapjack

The Marvelous Misadventures of Flapjack is an American animated television series made for Cartoon Network that started on June 5, 2008. The show was made by cartoonist Mark "Thurop" Van Orman, who has worked as a writer and storyboard artist on "Camp Lazlo" and "The Grim Adventures of Billy & Mandy". the main point of the marvelous misadventures of flapjack is the main characters (captain knuckles, flapjack & bubbie) trying to find the magical island of candied island. flapjack is an adventurous little boy taking after and learning from captain knuckles,knuckles plays the role of a drunken sailor
