D. J. King

Dwayne "D.J." King (born June 27, 1984) is a Canadian professional ice hockey left winger. He currently plays for the Ontario Reign of the ECHL. He previously played 118 games in the National Hockey League (NHL). He played for the St. Louis Blues and the Washington Capitals. 

Before playing in the NHL, King played 4 seasons in the Western Hockey League; 3 seasons with the Lethbridge Hurricanes and 1 season with the Kelowna Rockets. He was drafted by the St. Louis Blues with the 190th overall pick in the 2002 NHL Entry Draft.
