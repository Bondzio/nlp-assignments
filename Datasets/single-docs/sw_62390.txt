Björk

Björk was born Björk Guðmundsdóttir on 21 November 1965 in Reykjavík, Iceland. She is an Icelandic singer, composer and actress. She mostly writes her own songs. She is best known for telling people how she feels with emotions and for her unusual voice. She sings mostly in English but sometimes in Icelandic. She often makes music on her laptop.

She has a son and a daughter, Sindri, and Isadòra. She lives in New York City, but also owns a home in Reykjavík.

Björk was in the 2013 television documentary "When Björk Met Attenborough" with Sir David Attenborough.
