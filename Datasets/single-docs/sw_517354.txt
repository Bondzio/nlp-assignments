The Soul Sessions Vol. 2

The Soul Sessions Vol. 2 is the sixth studio album by English singer-songwriter Joss Stone, released on 20 July 2012 by S-Curve Records and her own label, Stone'd Records.

Like her debut album "The Soul Sessions" (2003) where the songs were covers of classic soul songs, "The Soul Sessions Vol. 2"s songs are covers of soul and R&B songs that were originally made during the 1970s.

"The Soul Sessions Vol. 2" received positive reviews from music critics.
