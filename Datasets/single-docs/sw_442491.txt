Witold Sobociński

Witold Sobociński (born October 15, 1929) is a Polish cinematographer, academic teacher as well as former jazz musician.

Sobociński studied at the National Film School in Łódź. He was a member of Melomani, in which he played the drums.

Sobociński was born on October 15, 1929 in Łódź, Poland.
