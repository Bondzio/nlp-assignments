Skyfall (song)

"Skyfall" is a song by Adele. It was used in a James Bond movie also called "Skyfall". The tone is dark and melancholy. It received the Best Original Song award at the 70th Golden Globes and 85th Academy Awards. The song hit #1 on some music charts across the world. It reached the top ten in the UK, US, and Canada.

It was released at 0:07 British Summer Time on 5 October 2012.
