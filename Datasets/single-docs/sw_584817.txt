Bruce Hill (racing driver)

Bruce Hill (July 9, 1949 – May 14, 2017) was an American former stock car racing driver. He was born Topeka, Kansas. He competed in the Winston Cup Series, ARCA, and in the NASCAR West Series. He also competed in late model races around his hometown of Topeka later in life. 

Hill died in 2017 due to complications from esophageal cancer at his Topeka home at the age of 67.
