No Scrubs

"No Scrubs" is a Grammy Award-winning song. It was recorded by American contemporary R&B girl group TLC. It was for their album, "FanMail". It was released in January 1999 as the lead single. "No Scrubs" became TLC's third number-one single and eighth top 10 single on the US "Billboard" Hot 100. It also earned them their second Grammy nomination for Record of the Year.
