Ted Goulait

He also played for many other teams in minor league competitions such as the Marion Diggers, the Springfield Reapers, the Toronto Maple Leafs, the Wilkes-Barre Barons and the Memphis Chicks.

Goulait was born in St. Clair, Michigan.

Goulait died on July 15, 1936 in his hometown of St. Clair, aged 46.
