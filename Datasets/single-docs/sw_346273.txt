Louis Pailhas

Louis Pailhas, born the 2nd of March 1926 in Foix, is a French politician and aerospace engineer.
From December 1967 to 1982, he was head of the French Civil Aviation University.

Graduate from French Civil Aviation University (Promotion 1949), he has done different job in civil aviation before joining the French Civil Aviation University in December 1967, the first to hold that position in Toulouse. He resigned in January 1982.

After that, in 1998, he is assistant of the Mayor of Toulouse, and then deputy Mayor for science, aeronautics and space activities in 2001.
