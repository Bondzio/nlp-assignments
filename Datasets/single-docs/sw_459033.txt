Victoria Justice

Victoria Dawn Justice (born February 19, 1993) is an American singer-songwriter, dancer, rapper and actress. She appeared as one of the main stars of "Zoey 101". She soon had the main lead in another Nickelodeon series "Victorious". She also appeared in the movies "Unknown" and in "The Garden". In October 2013, she obtained the lead role on MTV's "Eye Candy" playing Lindy Sampson. The series premiered on January 12, 2015.
