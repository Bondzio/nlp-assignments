Anne Jackson

Anne June Jackson (September 3, 1925 â€“ April 12, 2016), also known as Anne Wallach, was an American movie, television, and stage actress. She was mostly known for her role as a doctor in "The Shinning" (1980). She was born on September 3, 1926 in Milvale, Pennsylvania. She was educated at the Actors Studio. She was married to Eli Wallach from 1948 until his death in 2014. They had 3 children. 

Jackson died on April 12, 2016 at her apartment in Manhattan, New York, aged 90.
