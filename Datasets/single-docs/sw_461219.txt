Patrick Wilson

Patrick Joseph Wilson (born July 3, 1973) is an American actor and singer. He is known for his roles in "The Alamo", "The Phantom of the Opera", "Little Children", "Watchmen", "The A-Team", "The Ledge", "Insidious", and "The Conjuring". 

Wilson appeared in a voice cameo as the President of the United States in "" (2016) and will appear as Ocean Master in "Aquaman" (2018).
