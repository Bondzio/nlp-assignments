Pat O'Brien

Pat O’Brien (William Joseph Patrick O'Brien; November 11, 1899 – October 15, 1983) was an American movie, radio, and stage actor. He won a Daytime Emmy Award during his career. O'Brien was born in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. 

He began acting in 1920. He appeared in over 100 movies. He is known for his roles in "Knute Rockne, All American", "Cowboy from Brooklyn", and in "Some Like It Hot". 

O'Brien died from a heart attack in Santa Monica, California, aged 83.
