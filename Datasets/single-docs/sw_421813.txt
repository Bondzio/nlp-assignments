The Simpsons: Road Rage

The Simpsons: Road Rage is a 2001 action-adventure video game based on the animated television show "The Simpsons". It was released for the Nintendo GameCube, Xbox, Playstation 2 and Game Boy Advance. It is similar to the game "Crazy Taxi", in that the player has to pick up people and drive them to where they want to go as fast as they can.
