Jason Acuña

Jason Acuña (born May 16, 1973), he is mostly known as Wee-Man, is an American TV host and actor. He is one of the crew members for "Jackass" and stared in all of its movies.

Acuña was born on May 16, 1973 in Pisa, Italy with a Chilean origin. He was raised in Torrance, California. He studied at North High School.
