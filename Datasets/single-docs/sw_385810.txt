Juárez Municipality, Chihuahua

Juárez is a one of the 67 municipalities of Chihuahua, in northern Mexico. The municipal seat is Ciudad Juárez. The municipality's land area is .

In the 2010 INEGI Census, the municipality reported a population of 1,332,131, of whom 1,321,004 (over 99%) lived in the municipal seat. 

The municipality is named for 19th-century president Benito Juárez, as is the city of Ciudad Juárez.
