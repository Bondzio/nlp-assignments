Cagliari

Cagliari () is a city in the region of Sardinia in Italy. It is also the capital of the "Province of Cagliari". Cagliari has one of the biggest ports in the Mediterranea Sea and an international airport.

Cagliari has a hot-summer Mediterranean climate ("Csa" in the Koeppen climate classification).
