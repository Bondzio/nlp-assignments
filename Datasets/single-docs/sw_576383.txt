Yisrael Beiteinu

Yisrael Beiteinu, Hebrew: ישראל ביתנו , meaning "Israel Our Home", is an Israeli political party.

Its ideology is right-wing conservative, nationalist, populist, secular, and Zionist. Its focus is especially Jews originating from the former Soviet Union.

Political leader is Avigdor Lieberman.

Yisrael Beiteinu was founded by Lieberman in 1999. 

Number of seats in the 20th Knesset: 6 (-7).

Participating in the Fourth Netanyahu Government.
