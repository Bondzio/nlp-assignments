Carl Johan Thyselius

Carl Johan Thyselius, born 8 June 1811, dead 11 January 1891, was a Swedish politician, government official. He served as Justice Councillor (1856-1860), "Minister for Ecclesiastical Affairs" (1860-1863), Minister for Civil Affairs (responsible for trade, industry and ship transport; (1875-80). He,served as the Prime Minister from 1883 to 1884.
