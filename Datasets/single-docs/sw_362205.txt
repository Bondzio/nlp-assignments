Clark Gregg

Robert Clark Gregg (born April 2, 1962) is an American director, screenwriter, actor and voice artist. He has acted in both television and movies. Gregg is known for his role as Phil Coulson in "Iron Man", "Iron Man 2", "Thor", and "The Avengers". 

Gregg was born in Boston, Massachusetts. Gregg has been married to Jennifer Grey since 2001. They have a daughter, Stella Gregg.
