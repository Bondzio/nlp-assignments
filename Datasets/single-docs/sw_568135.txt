Tiffany Trump

Tiffany Ariana Trump (born October 13, 1993) is an American internet personality. She is the daughter of the President of the United States, Donald Trump, and his second wife Marla Maples.

Trump is a frequent poster to Instagram. As of 2016, she had more than 460,000 followers. She worked as an intern at "Vogue" and modeled for a 2016 Andrew Warren fashion show during New York Fashion Week.
