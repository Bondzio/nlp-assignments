Dübendorf

Dübendorf is a municipality in the district of Uster in the canton of Zurich in Switzerland.

It is a suburb of Zürich in Switzerland with a population of about 26,000 (2014). It is the fourth largest city in the canton, after Zürich, Winterthur and Uster.
