Nazim Al-Haqqani

Mehmet Nazım Adil or Muhammad Nazim Adil al-Qubrusi al-Haqqani (; April 21, 1922 – May 7, 2014), often called Shaykh Nazim (), was a Turkish-Cypriot Sufi Sheikh. He was the leader of the Naqshbandi-Haqqani Order. Al-Haqqani predicted many events, all were not true. He was also a poet. He was born in Larnaca, Cyprus. 

Nazim al-Haqqani died at the age of 92 in Northern Cyprus from multiple organ failure.
