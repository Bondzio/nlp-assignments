Edna Doré

Edna Doré, née Gorring, (31 May 1921 – 11 April 2014) was a British actress. Doré was known for her bit-part roles in situation comedies. She is mostly known for playing the character of Mo Butcher in the BBC soap opera "EastEnders" (1988–1990). She was born in Bromley, Kent, England.

Doré died from emphysema at her Sussex, England home, aged 92.
