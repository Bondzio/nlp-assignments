Jake Dowell

Jacob Dowell (born March 4, 1985 in Eau Claire, Wisconsin) is a American professional ice hockey center that currently plays for the Dallas Stars of the National Hockey League (NHL). He was selected by the Chicago Blackhawks with the 140th pick in the 2004 NHL Draft and played 4 seasons with them before being signed to the Dallas Stars on July 1, 2011.

Dowell also played for the Chicago Blackhawks AHL farm team, the Rockford IceHogs.
