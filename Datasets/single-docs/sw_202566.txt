Ja Rule

Jeffrey Atkins (born February 29, 1976), better known by his stage name Ja Rule, is an American recording artist and singer signed to The Inc. and Universal Records, formerly of Def Jam Recordings. A Hollis, New York native, Ja Rule has released seven albums. Rule's eighth album is called "Renaissance Project". It has not been released yet. He has had a longtime feud with 50 Cent.
