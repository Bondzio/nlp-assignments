Juan Carlos Tedesco

Juan Carlos Tedesco (February 5, 1944 – May 8, 2017) was an Argentine academic and policy maker. He served as the Education Minister from December 2007 to July 2009 during the Cristina Fernández de Kirchner presidency. He was born in Buenos Aires.

Tedesco died on May 8, 2017 in Buenos Aires at the age of 73.
