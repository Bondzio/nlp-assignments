Francisco Jesus Mendez Cestero (born September 3, 1976 in Sabadell) is a track and field athlete from Spain. He is also a screenwriter. He has a physical disability: He is T54/F54 type athlete. He competed at the 1996 Summer Paralympics. He finished third in the discus F53 event. He competed at the 2000 Summer Paralympics. He finished third shot put F54 event. He competed at the 2004 Summer Paralympics.
