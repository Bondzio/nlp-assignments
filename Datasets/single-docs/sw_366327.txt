Nick Frost

Nicholas John "Nick" Frost (born 28 March 1972) is an English movie, television, stage, voice actor, comedian and screenwriter. He is best known for his roles "Spaced, as Ed in "Shaun of the Dead, Danny Butterman in "Hot Fuzz", Clive Gollings in "Paul", and as Ron in "Attack the Block". He is also well known for working with Simon Pegg.

Frost was born on 28 March 1972 in Dagenham, East London, England. He studied at Bell High School. He is married to Christina Frost.
