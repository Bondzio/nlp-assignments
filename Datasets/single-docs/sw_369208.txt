René Auberjonois

René Murat Auberjonois (born June 1, 1940) is an American movie, television, stage, and voice actor. He is known for his roles as Father Mulcahy in movie version of "M*A*S*H", as Chef Louis in "The Little Mermaid", and as Odo in "" television program. He has won an Drama Desk Award and a Tony Award.

Auberjonois was born on June 1, 1940 in New York City, New York. He has been married to Judith Mihalyi since 1963. They have two children.
