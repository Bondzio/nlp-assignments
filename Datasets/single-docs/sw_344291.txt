Julie Walters

Julie Walters CBE (born Julia Mary Walters, 22 February 1950) is a British actress. She was born at St Chad's Hospital, Edgbaston, Birmingham, which was then the hospital covering Smethwick, Staffordshire. Her movie roles include the title role in "Educating Rita", June Edwards in "Buster" and Sandra Wilkinson in "Billy Elliot". She has also acted on television and on stage.


Julie Walters has won 7 BAFTA Awards, ( including a special BAFTA that she received at a tribute evening in 2003 ) a Golden Globe, an Olivier, two International Emmys and has been nominated for two Academy Awards.
