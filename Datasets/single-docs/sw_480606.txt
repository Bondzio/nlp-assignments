Relitahallen

Relitahallen is an indoor bandy venue the town of Uppsala in Sweden. It was opened on 11 September 2011 with the game IK Sirius-HK Jenisej Krasnojarsk 5-4. 

On 6 and 7 September 2013, double international games between Sweden and Finland were played inside. Both A- and U23 international games were played.
