Columbia County, Wisconsin

Columbia County is a county in the U.S. state of Wisconsin. As of 2000, 52,468 people lived in Columbia county. Portage is the county seat and largest city in the county.

Columbia County is part of the Madison Metropolitan Statistical Area.
