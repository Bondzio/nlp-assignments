Marujita Díaz

Marujita Díaz (María del Dulce Nombre Díaz Ruiz; 27 April 1932 – 23 June 2015) was a Spanish singer and actress. She was born in Sevilla, Spain. Díaz was known for presenting the very popular show "Música y estrellas". 

Díaz died of respiratory failure caused by colon cancer in Madrid, aged 83.
