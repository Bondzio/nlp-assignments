Jim Cummings

James Jonah "Jim" Cummings (born November 3, 1952) is an American voice actor. He has appeared in almost 400 roles on television and on movies. He appeared on many movies such as in "Aladdin", "The Lion King", "Antz", "The Road to El Dorado", "Shrek", and "The Princess and the Frog". Jim Cummings is probably best known for his work on "Darkwing Duck," Winnie the Pooh, and Tigger.

Cummings was born on November 3, 1952 in Youngstown, Ohio.
