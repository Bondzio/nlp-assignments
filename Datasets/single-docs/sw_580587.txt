Work at a Pizza Place

Work at a Pizza Place is an American animated television series produced by Warner Bros. Animation. 
It will be aired on Cartoon Network in late 2005 and finished in early 2008. 

The work at a Pizza Place is based on the novel of the kitchen of a late Mexican author Alejandro Martinez. 

In Indonesian's Spacetoon, It will aired together with The Smurfs, but in 2017, it finally retired. The series is on Toon Disney in France, Japan and Finland.
