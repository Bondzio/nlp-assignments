Svenska dansbandsveckan

Svenska dansbandsveckan is a dansband music festival. It is held in the Orrskogen folkpark of Malung in Sweden. It is held every Week 29. The event was first held in 1986. Usually the event is opened with the Guldklaven Awards being awarded.

In 2012 the event was visited by totally 50 700 people. That menat a new record had been broken.
