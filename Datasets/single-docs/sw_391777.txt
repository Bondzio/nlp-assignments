Tommy Kelly (actor)

Tommy Kelly (April 6, 1925 â€“ January 26, 2016) was an American actor and teacher. He was known for his roles in "Peck's Bad Boy with the Circus", "Military Academy", "The Adventure of Tom Sawyer", and in "They Shall Have Music".

Kelly was born on April 6, 1925 in New York City, New York. His ancestry is Irish. Kelly used to be a teacher in Washington, D.C.. He retired from acting in 1950. Kelly died in Greensboro, North Carolina from congestive heart failure on January 26, 2016 at the age of 90.
