Premio Lo Nuestro

Lo Nuestro is an awards show for Latin music. It is produced by television network Univision. The awards began in 1989. Artists with awards are Celia Cruz, Gloria Estefan, Juan Gabriel, Juanes, Luis Miguel, Maná, RBD, Selena, Shakira and Thalía. 

The artist with the most Premios Lo Nuestro awards is Puerto Rican singer Olga Tañón.
