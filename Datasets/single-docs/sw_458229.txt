Vancouver Stealth

The Vancouver Stealth is a professional box lacrosse team. It plays in the West Division of the National Lacrosse League. It is based in Langley, British Columbia, which is part of the Metro Vancouver area. The team plays its home games in Langley Events Centre. The team was announced on June 27, 2013. They started playing in the 2014 season.

The team was previously known as the Albany Attack from the 2000 season until the 2003 season. Later they played as the San Jose Stealth from the 2004 season until the 2009 season. They were the Washington Stealth from the 2010 season until the 2013 season.
