Marc Zumoff

Marc Zumoff (born November 19, 1955) is an Emmy Award-Winning American sportscaster. He calls games for the Philadelphia 76ers. He also did play-by-play for the NBA on TNT and the NBA on TBS. He is a fill-in play-by-play announcer for the Philadelphia Union. He was a play-by-play announcer for College Football on CN8. He also called the Philadelphia Big 5, the Philadelphia Flyers, the Philadelphia Fever, and Temple Owls football and basketball.
