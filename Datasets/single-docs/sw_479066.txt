Västra Torup

Västra Torup is a minor locality in Hässleholm Municipality in Skåne County in Sweden. In 2010, 198 people lived there In 2005, 210 people lived there, Earlier, Västra Torup was classified as a locality by Statistics Sweden. The Ingelbo Moose Park is located in Hissmossa north of Västra Torup
