Ray Traylor

Raymond Washington Traylor, Jr. (May 2, 1963 â€“ September 22, 2004) was a professional wrestler who is best known for wrestling with the WWF under the ring name (The) Big Bossman and with World Championship Wrestling (WCW) under the name many different ring names. 

During his time with the WWF, he won the WWF Hardcore Champion four times where his fourth reign is credited as being the longest in WWF/E History. He also won the WWF Tag Team Championship one time with Ken Shamrock.

He died of a heart attack in his Acworth, Georgia home on September 22, 2004.
