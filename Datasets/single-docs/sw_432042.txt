Dr. Seuss' The Lorax

Dr. Seuss' The Lorax is an American animated movie. It Is based on the book "The Lorax" by Dr. Seuss. It is a family comedy. It is directed by Chris Renaud. It stars Danny DeVito, Ed Helms, Zac Efron, Taylor Swift, Betty White, Rob Riggle and Jenny Slate. It was released on March 2, 2012. The movie received positive reviews. It has songs like Let it Grow and How Bad Can I Be.
