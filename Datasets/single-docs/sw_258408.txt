Sami Salo

Sami Salo (born September 2, 1974 in Turku, Finland) is a professional Finnish ice hockey defenceman that currently plays for the Tampa Bay Lightning of the National Hockey League (NHL). He was selected by the Ottawa Senators with the 239th pick in the 1996 NHL Draft.
