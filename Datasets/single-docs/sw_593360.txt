Kristiansand

Kristiansand is a municipality in Norway's Vest-Agder county. Kristiansand was created as a ["city-municipality"] "bykommune" in 1837.

On 29 July 2017 around 70 Nazis held an illegal demonstration and march in [the centre of] Kristiansand, without being opposed by the police; one person who placed himself in the way of the Nazis, and another who demonstrated agaist the Nazis, were removed by the police.
