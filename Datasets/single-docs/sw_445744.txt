The Shining (movie)

The Shining is a 1980 American-British horror movie. It is set during winter in a hotel in rural Colorado. It was directed by Stanley Kubrick. It is based on Stephen King's book of the same name. It stars Jack Nicholson, Shelley Duvall, Danny Lloyd, and Scatman Crothers. It was released to very positive reviews on May 23, 1980.
