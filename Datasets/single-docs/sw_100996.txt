Leeds United A.F.C.

Leeds United A.F.C. are an English Association football club in Leeds, West Yorkshire. They play their home matches at Elland Road. The club has spent much of its time since it was founded in the top division of English football.

Leeds United was established in 1919, having previously been known as Leeds City.

Leeds United won the League Cup in 1968.

Leeds United won the FA Cup in 1972

Leeds United currently holds rivalries with Manchester United, Chelsea FC, Liverpool FC, Hull City, and other clubs that could be considered as rivals are Manchester City FC, , Sheffield Wednesday FC, Doncaster Rovers, Millwall FC and Barnsley FC.
