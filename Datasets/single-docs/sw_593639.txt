Punta di Grumo Beach (Lierna)

Punta di Grumo Beach is a popular beach and the name of the surrounding suburb in Lierna, Como Lake, Italy. 
Punta di Grumo Beach is one of the most visited tourist sites in Lake of Como.

Punta di Grumo Beach is about long and receives many visitors throughout the year. 

In 2011 Lierna Council implemented Wi-Fi to Punta di Grumo Beach users. Service is free with limits on access periods and downloads per use. 

"La Punt" Beach is represented in one of the most popular amateur sporting across Como Lake.
