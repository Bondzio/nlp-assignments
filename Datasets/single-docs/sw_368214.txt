Anthony Bell

Anthony Bell (born 1973, United States), sometimes known as Tony Bell is an American animator, movie director, screenwriter and story artist. He started his career as a character layout artist in "The Simpsons" in the early nineties and he also directed a few episodes in "Rugrats". Bell also directed some episode for the Adult Swim animated TV show, "The Boondocks". His most recent and famous work is for being the co-director of the 2010 computer-animated comedy-drama movie, "Alpha and Omega" (along with Ben Gluck), where he also served as story artist. Anthony is now working as the director on a new animated comedy-drama movie, called "Norm of the North". He also wrote the story for the movie.
