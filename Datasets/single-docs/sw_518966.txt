Shawn Mendes

Shawn Peter Raul Mendes (born August 8, 1998) is a Canadian singer-songwriter. He became known in 2013, when he began posting cover versions of songs by artists such as Justin Bieber on the video sharing application Vine. In 2014, he signed a recording contract with Island Records.

Mendes released his first single, "Life of the Party". The song peaked at number 1 on the real-time "Billboard" Twitter Trending 140 chart. Mendes also released a four-track EP, "The Shawn Mendes EP". It charted at number 5 on the "Billboard" charts.

He is known for the songs "Life Of The Party", "Stitches" (his biggest hit and a UK number-one), and "Treat You Better", and more recently, There's Nothing Holding Me Back.

Mendes has now had two world tours for his albums Handwritten and Illuminate and is very commonly known.
