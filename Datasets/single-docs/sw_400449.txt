Purificación Santamarta Bravo

Purificación Santamarta Bravo (born May 25, 1962 in Burgos) is a track and field athlete from Spain. She has a disability: She is blind and a T11/B1 type athlete. She raced at the 1996 Summer Paralympics. She finished first in the 400 meter T11 race, in the 200 meter T11 race and in the 100 meter T11 race. She raced at the 2000 Summer Paralympics. She finished first in the 400 meter T11 race. She finished second in the 100 meter T11 race. She raced at the 2004 Summer Paralympics. She finished third in the 200 meter T11 race.
