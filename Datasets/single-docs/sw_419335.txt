Gravity (movie)

Gravity is a 2013 American space drama science fiction thriller movie. It is a 3D movie. 

It was co-written, co-produced, co-edited and directed by Alfonso Cuarón. The movie stars Sandra Bullock and George Clooney as surviving astronauts from a space shuttle destroyed by debris. It was released on October 4, 2013. It received positive reviews.

The movie has gotten many awards, and won seven awards in the 86th Academy Awards, with Alfonso Cuarón receiving Best Director.
