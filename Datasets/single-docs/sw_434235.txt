TEENick

TEENick was a television programming block on Nickelodeon. The television shows were for a teenage audience. 

The block began on March 6, 2001 and lasted until February 8, 2009. Nick Cannon was host of the program block. 

In 2009 Nickelodeon began a new television channel named TeenNick. The programming block continued to show shows for teenagers but no longer used the TEENick name.
