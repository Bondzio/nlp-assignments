Melendi

Ramón Melendi Espinosa (born 21 January 1979 in Oviedo, Asturias) is a Spanish singer. He studied in the school, Leopoldo Alas "Clarín" with the Formula 1 pilot, Fernando Alonso. He wrote a song for him called "Magic Alonso".

In 2003 he made his first album "Sin noticias de Holanda" which had 12 songs. In December, the same year he made two new songs "Asturias" and "Moratalá" which were very successful. In 2004 one of his songs "Con la luna llena" was chosen to represent "La vuelta ciclista España". One year later he made his second single album "Que el cielo espere sentao". He sold more than 200,000 copies of it. Some years later He made three albums more.

Nowadays he plays in concerts around Spain and South America.
