Hu Yaobang

Hu Yaobang was a politician of the People's Republic of China.

Hu was a friend and political ally of Deng Xiaoping. They fought together in the Chinese Civil War. Both were part of the communist force that took power of Sichuan in 1949. Hu became the leader of the Communist Youth League from 1952â€“1966.

During the Cultural Revolution, Hu was said to not be true to the Communist Party of China and Mao Zedong. He was rehabilitated twice. He was an important supporter of Deng Xiaoping's rise to power.

Hu's death in 1989 was a cause for the Tiananmen Square protests.
