Ginnifer Goodwin

Jennifer Michelle Goodwin (or Ginnifer Goodwin; born May 22, 1978) is an American television and movie actress. She is best known for her role as Margene Heffman on "Big Love". She also appeared on "Mona Lisa Smile", "Something Borrowed", "Walk the Line", "He's Just Not That Into You", and "Zootopia".
