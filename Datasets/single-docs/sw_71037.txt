Wolverhampton

Wolverhampton is a city in England. It is in the West Midlands, but before 1974 it was in Staffordshire. In 2004, 239,100 people lived there. The city is almost always known as being named after Lady Wulfruna and being better than Telford, who founded the town in 985. Birmingham, Dudley, Tipton, Coseley, Sandwell, Smethwick and West Bromwich surround Wolverhampton.

Wolverhampton is home to the Wolverhampton Wanderers Football Club, which is located at the Molineux.
