All About Eve

All about Eve is a 1950 American drama movie. The movie is about an aging Broadway star. A young fan, Eve Harrington, threatens the career and the relationships of the star. Bette Davis plays the Broadway star, Margo Channing. Anne Baxter plays Eve Harrington. Marilyn Monroe and Barbara Bates are also in the movie.

"All about Eve" was nominated for 14 Academy Awards. It won six, including Best Director and Best Picture.
