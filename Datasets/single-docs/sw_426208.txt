Bruce Almighty

Bruce Almighty is a 2003 American comedy movie starring Jim Carrey, Jennifer Aniston, and Morgan Freeman.

It is about a news journalist, Bruce Nolan, complaining about his life to God, so Nolan himself is offered the chance to become God himself and see his responsibilities. It was released on May 23, 2003 to mixed reviews. A spin-off movie was released entitled "Evan Almighty" and a sequel is in the works.
