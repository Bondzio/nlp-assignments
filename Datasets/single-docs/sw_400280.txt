Raquel Diaz Caro

Raquel Diaz Caro (born May 13, 1974 in Barcelona) is a track and field athlete from Spain. She has a disability: She is blind and a T11/B1 type athlete. She raced at the 1996 Summer Paralympics. She finished third in the 100 meter T11 race and fourth in 200 meter T11 race. She raced at the 2000 Summer Paralympics.
