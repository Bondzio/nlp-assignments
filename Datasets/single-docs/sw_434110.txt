Clear and Present Danger

Clear and Present Danger is a 1994 American spy movie. The movie is about agents in the CIA, FBI and murder around Washington, D.C.. Harrison Ford plays Jack Ryan. Anne Archer plays Cathy Ryan. Thora Birch plays Sally Ryan.

This movie was released August 3, 1994. The run time is 141 minutes. Its reviews were excellent. It made a lot of money.
