Kumaran Mahalingam

Kumaran Mahalingam (born 02 March 1981) is an Indian Geologist, Standup Paddler and a Conservation Educator. He holds three Limca book of Records for different feats in stand up paddling. He is also a certified wilderness first responder from National Outdoor Leadership School, USA.

Kumaran M was born on 02 March 1981 in Chennai. He is a Graduate in Geology from Presidency College, Chennai. Currently he works at Cairn India.
