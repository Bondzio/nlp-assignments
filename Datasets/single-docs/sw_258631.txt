Volkswagen Caddy

The Volkswagen Caddy is a van produced by Volkswagen in three generations since 1983.

The first generation Caddy were produced from 1983 until 1992. It was based on the Mk1 Golf.


The second generation Caddy were produced from 1995 until 2003. Two versions were available: a van based on the Mk3 Polo, and a pickup truck based on the Å koda Felicia.


The third generation were introduced in 2003. It is based on the Mk5 Golf.


The fourth generation will be launched in 2011. It is based on the Mk6 Golf.
