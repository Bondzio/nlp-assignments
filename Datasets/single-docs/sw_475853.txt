Charb

Stéphane Charbonnier (21 August 1967 – 7 January 2015), better known as Charb, was a French cartoonist, journalist, and caricaturist. He was best known for his work on the satirical newspaper "Charlie Hebdo".

Charb was born in Conflans-Sainte-Honorine.

Charb was among the victims who were killed in a shooting at "Charlie Hebdo"<nowiki>'</nowiki>s Paris headquarters on 7 January 2015. He was 47.
