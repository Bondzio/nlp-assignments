Veronica Lazăr

Veronica Lazăr (16 October 1938 - 8 June 2014) was a Romanian-Italian actress. She was known for her roles in "Last Tango in Paris" (1973), "La Luna" (1979), "The Sheltering Sky" (1990), and "Besieged" (1998).

She married Italian actor Adolfo Celi in 1966. They had two children, director Leonardo Celi and actress Alessandra Celi. Her last role was in "Me and You" (2012).
