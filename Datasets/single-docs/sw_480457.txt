Lili Taylor

Lili Anne Taylor (born February 20, 1967) is an American actress. She appeared in many independent movies. She was also in big budget movies including "Ransom" (1996) and "The Conjuring" (2013). She played Grace Stalker in "Arizona Dream". She played Jojo Barbosa in "Mystic Pizza" in 1988.

Taylor was born in Glencoe, Illinois.
