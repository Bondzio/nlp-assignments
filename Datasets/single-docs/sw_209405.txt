The Cosmos Rocks

The Cosmos Rocks is an album by Queen + Paul Rodgers. It was released on 15 September, 2008 in the Europe and on 28 October, 2008 in North America. The album has 14 songs written by Brian May, Paul Rodgers and Roger Taylor. It is the only studio the former Queen members recorded with Paul Rodgers. The album received mostly bad reviews and it did not sell very many copies.
