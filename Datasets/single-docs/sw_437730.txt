Liev Schreiber

Isaac Liev Schreiber (; born October 4, 1967) is an American actor, producer, director, and screenwriter. He was known for his role in "Scream", "Phantoms", "The Sum of All Fears", "", "Salt", "Taking Woodstock", and in "The Butler".

Schreiber was born on October 4, 1967 in San Francisco, California.
