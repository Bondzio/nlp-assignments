Silent Hill

Silent Hill is a horror video game series and there is a movie based on the same title. The games are mostly about bad or evil things that happen in the town called Silent Hill. The series is known for its monsters, especially Pyramid Head, and for its music.

There have been 8 main games and 4 spin-off games. There was also an HD Collection released with remastered ports of "Silent Hill 2" and "Silent Hill 3". A game titled "Silent Hills" was in development for the PlayStation 4. On April 27, 2015, it was confirmed by Konami that it had been cancelled.
