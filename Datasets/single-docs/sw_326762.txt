Phoebe Tonkin

Phoebe Jane Elizabeth Tonkin (born July 12, 1989 in Sydney, Australia) is an Australian actress
She is best known for portraying Cleo Sertori in "" and Fiona Maxwell in "Tomorrow, When the War Began". As of 2011, she portrays Faye Chamberlain in "The Secret Circle". 
She was nominated 2008 Australian Film Institute Awards Best Lead Actress in Television Drama for "".
