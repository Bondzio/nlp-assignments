Västerstrands AIK

Västerstrands AIK is a sports club in the town of Karlstad in Sweden. The club was founded on 11 October 1940. It currently only runs a women's bandy team.

The women's bandy team has won the Swedish national championship in the years of 1991, 1992, 1994, 1997, 2001, and 2002. It has also ended up as runner-up several times. The team colours are black and yellow.
