King of the Hill

King of the Hill was an American 30-minute animated cartoon that ran from 1997 to 2009 on FOX, and from 2010 on Adult Swim. It featured a husband, a wife, a son, and their live-in niece. FOX, the network running the series, would not be showing new episodes anymore after thirteen television season. Instead, Cartoon Network will show the series late at night.
