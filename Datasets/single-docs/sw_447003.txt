Transcendence (movie)

Transcendence is a 2014 American science fiction drama movie directed by Wally Pfister. It is produced by Christopher Nolan. It stars Johnny Depp, Rebecca Hall, Kate Mara, Cillian Murphy, Paul Bettany, and Morgan Freeman. 

Transcendence was released in New York City on April 10, 2014. It was released on April 18, 2014 in other cities and countries.
