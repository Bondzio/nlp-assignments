Ashley Cole

Ashley Cole (born 20 December 1980) is an English football player. He is notable for playing for England 77 times. 
He now plays for LA Galaxy as a defender in the left back position.

Cole married Cheryl Tweedy in July 2006. She divorced him in September 2010.
