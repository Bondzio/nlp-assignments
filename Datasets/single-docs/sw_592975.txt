Tomás Luis de Victoria

Tomás Luis de Victoria is a spanish composer of renaissance music, (1548 – 27 August 1611). was one of the most important composers of the Counter-Reformation, along with Giovanni Pierluigi da Palestrina and Orlando di Lasso. Victoria was not only a composer, but also an accomplished organist and singer as well as a Catholic priest. However, he preferred the life of a composer to that of a performer.
