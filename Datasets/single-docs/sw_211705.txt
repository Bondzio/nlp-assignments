Headphones

Headphones are electronic audio devices that people wear over their ears. They let people listen to things on a walkman, MP3 player, mobile phone or computer. Headphones come in many different sizes from big to small. They are also known as earphones, earbuds, stereophones, headsets or even cans.
Headphones typically uses small plug for connection.

There is a risk of hearing loss if listening to headphones with a high volume for a long time.
