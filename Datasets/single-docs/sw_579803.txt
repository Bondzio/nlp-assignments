Chrystia Freeland

Christina Alexandra "Chrystia" Freeland (born August 2, 1968 in Peace River, Alberta) is a Canadian politician of the Liberal Party of Canada. She has been foreign minister since 2017.

Freeland has been a member of parliament since 2013. From 2015 to 2017 she was also minister of trade.

Previously she was a freelance journalist. She has written several non-fiction books.
