Erika Slezak

Erika Slezak (born August 5, 1946) is a soap opera actress. She is strongly known because of her role as Victoria Lord in the television series "One Life to Live". She was born around California.

Slezak has won six Daytime Emmy Awards in the category of "Outstanding Lead Actress in a Drama Series". This is out of nine nominations for her role as Viki. Additionally, Slezak won a "Soap Opera Digest" Award for Favorite Couple with Mark Derwin in 2000.
