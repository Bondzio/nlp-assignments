El Chico Del Apartamento 512

"El Chico Del Apartamento 512" () is the sixth and final single released from Selena's grammy nominated album "Amor Prohibido" (1994). The song never had a music video because it was released one day before Selena was killed. The song had positive feedback from music critics. The song was performed on C minor with 102 beats per minute. The song was covered by many Latin American artists.
