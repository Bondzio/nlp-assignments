Amman

Amman, sometimes spelled Ammann (), is the capital city of Jordan. It is the official capital and commercial center of Jordan. It is the capital city of the Amman Governorate. It is the largest city in Jordan in terms of area and number of people. Amman has about 4 million people (around 42% of the number of people in Jordan in 2015).
