United States Hockey Hall of Fame

The United States Hockey Hall of Fame was made in 1973. The goal was to keep the rich history of ice hockey in the United States and to honor the extraordinary contributions of select players, coaches, administrators, officials and teams. It is in Eveleth, Minnesota. 

There is also The United States Hockey Hall of Fame Museum. The museum keeps special items related to American hockey.
