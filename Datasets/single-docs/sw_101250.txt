All We Know Is Falling

All We Know Is Falling is the first album of the band Paramore. It was recorded in just three weeks. In April 2006, the CD was released in the UK. In 2005, a Japanese version was released, with the bonus track "O, Star".



Paramore released three videos from this album. The first was for the single "All We Know", followed by "Pressure", and "Emergency".
