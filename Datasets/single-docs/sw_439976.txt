Bob L. Harris

Bob Lee Harris (October 11, 1922 - February 14, 2014) was an American actor, singer, and the father of Ed Harris. He was known for his roles in "Riders of the Purple Sage", "Pollock", and in "Appaloosa". He also appeared on television as Borklund in "American Playhouse".

Harris was born on October 11, 1922 in Walters, Oklahoma. He died on February 14, 2014 at the age of 91 from a long illness.
