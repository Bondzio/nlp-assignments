Werner Sombart

Werner Sombart (January 19, 1863â€“May 18, 1941) was a German economist and sociologist and was an important social scientist to Europe during the early 20th century.

Sombart was born in Ermsleben, Harz. He was the son of Anton Ludwig Sombart, a rich liberal politician, industrialist. 
Werner studied at the universities in Pisa, Berlin, and Rome, learning both law and economics. In 1888, he received his Ph.D. from Berlin.
