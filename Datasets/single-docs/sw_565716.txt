Wallington railway station

Wallington station opened in 1847. The trains for this station are operated by Southern (train operating company). This station is in fare zone 5.

There are two platforms for this station. Platform 1 is for trains towards Victoria station via Crystal Palace and Norbury. Platform 2 is for trains towards Sutton (Surrey), Epsom and Epsom Downs.
