Yoshi's New Island

Yoshi's New Island is a platform video game for the Nintendo 3DS game console. It is a part of the "Yoshi" series. It is the third game in the "" series. It is the sequel to "Yoshi's Island DS". It was released on March 14, 2014.

The gameplay of "Yoshi's New Island" is similar to the previous two games in the "Yoshi's Island" series. The player moves Yoshi through many stages. Yoshi has to get through the stages by not getting hit by enemies or losing the baby he is holding.
