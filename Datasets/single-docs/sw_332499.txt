Kim Jong-un

Kim Jong-un (born 8 January 1983) is the current leader of Democratic Republic of North Korea In December 2011, he became the leader of North Korea after his father, Kim Jong-il died on 17 December 2011. His grandfather, Kim Il-Sung was the first leader of North Korea.

Little is known about his personality and habits, apart from the fact that he likes basketball. He is married to Ri Sol-ju. They married in 2009. They had a daughter in 2010. Kim received his education in the West. He attended a state school in Switzerland from 1998 to 2000 posing as a diplomat's son named Pak Un. He is the world's youngest head of state.
