Gol Transportes Aéreos Flight 1907

Gol Transportes Aéreos Flight 1907, was flying from Manaus to Rio de Janeiro, the plane was a Boeing 737-800 with 154 people on-board. On September 29, 2006 the plane collided with a Embraer Legacy 600 while cruising over the Amazon. The Embraer landed safety at a military base with no loss to the seven people on-board, but the 737 crashed into the Amazon killing all 154 people on-board.
