Fé Sciarone

Félicité Louise Henriette "Fé" Sciarone (12 August 1922 – 8 April 2014) was a Dutch radio actress and comedienne. Her best known appearances include "Dood in de jungle" (1955), "Een merkwaardig geval van zielsverhuizing" (1969), "De generaal" (1976), "Dit is uw kans" (1979) and "Vijf gemaskerde mannen" (1982). She started her career in 1950 and retired in 1984. She was born in The Hague, South Holland.

Sciarone died on 8 April 2014 in Amsterdam, North Holland, aged 91.
