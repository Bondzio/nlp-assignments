Bobcat Goldthwait

Robert Francis "Bobcat" Goldthwait (born May 26, 1962) is an American actor, comedian, screenwriter, and director. He is known for his energetic stage personality and his gruff, high-pitched voice. Some movies that he has directed are "Sleeping Dogs Lie" (2006), "World's Greatest Dad" (2009) and "God Bless America" (2011). His voice acting credits include "Hercules" (1997) and "Leroy & Stitch" (2006). He has also had guest appearances on television shows like "MADtv", "Bob's Burgers", "Regular Show", "Adventure Time", "Fish Hooks", "Sabrina, the Teenage Witch", "Married... with Children" and "The Simpsons".

Goldthwait was born in Syracuse, New York.
