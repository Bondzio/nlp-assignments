The Grand Budapest Hotel

The Grand Budapest Hotel is a 2014 British-German-American comedy movie. It is about a caretaker who teams up with one of his employees to prove that he is innocent of murder. Ralph Fiennes plays the caretaker. Tilda Swinton plays Céline. Léa Seydoux plays plays Clotilde. Harvey Keitel plays Ludwig.

This movie has an R rating from the Motion Picture Association of America. The reviews of the movie were very positive.
