Olga Kurylenko

Olga Kurylenko (born 14 November 1979) is a French actress and model. She started acting in 2005. She became famous by playing Bond girl Camille Montes in the movie "Quantum of Solace". She also played Julia in the 2013 movie "Oblivion". 

Kurylenko was born in Berdyansk, Ukrainian SSR, Soviet Union.
