Oleg Bryjak

Oleg Bryzhak (October 27, 1960 â€“ March 24, 2015) was a Kazakhstani-German bass-baritone opera singer. He was born in Jezkazgan, Kazakh SSR.

Bryzhak had been a protodeacon in an Ukrainian Orthodox church in Krefeld.

Bryzhak died on March 24, 2015 along with fellow singer Maria Radner in the Germanwings Flight 9525 crash in France. He was returning from performances of Richard Wagner's "Siegfried" at the Gran Teatre del Liceu in Barcelona.
