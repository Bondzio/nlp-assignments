Gavin MacLeod

Gavin MacLeod (born Allan George See, February 28, 1931) is an American movie, stage, and television actor who is known for his role as Captain Merrill Stubing in "The Love Boat".

He was born in Mount Kisco, New York on February 28, 1931. He was married to Joan Devore from 1954 until they divorced in 1972, they had four children. He was married to Patti Kending from 1972 until they divorced in 1982 and remarried in 1985 and are still married.
