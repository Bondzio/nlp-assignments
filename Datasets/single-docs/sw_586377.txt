Molly Peters

Molly Peters (15 March 1942 â€“ 30 May 2017) was an English actress and model. She was born in Walsham-le-Willows, Suffolk, England. She was best known for her role in the James Bond movie "Thunderball" as Patricia Fearing. Her career lasted from 1964 through 1967.

Peters appeared in "Playboy", in the November 1965 issue. Her appearance was as part of a photo essay entitled "James Bond's Girls", by Richard Maibaum.

On 30 May 2017, Peters died at the age of 75 from complications of a stroke in London, England.
