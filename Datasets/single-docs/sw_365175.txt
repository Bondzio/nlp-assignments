Josh Brolin

Josh James Brolin (born February 12, 1968) is an American movie, television, stage, and voice actor. He is known for his roles in; "W.", "No Country for Old Men", "Milk", "True Grit", and in "Men in Black 3".

Brolin was born on February 12, 1968 in Santa Monica, California to James Brolin and to Jane Cameron Agee. He was raised in Corpus Christi, Texas. 

Brolin was married to Alice Adair from 1988 until they divorced in 1992. He has been married to Diane Lane since 2004. Brolin has two children from Adair. Lane filed for divorced in February 2013.
