Melun Villaroche Aerodrome

Melun Villaroche Aerodrome () is an aerodrome north of Melun. Melun is a commune in the Seine-et-Marne department in the Île-de-France region in north-central France.

The airport is east-southeast of Moissy-Cramayel and southeast of Paris. A campus of the "École nationale de l'aviation civile" (French civil aviation university) is also in the aerodrome.
