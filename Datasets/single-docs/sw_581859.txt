Cecilia Alvear

Cecilia Alvear (November 5, 1939 â€“ April 21, 2017) was a Ecuadorian-American journalist in television news. She was the former President of the National Association of Hispanic Journalists. She was known as a Field Producer with NBC Network News in Burbank, California. In 1995 and 1996, Alvear was Editor at Large of "Si", a short-lived magazine depicting the Latino experience in the U.S.

Alvear died of breast cancer on April 21, 2017 in Los Angeles, California at the age of 77.
