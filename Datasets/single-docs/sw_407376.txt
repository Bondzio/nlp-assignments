The Right Stuff

The Right Stuff is a 1983 movie. It is about astronauts and the military in 1947. Dennis Quaid, Barbara Hershey and Lance Henriksen are in the cast. "The Right Stuff" got positive reviews from the movie critics. The movie got limited release in the United States in October 1983 and nationwide on February 17, 1984. The movie performed well in the box office.
