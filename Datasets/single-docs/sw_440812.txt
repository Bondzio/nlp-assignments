Alexander Payne

Alexander Payne (born Constantine Alexander Payne; February 10, 1961) is an American movie director, screenwriter, and producer. He is best known for his movies "Election" (1999), "About Schmidt" (2002), "Sideways" (2004), "The Descendants" (2011), and "Nebraska" (2013).

Alexander Payne was born in Omaha, Nebraska. He married Canadian actress Sandra Oh in 2003. They divorced in 2006.
