Poltergeist (movie)

Poltergeist is a 1982 American horror movie set in Orange County, California. It was directed by Tobe Hooper. It stars Craig T. Nelson, JoBeth Wiliams, Beatrice Straight, Dominique Dunne, Oliver Robins and Heather O'Rourke.

It was followed by two sequels: "" and "Poltergeist III". It was remade in 2015.
