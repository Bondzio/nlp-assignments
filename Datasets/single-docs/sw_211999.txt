Watan Hamara Azad Kashmir

Watan hamara Kashmir is considered to be the official regional state song for Greater Kashmir, adopted by Azad Kashmir as a state anthem. It was written in the mid-1960s by Abu-Al-Asar Hafeez Jullundhri. It is inspired by the conflict of Indo-Pakistani War of 1965. The first few lines are official recognised by the Kashmiris in Muzaffarabad.
