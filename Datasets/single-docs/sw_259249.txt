Cliff Curtis

Clifford Vivian Devon "Cliff" Curtis (born July 27, 1968 in Rotorua, New Zealand) is a New Zealand actor. Curtis has been in many different movies as well as television shows. Curtis started acting musicals like "Fiddler on the Roof" and "Man of La Mancha". Curtis made his big screen debut in the Oscar-nominated film, "The Piano".

Curtis was born to an amateur dancer and is one of nine children. Curtis is married. He has two children.
