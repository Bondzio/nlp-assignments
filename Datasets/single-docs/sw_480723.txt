Cheek to Cheek

Cheek to Cheek is a collaborative album between American pop singer-songwriter Lady Gaga and American jazz artist Tony Bennett. The album won the 2015 Grammy Award for Best Traditional Pop Vocal Album. It was Gaga's sixth Grammy win. The album debuted at number one on the US "Billboard" 200 chart. It was Gaga's third consecutive number-one album in the United States.
