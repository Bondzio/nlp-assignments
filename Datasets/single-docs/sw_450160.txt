Lakewood, Ohio

Lakewood is a city in Ohio, near Cleveland. It is in the northeastern area of the state. In the 2010 United States Census, 52,131 people lived there.

Lakewood is led by a mayor and a city council. The people in Lakewood vote to choose the mayor and city council. There are seven people in the city council.

Lakewood is about 6 miles (9.7 km) west of Cleveland. The city has a total area of 6.69 square miles (17.33 km).

Famous people from Lakewood include Teri Garr.
