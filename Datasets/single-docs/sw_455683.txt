Seoul Metropolitan Subway

Seoul Metropolitan Subway, often called Seoul Subway, is a rail transit system of subways and commuter trains that connects Seoul and suburban areas in its vicinity.

The system is consisted of 9 numeric-named lines (Line 1 ~ Line 9), Gyeongui Line, Bundang Line, Shinbundang Line, A'REX, Jungang Line, Gyeongchun Line, Suin Line, Incheon Line 1, Everline and U Line.

Since 2010, all stations have smartphone like 46-inch touchscreen kiosk, so that passengers can look at the map, make free phone calls, or get tourist information. All lines use special RFID cards, such as T-money, or NFC technology to impose traveling fee.
