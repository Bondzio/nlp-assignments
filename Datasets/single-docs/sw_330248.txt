Donna Reed

Donna Reed (born Donna Belle Mullenger, January 27, 1921 - January 14, 1986) was an American actress. She was born in Denison, Iowa. 

Reed had a major role in "It's a Wonderful Life". She won an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress for her role in "From Here to Eternity". She won a Golden Globe Award for Best TV Star - Female for her central role in "The Donna Reed Show".

She died of pancreatic cancer in Beverly Hills, California.
