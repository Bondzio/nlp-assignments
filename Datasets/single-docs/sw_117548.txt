Rosario Dawson

Rosario Dawson (born May 9, 1979) is an American actress and singer. Her first movie was "Kids" in 1995. Since then, she has become known for her roles in "Men in Black II" (2002), "25th Hour" (2002), "The Rundown" (2003), "Alexander" (2004), "Sin City" (2005), "Death Proof" (2007), "Eagle Eye" (2008), "Unstoppable" (2010), "César Chávez" (2013), "Trance" (2013), "The Lego Batman Movie" (2017).
