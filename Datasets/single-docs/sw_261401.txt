Smokey and the Bandit

Smokey and the Bandit is a 1977 action-comedy movie that is produced by Mort Engelberg, directed by Hal Needham and starred Burt Reynolds, Sally Field, Jackie Gleason, Jerry Reed and Mike Henry. 

Smokey and the Bandit spawned two sequels: "Smokey and the Bandit II" and "Smokey and the Bandit 3".
