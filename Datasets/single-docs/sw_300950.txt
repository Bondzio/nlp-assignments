Robin Hood (roller coaster)

Robin Hood is a wooden roller coaster at Walibi Holland in the Netherlands.

Robin Hood was designed by "Stand Company" and built by Vekoma in 2000. It has a capacity of 815 people per hour. The coaster has 2 trains, both having 4 cars with 6 seats (per train 24 people). A ride takes about 2.5 minutes and the coaster is about 1 kilometer long.
