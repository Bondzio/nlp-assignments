Hans Breukhoven

Free Record Shop had hundreds of stores in the Netherlands, Belgium and Luxembourg. He was born in Rotterdam, South Holland.

Breukhoven died from pancreatic cancer on 20 January 2017 in London, United Kingdom. He was 70.
