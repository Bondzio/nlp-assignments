Anne Aasheim

Anne Aasheim (22 April 1962 â€“ 30 March 2016) was a Norwegian editor. She was born in Porsgrunn, Norway. She started her journalist career in the neighboring city's largest newspaper "Varden" in the late 1970s. She worked in "Dagen" and "Bergens Arbeiderblad" before being hired in NRK Hordaland in 1988.

Aasheim died of lung cancer in Oslo, Norway on 30 March 2016, aged 54.
