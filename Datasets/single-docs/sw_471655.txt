Florinda Meza

Florinda Meza García (born 8 February 1949) is a Mexican actress, television producer, and screenwriter. She is best known as Doña Florinda in "El Chavo del Ocho", La Chimoltrufia in "Chespirito", and other various roles in "El Chapulín Colorado".

Meza was married to Roberto Gómez Bolaños from 2004 until his death in 2014.
