A Million Ways to Die in the West

A Million Ways to Die in the West is an American western-comedy movie. It is about Arizona in 1882, when a sheep herder loses his girlfriend, then gets another woman. 

Seth MacFarlane plays Albert, Liam Neeson plays Clinch, Charlize Theron plays Anna, Sarah Silverman plays Ruth, and Neil Patrick Harris plays Foy. Christopher Lloyd, Ewan McGregor, Gilbert Gottfried, and Bill Maher have cameos in the movie.

The movie has an R rating from the MPAA. It was released on May 30, 2014.
