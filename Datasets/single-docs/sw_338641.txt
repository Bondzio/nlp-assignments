Turkmen language

Turkmen (, Cyrillic: , Persian: ) is the national language of Turkmenistan. It is spoken by about 7,000,000 people in Turkmenistan, and by an additional approximately 380,000 in northwestern Afghanistan and 500,000 in northeastern Iran.

The leading Turkmen poet is Magtymguly Pyragy, who wrote in the 1700's.
