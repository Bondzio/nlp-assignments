Magda Amo Rius

Magda Amo Rius (born July 23, 1973 in Barcelona) is a track and field athlete and a ski athlete from Spain. She has a disability: she cannot see. She is a B2 type skier. She ski raced at the 1992 Winter Paralympics. She came in third in the giant slalom race. She ski raced at the 1994 Winter Paralympics. She came in second in the downhill race. She jumped at the 1996 Summer Paralympics. She jumped the most long in the long jump. She ski raced at the 1998 Winter Paralympics. She finished first in the Giant, Super Giant, Slalom and Downhill races.
