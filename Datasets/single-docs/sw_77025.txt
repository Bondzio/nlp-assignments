Ashley Massaro

Ashley Marie Massaro (born May 26 1979), better known simply as Ashley, is an American pictorial model and professional wrestler, best known for her time with World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE) while wrestling on its "Raw" brand. She was the winner of the Raw Diva Search 2005 and was on the cover of the April 2007 issue of "Playboy Magazine". She was a contestant on the of "Survivor" in China, but was the second contestant to be eliminated.
