Johan van Hulst

Johan Wilhelm van Hulst (born January 28, 1911) is a Dutch retired politician. He served as Member of the Senate from July 3, 1956 until June 10, 1981. He was the Parliamentary leader of the Christian Historical Union in the Senate from December 10, 1968 until June 8, 1977 when the Christian Historical Union merged into the Christian Democratic Appeal. 

Van Hulst is also a chess player and a former academic.
