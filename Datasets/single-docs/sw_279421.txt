Honda Odyssey

The Honda Odyssey is a minivan that has been made by Honda since 1994. From 1994-1999 it was also sold as the Isuzu Oasis. A larger version of the minivan has been available since 1999 in North America. It was redesigned in 2005 and 2011. The 2015 model was awarded the Insurance Institute for Highway Safety's top rated pick because of it's collision avoidance system, hardly any injuries.
