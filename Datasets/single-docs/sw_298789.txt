Ksenia Khairova

Ksenia Leonidovna Khairova (, born Talyzina (); March 29, 1969) is a Soviet and Russian stage and movie actress.

Ksenia Leonidovna Talyzina was born in Moscow on March 29, 1969. Her parents divorced shortly after her birth.

She went school with in-depth study of languages.

In 1990 she graduated GITIS (Eugeny Lazarev's rate).

Her first role was a small part in the movie "Nikolai Vavilov".

She is actress TSATRA.

She now acts mostly in series.

She divorced Khairov. They have a daughter, Anastasia Talyzina (born in 1999).
