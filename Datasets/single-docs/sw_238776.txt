Joey Logano

Joseph Thomas Logano (born May 27, 1990) is an American racecar driver who drives the No. 22 Shell / Pennzoil Ford Fusion in the Sprint Cup Series for Penske Racing. Until the start of the 2013 season, Logano drove the #20 Home Depot Toyota Camry for Joe Gibbs Racing. Logano won the 2009 rookie of the year award, and with his win at the New Hampshire Motor Speedway became the youngest drive to win a race in NASCAR history. He also won the 2012 Pocono 400 presented by #NASCAR at Pocono Raceway from the pole, ending a 31 race losing streak for pole winners.
