Clara Blandick

Clara Blandick (June 4, 1880 - April 15, 1962) was an American actress. She acted in stage plays and movies. She played Auntie Em in the movie "The Wizard of Oz". She also did many other movies. She was raised in Boston. She moved to New York City for her acting career. Blandick committed suicide at age 81 using sleeping pills.

Later information indicates birth year 1876.
