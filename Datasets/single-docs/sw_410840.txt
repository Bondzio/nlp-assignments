Richard Erdman

Richard "Dick" Erdman (born June 1, 1925) is an American actor, movie director and movie producer. He was known for his roles in "Stalag 17", "Cry Danger", "The Men", and in "Anything Goes". He also appeared as Leonard Rodriguez in "Community".

Erdman was born on June 1, 1925 in Enid, Oklahoma. He was married to Leza Holland from 1948 until they divorced in 1950. He has been married to Sharon Randall since 1953. They had one daughter, Erica who died in 2010.
