Rod Liddle

Rod Liddle is an English journalist. 

Liddle was born in Abbey Wood, London to a train driver. He was brought up in Nunthorpe from the age of eight. At 16 he was a member of the Socialist Workers Party. He stayed a member for about a year. 

Liddle met Rachel Royce at the BBC in 1993. In January 2004 Liddle married her in Malaysia.
