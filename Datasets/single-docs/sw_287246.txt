Spyro 2: Ripto's Rage!

Spyro 2: Ripto's Rage! (known as Spyro 2: Gateway to Glimmer in Europe and Australia and Spyro X Sparx Tondemo Tours in Japan) is a platform video game that was published by Sony Computer Entertainment. It was released for the PlayStation video game system on October 31, 1999 in North America, on November 5, 1999 in PAL region and on March 16, 2000 in Japan.

The game got a 8.6 out of 10 from GameSpot and a 8.8 out of 10 from IGN. 

It is the second game in the "Spyro" video game series.
