Fetty Wap

Willie Maxwell II (born June 7, 1991), better known by his stage name, Fetty Wap, is an American rapper.

He became known for his 2014 single "Trap Queen" which was able to peak at #2 on the US "Billboard" Hot 100 chart in May 2015 and helped him gain a record deal with 300 Entertainment.

Wap released his first self-titled studio album on September 25, 2015. The album featured the single "Trap Queen", "679", "My Way", and "Again". The album debuted at #1 on the US "Billboard" 200. Wap was nominated for two Grammy Awards in the categories of Best Rap Performance and Best Rap Song at the 2016 Grammy Awards.

Maxwell had glaucoma in both eyes as a child. In a 2015 interview, he said that doctors were only able to save his right eye and gave him a glass eye.
