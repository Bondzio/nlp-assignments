The Only Easy Day Was Yesterday

The Only Easy Day Was Yesterday is an EP from the American rock band 12 Stones. It was released on July 20, 2010 by Wind-up Records and debuted at #103 on the Billboard 200, and it peaked at #5 in the Christian albums chart.

At first, the band was going to release a full album but due to their record label, they were forced to release an EP. It's a 5 track EP which has the single "We Are One". "We Are One" was also the entrance theme for the WWE group, The Nexus.
