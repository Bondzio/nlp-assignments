Kim Seok-ju

Kim Seok-ju(김석주, 金錫冑, 1634 - September 20 1684) was Korean Joseon Dynastys Politicians, Confucian scholars, writers. elder cousin of Queen Myungsung and paternal first cousin once removed of 19th king Sukjong. a penname was Shigam(식암, 息庵) and Chinese style name was Sabaik(사백, 斯百).
