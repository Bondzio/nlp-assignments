ChristianUnion

The ChristianUnion, Dutch: "ChristenUnie" (shortened "CU"), is a Dutch political party.

The ChristianUnion is an orthodox Christian democratic party.

It was established in 2001 as a merger of the Reformed Political Alliance (GPV) and the Reformatory Political Federation (RPF).

Political leader is Gert-Jan Segers.

Number of seats in the 2017 elections: 5 (/).
