ESCP Europe

ESCP Europe Business School is a European higher studies establishment. The school is in 6 places, in Paris, France, in the United Kingdom (London), in Spain (Madrid), in Italy (Torino), in Poland (Warsaw), and in Germany (Berlin), where it is a university. It is the oldest business school in the world established in 1819. and therefore presents itself as ""The World's First Business School"".

ESCP Europe is specialist in the fields of economics, social sciences and management.
