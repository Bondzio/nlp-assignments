Arte Johnson

Arthur Stanton Eric "Arte" Johnson (born January 20, 1929) is a American retired comedian and television, movie, and voice actor. His career has spanned for nearly 50 years. His career started in 1954 up until his retirement in 2005. Johnson was a regular cast in "Rowan & Martin's Laugh-In". His well-known "character" was that of a German soldier with his famous the catchphrase: "Verrrry interesting, but...['stupid', 'not very funny', and other versions]". He has won a Saturn Award.

Johnson was born in Benton Harbor, Michigan. Johnson was educated at the University of Illinois. Johnson is currently married to Gisela Johnson since 1968.
