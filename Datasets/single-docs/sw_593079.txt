Junkie XL

Antonius Tom Holkenborg (born 8 December 1967), known by his stage name Junkie XL and occasionally JXL, is a Dutch multi-instrumentalist, composer, DJ, producer, and engineer. Originally known for his trance productions, he has moved to producing electronica and big beat music and film scores in recent years. Junkie XL is probably best known for his remix of Elvis Presley's "A Little Less Conversation".

In movie scores, he is perhaps known for his work in the DC Extended Universe beginning with "" with Hans Zimmer, contributing to the theme song for "Wonder Woman" and will compose the score of "Justice League" with Danny Elfman.

Junkie XL was born in Lichtenvoorde, Gelderland, Netherlands.
