Usnija Redžepova

Usnija Redžepova (; 4 February 1946 – 1 October 2015) was a Macedonian actress and singer. She appeared in the movies "Zenidba nosaca Samuela" (1973), "The Dervish and Death" (1974) and "Kostana" (1980). She was given a national pension from the Serbian government on 29 December 2011.

Redžepova was born in Skopje. She was of Romani and Turkish ancestry. She died from lung cancer in Belgrade, aged 69.
