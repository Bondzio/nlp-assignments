Clarence Williams III

Clarence Williams III (born August 21, 1939) is an American actor. He is best known for his role as "Linc Hayes" on the iconic "hippie" cop show "The Mod Squad" (1968-1973). He was nominated for a Tony Award. His latest appearance was in the 2013 movie "The Butler".

Williams III was born on August 21, 1939 in New York City, New York.
