Love Games: Bad Girls Need Love Too

Love Games: Bad Girls Need Love Too is a reality television show. It first premiered on Oxygen on March 16, 2010. Its a spin-off show from the "Bad Girls Club". American comedian Bret Ernst was the host of the first season. The show was renewed for its third season Second season of "Bad Girls Club" Tanisha Thomas began hosting the season starting with the second season. There has been three complete seasons of "Love Games: Bad Girls Need Love Too"'. The show is about past season "bad girls" looking for love.
