CrazySexyCool: The TLC Story

CrazySexyCool: The TLC Story is a television movie about the R&B/hip hop group TLC. The title comes from the group's second album, "CrazySexyCool". It aired on VH1 on October 21, 2003.

The film got 4.5 million viewers when it aired on VH1, making it the highest-rated television movie of 2013, and the highest-rated television movie in VH1 history.
