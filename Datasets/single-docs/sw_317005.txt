Amy Poehler

Amy Meredith Poehler (; born September 16, 1971) is an American actress, comedian, director, producer and writer. She was a cast member of the television program "Saturday Night Live" between 2001 and 2008.

She studied at Boston College and at The Second City.

Poehler has been married to Will Arnett since 2003. They have two children. In September 2012, Poehler and Arnett separated.
