Marcos Francisco Dueñas Jimeno

Marcos Francisco Dueñas Jimeno (born April 9, 1978 in Granada) is a track and field athlete from Spain. He has a disability: He is missing part of a leg and is a T44 type athlete. He is a physiotherapist. He raced at the 1996 Summer Paralympics. He raced at the 2000 Summer Paralympics. He finished second in the 4 X 400 meter T42-T46 race.
