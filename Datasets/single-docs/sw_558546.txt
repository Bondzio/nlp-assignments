List of Garja gods

In Bodo language 'garja' means 'god' or 'deity'. The garja are propitiated at a place which is away from home. 
1. Lakhi garja
2. Sew garja
3. Duaramari garja
4. Tulsing-Mulsing garja
5. Sindu garja
6. Thong-thongali garja
7. Thia garja
8. Daoang garja
9. Batho garja
10. Soka garja
11. Ihew garja
12. Phew garja
13. Maya garja
14. Dhan khuber garja
15. Shaya garja
16. Borai raja garja
17. Dano garja
18. Gola kata garja
