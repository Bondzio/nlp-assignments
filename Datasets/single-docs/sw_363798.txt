Richard Riehle

Richard Riehle (born May 12, 1948) is an American movie, television, voice, and stage actor who is known for his roles in "", "The Man from Earth", "Treasure Buddies", "Santa Buddies", "Office Space", and "Chillerama".

Riehle was born on May 12, 1948 in Menomonee Falls, Wisconsin and studied at the University of Notre Dame and at the University of Minnesota.
