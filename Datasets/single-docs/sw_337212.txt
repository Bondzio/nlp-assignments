Child's Play

Child's Play is a 1988 American horror movie set in Chicago. It was directed by Tom Holland. It stars Catherine Hicks, Chris Sarandon, Alex Vincent and Brad Dourif. The movie is about a serial killer doll. It received moderate success upon release, and has gained cult following with the fans of horror. A remake was discussed and is now slated for 2012 or 2013 release.
