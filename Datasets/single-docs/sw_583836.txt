Arthur Moulin

Arthur Moulin (4 July 1924 â€“ 9 May 2017) was a French politician. He served as a member of the National Assembly from 1958 to 1973, representing Nord. He also served as a member of the French Senate from 1983 to 1992, representing Nord. He was the mayor of Avesnes in Pas-de-Calais from 1971 to 1989. He was born Saint-Aubin, Nord, France.

Moulin died in Avesnes, France on 9 May 2017 at the age of 92.
