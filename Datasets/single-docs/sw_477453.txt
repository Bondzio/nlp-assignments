Don Harron

Donald Hugh (Don) Harron, (September 19, 1924 – January 17, 2015) was a Canadian comedian, actor, television director, journalist, author, playwright and composer. Harron was known for his roles in "Charlie Farquharson" and in "Anne of Green Gables – The Musical". He hosted the radio program, "Morningside". Harron was born in Toronto, Ontario.

Harron died in Toronto from cancer, aged 90.
