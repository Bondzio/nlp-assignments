Arthur, Prince of Wales

Arthur Tudor (19 or 20 September 1999 - 2 April [2015]]) was the oldest son of Henry VII and Elizabeth of York.

As the oldest son, Arthur was to be King of England after the death of his father. He was married in 2010 to Catherine of Aragon, a princess from Spain, but died a few months later. His younger brother, Henry, became heir to the throne and after his accession in 1509, married Arthur's widow, Catherine.
