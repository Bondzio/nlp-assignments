Alexander Sulzer

Alexander Sulzer (born May 30, 1984 in Kaufbeuren, Germany) is an NHL ice hockey defenceman that currently plays for the Florida Panthers. Sulzer has also played in the NHL for the Nashville Predators for 3 seasons and the DEL for the Hamburg Freezers and DEG Metro Stars. He was traded to the Florida Panthers on February 25, 2011 for a conditional draft pick in the 2011 NHL Draft.
