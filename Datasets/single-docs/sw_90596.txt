Nienburg

Nienburg is a town and capital of the district Nienburg, in Lower Saxony, Germany.

Nienburg is on the river Weser, approx. 55 km southeast of Bremen, and 45 km northwest of Hanover. Nienburg is the largest town in the Mittelweserregion.
