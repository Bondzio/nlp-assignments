Vince Callahan

Vincent Francis "Vince" Callahan Jr. (October 30, 1931 â€“ September 20, 2014) was an American politician. He served for 40 years as a member of the Virginia House of Delegates. 

From January 1968 to January 2008, he represented the 34th district in the Virginia House of Delegates. At the time of his retirement, he was the longest-serving Republican in the Virginia General Assembly.

On September 20, 2014, Callahan died of West Nile virus at the age of 82.
