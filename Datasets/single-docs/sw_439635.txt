Per Theodor Haugen

Per Theodor Haugen (born 8 October 1932) is a Norwegian actor. He started stage acting in 1950. He had a supporting role in the comedy movie "Støv på hjernen" (1959).

He was born in Kongsberg. He was married to actress Sissel Sellæg who died on 5 January 2014 in Oslo. They had a son together, actor Kim Haugen.
