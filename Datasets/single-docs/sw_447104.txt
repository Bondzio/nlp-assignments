Karpal Singh

Karpal Singh s/o Ram Singh (28 June 1940 - 17 April 2014) was a Malaysian lawyer and politician. He served as a Member of Parliament of Bukit Gelugor. He served as a MP from March 2004 until his death in April 2014. Singh was a member of the Democratic Action Party. He was born in George Town, Penang.

Singh died at the age of 73 in a car accident near Gopeng, Perak.
