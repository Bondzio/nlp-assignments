Alan Tudyk

Alan Wray Tudyk (born March 16, 1971) is an American movie, television, stage, and voice actor who is known for his roles as Hoban "Wash" Washburne in "Firefly", Doc Potter in "", and as Dutch in "". He did the voice of the scimitar cat Lenny from the movie "Ice Age". He was the voice of the Candy King in the 2012 animated movie "Wreck-It Ralph".

Tudyk was born on March 16, 1971 in El Paso, Texas. Tudyk was raised in Plano, Texas. He studied at Plane Senior High School, Lon Morris High School, and at the Juilliard School.
