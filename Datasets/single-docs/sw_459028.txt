Freddie Prinze, Jr.

Freddie James Prinze, Jr. (; born March 8, 1976), is an American actor, filmmaker and television producer and director. 

Prinze, Jr. was born in Los Angeles, California. He was raised Roman Catholic.

He is known for his roles in "I Know What You Did Last Summer" (1997) and its sequel, "I Still Know What You Did Last Summer" (1998), as well as "She's All That" (1999), "Summer Catch" (2001), "Scooby-Doo" (2002), "" (2004), and "Delgo" (2008).
