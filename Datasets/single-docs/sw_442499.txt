Robby Müller

Robby Müller (born 4 April 1940) is a Dutch cinematographer. He has worked with director Wim Wenders. He is known for his works in "To Live and Die in LA", Sally Potter's "The Tango Lesson", "My Brother Tom", "Breaking the Waves" and "Dancer in the Dark".

Müller was born in Willemstad, Curaçao.
