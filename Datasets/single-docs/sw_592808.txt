D. L. Menard

Doris Leon "D. L." Menard (April 14, 1932 â€“ July 27, 2017) was an American singer-songwriter and guitarist in contemporary Cajun music. He was called the "Cajun Hank Williams." He was born in Erath, Louisiana. In 2010, his album Happy Go Lucky was nominated for a Grammy Award in the Best Zydeco or Cajun music album category.

Menard died on July 27, 2017 in Scott, Louisiana of a heart attack at the age of 85.
