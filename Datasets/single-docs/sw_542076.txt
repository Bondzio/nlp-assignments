Madeleine LeBeau

Marie Madeleine Berthe LeBeau (10 June 1923 – 1 May 2016) was a French movie actress. She was known for her roles in "Hold Back the Dawn", "Gentleman Jim", "Casablanca", "Paris After Dark", and "8½".

LeBeau died on 1 May 2016 in Estepona, Spain, after breaking her femur in a fall, aged 92.
