Peter Andre

Peter Andre (born Peter James Andrea on 27 February 1973) is a British-Australian singer and television personality.

Andre was born in Brent, north-west London to Greek Cypriot parents. He grew up in London until moving to the Gold Coast, Queensland, Australia when he was six. 

His first single was released in 1992 and his first album in 1993.

He and Katie Price were contestants in series 3 of the British version of "I'm a Celebrity ... Get Me Out of Here!" from January-February 2004. He and Price began a relationship during that time. Andre and Price had a son in 2005. The couple married later that year and their daughter was born in 2007. The couple divorced in 2009. Andre is in a relationship with Emily MacDonagh. The couple's daughter was born in January 2014.
