La Grange began working as a music critic in 1952, writing articles for the "New York Herald Tribune" and "The New York Times", and the magazines "Opera News", "Saturday Review", "Musical America", and "Opus".

La Grange died in Paris on 27 January 2017 from complications of pneumonia, aged 92.
