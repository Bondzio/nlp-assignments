Amazon Echo

Amazon Echo is a voice command device. It is a product of Amazon.com. The product can play music and answers questions. Amazon Echo is 9-inch tall. It is a cylinder-shaped device. It responds to the name "Alexa". This can be changed to another name.

Amazon Echo was released on November 6, 2014 through Amazon.com. Many critics gave it positive reviews. Gizmodo believed that Amazon Echo is "the most innovative device Amazon made in years." Many critics believe Amazon Echo is similar to Apple Inc.'s Siri. Amazon Echo is the second device released by Amazon.com in 2014, behind Amazon Fire TV.
