Waiting for Tonight

"Waiting for Tonight" is a song by Jennifer Lopez. It is from her first album, "On the 6". The song was released in 1999. It is a cover of a song that was originally recorded by a girl group called 3rd Party in 1997. Critics liked Lopez' version. It was more of a house version than Eurodance.
