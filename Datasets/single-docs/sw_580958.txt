Morgan Woodward

Thomas Morgan Woodward (born September 16, 1925) is an American actor. Woodward is best known for his returning role on the soap opera "Dallas" as Marvin "Punk" Anderson. He also played the silent Boss Godfrey (the Walking Boss) in "Cool Hand Luke" (1967), and has the most guest appearances on "Gunsmoke". His career began in 1956.

Woodward guest starred in two different episodes of the original series of "" as two different characters.

Woodward was born in Fort Worth, Texas. He studied at the University of Texas at Austin and at the University of Texas at Arlington. He retired from acting in 1998. Woodward lives in a ranch in Paso Robles, California.
