Pope Gregory III

Pope Gregory III (; died 28 November 741) was an Syrian priest of the Roman Catholic Church and the 90th Pope from 11 February 731 to 28 November 741.

Gregory was born in Syria, but the date of his birth is not known. 

Gregory was elected pope in 731. During his reign, the issue of iconoclasm or image worship was an important controvery.

After the reign of Gregory III, there were no other non-European popes until Pope Francis in 2013.
