Bugatti

Bugatti is a French supercar company currently owned by Volkswagen. They made cars all the way back in the early 1900s. They made cars such as the Bugatti Veyron. Unlike the regular Veyron, with 300 produced, there are only 20 supersport versions, which currently holds the record for world's fastest car. All Bugattis have a 16-cylinder engine and all cost between $1.8 million to $2.2 million (not counting the extras).
