Omar Torrijos

Omar Efrain Torrijos Herrera leader of Panama from 1968 to 1981. He is more commonly known as Omar Torrijos.

Torrijos was the Commander of the Military of Panama. Even though Panama had a President, Torrijos was the leader. He is best known for making a treaty giving Panama full control of the Panama Canal.
