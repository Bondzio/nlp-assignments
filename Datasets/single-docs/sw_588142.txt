Geoffrey Rowell

Douglas Geoffrey Rowell (13 February 1943 â€“ 11 June 2017) was an Anglican bishop. He served as Bishop of Basingstoke and then as the third Bishop in Europe until his retirement on 8 November 2013. Following his retirement he ministered as an assistant bishop in the Diocese of Chichester (from 2013) and in the Diocese of Portsmouth (from 2015). 

Rowell died in the early morning of Trinity Sunday, 11 June 2017, in Worth, West Sussex, aged 74.
