Kia Forte

The Kia Forte is a compact car that is built by Kia Motors. The Kia Forte has had two generations, the newest one in 2014. In some places like Costa Rica, Australia and Brazil, the Forte is known as the Kia Cerato, which replaces its former car of the same name. The car is known as the Kia Cerato Forte in Colombia and Singapore and is known as the Naza Forte in Naza of Malaysia where it has been assembled since 2009.
