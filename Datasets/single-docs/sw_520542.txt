Greg Garcia

Gregory Thomas Garcia (born April 4, 1970) is a American television producer and screenwriter. He was born in Arlington County, Virginia. He was the creator and producer of the comedy series, <nowiki>Yes, Dear</nowiki>. He was the creator and executive producer of "My Name Is Earl". He also worked for the series "Family Matters". He was the consultant in "Family Guy". He produced the Fox series "Raising Hope". He left that series in 2013.
