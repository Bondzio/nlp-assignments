Mercedes-Benz T2

The Mercedes-Benz T2 (Transporter 2) is a series of vans and step-entrance minibuses produced by Daimler-Benz in two generations from 1967-1996.

The first generation T2 was launched in 1967 and it was available as a box van, a flat bed light truck or a minibus.

The second generation T2 was introduced in 1986, and was built until 1996. 

The T2 is still built in Argentina as the L 710 and the LO 915

In 1996, the T2 was replaced by the Mercedes-Benz Vario.
