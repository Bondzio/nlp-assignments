Revolution (Six Flags Magic Mountain)

Revolution (previous names "La Revoluci√≥n" and "Great American Revolution") is a steel roller coaster at Six Flags Magic Mountain in California. It was built in 1976 by Anton Schwarzkopf and it was one of the first roller coasters with a looping. The track is 3,457 feet long (1,054 meter) and has a maximum speed of 55 miles per hour (89 km/h). One ride takes 2 minutes and 12 seconds and the coaster can process 1400 people per hour.
