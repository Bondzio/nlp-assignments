Téa Leoni

Elizabeth Téa Pantaleoni (born February 25, 1966), better known by her stage name Téa Leoni, is an American actress. She has starred many movies. These include: "Bad Boys", "Deep Impact", "The Family Man", "Jurassic Park III", "Fun with Dick and Jane", "Spanglish", "Ghost Town" and "Tower Heist".

Leoni separated from her husband, David Duchovny, in 2011.
