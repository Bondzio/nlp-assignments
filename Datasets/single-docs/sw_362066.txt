Marv Albert

Marv Albert (born Marvin Philip Aufrichtig; June 12, 1941) is an American sportscaster. He is a member of the Basketball Hall of Fame. He is commonly called "the voice of basketball." From 1967â€“2004, he was also known as "the voice of the New York Knicks." 

Albert has called the play-by-play of six Super Bowls, NBA Finals and seven Stanley Cup Finals. 

On June 6, 2011, it was said that Albert was joining CBS Sports to call play-by-play for "The NFL on CBS".
