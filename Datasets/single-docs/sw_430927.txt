Forbes (band)

Forbes was a Swedish dansband of the 1970s.

The band represented Sweden in the Eurovision Song Contest in 1977, with the entry "Beatles". The song is about the British band The Beatles. Despite the popular topic of the song, Forbes ended in last place and received only two points. It was one of the worst placements ever for a Swedish entry at Eurovision.
