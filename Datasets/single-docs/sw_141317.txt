Tollywood

Telugu Cinema (Telugu: తెలుగు సినీపరిశ్రమ), refers to the cinema in Telugu language, sometimes referred as Tollywood, a portmanteau of "Telugu" and "Hollywood".

Tollywood ( Telugu movies) refers to the movies produced in Andhra pradesh and telengana states based on Telugu film industry in the Republic Of India: The origins of that name, Tollywood a portmanteau of the words Tollygunge and Hollywood are disputed.
