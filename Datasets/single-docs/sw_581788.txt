Giannino Castiglioni

Giannino "Gio" Castiglioni (4 August 1884 â€“ 27 August 1971) was an Italian architect, Urbanist, artist, painter and sculptor, his style was representational.

In 1951, Castiglioni designed the master plan, the square, and several individual buildings of Lierna, Lake Como, above the implantation of an ancient medieval defensive village, in a simple, functional, concrete and elegant. On the basis of Italian architectural rationalism it was stated that: ""There is no incompatibility between our past and our present. We do not want to break with tradition: it is the tradition that transforms, assumes new aspects, under which few recognize it"".
