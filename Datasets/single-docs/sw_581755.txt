Von Brunn was a white supremacist. and Holocaust denier He was born in St. Louis, Missouri.

Von Brunn was named the prime suspect in the shooting, and was charged with first-degree murder and firearms violations. While awaiting trial, von Brunn died on January 6, 2010 in Butner, North Carolina from congestive heart failure caused by sepsis, aged 89.
