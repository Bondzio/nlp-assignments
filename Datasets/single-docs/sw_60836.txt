Ogden Nash

Ogden Nash (August 19, 1902 - May 19, 1971) was an American poet. His poetry is thought by many people to be very funny. Nash was born in Rye, New York on August 19, 1902. In 1932, he joined the staff of "The New Yorker". He died of Crohn's disease on May 19, 1971 in Baltimore, Maryland.
