Hu Chunhua

Hu Chunhua (; born April 1963) is a Chinese politician. He is a member of the Communist Party's Politburo. He is the Communist Party Secretary of Guangdong province, the province's top political office.

Hu was then promoted to Party Secretary of Inner Mongolia in 2009. As one of the youngest provincial leaders, Hu is seen as a promising candidate in the 'sixth generation' of Chinese leadership.
