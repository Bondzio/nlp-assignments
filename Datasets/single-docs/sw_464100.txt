Ulrick Chérubin

Ulrick Chérubin (December 24, 1943 — September 25, 2014) was a Canadian politician, who served as mayor of Amos, Quebec from 2002 until his death in 2014. He was one of the first Black Canadians to be elected a mayor in Quebec. Chérubin was born in Jacmel, Haiti.

Chérubin died in Amos, Quebec, Canada, aged 70.
