NYC 22

NYC 22 (formerly known as Rookies and then The 2-2) is a police drama that began airing on April 15, 2012, as a midseason replacement for "". It aired on Sundays at 10:00 PM on CBS in the United States. 
On May 13, 2012, the series was cancelled by CBS. 

The series shows a diverse group of rookie New York Police Department officers, they patrol Upper Manhattan.
