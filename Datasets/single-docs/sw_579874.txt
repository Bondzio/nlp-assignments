Y. A. Tittle

Yelberton Abraham Tittle Jr. (born October 24, 1926), better known as Y. A. Tittle, is an American professional American football quarterback. He played in the National Football League (NFL) for the San Francisco 49ers, New York Giants, and Baltimore Colts, after spending two seasons with the Colts in the All-America Football Conference (AAFC).

Tittle was born in Marshall, Texas. He studied at Louisiana State University. He was married to Minnette Tittle until her death in 2012. They have one daughter, Dianne. Tittle is suffering from a severe case of dementia.
