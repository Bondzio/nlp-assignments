Carlo Ancelotti

Carlo Ancelotti (born 10 June 1959) is a Italian football manager and former player. He has played for Italy national team. He was a successful manager at Milan, helping them win the UEFA Champions League twice, the Coppa Italia once, the Serie A once, the Italian Supercup once, the UEFA Super Cup twice and the FIFA Club World Cup once. He is now the manager of La Liga club Real Madrid.
