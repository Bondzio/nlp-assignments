Caroline Aaron

Caroline Aaron (earlier: Caroline Sidney Abady; born August 7, 1952) is an American actress. She played the character of Martha Ingels in the 1992 film "This is My Life". She also played the part of Annie in "21 Jump Street" (a 2012 action comedy). She has a number of other acting roles to her credit in both film and television.

Aaron was born in Richmond, Virginia.
