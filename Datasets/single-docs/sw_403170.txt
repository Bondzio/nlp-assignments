Juan José Méndez Fernández

Juan Jose Mendez Fernandez (born March 27, 1964 in Barcelona) is a cyclist from Spain. He has a disability: He is a C1 type athlete. He competed at the 2004 Summer Paralympics in cycling. He was the number three cyclists to finish in the Road Trial LC4 race. He competed at the 2008 Summer Paralympics in cycling. He was the number two cyclists to finish in the Road Trial LC4 race. He was the number three cyclists to finish in the Individual Pursuit track LC4 race. He competed at the 2012 Summer Paralympics in cycling.
