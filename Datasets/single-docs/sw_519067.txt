King Kong (character)

King Kong is a giant movie monster, resembling a huge gorilla. The character first appeared in the 1933 film "King Kong". The movie is a classic adventure-fantasy. The film was remade in 1976 and once again in 2005. The character has become one of the world's most famous movie icons. Kong has inspired countless spin-offs, comic books, team ups with other monsters and more. There was even a stage play. His role in the different movies varies, ranging from a rampaging monster to a vegetarian who only wants to be left alone.
