Civic Star

The Civic Star () is a French award.

It was established by the Académie française in 1930. 

The star distinguishes those who contribute to the enrichment of the community, improvement of social life,and the progress of Humanity. It is particularly for those whose lives are of hard work, sacrifice and would remain unknown without this award. It is divided into four degrees: Bronze, Silver, Gold and Silver-gilt.

The star's motto is "" ('To honor the civic virtues - Serving national prestige').
