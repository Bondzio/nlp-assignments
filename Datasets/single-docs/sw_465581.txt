Olle Widestrand

John Olof "Olle" Widestrand, born 9 July 1932, is a Swedish parish-musician, teacher and composer. He is represented in Den svenska psalmboken 1986 with two works (number 61 and 90) and several other hymnals with one hymn. He has written several children's songs and published songbooks and educational materials for schools. He lives in the town of Jönköping.
