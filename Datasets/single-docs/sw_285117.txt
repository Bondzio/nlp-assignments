Darren Aronofsky

Darren S. Aronofsky (born February 12, 1969 in Brooklyn, New York) is an American movie director. His best known movies that he has directed are "Requiem for a Dream", "The Wrestler" and "Black Swan". He attended Harvard University where he graduated in 1991 and majored in social anthropology. He is Jewish and has Ukrainian and Russian descent.
