Mercedes-Benz Sprinter

The Mercedes-Benz Sprinter is a series of vans, trucks and buses produced by Mercedes-Benz since 1995. It was facelifted in 2000 and entered its second generation in 2006.

The Sprinter is available with 4-, 5- and 6-cylinder diesel engines and 4- and 6-cylinder petrol engines.

The Volkswagen LT and the Volkswagen Crafter were derivatives of the Sprinter.
