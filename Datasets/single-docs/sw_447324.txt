Love in the Time of Cholera (movie)

Love in the Time of Cholera is a 2007 drama movie directed by Mike Newell. It is based on Gabriel García Márquez's novel "Love in the Time of Cholera". It stars Javier Bardem, Benjamin Bratt, Hector Elizondo, Liev Schreiber, John Leguizamo, and Fernanda Montenegro. It was released on November 16, 2007.
