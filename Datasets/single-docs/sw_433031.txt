Captain EO

Captain EO is an American short 3D science fiction movie. It is 17 minutes long. It is about an astronaut called Captain EO. He is played by Michael Jackson. George Lucas was the executive producer. "Captain EO" is one of the first 4D films. The film played at Disneyland and EPCOT Center. In 1996 "Captain EO" was shown on MTV. This is the only time it was shown on TV. It has never been released on DVD or video.
