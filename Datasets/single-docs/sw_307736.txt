The Eminem Show

The Eminem Show is the fourth studio album by American rapper, Eminem. "The Eminem Show" was released on May 28, 2002 and was produced by Dr. Dre, Eminem, Jeff Bass and Mr. Porter. The album was given 4 out of 5 stars by Allmusic. On March 7, 2011, the album was certified ten-times-platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America for sales of over ten million copies.
