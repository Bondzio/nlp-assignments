17 Super Exitos

Mis Mejores Canciones - 17 Super Exitos (English: "My Best Songs - 17 Super Hits") is a compilation album by Mexican-American Tejano pop singer, Selena. The album sold more than 1,200,000 copies in the United States being certified 6Ã— Platinum by the RIAA.
