Chilean Communist Party (Proletarian Action)

The Chilean Communist Party (Proletarian Action) (Spanish: "Partido Comunista Chileno (Acción Proletaria) PC(AP)") is a non-registered hard-line communist political party in Chile. It was founded in November 8, 1979 and its first secretary is Eduardo Artés.

The party believes in classical Marxism–Leninism. The party is highly critical of the more known Communist Party of Chile and its moderate views.

The party has no parliamentary representation due to their non-registered status.
