20th Century Fox Video

20th Century Fox Video was a video company is formed in 1981 by 20th Century Fox and replaced Magnetic Video. Its 20th Century Fox, United Artists, Avco Embassy Pictures, ITC Film Distributors Ltd. and ABC Pictures movies, Fox and Viacom shows on videocassette and laserdisc. In 1982, it merged with CBS Video Enterprises to form CBS/Fox Video.
