Surf City, North Carolina

Surf City is a town in Pender and Onslow counties in the U.S. state of North Carolina. As of 2000, 1,393 lived in the town.

Surf City is located at (34.428137, -77.543198). According to the United States Census Bureau, the town has a total area of 5.3 square miles (13.7 km²). 4.2 square miles (10.9 km²) of it is land and 1.1 square miles (2.8 km²) of it (20.45%) is water.
