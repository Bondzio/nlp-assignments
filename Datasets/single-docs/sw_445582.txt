¡Dos!

¡Dos! is the tenth studio album by American punk rock band, Green Day. "¡Dos!" was released on November 9, 2012. It is the second album in the ¡Uno!, ¡Dos!, ¡Tré! trilogy.

The album featured the single "Stray Heart".

The songs "Stop When the Red Lights Flash", "Amy" and "Nightlife" were featured on the episode "Unspoken" of "". Their song "Stop When the Red Lights Flash" is also featured on the soundtrack of the 2012 racing video game, "".
