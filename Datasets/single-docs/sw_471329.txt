Dave (movie)

Dave is a 1993 American comedy movie directed by Ivan Reitman. It stars Kevin Kline, Sigourney Weaver, Frank Langella, Kevin Dunn, Ving Rhames, and Ben Kingsley. 

It is about the President of the United States (Kevin Kline) suffering a stroke and the White House Chief of Staff (Frank Langella]] and assistant (Kevin Dunn) hiring a double (also Kevin Kline) to impersonate the president.




It's movie score was composed by James Newton Howard. It was released to very positive reviews on May 7, 1993. It has a 94% on Rotten Tomatoes. Critic Roger Ebert gave the movie 3.5/4 stars. It was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Screenplay.
