Máximo Alcócer

Máximo Luis Alcócer (15 April 1933 – 13 May 2014) was a Bolivian footballer whose career lasted for almost ten years. His career debut began in 1954. He retired in 1963. Alcócer played for Club Union Maestranza and Club Aurora. Alcócer was born in Cochabamba, Bolivia.

Alcócer died in North Carolina, United States, aged 81.
