Daniel Hudson (baseball)

Daniel Claiborne Hudson (born March 9, 1987 in Lynchburg, Virginia) is an American baseball player. He currently plays as a pitcher for the Arizona Diamondbacks of Major League Baseball (MLB).

Hudson formerly played for the Chicago White Sox from 2009-2010 and was traded to the Diamondbacks on July 30, 2010 along with minor league pitcher David Holmberg for Edwin Jackson.
