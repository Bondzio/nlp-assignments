Barcelona–El Prat Airport

Barcelona–El Prat Airport (Catalan: "Aeroport de Barcelona – el Prat", Spanish: "Aeropuerto de Barcelona-El Prat"), simply known as Barcelona Airport, is Spanish airport. It is located southwest of the centre of Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain. It is in the municipalities of El Prat de Llobregat, Viladecans, and Sant Boi.
