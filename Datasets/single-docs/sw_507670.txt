Umeå IK

Umeå IK is a women's association football club that is from Umeå, Sweden. It was founded in 1917 It was started as a as a general sports club. 

The club won the Swedish national championship in 2000, 2001, 2002, 2005., 2006, 2007 and 2008.

It has also won 2 UEFA Women's Champions League titles. The team's colours are black and yellow.
