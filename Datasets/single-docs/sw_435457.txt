Mad Max (video game)

Mad Max is an upcoming video game. It is set in an open world environment after civilization has been destroyed. It is based on the "Mad Max" film series. It is published by Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment and developed by Avalanche Studios.

The game was officially revealed at "E3" on June 10, 2013 during a Sony press conference. It is set to be released for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 3, PlayStation 4, Xbox 360 and Xbox One in 2015.
