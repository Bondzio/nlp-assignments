Bosque Capão da Imbuia

The Bosque do Capão da Imbuia ("grove") is a park in the city of Curitiba in Paraná, Brazil.

With a large size area of native forest, the grove has many attractions for tourists.

The oldest tree in Curitiba is an "Ocotea porosa" that is almost a thousand years old. This tree is in "Bosque do Capão da Imbuia".
