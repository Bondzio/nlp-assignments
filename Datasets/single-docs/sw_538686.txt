Nan Chiau High School

Nan Chiau High School is a co-educational secondary school in Singapore. It is one of the 11 schools in Singapore that offer the Special Assistance Plan (SAP) Special/Express stream.

The school is affiliated to Singapore Hokkien Huay Kuan (also known as the Singapore Hokkien Association).


The school motto is Sincerity & Perseverance (诚、毅). It is set after the philosophy of its founder, Mr Tan Kah Kee.


Nan Chiau High School keeps its original song. Nan Chiau Primary School, which was once part of it, has the same school song.  

Nan Chiau High's school uniform is closely linked to its heritage. 

Boys wear white shirts with red words “南僑” and navy blue pants. Girls wear white blouses with red words “南僑” and with navy blue pleated skirts. A dark red tie is worn at important events and assemblies.
