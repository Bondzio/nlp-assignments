Binali Yıldırım

Binali Yıldırım (born 20 December 1954) is the 27th and current Prime Minister of Turkey and the leader of the Justice and Development Party (AKP) since May 2016. He was the Minister of Transport, Maritime and Communication from 2002 to 2013 and again between 2015 and 2016. Between 2014 and 2015, he served as Senior Advisor to President Recep Tayyip Erdoğan.
