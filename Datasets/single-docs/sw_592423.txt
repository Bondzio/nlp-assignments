Mera (comics)

Mera () is a fictional character appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics. She was developed by Jack Miller and Nick Cardy. The character first appeared in "Aquaman" #11 (September 1963) as a queen of the sea. Mera was ranked 81st in "Comics Buyer's Guide's" "100 Sexiest Women in Comics" list.

American actress Amber Heard will portray Mera in the upcoming live-action movie "Justice League", directed by Zack Snyder,and will return in "Aquaman", directed by James Wan.
