The Bad Girls Club (season 5)

The Bad Girls Club (season 5) (or "Bad Girls Club:Miami") was the fifth season of "The Bad Girls Club". It aired on Oxygen on August 3, 2010. 

Season five's debut episode had beat the previous season. It was watched by 1.34 million people. It became the #1 program for women 18-34. The season also outperformed CBS and ABC. It was declared as the "most watched" in Oxygen history. Filming was done in Maimi, Florida and South Beach, Florida. Season five also had a high number of replacement "bad girls".
