13 Going on 30

13 Going on 30 is an American romantic comedy fantasy movie. The movie is about an unpopular girl celebrating turning 13 in May 1987. She wakes up in 2004 and is suddenly 30 years old. Jennifer Garner plays Jenna. Kathy Baker plays Beverly.

The movie opened in April 2004. The reviews were very positive. It made a lot of money.
