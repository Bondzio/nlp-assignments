The Click (song)

"The Click" is the third single off the debut album by pop punk band Good Charlotte called "Good Charlotte". The song was released in 2001. It didn't appear on the track listing of "Good Charlotte" but was on the track listing of the Japanese and 2003 re-release of "Good Charlotte". The song was also the theme song for the television show "Undergrads" and the band have also been mentioned in numerous episodes and made an appearance in the 12th episode where they played "The Click".
