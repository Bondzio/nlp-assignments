Shenandoah Valley Academy

Shenandoah Valley Academy (SVA) is a school in the town of New Market, Virginia. The school is run by the Adventist Church and has High School age boys and girls. Some students live on campus in the dorms. Other students come on school days as day students. SVA has three diploma programs. It has a performing arts department with five music groups. It has an athletics department which coordinates teams to play within the school, and to play other schools.
