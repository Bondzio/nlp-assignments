Derek McLane

Derek McLane (born June 14, 1958, in London) is an American set designer for Broadway plays. He did a Master of Fine Arts at Yale School of Drama in Set and Costume Design. He also graduated from Harvard University. He designed many Broadway shows in the last decade, including "The Heiress", "33 Variations", "Macbeth", "Follies", "Anything Goes", and "Ragtime".
