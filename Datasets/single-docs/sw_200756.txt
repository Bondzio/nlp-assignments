Cuba Gooding, Jr.

Cuba Gooding, Jr. (born January 2, 1968) is an American actor. He is best known for his role in movie "Jerry Maguire" with Tom Cruise, for which Gooding won an Academy Award. He was also in the movies "Boat Trip", and "Snow Dogs".

Gooding was born in New York City. His father is singer Cuba Gooding, Sr.. The family moved to Los Angeles, California in 1972. Gooding has been a born-again Christian since he was 13.
