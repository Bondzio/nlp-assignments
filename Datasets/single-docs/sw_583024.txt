Victor Lanoux

Victor Lanoux (18 June 1936 â€“ 4 May 2017) was a French actor. He was best known to English speaking audiences for his role as Ludovic in "Cousin, Cousine".

In 1961 he met Pierre Richard who was looking for someone to partner his cabaret act. They worked together for a number of years, including a season at the Bobino and a tour of "Le Gorille".

Lanoux died in Royan, France on 4 May 2017 from complications of a stroke at the age of 80.
