David Aardsma

David Allen Aardsma is an Major League Baseball pitcher. He plays for the Seattle Mariners and is the team's closer. He was born in Denver, Colorado on December 27, 1981.

In 2009, Aardsma earned 38 saves for the Mariners, which tied him for fourth place in the American League with 38 saves.

If the names of all players in Major League Baseball history were listed in alphabetical order, Aardsma's name would come first.
