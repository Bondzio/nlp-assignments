Marc Maron

Marc David Maron (born September 27, 1963) is an American stand-up comedian, podcast host and actor. He has been host of "The Marc Maron Show" and co-host of both "Morning Sedition" and "Breakroom Live". Since 2013, he starred in his own show "Maron".

Maron was born in Jersey City, New Jersey. He is Jewish. He grew up in New Jersey, Alaska and Albuquerque.
