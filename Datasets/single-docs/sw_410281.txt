Grand Theft Auto V

Grand Theft Auto V is a action-adventure video game that was made and released by Rockstar Games. It is the fifteenth game in the "Grand Theft Auto" video game series. It was also the first main game since 2008 when Grand Theft Auto IV was released.

The game was released all around the world on September 17, 2013 on the Playstation 3 and Xbox 360. In 2014 Rockstar Games released GTA V on Playstation 4, Xbox One. 

In 2015, the game was released for Microsoft Windows. The game sold 54 million copies, becoming the fourth best selling game of all time.
