Carlos Boozer

Carlos Boozer (born November 20, 1981 in Aschaffenburg, West Germany) is a former American basketball player. During his NBA career, he played for the Cleveland Cavaliers, Utah Jazz, Chicago Bulls and Los Angeles Lakers. His nicknames are Booz, CBooz, and Booz Cruise.

He won a gold medal with Team USA in the 2008 Summer Olympics Games in Beijing and is a two-time NBA all-star in 2007 and 2008.

Boozer was drafted out of Duke University by the Cleveland Cavaliers and played with them in the 2002-03 and 2003-04 NBA seasons.

Boozer was known to yell, "Gimme that!" when he got a rebound.
