Si Una Vez

"Si Una Vez" () is a song by American singer Selena. It was released as the fifth single from "Amor Prohibido" (1994).

In 1994, Puerto Rican merengue musician Manny Manuel covered the song on his debut album, "El Rey de Corazones". The track peaked at #18 on Hot Latin Tracks and #1 on the Latin Tropical Airplay.
