Chris Grayling

Christopher Stephen Grayling (born April 1, 1962) is an English politician and current Secretary of State for Transportation. He is the Member of Parliament for the Epsom and Ewell constituency in the United Kingdom. He was first elected in the 2001 general election.

He is a member of the Conservative Party.

He was appointed as a Privy Counsellor on 9 June 2010.
