Mariana Briski

Mariana Marcela Briski (14 September 1965 – 14 August 2014) was an Argentine actress, director, producer, screenwriter, author, and professor. She was known for her roles in "It's Not You, It's Me" (2004), "The Wind" (2005), and "Salsipuedes" (2011). Briski was also known for her television role in "Primicias". 

Briski was born in Córdoba, Argentina. She was married to Hernán Ventura from 1988 until her death in 2014. They had one son, Pedro Briski. Briski died from lung cancer and breast cancer in Buenos Aires, Argentina, aged 48. She was of Jewish descent.
