Hundred Flowers Awards

The People's Hundred Flower Award () or Hundred Flowers Awards are awards in film in China. They are like the Golden Globe Award. 

The awards were given in 1962 and 1963. They stopped until 1980. Then the awards were given every year from 1980 to 2004. Since 2004 the Hundred Flowers is awarded on even years. The Golden Rooster Awards are given on odd years.

Beginning in 2006, Chinese-language films from Taiwan and Hong Kong are also allowed to win the awards.
