Zach Braff

Zachary Braff (born April 6, 1975, South Orange, New Jersey) is an American television and movie actor, comedian, director, screenwriter and producer. He became famous in the early 2000s playing the part of "Dr. John Dorian" on the NBC series "Scrubs". Since then, he has written, starred in and directed the "Garden State". He also has directed multiple music videos. Braff is a Democrat and attended the 2012 Democratic National Convention in support of President Obama's re-election. He is to direct his fist movie. It is called "Wish I Was Here".
