People's Party for Freedom and Democracy

The People's Party for Freedom and Democracy, Dutch: "Volkspartij voor Vrijheid en Democratie" (shortened "VVD"), is a Dutch political party.

The People's Party for Freedom and Democracy is a conservative liberal party.

It was established in 1948 by a merger of the Freedom Party (PvdV) and the Oud Committee.

Political leader is Mark Rutte (also Prime Minister since 2010).

Number of seats in the 2017 elections: 33 (-8).
