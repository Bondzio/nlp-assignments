James B. Nutter Sr.

James Bradford Nutter, Sr. (January 23, 1928 â€“ July 7, 2017) was an American businessman and philanthropist. He was the founder and chairman of James B. Nutter & Company, a privately owned mortgage banking firm headquartered in Kansas City, Missouri. It is one of the oldest and largest family-owned and operated mortgage lending firms in the United States. Nutter was named 2012 Kansas Citian of the Year by the Greater Kansas City Chamber of Commerce.

Nutter Sr. died in Kansas City on July 7, 2017 from stroke-related complications at the age of 89.
