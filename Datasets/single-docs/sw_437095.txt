Louan Gideon

Louan Gideon (November 12, 1955 - February 3, 2014) was an American actress. She is best known for her role as Danielle Atron in "The Secret World of Alex Mack". She was also in "Search for Tomorrow".

Gideon died on February 3, 2014 in Asheville, North Carolina from cancer, aged 58.
