Roy Heather

Roy Heather (20 May 1935 â€“ 3 September 2014) was a British actor. He was best known for his role as the cafe owner Sid in the television series "Only Fools and Horses". He also appeared in "The Bill", "My Family", "Casualty" and "It Ain't Half Hot Mum".

Heather was born in Stoke Poges, Buckinghamshire. He was married to actress Sarah Vernon from 1982 until their divorce in 1987. He was married to Sara Taylor from 10 May 1993 until his death.

Heather died after a long illness on 3 September 2014 in Purbrook, Hampshire, aged 79.
