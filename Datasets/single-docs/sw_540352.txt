Chinatown MRT Station

Chinatown MRT Station (NE4/DT19) is an MRT Station in Singapore. It is an interchange station along the North East MRT Line and Downtown MRT Line. It became the first MRT station with both lines operated by SBS Transit. 

It is currently the terminus on the Downtown MRT Line. After construction is complete, Fort Canning MRT Station will be the terminus.
