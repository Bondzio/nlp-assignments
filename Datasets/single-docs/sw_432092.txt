Kaya Scodelario

Kaya Rose Scodelario (born Kaya Rose Humphrey; born 13 March 1992) is an English actress and former model. She plays Teresa in "The Maze Runner" in 2014. She also played Peshet in the 2010 movie "Clash of the Titans".

Scodelario was born in London. She changed her surname to that of her Brazilian mother.
