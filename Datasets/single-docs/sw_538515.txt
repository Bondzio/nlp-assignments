Choa Chu Kang MRT/LRT Station

Choa Chu Kang MRT/LRT Station (NS4/BP1) is an MRT Station in Singapore. It is the interchange station between the North South MRT Line and Bukit Panjang LRT Line. It is located in Choa Chu Kang.

The station was the first to provide an interchange between the MRT and the LRT.
