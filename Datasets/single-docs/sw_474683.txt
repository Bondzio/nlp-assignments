The Mimmi series () is a book series written by Viveca LÃ¤rn (earlier Viveca Sundvall). The books tell the stories about a girl in late Kindergarten-early elementary school age in Sweden called Mimmi. The books are told from the me-perspective. Many of the books are written as a diary. The books were originally published between 1979 and 1996.

Mimmi's father Oskar is a postman. Her mother Elin is a waitress at restaurant "Gyllene Svanen".
