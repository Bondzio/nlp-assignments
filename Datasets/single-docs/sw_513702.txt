Willy Stähle

Willy Juana Stähle (16 June 1954 – August 2015) was a Dutch world water skiing champion. She won a gold for women's figure skiing and a silver medal for women's salom at the 1972 Summer Olympics in Munich, Germany. She was the Dutch Sportswoman of the Year in 1971.

Stähle was born in Amsterdam. Her death was announced on 1 September 2015.
