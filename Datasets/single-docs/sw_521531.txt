Samantha Cristoforetti

Samantha Cristoforetti (born April 26, 1977) is an Italian European Space Agency astronaut, Italian Air Force pilot an engineer. She is the first Italian woman in space and holds the record for the woman with more time in space with 199 days and 16 hours. Cristoforetti is also the first person in prepare an espresso in space.
