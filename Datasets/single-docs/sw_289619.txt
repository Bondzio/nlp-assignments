Noah Cyrus

Noah Cyrus is Miley Cyrus's younger sister. She was born in Nashville, Tennessee. She has been in some TV shows like "Hannah Montana" but had very small roles. 
She appeared in the straight-to-DVD movie "". She was also in the film Ponyo with Frankie Jonas the youngest brother of the Jonas Brothers and played the character Yuria Nara. She also sang the theme song for Ponyo with Frankie too.
