Dax Shepard

Dax Randall Shepard (born January 2, 1975 in Milford, Michigan) is an American television actor, movie actor and comedian. Shepard has appeared in many movies such as; "Cheaper by the Dozen", "Zathura", "Idiocracy", "Baby Mama", "Old Dogs", "The Freebie", and in the 2012 comedy "Hit and Run".

Shepard started dating actress Kristen Bell in 2007. Their daughter, Lincoln Bell Shepard, was born on March 28, 2013. Dax and Bell married on October 17, 2013. Their second daughter, Delta Bell Shepard, was born on December 19, 2014.
