2Bobule

2Bobule () is a 2009 Czech comedy movie directed by Vlad Lanné. The movie follows Marie (Jana Pidrmanová), Jirka (Lukáš Langmajer) and their wealthy parents (Jiří Krampol and Jana Švandová). It also follows a murderer called Jiří Tichý (Jiří Korn). The movie happens mostly in southern Moravia.
