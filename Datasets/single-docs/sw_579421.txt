Onision

Gregory Jackson (born November 11, 1985), better known as Onision, is an American Internet personality, musician, entertainer and website developer. The videos he upload to his YouTube channel mostly revolve around around topics such as self-harm, suicide and body issues. Onision has massed up over 2.2 million subscribers on his main channel (Onision: link below), 1.9 million subscribers on his OnisionSpeaks channel, and 2.4 million subscribers on his UhOhBro.
