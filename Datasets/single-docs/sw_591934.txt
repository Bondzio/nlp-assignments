Matthew Willson

Matthew Willson is an award-winning Canadian actor based in Paris, Ontario. In 2010, Matthew Willson won an Audience Choice Award from Hamilton Film Festival.
Willson is known for playing "Preacher" in the movie "Arrival".

Willson was raised in Paris, Ontario. Willson had learned techniques of acting. Later he founded Willson Entertainment Productions. In 2010, Matthew starred in a feature-length movie "Skinning the Cat". Matthew also appeared in "The Negotiation" in 2015. In 2016, he played a role in "Arrival" along with Amy Adams and Jeremy Renner. Matthew also appeared in Canadian Comedy Awards and several TV movies.
