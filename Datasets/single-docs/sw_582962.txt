Charles Kettles

Charles S. Kettles (born January 9, 1930) is a retired United States Army lieutenant colonel. He is a Medal of Honor recipient for his services in the United States Army. Kettles received the Medal of Honor on July 18, 2016, nearly 50 years after his actions while serving as a flight commander assigned to 176th Aviation Company (Airmobile) (Light), 14th Combat Aviation Battalion, Americal Division.
