Dominic Chianese

Dominic Chianese (; born February 24, 1931 in The Bronx, New York) is an American actor, singer, musician, activist, and philanthropist. He is best known for his role as Corrado "Junior" Soprano on the HBO TV series "The Sopranos". In 2010, Chianese received the Ellis Island Medal of Honor in recognition of his humanitarian efforts.
