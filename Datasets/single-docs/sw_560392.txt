Lampeter

Lampeter ( or, more informally, "Llambed") is a small market town in Ceredigion, South West Wales. It is at the confluence of the River Teifi and the Afon Dulas. It is the third largest urban area in Ceredigion after Aberystwyth and Cardigan. One of the oldest universities in Britain, The University of Wales, Trinity St David, is located here.
