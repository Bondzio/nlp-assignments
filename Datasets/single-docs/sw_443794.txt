Jessica Iskandar

Jessica Iskandar (born 29 January 1988) is an Indonesian actress, comedian, singer and model. 

Iskandar was a model at John Casablanca. After trying casting in some agencies, she got the role as Kara in the movie "Dealova" in 2005. She also had a small role in the Malaysian movie "Diva" (2007). Currently, she is in a soap opera. She is also in ads. She has been a presenter since March 2011. She played in "Campur-Campur" in ANTV.

Currently Iskandar is studying interior design at Trisakti University, Jakarta. She reportedly did not go to college again after three semesters for career matters.
