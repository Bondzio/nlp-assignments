Skellefteå AIK

Skellefteå AIK is an ice hockey club in the town of Skellefteå in Sweden. It was established in 1921. The club adopted the game of ice hockey in 1943. It won the Swedish national championship in 1978, 2013 and 2014. The team also lost the Swedish national finals in 2011, 2012 and 2015.
