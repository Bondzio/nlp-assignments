Paul Mantee

Paul Mantee (January 9, 1931 â€“ November 7, 2013) was an American movie and television actor. He was known for his roles in "Cagney & Lacey", "Simon & Simon", "Barnaby Jones", and in "Quincy, M.E.".

Mantee was born Paul Marianetti in San Francisco, California on January 9, 1931. He studied at San Mateo Junior College. Mantee enlisted in the U.S. Navy for four years during the Korean War that made him decide on acting as a career. 

He was married to Suzy Davis Mantee. He was formerly married to actress Ann Newman. Mantee died on November 7, 2013 in his Los Angeles, California home from prostate cancer, aged 82.
