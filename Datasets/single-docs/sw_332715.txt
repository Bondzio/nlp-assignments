Ben Kingsley

Ben Kingsley (born Krishna Bhanji, 31 December 1943) is a British actor. He was born in Scarborough, North Yorkshire, England. His father was an Indian who was born in Kenya. He is best known for his role as Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi in "Gandhi", for which he won an Academy Award, a BAFTA Award and a Golden Globe Award. The first movie he acted in was "Fear Is the Key" in 1972.
