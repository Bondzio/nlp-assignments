Adnan Gulshair el Shukrijumah

Adnan Gulshair el Shukrijumah (, "ʿAdnān Shukrī Jumaʿah") (4 August 1975 – 6 December 2014) was a member of al-Qaeda. He was born in Saudi Arabia and grew up in the United States.

In March 2003, a provisional arrest warrant was issued calling him a "material witness". He was listed on theFederal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) on the Seeking Information - War on Terrorism list, and since then United States Department of State, through the Rewards for Justice Program, has offered a bounty of up to US$5 million for information about his location.

Media reports surfaced in 6 December 2014 indicating that he was killed in a manhunt operation by Pakistan in South Waziristan.
