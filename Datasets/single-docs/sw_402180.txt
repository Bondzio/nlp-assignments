Munenori Kawasaki

Munenori Kawasaki (川﨑 宗則, Kawasaki Munenori) (born June 3, 1981) is a Japanese baseball player. As of August 2016, he plays as an infielder for the Chicago Cubs of Major League Baseball.

He has also played for the Fukuoka SoftBank Hawks of Nippon Professional Baseball, the Seattle Mariners, and the Toronto Blue Jays. He also played for the Japanese national baseball team in the 2008 Summer Olympics and both the 2006 and 2009 World Baseball Classics.
