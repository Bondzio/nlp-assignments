Andy Hilbert

Andrew John Hilbert (born February 6, 1981 in Howell, Michigan) is a professional ice hockey center that currently plays for the New York Islanders of the NHL. He was drafted with the 37th overall pick by the Boston Bruins in the 2000 NHL Entry Draft. Hilbert has also played 3 seasons with the Boston Bruins, 1 season with the Chicago Blackhawks, 1 season with the Pittsburgh Penguins and 1 season with the Minnesota Wild.
