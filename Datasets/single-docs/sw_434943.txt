Jason Sudeikis

Daniel Jason Sudeikis ( ; born September 18, 1975) is an American actor, voice actor, writer and comedian. Sudeikis was born on September 18, 1975 in Fairfax, Virginia.

In 2003, he was hired as a sketch writer for "Saturday Night Live" and became a cast member from 2005 to 2013. He has appeared on television in "30 Rock", "The Cleveland Show", "Eastbound & Down", and other shows. He played "Schmitty" on "It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia". He is also known for his roles in the films "Hall Pass", "Horrible Bosses", "Epic" and "We're the Millers".
