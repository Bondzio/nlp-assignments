Maduluwawe Sobitha Thero

Maduluwawe Sobitha Thero (; 29 May 1942 â€“ 8 November 2015) was a Sinhalese Buddhist Bikku. He was a well-known socialist, social justice activist. He led campaigns that toppled governments in several times and a Buddhist style non violent revolutionary. He was the chief of the Kotte Naga Vihara. He played a key role to bring a new government in 2015 defeating Mahinda Rajapaksa. He supported common candidate Maithripala Sirisena for presidency, and Ranil Wickremesinghe to form a new government. 

Ven Sobitha died on 8 November 2015 from multiple organ failure at the Mount Elizabeth Hospital in Singapore. He was 73.
