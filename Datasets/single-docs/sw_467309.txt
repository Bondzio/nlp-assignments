Hugo Carvana

Hugo Carvana (4 June 1937 – 4 October 2014) was a Brazilian movie and television actor and director. He appeared in 65 movie since 1955. He was known for his roles in "Entranced Earth" and in "Antonio das Mortes". He was also known for directing "Casa da Mãe Joana".

Carvana died of an illness in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, aged 77.
