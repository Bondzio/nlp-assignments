Sabine Oberhauser

Sabine Oberhauser (30 August 1963 â€“ 23 February 2017) was an Austrian medical doctor and politician. She was a member of the Social Democratic Party. She was the Minister for Health and Minister for Women from 2014 until her death. She had also been a member of the National Council since 2006.

Oberhauser was born in Vienna. She was married and had two daughters.

Oberhauser said she had abdominal cancer in 2016. She died on 23 February 2017, at the age of 53.
