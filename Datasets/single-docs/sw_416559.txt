Harju County

Harju County () is one of the 15 counties of Estonia. It is in northern Estonia. Around 528,468 people live in Harju County. This is about 39.4% of the total population in Estonia (as of January 2011). 

The county is divided into municipalities. There are 6 urban municipalities ( – towns) and 18 rural municipalities ( – parishes) in Harju County.
