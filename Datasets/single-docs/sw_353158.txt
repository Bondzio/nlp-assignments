David Mitchell

David James Stuart Mitchell (born 14 July 1974) is a British actor, comedian, television personality and television writer. He was born in Salisbury, Wiltshire. His mother is Welsh; his father is of Scottish descent. The family moved to Oxford during his late childhood. He studied history at Peterhouse, Cambridge. Mitchell stars in "Would I Lie to You?", "Peep Show" and "That Mitchell and Webb Look". He often works with Robert Webb. 

Mitchell married Victoria Coren on 17 November 2012 in Belsize Park, North London. The couple's daughter was born in May 2015.
