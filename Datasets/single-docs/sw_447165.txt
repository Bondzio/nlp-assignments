The Boondocks (TV series)

The Boondocks is an American adult animated sitcom produced by Adult Swim. The series premiered in November 6, 2005. It is set in South Side, Chicago, Illinois. 

It is about a black family called the Freemans moving in Woodcrest, a white suburb, of Chicago. They deal with many situations such as drugs, alcoholism, poverty, racism, and child abuse.
