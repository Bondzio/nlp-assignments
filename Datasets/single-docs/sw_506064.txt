1. FFC Frankfurt

1. FFC Frankfurt is a German women's association football team that represents Frankfurt and plays in the Frauen Bundesliga. The team is the most successful German team, both in the league and in Europe. Frankfurt has won 7 Bundesliga titles, 4 UEFA Women's Champions League titles and 9 DFB-Pokal titles. Some notable players include Birgit Prinz, Fatmire Alushi, Melanie Behringer, Célia Šašić and Simone Laudehr.
