Rob Corddry

Robert William "Rob" Corddry (born February 4, 1971) is an American movie, television, voice actor, television director and producer, writer and comedian who is known for his roles in "Hot Tub Time Machine" and in "Childrens Hospital". He has won an Emmy Award in 2012 for "Children Hospital".

Corddry was born on February 4, 1971 in Weymouth, Massachusetts. He studied at Weymouth North High School and at University of Massachusetts Amherst.
