Belmont University

Belmont University is a private, coeducational university in Nashville, Tennessee. The university has about 5,936 students. It was founded in 1890. The current president of the university is Robert Fisher.

The athletics teams of the university, called the Bruins, mostly play in the Ohio Valley Conference (OVC). The men's soccer team plays in the Horizon League because the OVC does not run a men's soccer league.
