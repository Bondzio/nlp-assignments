Joe Pittman

Joseph Wayne "Joe" Pittman (January 1, 1954 â€“ June 12, 2014), nicknamed "Shoe" or "Shoes", was an American baseball backup infielder and outfielder. He played in Major League Baseball for the Houston Astros, San Diego Padres and San Francisco Giants in between the seasons of 1981 and 1984.

Pittman was born in Houston, Texas. He was African American.

Pittman died after collapsing at a construction job on June 12, 2014 in Freeport, Texas, aged 60.
