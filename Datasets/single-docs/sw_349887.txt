Emily Blunt

Emily Olivia Leah Blunt (born 23 February 1983) is a British and American actress. She was born in Wandsworth, London. She is a niece of MP Crispin Blunt (born 1960) and a granddaughter of Peter Blunt (1923-2003).

Blunt's television roles include the title character "Gideon's Daughter", for which she won a Golden Globe Award. Her movie roles include Emily Charlton in "The Devil Wears Prada", the title role of Queen Victoria in "The Young Victoria" and Elise Sellas in "The Adjustment Bureau".

Blunt married John Krasinski in Como, Italy on 10 July 2010. The couple have two daughters, born in 2014 and 2016. Blunt became a dual British-American citizen in 2015. 

Blunt has been diagnosed with obsessive-compulsive disorder.
