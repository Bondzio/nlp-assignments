Glenview, Illinois

Glenview is a village in northeastern Illinois, USA. it is about north of downtown Chicago in Cook County, Illinois. According to the 2010 United States Census, the village population was 44,692.

The village was named by a popular vote in 1895. It was incorporated as a village in 1899. There were 325 adults in Glenview that year.
