Georgina Henry

Georgina Clare Henry (8 June 1960 â€“ 7 February 2014) was a British journalist. She worked with "The Guardian". She held several senior positions at the newspaper.

Henry was born in Aden, Yemen (then a British colony) on 8 June 1960. Henry was educated at Cranbrook School, Kent and King's College London where she read history. At King's College she met Ronan Bennett, subsequently a writer, who became her life-long partner.

Henry died on 7 February 2014 in London, England from eye cancer, aged 53.
