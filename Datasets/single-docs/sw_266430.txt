Rebecca St. James

Rebecca St. James (born Rebecca Jean Smallbone; July 26, 1977) is a Christian rock singer. She is signed to Forefront Records.

St. James has sold a total of two million albums.

St. James is originally from Australia. She now lives in Nashville, Tennessee. Her brothers are Joel and Luke Smallbone from the band for King & Country.
