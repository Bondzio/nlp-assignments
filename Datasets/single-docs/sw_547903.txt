María Dolores Pradera

María Dolores Fernández Pradera (born 29 August 1924 in Madrid) is a Spanish melodic singer and actress. She one of the most famous singers in Spain and Latin America. She is also known as María Dolores or La Pradera. She started her career as an actress, working in the theatre and in some of the most famous Spanish movies from the 1940s: “¡A mí la legión!” (1942), “La Lola se va a los puertos” (1947), or “Agustina de Aragón” (1950).
