Occupation government's prison camps in Norway during World War Two

The occupation government built and used prison camps in Norway during World War Two. 709 prison camps [or concentration camps, and some death camps] were counted by a project, that was under advice by Randi Bratteli and some others; other numbers say "around 500" prison camps.
