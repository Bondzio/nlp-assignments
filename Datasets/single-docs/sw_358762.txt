Scott Grimes

Scott Richard Grimes (born July 9, 1971) is an American actor and songwriter. Grimes is known for his roles as Dr. Archie Morris in "ER", Will McCorkle in "Party of Five", Donald Malarkey in "Band of Brothers", and as Steve Smith in "American Dad!". Grimes was born in Lowell, Massachusetts. Grimes was married to Dawn Bailey-Grimes from 1997 until they divorced in 2007, they had 2 children.
