Samaira Sandhu

Samaira Sandhu is a former beauty queen, actress and an author. She debuted in the Tamil film "Dhayam" which was released on March 24, 2017.

Samaira was born in Punjab, India and lived her life in Chandigarh. She was completed B.Tech and MBA and worked for some time in a multinational company as a software engineer. She was an active participant of theater and sports during college and school. In 2015, she turned her profession from a software engineer in to a beauty queen and then an actor.

Samaira wrote her first novel "Heaven in a Hell" in 2015.

Samaira Sandhu is the brand ambassador of two NGOs, Chandigarh Round Table (CRT) and Handicapped Children and Women Association (HCWA).
