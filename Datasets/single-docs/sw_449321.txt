Katharine Isabelle

Katherine Isobel Murray (also Katharine Isabelle; born November 2, 1981) is a Canadian actress. She played Chloe in the movie "Cousins" (1989). She also played Lindsay Clark in the movie "Disturbing Behavior" (1998). She played Julia Baker in the movie "Countdown" (2016).

Isabelle was born in Vancouver, British Columbia.
