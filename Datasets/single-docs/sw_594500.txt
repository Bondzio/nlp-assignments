Urban Massage Ltd

Urban Massage is a massage therapy service app based in London. Urban Massage became popular for pregnancy, sports, relaxing massage therapy by professional wellness therapists. According to Startups.com, Urban Massage is ‘the Uber of massage’.

"Urban Massage" was founded by Jack Tang and Giles Williams in 2014. Urban Massage has been providing private massage therapy services since than.
Urban Massage has raised a cumulative of $1.5 million from four investors include Felix Capital, Firestartr, Passion Capital, Samos. Urban Massage has been featured by Daily Record (Scotland), The Scotsman, TechCrunch, AngelList.
Urban Massage has released Mobile apps for iOs and Android platform to booking massage on-demand, spa treatments at homes, hotels and offices. In 2016, they introduces Urban Classic massage.
