Brian Cox (physicist)

Brian Edward Cox OBE (born March 3, 1968) is a British particle physicist. He is also a professor at the University of Manchester and a Royal Society University Research Fellow. He works on the ATLAS experiment at the Large Hadron Collider (LHC). Cox was born in Chadderton, Oldham, Lancashire. He is the presenter of some BBC science television programmes. He used to be a keyboard player for pop band D:Ream in the 1990s.
