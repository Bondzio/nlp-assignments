Leon Brittan

Leon Brittan, Baron Brittan of Spennithorne QC, PC, DL (25 September 1939 â€“ 21 January 2015) was a British politician, Conservative Member of Parliament and barrister, as well as member of the European Commission and Home Secretary of the United Kingdom.

Brittan died of cancer in London, aged 75.

In October 2014, a Labour MP used parliamentary privilege to refer to claims that Brittan had been linked to child abuse. On 21 March 2016, the Metropolitan Police confirmed that "Operation Midland", the investigation into the alleged paedophile ring, had been closed without any charges being brought.
