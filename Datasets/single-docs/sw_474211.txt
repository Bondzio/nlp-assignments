Morteza Ahmadi

Morteza Haj Seyed Ahmadi (‎; 1 November 1924 – 21 December 2014) was an Iranian actor, singer and writer. Ahmadi was known for his works in "Autobus", "Madrak-e Jorm" and "Khane-kharab". He was born in Tehran.

Ahmadi died on 21 December 2014 at his private house in Tehran, aged 90.
