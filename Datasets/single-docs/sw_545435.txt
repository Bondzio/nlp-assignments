Etzelkofen

Etzelkofen was a municipality in the administrative district of Bern-Mittelland in the canton of Berne in Switzerland.

On the 1 January 2014 the former municipalities of Etzelkofen, Büren zum Hof, Grafenried, Switzerland, Limpach, Mülchi, Schalunen and Zauggenried merged into the municipality of Fraubrunnen.
