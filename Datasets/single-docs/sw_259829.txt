Nanaimo

Nanaimo is a city in British Columbia with a population of 78,692. 

The mayor of Nanaimo is John Ruttan.

Nanaimo is located on Vancouver Island and is the second largest city on Vancouver Island.

Nanaimo is ranked 62nd overall in the 100 largest municipalities in Canada and is nicknamed the "Bathtub Racing Capital of the World" and the "Harbour City".
