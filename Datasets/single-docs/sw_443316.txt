Frightwig

Frightwig are an American punk rock band that was founded in 1982. All of the members are women. They are feminists. Deanna Ashley and Mia Levin started the band in San Francisco. In 1984 they released their first album "Cat Farm Faboo". In 1986 their second album was released. In 1993 they released an extended play called "Phone Sexy". In 1994 the band broke up. The band got back together in 2012.
