Hamza Ali

He played for Hampshire, MCC Young Cricketers and Rawalpindi Rams. He played with his right-arm. He was a fast-medium bowler. He also batted right handed. Ali attended City Academy Bristol and Filton Sixth Form College.

Ali died after drowning at the River Avon, Bristol on 9 June 2016, aged 20.
