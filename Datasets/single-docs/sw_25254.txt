Southeast Asia

Southeast Asia or Southeastern Asia is the southeastern part of Asia. There are ten countries in this region. All the countries in Southeast Asia are a part of ASEAN. Some of the countries are mostly on the Asian mainland; they were formerly called Indochina. The others are only on islands.
