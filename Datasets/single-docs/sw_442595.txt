Icchokas Meras

Icchokas Meras (8 October 1934 – 13 March 2014) was a Lithuanian writer and screenwriter. On 6 December 2010, he was awarded the Lithuanian National Prize. He was born in Kelmė, Šiauliai County. He was Jewish and survived the Holocaust.

Meras died on 13 March 2014 in Tel Aviv, Israel. He was 79 years old.
