Ted (movie)

Ted is a 2012 American comedy movie, directed, produced, and co-written by Seth MacFarlane. It is set in Boston, Massachusetts. It stars Mark Wahlberg, Mila Kunis, and MacFarlane as the voice of Ted. It also features Giovanni Ribisi, Joel McHale, Jessica Barth, and Aedin Mincks.

A sequel titled "Ted 2" was released on June 26, 2015.
