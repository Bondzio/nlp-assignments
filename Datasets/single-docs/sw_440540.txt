Gérard Kango Ouédraogo

Gérard Kango Ouédraogo (born 19 September 1925) served as Prime Minister of Upper Volta (now Burkina Faso) from 13 February 1971 to 8 February 1974. He was President of the National Assembly of Burkina Faso from 1978 to November 25, 1980. 

He was born in Ouahigouya. 

He served in the French National Assembly from 1956-1959.
