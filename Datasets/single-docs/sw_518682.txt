The Ultimate (Grace Jones album)

The Ultimate is a the second greatest hits album by Jamaican recording artist Grace Jones released in 1993. The album would have the same songs as her 1985 "Island Life" greatest hits album as well as four other songs. "The Ultimate" was released only in the Netherlands, where it enjoyed success.


Note: Edits of "Slave to the Rhythm", "Do or Die" and "The Fashion Show" are included.
