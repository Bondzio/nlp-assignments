We Belong Together

"We Belong Together" is a 2005 hit song by Mariah Carey off of her album "The Emancipation of Mimi". The song is available in several versions. It reached #1 on the "Billboard" Hot 100 and stayed there for 14 weeks. It was also No.1 in Australia and the Netherlands. It was the best-selling song in the US of 2005. It won two Grammy Awards.
