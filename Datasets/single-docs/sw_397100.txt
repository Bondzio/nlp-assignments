Jason Statham

Jason Statham (born 12 September 1967) is an English actor, producer, martial artist, and former diver on the British National Diving team.

Many of these roles are as antihero in action movies. He does most of his own stunts.

Statham was born on 12 September 1967 in Shirebrook, Derbyshire, England. He was raised in Great Yarmouth, Norfolk. Statham has been partners with Kelly Brook from 1997 until they separated in 2004. Then he was partners with Rosie Huntington-Whiteley since 2010.
