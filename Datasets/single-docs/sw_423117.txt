Anna Favella

Anna Favella (September 21, 1983) is an Italian actress. She works in television and movies. She is known for roles in the TV series "Terra ribelle" and in the movie "Mr. America".

Anna Favella was born and raised in Rome.

Favella started acting when she was very young. Her first minor roles were in programs such as "Enrico Mattei - L'uomo che guardava al future" and "Don Matteo 7". Her first major role was in a TV series called "Terra Ribelle", where she played the role of the main character, Elena Marsili. In 2013, Favella was the main character in the movie "Mr. America".
