Zhang Zhen (general)

Zhang Zhen (; 5 October 1914 – 3 September 2015) was a general of the People's Liberation Army of China, and a former member of the Central Military Commission of the Communist Party of China. Zhang was born in Pingjiang County, Hunan Province. 

His original name was Zhang Jiansheng(), also named as Zhang Zushou(), Zhang Zhongtian(). He joined the Chinese Communist Youth League in April 1930, and joined the Communist Party of China in July of the same year.
