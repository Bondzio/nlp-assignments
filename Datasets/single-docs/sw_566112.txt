SOCATA MS.880 Rallye

The SOCATA MS.880 Rallye is a French light aircraft used by civilian and military users. It's first mission is training. 

It was create by Morane-Saulnier and after built by SOCATA. A military light attack version is known as Rallye 235 Guerrier.
Some were built under licence in Poland as PZL Koliber.
