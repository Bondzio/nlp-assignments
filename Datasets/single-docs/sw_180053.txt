Dinornis

Dinornis is an extinct genus of ratite birds. They are ostrich-like birds of gigantic size, known as Moa. They were unique to New Zealand. The genus has five species:

"D. novaezealandiae" 
"D. giganteus" <br>
"D. struthoides"<br>
"Dinornis" new lineage A
