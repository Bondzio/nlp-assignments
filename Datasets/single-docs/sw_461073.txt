Gabe Newell

Gabe Logan Newell (born November 3, 1962) is an American businessman. He is the co-founder and managing director of video game development and online distribution company Valve Corporation. He co-founded Valve with fellow Microsoft employee Mike Harrington. He is also known for founding online video gaming distributor Steam.
