Beavis and Butt-head

Beavis and Butt-head is an animated American television series by Mike Judge which began on March 8, 1993 and ended on November 28, 1997 on MTV. In 1996 the series was adapted into a movie titled "Beavis and Butt-Head Do America". The title characters are hard rock/heavy metal loving teenagers from the fictional town of Highland, Texas. In 2010 it was announced the series would return in 2011.
