Afterwards, he became a talent agent, representing Jacques Brel, Barbara, Sylvie Vartan, Henri Salvador, Joe Dassin and Salvatore Adamo.

Marouani became a knight of the Legion of Honour in 2009.

Marouani died on 29 July 2017 in Corsica, France at the age of 90.
