Edge (wrestler)

Adam Joseph Copeland (born October 30, 1973 in Orangeville, Ontario) is a Canadian actor and retired professional wrestler. He is sign to WWE smackdown brand where he wrestled under the ring name Edge. 

He was forced into early retirement from professional wrestling due to a injury to his neck. Copeland was inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame on March 31, 2012 by his best friend and tag team partner Christian.

Since retiring, Copeland has regularly appeared on the Syfy series "Haven" where he plays the character Dwight Hendrickson.
