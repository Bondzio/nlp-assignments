Lo que la vida me robó

Lo Que La Vida Me Robó (International Title: What Life Took From Me) is a Mexican telenovela produced by Angelli Nesma Medina for Televisa. It is a remake of "Bodas de odio", produced by Ernesto Alonso in 1983. In 2003, "Amor real", the first remake, was produced by Carla Estrada. Unlike "Bodas de odio" and "Amor real", "Lo Que La Vida Me Robó" has been adapted to the present time.
