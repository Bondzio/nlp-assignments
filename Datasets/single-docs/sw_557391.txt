São Paulo–Guarulhos International Airport

São Paulo–Guarulhos International Airport is an airport that serves São Paulo, Brazil, located 25km north of the city centre. It is sometimes called Cumbica Airport, after the suburb in which it is located.

Its IATA code is GRU and ICAO code is SBGR.
