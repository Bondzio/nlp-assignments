Heukseok Station

Heukseok(Chung-Ang University) Station is a train station of Seoul Subway Line 9, located in Dongjak District, Seoul, South Korea.

When deciding station name of Line 9, Chung-Ang Univeristy, located near Heukseok Station, suggested the station name as "Chung-Ang University Station"() or "Chung-Ang UniversityÂ·Heukseok Station"(), but station name is decided as "Heukseok Station".

Later, Seoul Government changed that decision, and add "Chung-Ang University" in station name. So, final station name is decided as "Heukseok(Chung-Ang University)"(.


Station has 2 side platforms, 2 tracks.

Data of 2009 is calculated from July 24 (First Opened) ~ December 31.
