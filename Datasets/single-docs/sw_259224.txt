Greg Baldwin

Greg Baldwin (born September 13, 1960 in Grants, New Mexico) is an American actor. Raised in Spring, Texas, he currently lives in the San Fernando Valley with Melissa Baldwin twenty-five year old wife and their two children, Sydney and Cooper.

He has worked in the theatre extensively, appearing as (among others) Mushnik in "Little Shop Of Horrors", Dr. Zubritsky in Neil Simon's "Fools" and Count Otto Von Bruno in "Bullshot Crummond." He is also a voice actor, with some of his roles including Boris The Cosmonaut Monkey in "Brandy & Mr. Whiskers," Daddy Bailey in "Xiaolin Showdown" and Jack M. Crazyfish in "Spongebob SquarePants." His voice can also be heard in many video games, such as "", "F.E.A.R." and "Bioshock", among others. He was chosen to take over the role of Iroh on "" after the death of the original voice actor, Mako. He also voices the ancient Jedi Master Tera Sinube on "".
Greg Baldwin attended the University of Houston.
