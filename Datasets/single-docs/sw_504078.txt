Wind of Change

"Wind of Change" is a 1990 rock ballad by the Scorpions. The song is about the ending of the Cold War and hope when tense conditions came up. 

The song was released in January 1991. It became a worldwide hit single during 1991. The song has sold millions of copies worldwide since then. It hit #4 on the American "Billboard" Hot 100.

There is also a Russian language version of the song.
