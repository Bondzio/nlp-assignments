Ed Nimmervoll

Edward Charles "Ed" Nimmervoll (21 September 1947 – 10 October 2014), was a Austrian-born Australian rock music journalist, author and historian. He worked with rock magazines "Go-Set" (1966–1974) and "Juke Magazine" (1975–92) both as a journalist and as an editor.

Nimmervoll died of brain cancer on 10 October 2014, at the age of 67.
