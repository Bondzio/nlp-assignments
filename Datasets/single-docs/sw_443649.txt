Panasonic

Panasonic Corporation, formerly known as Matsushita Electric Industrial Co., Ltd, is a multinational electronics company. Panasonic is known for their electronics products. The company was created by Konosuke Matsushita in 1918. It is the fourth-largest television manufacturer in the world. The company was known as Matsushita Electric Industrial Co., Ltd. from 1935 to October 1, 2008.
