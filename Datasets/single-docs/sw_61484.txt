ITV

Independent Television (generally known as ITV but also as ITV Network or Channel 3) is a public broadcasting service in the United Kingdom. It is a television network of British broadcasters. It was set up in 1955 by the Independent Television Authority (ITA) to provide competition to the BBC.

ITV was originally set up as one network of television stations and stayed that way until 2002. Then they were split into: ITV 1, ITV2, ITV3 and ITV4. These channels are also available in High definition.
