Division of Port Adelaide

The Division of Port Adelaide is an Australian electoral division in the state of South Australia. It includes the suburbs of Alberton, Beverley, Birkenhead, Cheltenham, Findon, Kilkenny, Largs Bay, Mansfield Park, North Haven, Ottoway, Parafield Gardens, Paralowie, Pennington, Port Adelaide, Queenstown, Rosewater, Salisbury Downs, Semaphore, Woodville, West Croydon, and part of Seaton. The seat also includes Torrens Island and Garden Island.

It was set up in 1949, and is named after Port Adelaide, the working port of Adelaide.
