Östen Warnerbring

Östen Warnerbring, born 22 November 1934, died 18 January 2006, was a Swedish singer, musician, composer and lyricist. He represented Sweden in the Eurovision Song Contest 1967 with the song "Som en dröm". The music was written by Marcus Österdahl & Curt Pettersson. The lyrics were written by Patrice Hellberg.
