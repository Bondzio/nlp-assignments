Stanisław Mikulski

Stanisław Mikulski (1 May 1929 – 27 November 2014) was a Polish theatre, television, movie actor. He was a host of the Polish game show "Wheel of Fortune" ("Koło Fortuny"). Mikulski was known for starring in the leading role in the 1967-1968 television series "Stawka większa niż życie". 

Mikulski died in Warsaw, Poland, aged 85.
