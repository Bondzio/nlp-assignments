C. T. Vivian

Cordy Tindell "C. T." Vivian (born July 30, 1924) is an American minister and author. He was a close friend and lieutenant of Reverend Martin Luther King, Jr. during the American Civil Rights Movement. Vivian continues to live in Atlanta, Georgia. He recently founded the C. T. Vivian Leadership Institute, Inc. Rev. Vivian is a member of Alpha Phi Alpha fraternity. On August 8, 2013, President Barack Obama named Rev. Vivian as a recipient of the Presidential Medal of Freedom.
