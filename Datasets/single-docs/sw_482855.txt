Drew University

Drew University is a selective coeducational private university located in Madison, New Jersey, USA. Drew has been nicknamed the "University in the Forest" because of its wooded campus. The Princeton Review has called Drew one of "America's Top Ten Most Beautiful Colleges". For 2015 it has an enrollment of 1,493 undergraduates.
