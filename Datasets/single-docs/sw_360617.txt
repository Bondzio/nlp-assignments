Let Me Stand Alone

Let Me Stand Alone is a book, containing collected writings, including diaries and letters, of Rachel Corrie. It was published by W. W. Norton & Company in 2008. Corrie was killed in 2003 by an armored bulldozer belonging to the Israel Defense Forces, while she was protesting against the destruction of Palestinian homes.

The "collection of her journal entries opens a window on the maturation of a young woman seeking to make the world a better place."
