Royal Oak, Michigan

Royal Oak is a city in the state of Michigan. It has a population of 60,062. 

It is in Oakland County. The mayor of Royal Oak is Jim Ellison.

The racial makeup is 94.80% White, 1.54% African American, 0.26% Native American, 1.56% Asian, 0.05% Pacific Islander, and 0.38% from other races.
