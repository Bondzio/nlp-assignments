Wolves in Wolves' Clothing

Wolves in Wolves' Clothing is the tenth studio album by American punk rock band, NOFX. "Wolves in Wolves' Clothing" was released on April 18, 2006. The producers of the album were Bill Stevenson, Jason Livermore and Fat Mike. The first 1062 vinyl copies of "Wolves in Wolves' Clothing" were pressed yellow/green vinyl.
