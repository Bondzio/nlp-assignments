Leominster

Leominster is a small town in the English county of Herefordshire. The name is said as "Lemster". Leominster is about 12 miles north of Hereford and 11 miles south of Ludlow. There are about 11,000 people living in Leominster. It is a historic market town going back to the seventh century. Markets are still held every Friday in Corn Square. The Leominster Priory, originally a monastery, began in 663 AD.
