Claes Lövgrens

Claes Lövgrens is a dansband from Billesholm in Sweden. The band was established in 1994. In the early 2010s, the band has scored album chart successes in Sweden. The band is named after singer Claes Lövgren. In 2007, the band's drummer Johan Fredriksson was awarded the "drummer of the year".

Claes Lövgrens has played together since 1994. Since new-year 2002-2003, the band is a full-time band, touring all over Sweden
