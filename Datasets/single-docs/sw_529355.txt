John Higgins

John Higgins (born 18 May 1975) is a Scottish professional snooker player. He has won the World Championship four times. 

In the modern snooker era only Stephen Hendry (7), Steve Davis (6), Ray Reardon (6) and Ronnie O'Sullivan (5) were more often world champion. 

Higgins started his professional career in 1992. Because a betting scandal he was 2010 banned for six months, but successfully returned to the World Snooker Tour.
