Tim Curry

Timothy James "Tim" Curry (19 April 1946 in Grappenhall, Cheshire, England ), is an English actor, voice actor, singer and composer. He is well known for his roles in "The Rocky Horror Picture Show", "Clue", "It", "" and "The Three Musketeers". He has released three albums.

On May 24, 2013, it was reported that Curry had had a massive stroke in his Los Angeles, California home. He later recovered from the stroke. Later Curry's agent stated that the stroke had been in July of 2012 and that Curry had been going to physical therapy.
