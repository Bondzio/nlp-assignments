Mulholland Falls

Mulholland Falls is a 1996 American neo-noir crime drama movie. John Malkovich plays Thomas Timms, Melanie Griffith stars as Katherine Hoover and Jennifer Connelly plays Allison Pond. The movie is set in the 1950s in Los Angeles. It is about different types of organized crimes happening in the area.

This movie was released April 1996 for the United States. The reviews were excellent. The box office intake was rather poor: the movie only got back almost $12 million, roughly half its budget of $29 million.
