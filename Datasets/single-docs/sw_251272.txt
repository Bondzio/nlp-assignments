HiT Entertainment

HiT Entertainment is a British-American TV entertainment company owned by Mattel. They have TV characters such as Angelina Ballerina, Barney & Friends, Bob the Builder, Fifi and the Flowertots, Mopatop's Shop, Roary the Racing Car, Fireman Sam, Fraggle Rock, Kipper, Pingu, Rainbow Magic and Thomas & Friends. Hit Movies is a division of Hit Entertainment. They have movies for the whole family to enjoy, such as film franchises based on Thomas & Friends, Bob the Builder, and new characters and stories.

Hit Entertainment was founded in 1989. It has headquarters in London, United Kingdom & New York City, United States.
