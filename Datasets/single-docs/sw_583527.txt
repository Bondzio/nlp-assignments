Eva García Sáenz de Urturi

Eva García Sáenz de Urturi (1972 in Vitoria-Gasteiz, Basque Country) is a Spanish novelist.

She moved out to Alicante, Valencian Community, when she was 15. She lives there since then. After getting a degree as optometrist, she got a job at University of Alicante into the optical sector.

In 2012 wrote "La saga de los longevos" being this one her first novel. The book was published via Amazon.com by her own after she tried to contact with several publishing houses without success. However "La saga de los longevos" became a hit.

Two years later she wrote "Los hijos de Adán" and "Pasaje a Tahití", also with a good reception.

In 2016 she published her fourth book: "El silencio de la ciudad blanca" settle in her home town. In this one, she joined into a police academy, where she took two courses of crime scene investigation and reveal of footprints.
