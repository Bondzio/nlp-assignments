Simon Cowell (presenter)

Simon Cowell (MBE) is a British television presenter and author who hosts the Animal Planet documentary series "Wildlife SOS". He is the co-founder of a Leatherhead, Surrey based charity called Wildlife Aid.

In February 2006, Simon was awarded an MBE for his services to wildlife.

Simon lives in Leatherhead, Surrey. He received a PhD for Biological sciences at Jesus College, Cambridge.
