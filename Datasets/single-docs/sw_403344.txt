Youssef El Haddaqui Rabil

Youssef El Haddaqui Rabil (born December 28, 1988) is a 5-per-team football player from Spain. He has a disability: he is blind and is a B1 type sportperson. He played 5-per-team football at the 2012 Summer Paralympics. His team finished third after they played Argentina and, won 1-0.
