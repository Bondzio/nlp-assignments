Olivenza

Olivença (Spanish: Olivenza) is a territory disputed by Portugal and Spain. 
The territory was under Portuguese rule between 1297 (Treaty of Alcañices) and 1801. Then, it was occupied and ruled by Spain.

Portugal does not recognize the Spanish rule of the Olivença/Olivenza territory. 
Therefore, the border between Portugal and Spain in the Olivença/Olivenza region has never been defined.

Olivença/Olivenza territory is 750 Km2 in area.
