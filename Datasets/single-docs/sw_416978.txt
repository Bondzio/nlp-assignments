Māori Wikipedia

Māori Wikipedia is Wikipedia in the Maori language. It is a free and publicly editable online encyclopaedia. Was created February 28, 2004. As of May 13, 2012 the Mongolian Wikipedia contains 7599 articles. The number of active participants is small, about 104 people (August 2011), while the number of registered users is 17,763 people.

Separate Wikipedias have been created for several Maori dialects, including Buryat () and Kalmyk ().
