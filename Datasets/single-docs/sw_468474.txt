Chelsea Lately

Chelsea Lately is a late night comedy talk show by Chelsea Handler on the E! network. Every episode was presented in a half-hour format, and was broadcast Monday through Friday. The final episode aired on August 26, 2014.

From 2012 to 2013, viewership went up from 613,000 to 571,000.

The live hour-long series finale aired August 26 at 11 pm and was viewed by 1.003 million people.

In 2009, the program won the teen choice award.
