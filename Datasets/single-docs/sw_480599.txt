Teaching Mrs. Tingle

Teaching Mrs. Tingle is a 1999 American black comedy-drama movie. It was directed by Kevin Williamson and was produced by Cathy Konrad. "Teaching Mrs. Tingle" was released on August 20, 1999. The movie was originally going to be called Killing Mrs. Tingle but it was delayed and retitled because of the Columbine High School massacre.

The movie gained negative reviews from critics and holds a 19% rating on Rotten Tomatoes.
