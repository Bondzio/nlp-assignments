Untitled Anand L Rai film

Untitled Anand L Rai film is an upcoming Indian action comedy movie. It was written, directed and co-produced by Anand L Rai. Shah Rukh Khan, Katrina Kaif and Anushka Sharma have the lead roles. This is the fourth time that Shah Rukh Khan and Anushka Sharma worked together after "Rab Ne Bana Di Jodi" (2008), "Jab Tak Hai Jaan" (2012) and "Jab Harry Met Sejal" (2017). It is the second time that Shah Ruh Khan and Katrina Kaif worked together after "Jab Tak Hai Jaan" (2012).

Principal photography of the movie began in May 2017 in Mumbai.
