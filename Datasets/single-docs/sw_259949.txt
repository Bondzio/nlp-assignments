David Legwand

David A. Legwand (born August 17, 1980) is an American professional ice hockey center. He currently plays for the Ottawa Senators of the National Hockey League (NHL). He has also played for the Nashville Predators and the Detroit Red Wings.

Before playing in the NHL, Legwand played 2 seasons with the Plymouth Whalers of the Ontario Hockey League (OHL). Legwand won the Red Tilson Award for being the most outstanding player in the league. 

He was drafted 2nd overall by the Nashville Predators in the 1998 NHL Entry Draft.
