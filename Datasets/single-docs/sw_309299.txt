The Bad Girls Club (season 3)

The Bad Girls Club (season 3) is the third season of "The Bad Girls Club". It aired on Oxygen on December 2, 2008. 

Season 3 was the first season to have one million views. It had beat the records from the second season and first season. Perez Hilton hosted the reunion special. After the show, the Ambers and Sarah competed on . They had done a pilot for their show called "The Amber Show". Oxygen did not pick it up. This was the first season to have the girls on vacation. This was also the first season to have 60 minute episodes.
