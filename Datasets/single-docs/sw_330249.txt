Danny Dyer

Danial Dyer (born 24 July 1977) is a British actor. He was born in Custom House, London. Dyer had major roles in "Human Traffic", "Borstal Boy", "Goodbye Charlie Bright", "The Football Factory", "The Business", "Severance" and "Outlaw". 

Dyer has also acted in theatre and on television, including two episodes each of "The Bill" and "Skins".and "EastEnders" Mick Carter (2013-Present). (Linda husband , Shirley and Buster son , Nancy , Ollie , Lee and Johnny Father)
