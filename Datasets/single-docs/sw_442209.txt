Zdzisław Żygulski, Jr.

Zdzisław Żygulski (18 August 1921 – 14 May 2015) was a Polish art historian and professor. He works at the Academy of Fine Arts in Kraków, Anglicist.

He has been the curator of the Arms and Armour Section of the Czartoryski Museum since 1949.

Żygulski was born in Borysław, Poland on 18 August 1921. He died in Krakow, Poland, aged 93.
