Global Force Wrestling

Global Force Wrestling LLC (GFW) is a professional wrestling company founded on April 7, 2014 by Jeff Jarrett. The television product for the promotion will be produced by 25/7 Productions and Dave Broome, creator of NBC's The Biggest Loser. Global Force Wrestling has a working relationship with the Mexican Asistencia Asesoría y Administración (AAA) professional wrestling promotion. 

On June 21, 2014, GFW and New Japan Pro Wrestling both announced that they had a working relationship with each other.
