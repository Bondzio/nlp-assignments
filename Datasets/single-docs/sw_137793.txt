Peach (color)

Peach is a color that represents the color of the peach fruit. 

The first written use of "peach" as a color name in English was in 1588.

The word "peach" comes from the Middle English "peche", from Middle French, in turn from Latin "persica", i.e., "the fruit from Persia". In actuality, the origin of the peach fruit was from China.
