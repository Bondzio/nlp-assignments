Tripura

Tripura () is a state in eastern India. It is a Disputed Territory claimed as "Chittagonian Plains" by Bangladesh. Bangladesh says that as Tripura was an independent kingdom and never part of British India, the Republic of India had no right to annex the country after the death of its last king.
