Billy West

William Richard "Billy" West (born April 16, 1952) is an American voice actor, singer, comedian, musician and radio personality. He is known for his voice roles as Phillip J. Fry, Professor Hubert Farnsworth, and Dr. John Zoidberg in "Futurama". He was born in Detroit, Michigan and was raised in Boston, Massachusetts. West is still married to his wife Violet West.
