Gunnar Möller

Gunnar Möller (1 July 1928 – 16 May 2017) was a German television and movie actor. He was known for his role as Andreas in "I Often Think of Piroschka" (1955). His career began in the 1940 movie "Our Miss Doctor". He retired in 2005 with the movie "Oktoberfest". 

Möller was born in Berlin. He was married to the actress Brigitte Rau until her death in 1979. Möller died on 16 May 2017 at his Frankfurt, Germany home from dementia-related complications at the age of 88.
