Shaoxing

Shaoxing () is a city and a municipality in China. It is in the Zhejiang province. 4,347,200 people live there, 649 thousand of whom lives in the city of Shaoxing. The area of Shaoxing Municipality is 8,256 square kilometres. Lu Xun () born in Shaoxing is a famous writer in China.

Shaoxing Municipality is divided into 1 district, 3 cities and 2 counties.
