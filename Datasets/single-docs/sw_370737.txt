Game Show Network

Game Show Network, or GSN, is a cable television network. It shows game show episodes. It airs in the United States. Some shows the network airs include "Pyramid", "Minute to Win It", "Family Feud", "Card Sharks", "Idiotest","Skin Wars" and others.

The company is jointly owned by DirecTV and Sony Pictures Television. The channel was launched on December 1, 1994.
