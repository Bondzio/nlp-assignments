Massive Attack discography

Massive Attack is a British trip-hop group. It was started in 1988 by Robert Del Naja, Grantley Marshall, and Andrew Vowles. They are in Bristol, England. They made 5 studio albums, 2 extended play (EP), 3 compliations, and 16 singles.

 I The "Massive Attack" EP and "Splitting the Atom" EP charted on the UK Singles Chart and Swiss Singles Top 100, despite being extended-play length releases.
