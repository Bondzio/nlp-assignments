Lamborghini Jalpa

The Lamborghini Jalpa is a sport car that was built by Lamborghini from 1981 to 1988. The Jalpa is named after the type of fighting bull. The Jalpa was replaced by the Lamborghini Gallardo. The designer of the Jalpa was Carrozzeria Bertone. The production of the Jalpa ended in 1988 due to failing sales of the car.
