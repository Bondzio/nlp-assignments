Shunichiro Okano

Okano was coach of the Japanese football team at the 1964 Summer Olympics in Tokyo in 1964. He won a bronze medal at the 1968 Summer Olympics in Mexico City.

In 1990, Okano became a member of the International Olympic Committee (IOC). After his retirement in 2011, he was granted honorary IOC membership status.

Okano died from lung cancer on 2 February 2017 at a Tokyo hospital. He was 85.
