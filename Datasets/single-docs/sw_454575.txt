Insurgent

Insurgent is a 2012 dystopian novel by Veronica Roth. The book is a sequel to the novel "Divergent" and is followed by the final book in the trilogy, "Allegiant". It was number four on the New York Times Best Seller List for Young Adult fiction.

"Insurgent" starts off right after "Divergent" ends, and continues to tell the story of Beatrice Prior.
