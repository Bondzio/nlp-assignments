Jim Jefferies (comedian)

Geoff James Nugent (born 14 February 1977), better known as Jim Jefferies, is an Australian stand-up comedian, actor and writer. He is well known in the United Kingdom and the United States. He also has a number of Netflix specials. In 2017, he began starring in the Comedy Central talk show "The Jim Jefferies Show".

Jefferies was born in Perth, Western Australia. He has a son, Hank, with actress Kate Luyben.
