Teori Zavascki

Teori Albino Zavascki (August 15, 1948 â€“ January 19, 2017) was a Brazilian justice of the Supreme Court of Brazil serving since November 29, 2012. At the time of his death, he was the justice in charge of the trials resulting from Operation Car Wash. He was born in Faxinal dos Guedes, Brazil. From 2003 through 2012, Zavascki served as Minister of Superior Court of Justice.

Zavascki was killed in a plane crash near Paraty, Brazil on January 19, 2017, aged 68.
