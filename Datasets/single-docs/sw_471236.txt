Anthony Anderson

Anthony Anderson (born August 15, 1970) is an American actor, screenwriter, voice artist, and comedian. He has starred in his own sitcom "All About the Andersons", as well as "The Bernie Mac Show" during the fifth and final season. 

He is known for his leading roles in television dramas, such as "K-Ville", "The Shield", and "Law & Order". Anderson has also had supporting roles in films, such as "Transformers", "The Departed", "", "Scream 4", and "Kangaroo Jack".
