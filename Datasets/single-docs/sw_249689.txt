Cape Town Stadium

Cape Town Stadium is a sports stadium in Cape Town, South Africa. It was first built in 1987. At that time, it was named the FNB Stadium. 

The stadium will host the 2010 FIFA World Cup. Is one of the 10 stadiums where World Cup games will be played. FIFA calls this stadium "Green Point Stadium".
