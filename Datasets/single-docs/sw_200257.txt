Sven Lõhmus

Sven Lõhmus (born July 13, 1972) is an Estonian music producer. He has worked with a number of successful Baltic pop acts, including pop-rock groups Vanilla Ninja, Suntribe and Urban Symphony.

Three times, in 2003, 2004, and 2010, he won the award for "best author" to the "Eesti Popmuusika Aastaauhinnad".
