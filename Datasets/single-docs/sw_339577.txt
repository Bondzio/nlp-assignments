Maidan Shar

Maidan Shar ( - Maidān Šhār; ; also "Maydan", "Meydan", "Maidan") is the capital of Wardak Province, Afghanistan. About 35,008 people live in the town as of 2003.

Most of the people in Maidan Shar do agriculture, livestock, gardening and trade. Many of the people are poor.

By the end of 2008, there were a total of 14 schools with about 9268 students. There were about 275 teachers teaching in these schools.
