Saint Petersburg Metro

Saint Petersburg Metro is the rapid transit system in the city of Saint Petersburg and Leningrad Oblast (Province) in Russia. It opened on 15 November 1955.

This metro is a combination of strong transport system and beautiful stations with expensive decorations. It is a good addition to the historic image of the city.

There are 5 metro lines and 67 stations. 2-3 million people use it every day.
