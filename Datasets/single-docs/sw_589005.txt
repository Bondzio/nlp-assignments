Saetgang Station

Saetgang Station is a train station of Seoul Subway Line 9, located in Yeouido, Seoul, South Korea.


Station has 2 side platforms, 4 tracks; two tracks are for passing express train. As local(All stop) train stops at platform, express train passes by using the passing track.

Data of 2009 is calculated from July 24 (First Opened) ~ December 31.
