Stoyanka Mutafova (n√©e Stoyana-Maria Konstantinova Mutafova; ; born 2 February 1922) is a famous Bulgarian actress. During her career, she has starred in over 53 theatrical plays and 25 movies.

Mutafova was nicknamed "Ms. Natural Disaster" for a play she performs in and "The Queen of Bulgarian comedy", she is an official applicant for a Guinness Book of World Record as the actress with the longest active professional career. 

At the age of 94 in 2016, she toured the theater halls in major cities of the United States, Canada, the Netherlands, Switzerland, Great Britain and Germany.
