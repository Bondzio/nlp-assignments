Anna Faris

Anna Kay Faris (born November 29, 1976) is an American movie, television, stage, voice actress, comedian and singer who is known for her roles in all four "Scary Movie"s, "Lost in Translation", "Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs" and "The House Bunny".

Faris was born on November 29, 1976 in Baltimore, Maryland. She was raised in Edmonds, Washington. She studied at Edmonds-Woodway High School and at the University of Washington. She was married to Ben Indra from 2004 until they divorced in 2008. She has been married to Chris Pratt since 2009. She has a son, Jack Pratt by her second husband Chris Pratt. In 2017, Faris and Pratt legally separated.
