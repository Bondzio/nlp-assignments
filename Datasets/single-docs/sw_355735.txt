Tucker Carlson

Tucker Carlson (born May 16, 1969) is an American political news and libertarian conservative commentator for Fox News. He is a contributing editor for the "Weekly Standard". He started his television career as a political analyst for the Cable News Network(CNN) and also a host on that network, co-hosting The Spin Room and Crossfire. He left CNN in 2004. He then started hosting a show on MSNBC. He currently co-hosts a weekend morning talk show on Fox News Channel. 

Tucker was born in San Francisco and grew up in Carlsbad, California.

In 2006, he was a contestant on "Dancing with the Stars". He was the first person eliminated from the competition.
