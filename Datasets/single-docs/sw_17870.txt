Jacksonville, Florida

Jacksonville is a city in northeast Florida, USA. It is home to the University of North Florida. It is the county seat of Duval County.

It is the largest city in the Continental United States in terms of area. It covers nearly all of Duval County in North Florida. It is also the largest city in Florida and one of the 15 largest cities in the United States. The Jacksonville Jaguars of the NFL play here.
