Uwe Friedrichsen

Uwe Friedrichsen (27 May 1934 – 30 April 2016) was a German movie and television actor. He was born in Hamburg, Germany. He was known for his roles in "Faust", "John Kling", "Maximilian von Mexiko", "Sesamstraße", and "Schwarz Rot Gold".

Friedrichsen died in Hamburg, Germany from complications of pneumonia on 30 April 2016, aged 81.
