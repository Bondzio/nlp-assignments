Love on the Brain

"Love on the Brain" is a song recorded by Barbadian singer Rihanna. It is the fourth single from her eighth studio album, "Anti". She wrote the song with Fred Ball and Joseph Angel, who produced it. The song has reached number 5 on the "Billboard" Hot 100, becoming her 30th top-ten hit in the United States.

The song begins with the lyrics, "And you got me like, "Oh" / What you want from me?" while Rihanna sings in the chorus, "Must be love on the brain / That's got me cursing my name."
