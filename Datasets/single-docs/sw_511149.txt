Susan Sheridan

Susan Rose Sheridan (24 February 1947 â€“ 9 August 2015) was a British actress. She was born in Newcastle, England.

Her voice acting roles included Noddy in the Cosgrove Hall/BBC Television series "Noddy's Toyland Adventures", Princess Sylvia in "Muzzy in Gondoland", Trillian in the radio series "The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy" and Princess Eilonwy in the animated movie "The Black Cauldron".

Sheridan died at home in London on 9 August 2015, aged 68.
