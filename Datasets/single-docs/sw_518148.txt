Living My Life (Grace Jones album)

Living My Life is the sixth studio album by Jamaican recording artist Grace Jones. The album was released on 7 November 1982 by Island Records.

"Living My Life" was Jones' third reggae, electronica and R&B album. It was also her last album to be recorded using those genres. Her next set of albums would mainly be recorded in a funk and R&B style.
