Emily Mortimer

Emily Kathleen A. Mortimer (born December 1, 1971) is a British movie, stage, television, stage actress, and comedian. She was in "30 Rock", "Scream 3", "Match Point", "Lars and the Real Girl", "Hugo", "Cars 2", "Shutter Island", "Harry Brown", "Cars 2", "The Pink Panther", "The Pink Panther 2", and "The Newsroom". She was born in London, England on December 1, 1971. She has been married to Alessandro Nivola since 2003.
