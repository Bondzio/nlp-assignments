Parides erithalion

The Variable Cattleheart ("Parides erithalion") is a species of butterfly. It belongs in the family Papilionidae. It is found in North America and South America.

The upper side of the wings is black. The hind wing (bottom wing) has a row of red spots. The underside of the wings is also black. The hind wing has a row of pink and whitish-colored spots.

The eggs are a brownish color. The caterpillar is brownish-black. It has white and reddish-brown areas.

The Variable Cattleheart feeds on "Aristolochia cordiflora".
