Bobby Roode

Robert F. "Bobby" Roode, Jr. (born June 1, 1977 in Peterborough, Ontario) is a Canadian professional wrestler. He is currently signed to World Wrestling Entertainment (2016-Present) where he is part of the NXT brand, and is the current reigning NXT World Heavyweight Champion. 

He was a member of the tag team Beer Money, Inc. along with James Storm. Roode also wrestled in dark matches with World Wrestling Entertainment.
