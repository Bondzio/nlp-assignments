Sukhumi

Sukhumi or Sokhumi () is a city in Georgia. It is the capital city of the Autonomous Republic of Abkhazia. 

Sokhumi was a multi-ethnic city. The people were Georgians, Abkhazians (Apsuans), Russians, Armenians and Greeks during Soviet Rule. During the Georgian-Abkhaz conflict Georgians in Abkhazia were forced to leave. Most Greeks have also left.
