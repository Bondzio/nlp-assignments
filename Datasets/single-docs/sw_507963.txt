Bob Corker

Bob Corker (born August 24, 1952) is an American politician. On January 3, 2007, he became the junior U.S. Senator from Tennessee. From 2001 to 2005, he was Mayor of Chattanooga.

Corker was born on August 24, 1952 in Orangeburg, South Carolina. He graduated from the University of Tennessee. He and his wife Elizabeth have two daughters.
