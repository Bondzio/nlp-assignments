Back to the Future trilogy

The Back to the Future trilogy is an American science fiction-comedy movie series, consisting of three movies:

The trilogy is about a teenager named Marty McFly (a role of Michael J. Fox) who is able to travel in time. This is due to the invention of an automobile time machine made by scientist Dr. Emmett L. Brown (a role of Christopher Lloyd).

Living in 1985, Marty McFly travels to future 2015 and also to past 1955 and 1885. During these times he has several adventures in his home town Hill Valley in California.
