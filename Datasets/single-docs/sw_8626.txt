Flubber

Flubber is a 1997 movie. It stars Robin Williams as Prof. Brainard. It was released on November 26, 1997. This movie is based on the movie "The Absent Minded Professor".

In "Flubber", Prof. Brainard discovers a new energy source, which he calls Flubber. He then has many problems such as missing his own wedding and trying to save his job.
