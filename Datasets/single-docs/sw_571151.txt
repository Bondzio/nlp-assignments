Ray Galton

Raymond Percy "Ray" Galton, OBE (born 17 July 1930) is an English scriptwriter. 

Galton is best known for the Galton and Simpson comedy writing partnership with Alan Simpson. He, along with Simpson, wrote the BBC sitcom "Steptoe and Son" (1962–1974), "Hancock's Half Hour" (1954–1961) and "Comedy Playhouse" (1961–1975).
