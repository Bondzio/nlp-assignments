Ghostbusters II

Ghostbusters II is a 1989 American supernatural fantasy comedy movie. It is produced and directed by Ivan Reitman. It is the sequel to the 1984 movie "Ghostbusters". 

It stars Bill Murray, Sigourney Weaver, Harold Ramis, Dan Aykroyd, Ernie Hudson, Annie Potts, Peter MacNicol, Wilhelm von Homburg, and Rick Moranis.

It follows the further adventures of the four parapsychologists and their organization which fights paranormal activities. It was released on June 16, 1989. The movie earned $215 million at the Box office and received mixed reviews.
