Carl von Moers

Carl von Moers (9 December 1871 in Neuwied - 26 May 1957) was a German horse rider who competed in the 1912 Summer Olympics.

He and his horse "May-Queen" won the silver medal as members of the German team at the 1912 Summer Olympics. But he was 15th in the individual eventing competition.

He also participated in the individual dressage event at the 1912 Summer Olympics with his horse "New Bank". They finished twelfth.
