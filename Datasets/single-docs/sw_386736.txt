Jean-Louis Trintignant

Jean-Louis Xavier Trintignant (born 11 December 1930) is an French actor. He won an César Award for Best Actor as Georges in the 2012 French movie "Amour".

Trintignant was born on 11 December 1930 in Piolenc, Vaucluse, France. He was raised in Piolenc and in Paris. Trintignant was married to Stéphane Audran until they divorced. Then he was married to Nadine Marquand until they divorced.
