Zlatan Ibrahimović

Zlatan Ibrahimović (born 3 October 1981) is a Swedish football player. He now plays for Manchester United F.C. 

Ibrahimovic's mother is Croatian; his father is Bosniak. Ibrahimovic lives in England.

He last played at Paris Saint-Germain F.C. before joining Manchester United F.C..
