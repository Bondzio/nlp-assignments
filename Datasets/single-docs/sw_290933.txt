Jaclyn Linetsky

Jaclyn Linetsky (January 8, 1986 – September 8, 2003) was a Canadian teenage actress who has voiced many different people. She was best known for voicing Caillou in the animated television show between 2000–2002. She later played as Megan in the television show "15/love". She died in a car crash on September 8, 2003 at the age of 17.
