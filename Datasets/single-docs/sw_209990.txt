Closer (movie)

Closer is a 2004 American romantic drama movie set in London, England. The movie stars Natalie Portman, Jude Law, Julia Roberts and Clive Owen. It was directed by Mike Nichols. The movie was nominated for an Academy Award. Portman and Owen both won Golden Globe Awards for their acting in this movie. The movie was written by Patrick Marber. It is based on his 1997 play of the same name.
