Panathinaikos F.C.

P.A.E. Panathinaikos is a football club which plays in Greece. The club based in Athens, the capital of Greece. The colours of Panathinaikos is white and green and its emblem is a shamrock. It is one of the most successful Greek team. It has won nineteen Greek championships and sixteen Greek cups. It is the unique Greek club which was a runner-up of the UEFA Champions League competition, in 1971.
