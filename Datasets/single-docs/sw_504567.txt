Ontario Liberal Party

The Ontario Liberal Party is a provincial political party in Ontario, Canada. The party has an ideology similar to the Liberal Party of Canada but the two parties are organizationally independent.

The party is descended from the Reform movement in the 1830s and 1840s. The party was organized in 1847 by George Brown. It won the provincial elections of 1871 and remained in power until the general election of 1905.

The Ontario Liberal Party has won every general election since 2003.
