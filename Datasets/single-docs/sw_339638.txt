The Godfather Part III

The Godfather Part III is a 1990 American crime drama movie that stars Al Pacino and Diane Keaton. The movie is set in 1979 and 1980. It is the sequel to "The Godfather" and "The Godfather Part II". It received mixed to positive reviews and did well the box office. This movie was released December 25, 1990 in the United States.
