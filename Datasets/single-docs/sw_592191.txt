America's Morning Headquarters

America's Morning Headquarters, often called AMHQ and formerly Your Weather Today and Morning Rush, is a morning television program on The Weather Channel. It airs live from Atlanta, Georgia from 5 to 9 a.m. Eastern Time on weekdays. The show gives weather information for Americans to get out the door. A weekend edition, called AMHQ Weekend, was launched on January 31, 2015.
