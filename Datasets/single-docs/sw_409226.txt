Irene

Irene is a female given name. It is Greek for "peace". This was the name of several saints, and an 8th century empress. The name was sixth most popular for girls in Spain in 2006. Around that same time, in the United States, the name ranked at 592 and fell to near 632 the following year. The name was used for several Atlantic tropical storms or hurricanes. Hurricane Irene in 2011 was especially destructive to the East Coast of the United States. This caused "Irene" to be retired in spring of 2012.
