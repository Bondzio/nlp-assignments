Gangawati

Gangawati (or Gangavati, ) is a town, municipality and taluk in the Indian state of Karnataka. It is part of the Koppal district.

In the 2001 census of India, Gangawati had a population of 93,249.

Near Gangawati is Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It is south-west of the town.
