Evelyn Nesbit

Florence Evelyn Nesbit (or just Evelyn Nesbit; 25 December 1884 â€“ 17 January 1967) was an American actress, model, and dancer. She was portrayed in "The Girl in the Red Velvet Swing" in 1955. Nesbit performed mostly in silent movies and stage plays. She also wrote two memoirs, "Prodigal Days" and "The Story of My Life".

Nesbit was born near Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. She died at age 82 in California.
