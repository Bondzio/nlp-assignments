Benny Banks (rapper)

Benjamen O'Leary better known by his stage name Benny Banks is a British rapper. He was born and raised in Islington, North London. He is popular for releasing his remix of Tyga's "Rack City". He is also popular for featuring on rapper Yungen's track "Ain't On Nuttin". He was recently signed to Warner Music. As of 2012 he had over three million hits on YouTube. His hit “Bada Bing” was a commercial success. It debuted 18 February 2012 at #93 on the British iTunes charts.
