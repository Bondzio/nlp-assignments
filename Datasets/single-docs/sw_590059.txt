Arrondissement of Dole

The arrondissement of Dole is an arrondissement of France. It is part of the Jura "département" in the Bourgogne-Franche-Comté region. Its capital, and subprefecture of the department, is the city of Dole.

When the "arrondissements" of Jura were created in 1800, Dole was one of them in the Jura department.
