Woody Johnson

Robert Wood "Woody" Johnson IV (born April 12, 1947) is an American businessman and philanthropist who has been confirmed to serve as the next United States Ambassador to the United Kingdom. He is a great-grandson of Robert Wood Johnson I (co-founder of Johnson & Johnson), and the owner of the New York Jets of the National Football League.

Johnson was nominated to be the United States Ambassador to the United Kingdom by then President-elect Donald Trump on January 19, 2017.
