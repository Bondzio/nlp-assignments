Tanya Roberts

Tanya Roberts (born Victoria Leigh Blum; Bronx, New York, October 15, 1955) is an American actress and comedienne. She has appeared in many films and television series.

She is known for her role as Julie Rogers on the fifth and final season of Charlie's Angels (1980–81) and as Midge Pinciotti on That '70s Show (1998–2001). She was a Bond girl in "A View to a Kill" (1985)
