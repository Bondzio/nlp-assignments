Ionuț Silaghi

Ionuț Silaghi, Count de Szilágyi-Oaș (born March 16, 1990) is a Romanian artist, collector of folk music, writer and engineer. <br> He is the current head of the Szilágyi-Oaș noble family and the current Secretary General of "Ansamblul Oaşul". He is a graduate of the Technical University of Cluj-Napoca and of the Babeş-Bolyai University. He also completed a master's degree at the Technical University of Cluj-Napoca.
