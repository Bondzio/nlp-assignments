Rafał Sznajder

Rafał Sznajder (13 October 1972 – 13 April 2014) was a Polish fencer. He competed in three Summer Olympic Games (1996, 2000 and 2004). He won a bronze medal at both the 1997 and 2001 World Fencing Championships. He was born in Będzin.

Sznajder died from a heart attack on 13 April 2014 in Plovdiv, Bulgaria, aged 41.
