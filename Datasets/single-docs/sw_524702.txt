Bad Rats: The Rats' Revenge

Bad Rats: The Rats' Revenge is a 2009 physics puzzle video game for Windows PCs. It was made by Invent4 Entertainment. It was published by Strategy First. The mission of the game is to sole physics-based puzzles to take revenge on cats. The game is known for its popularity, and terrible reputation, among Steam users

The goal of each stage is to use rats to activate an object to kill a cat. The player must place objects within a time limit. The player's score is based on the objects used and time taken.

The game did not receive professional reviews on Metecritic or GameRankings. According to kotaku, it is a "nearly universally reviled" game. It attracted a community of followers still active in 2015, six years after its release.
