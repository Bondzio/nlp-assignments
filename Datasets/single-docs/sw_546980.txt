Glarus Süd

Glarus Süd is a municipality in the canton of Glarus in Switzerland.

On 1 January 2011 the former municipalities of Betschwanden, Braunwald, Diesbach, Elm, Engi, Haslen, Linthal, Luchsingen, Matt, Mitlödi, Rüti, Schwanden, Schwändi and Sool merged into the new municipality of Glarus Süd.
