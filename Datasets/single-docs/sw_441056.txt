Kerser

Scott Barrow (born 21 August 1987), better known by his stage name Emcee Kerser or just simply Kerser, is an Australian rapper. His best known song is "Kerser Is the Sickest". He was released a number of mixtapes and three albums since 2011. His second album "No Rest For the Sickest" reached #15 on the ARIA albums chart.

Barrow was born in Campbelltown, Sydney, New South Wales.
