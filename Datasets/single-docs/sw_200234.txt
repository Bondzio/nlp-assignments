Meet the Robinsons

Meet the Robinsons is a 2007 American animated movie produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Pictures. It is the 47th movie in the Walt Disney Animated Classics series. The movie tells the story of a small boy named Lewis, who is very smart and invents things but he does not have any family, even though he wants one. Then one day, Lewis' world was changed when a boy called Wilbur, who has a time-machine, takes Lewis on a trip to the future to meet Wilbur's family - the Robinsons.

The story of this movie is based on a children's book called "A Day with Wilbur Robinson", written by William Joyce.
