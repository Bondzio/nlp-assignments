Boxing Helena

Boxing Helena is a 1993 American romantic drama movie. It is about a lonely surgeon from Atlanta who gets obsessed with an accident victim named Helena. Sherilyn Fenn plays Helena. This movie was originally marked NC-17 (no children 17 or under admitted). It was later re-rated R on appeal. The movie performed poorly in the box office. The reviews were very negative. This movie was released in September 1993.

There was controversy with the casting of this movie. Madonna and Kim Basinger were each cast as Helena, but backed out. Basinger was taken to court for leaving the role. She lost, and was ordered to pay 8.1 million dollars.The verdict was later set aside on appeal in 1994, but the actress later settled for $3.8 million.
