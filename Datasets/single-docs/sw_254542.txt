Betty Grable

Betty Grable (18 December 1916 â€“ 2 July 1973) was born Elizabeth Ruth Grable in St. Louis, Missouri. She was an American actress, dancer, singer, and model, famous for her sexy, perfect legs. She insured her legs for $1,000,000.

Her swimsuit photo made her the number 1 girl during World War II. Her photo later appeared in "LIFE magazine's" "100 Photos that Changed the World".

She was awarded a star (a metal award set in sidewalk cement) on the Hollywood Walk of Fame and a star on the St. Louis Walk of Fame. She died of lung cancer in Santa Monica, California at age 56.
