Aubrey Addams

Jennifer Hahner (born May 25, 1987), better known by her stage name Aubrey Addams, is an American pornographic actress. She found a porn producer in her area and shot her first scene with John the Stutterer from "The Howard Stern Show". She decided to move to Los Angeles, California to start a career in the industry.

Addams was born in Westfield, New Jersey. In 2005, shortly after graduating from high school, she left college. She is bisexual.
