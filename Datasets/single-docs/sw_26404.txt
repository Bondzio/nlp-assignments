Even number

An even number is an integer which is "evenly divisible" by two. This means that if the integer is divided by 2, it yields no remainder. Zero is an even number because zero divided by two equals zero, which despite not being a , is an integer. Even numbers are either positive or negative.

An integer that is not an even number is an odd number.
