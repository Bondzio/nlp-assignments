Leonor, Princess of Asturias

Leonor, Princess of Asturias is an Infanta of Spain. She is the present heiress to the Spanish throne.

Leonor is the elder daughter of King Felipe VI and Queen Letizia of Spain. She became heiress on her father's accession to the thron, 19 June 2014. Her younger sister, Infanta Sofía, follows her in the line of succession.


Infanta Leonor's full official title is: Her Royal Highness The Most Serene Infanta Doña Leonor de Todos los Santos de Borbón y Ortiz, Princess of Asturias, Princess of Viana, Princess of Girona, Duchess of Montblanc, Countess of Cervera and Lady of Balaguer.
