Lise Nørgaard

Lise Nørgaard née Jensen (born 14 June 1917) is a Danish journalist, author, and screenwriter. She is known for her book "Kun en pige" ("Only a girl") and for writing the screenplay of the television series "Matador". 

Nørgaard was born in Roskilde, Denmark.
