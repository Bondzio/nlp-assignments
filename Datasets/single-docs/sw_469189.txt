Victor Kostetskiy

Victor Alexandrovich Kostetskiy (Ви́ктор Алекса́ндрович Косте́цкий 12 April 1941 – 6 November 2014) was a Russian and Soviet actor. Kosteskiy was born in Zhmerinka. He was known for his roles in "Wedding Krechinsky", "Truffaldino from Bergamo", and in "Treasure Island".

Kostetskiy died on November 6, 2014 at the age of 74 in Saint Petersburg, Russia.
