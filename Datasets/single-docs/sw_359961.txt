Mr. Lawrence

Douglas Lawrence "Doug" Osowski (born January 1, 1969) mostly known as Mr. Lawrence or Doug Lawrence is an American voice, actor, comedian, writer, storyboard artist, animator, and director. He is known for his voice roles as Plankton, Larry the Lobster, and Elain in "Spongebob Squarepants".

Lawrence was born in East Brunswick Township, New Jersey on January 1, 1969.
