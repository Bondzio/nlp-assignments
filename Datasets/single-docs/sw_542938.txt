Sylvia Kauders

Sylvia Kauders (December 1, 1921 â€“ May 5, 2016) was an American movie, TV and theater actress. She was known for many small but known roles on Broadway and in movies. 

She was known for her roles in "American Splendor", "Analyze That", "Predator 2", "City Hall", two Woody Allen movies: "Crimes and Misdemeanors" (1989) and "Manhattan Murder Mystery" (1993). She appeared in "Inside Llewyn Davis" (2013) by Joel and Ethan Coen.

Kauders died on May 5, 2016 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania from a heart attack, aged 94.
