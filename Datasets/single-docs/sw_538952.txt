Spotswood, New Jersey

Spotswood is a town in Middlesex County, New Jersey, United States.

Its first settler James Johnston originally called the place "Spotteswoode", named for his old place of residence in Scotland. The settlement dates back to 1685.

Spotswood was originally part of East Brunswick Township, but decided to become its own town on April 15, 1908.
