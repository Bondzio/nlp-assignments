Bride of Chucky

Bride of Chucky is a 1998 American fantasy horror comedy movie. It is set in New Jersey. It is the fourth movie in the "Child's Play" series. It stars Jennifer Tilly as Tiffany and Brad Dourif as Chucky. Costars include Katherine Heigl and John Ritter. "Bride of Chucky" made almost $50 million worldwide, thereby becoming the most successful part of the series, although reviews were mixed.
