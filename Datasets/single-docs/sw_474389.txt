Patrick Harvie

Patrick Harvie (born 18 March 1973) is a Scottish politician. He is a co-convener (leader) of the Scottish Green Party. He is the leader of the party in the Scottish Parliament. The other co-convener is Maggie Chapman.

He became a Member of the Scottish Parliament (MSP) for the Glasgow region at the elections in May 2003..

He was first elected co-convener of the Scottish Green Party in 2008.
