Maury Povich

Maurice Richard "Maury" Povich (born January 17, 1939) is an American television talk show host. He hosted the tabloid talk show "Maury".

Povich graduated from the Landon School in 1957. He graduated from the University of Pennsylvania in 1962 with a degree in television journalism. 
Povich is married to former news anchor Connie Chung.

Povich hosted the 2000 revival of the television game show "Twenty One".
