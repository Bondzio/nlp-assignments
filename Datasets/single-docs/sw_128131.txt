Dannii Minogue

Dannii Minogue is an Australian pop singer, actress, presenter and model. She was a judge on the British television programme "The X Factor" from 2007 to 2010. She was born Danielle Jane Minogue in Melbourne on 20 October 1971 to a Welsh immigrant mother and Irish-Australian father. Her sister is singer Kylie Minogue. Her most successful hit was "Who Do You Love Now".
