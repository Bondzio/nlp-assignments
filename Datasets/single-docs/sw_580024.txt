Chuck Nergard

Charles Lester (Chuck) Nergard (born February 6, 1929) was an American politician in the state of Florida.

Nergard was born in Chicago in 1929 and moved to Florida in 1956. He studied at Schurz Junior College, Wright Junior College, and George Williams College.

As a Republican, he represented the 76th District which is in St. Lucie County, Florida. He served from 1967 to 1976 and from 1978 to 1990.

He is married to Catherine Misheck. They have two children. During his time in the House, Nergard was also a part-time real estate developer. His religion was listed as Lutheran.
