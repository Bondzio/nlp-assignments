Dumb and Dumber

Dumb and Dumber is a 1994 American comedy movie. This movie is about roommates in Providence, Rhode Island who drive to Aspen, Colorado. Jim Carrey and Jeff Daniels play the roommates. Lauren Holly plays Mary. Teri Garr plays Helen.

The movie was released on December 16, 1994 by Warner Bros. The reviews it received were very good. The movie was a box office success.

A prequel, "Dumb and Dumberer", was released in 2003. A sequel, "Dumb and Dumber To", was released in 2014.
