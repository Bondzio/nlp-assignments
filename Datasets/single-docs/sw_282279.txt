Fernando Pisani

Fernando Antonio Pisani (born December 27, 1976 in Edmonton, Alberta) is a Canadian professional ice hockey right winger who is currently a free agent. He was picked by the Edmonton Oilers with the 195th overall pick in the 1996 NHL Entry Draft. He formerly played for the Edmonton Oilers from 2002 to 2010, the Chicago Blackhawks in 2011 and also played for the SCL Tigers of the National League A and the Asiago Hockey A.S. of the Serie A during the 2004-05 NHL lockout.
