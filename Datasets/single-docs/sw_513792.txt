Dave Foley

David Scott "Dave" Foley (born January 4, 1963) is a Canadian actor, stand-up comedian, director, producer and writer. He is best known for his work in "The Kids in the Hall" and "NewsRadio" and for voicing Flik in "A Bug's Life".

Foley was born in Etobicoke, Ontario. His mother, Mary, was a homemaker. His father, Michael, was a steam-fitter. His father is of Irish descent and his mother is of English, Irish, Scottish and Welsh descent.
