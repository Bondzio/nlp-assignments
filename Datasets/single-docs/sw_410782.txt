List of television stations in the Philippines

This is a list of television stations in the Philippines. The major free-to-air networks are often known by the names of their main channels (example GMA-7 instead of GMA Network, Inc.). Analog TV in the Philippines began to switch off on February 28, 2017, and is scheduled to complete by 2023.

<nowiki>*</nowiki> Signals can be seen in some parts of Metro Manila.
