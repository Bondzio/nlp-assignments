America's Next Top Model, Cycle 19

America's Next Top Model, Cycle 19, called "College Edition", is the nineteenth installment of "America's Next Top Model". The first broadcast was on August 24, 2012. It is the first cycle that featured social media voting, challenge and judges score, and comeback series. The international destination for this cycle is Kingston, Jamaica. It also is the first cycle that featured an eliminated girl comeback the competition. Laura James from Cambridge won the competition.
