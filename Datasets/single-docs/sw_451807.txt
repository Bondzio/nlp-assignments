Marriage Italian Style

Marriage Italian Style is a 1964 movie. The movie is about a cynical business man who goes through an on-off relationship. Marcello Mastroianni plays Domenico. Sophia Loren plays Filomena.

This movie was nominated for two Academy Awards. In 1965, Sophia Loren was nominated for Best Actress in a Leading Role. In 1966, the movie was nominated for Best Foreign Language Film.
