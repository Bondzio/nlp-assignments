Pavel Morozenko

Pavel Semenovich Morozenko (; ; born July 5, 1939 â€“ died July 14, 1991) was a Soviet movie and theatre actor.

In 1960, Morozenko graduated from the "National University of theatre, cinema and television of Karpenko-Kary", and in the same year, he made his movie debut in "Roman and Francesca", a movie directed by Vladimir Denisenko. In 1973, he was awarded the title of "Honored artist of the Ukrainian SSR".

Morozenko died on July 14, 1991. He was aged 52.
