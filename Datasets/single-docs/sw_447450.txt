Not Alone (Aram Mp3 song)

"Not Alone" is a song by Armenian singer Aram Mp3. This song will represent Armenia in the Eurovision Song Contest 2014 in Denmark. Bet makers such as Bwin and Sky Bet predict that this song will come first.

The video was set in the Armenian capital Yerevan. The video shows a boyfriend and girlfriend having a fight in the girlfriend's car. The boyfriend's frustration then leads him to leave the car, which leaves both of them crying.

This song was performed in the Eurovision concert in Amsterdam.

The song will be sung first in the first semi-final of Eurovison 2014 which will be held in Denmark.
