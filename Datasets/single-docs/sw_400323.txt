Alicia Martinez Gragera

Alicia Martinez Gragera (born September 24, 1975 in Badajoz) is a track and field athlete from Spain. She has a disability: She has cerebral palsy and a T37 type athlete. She raced at the 1996 Summer Paralympics. She finished third in the 200 meter T34-T37 race. She raced at the 2000 Summer Paralympics in Sydney, Australia.
