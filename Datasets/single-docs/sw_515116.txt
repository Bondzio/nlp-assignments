Echo (Leona Lewis album)

Echo is the second studio album by British recording artist Leona Lewis. It was released in November 2009 by Syco.

The music on "Echo" is a mixture of europop and R&B. It also has a Cover version of the Oasis song "Stop Crying Your Heart Out" Two singles were released from the album. One of them was the R&B ballad "Happy". The album also features a duet with American pop rock group OneRepublic called "Lost Then Found".

"Echo" met mixed to favourable reviews by critics.
