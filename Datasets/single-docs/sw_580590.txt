Jonathan Banks

Jonathan Ray Banks (born January 31, 1947) is an American actor. His known movie roles were in the movies "Airplane!", "48 Hrs.", and "Beverly Hills Cop". He later became well known and won many awards for his role as Mike Ehrmantraut in the television series "Breaking Bad" and its spin-off, "Better Call Saul", as well as Frank McPike in "Wiseguy".

Banks played the voice of James Gordon in the 2015 video game "".

In 2012, Banks won a Saturn Award for Best Supporting Actor on Television.

Banks was born in Washington, D.C.. He was raised in Terre Haute, Indiana. He studied at the University of Indiana Bloomington.
