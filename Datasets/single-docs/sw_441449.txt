Alice in Videoland

Alice in Videoland is an electroclash band from Sweden. The band started in 2002. Toril Lindqvist is the singer. 

In 2003 the band released their first album "Maiden Voyage" in Sweden. In 2005 they released their second album "Outrageous!". The band was signed by the American record label Storming The Base. In 2008 the band's third album "She's a Machine!" was released. "She's a Machine" was released as the album's first single. In 2010 the band's fourth album "A Million Thoughts and They're All About You" was released.
