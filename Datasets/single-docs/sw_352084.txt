List of The Ren and Stimpy Show episodes

This is a list of episodes of the animated series "The Ren & Stimpy Show". It was first on Nickelodeon on August 11, 1991. It went for five seasons for 52 episodes.

Seven years after "The Ren and Stimpy Show" was cancelled by Nickelodeon, its creator, John Kricfalusi, made a new series about the characters. It was called "Ren & Stimpy "Adult Party Cartoon"". It was for a more adult audience.

NOTE: These episodes are listed by their production seasons rather than their broadcast seasons. Broadcast Season 1 consists of Production Seasons 1 and 2, and Broadcast Season 2 consists of Production Seasons 3, 4, and 5.
