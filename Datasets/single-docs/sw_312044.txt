Codelco

Codelco-Chile () is a state mining company in Chile that uses and exploits copper. 

It was founded in 1955 like the "Copper Office". In 1966, under President Eduardo Frei Montalva, the Copper Office changes the name to "Copper Corporation of Chile (Codelco)".
