She is also the first verified supercentenarian from Jamaica and the oldest verified Jamaican person ever. Her date of birth was variously reported as 4 March 1900, 10 March 1900, and 15 March 1900. However, it was officially recognised as 10 March 1900 by the Guinness World Records.

She turned 117 on 10 March 2017.

Brown was born when Jamaica was a part of the British Empire and is the last living former subject of Queen Victoria.
