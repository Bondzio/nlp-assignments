Innuendo (song)

Innuendo is the first single from the 1991 album "Innuendo", by the English rock band Queen. This single is one of the longest single released by Queen. The video is contains animation and clips from other Queen songs, like "The Miracle", "Breakthru", "The Invisible Man", "Scandal" and "I Want It All". The single "Innuendo" reached #1 on the UK Singles Chart in 1991.
