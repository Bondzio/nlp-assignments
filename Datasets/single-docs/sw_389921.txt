The Switch (2010 movie)

The Switch is a 2010 movie. It is a romantic comedy about a woman who wants to get pregnant. The movie stars Jennifer Aniston, Jason Bateman, Patrick Wilson and Juliette Lewis. "The Switch" was the last movie to be distributed by Miramax before that company was sold by Disney. It was released on August 20, 2010. The movie was not well liked by critics. It made almost 50 million dollars at the box office.
