Terry Crews

Terry Alan Crews, Jr. (born July 30, 1968) is an American stand-up comedian, actor, voice artist, and comedian. He is known for coming out in "Everybody Hates Chris", "The Expendables", "White Chicks", "The 6th Day", "Norbit", "Are We There Yet?", and other movies and television series. He is a retired NFL player. Crews is from Flint, Michigan. Crews will be the next host of "Who Wants To Be A Millionaire" on Fall 2014.
