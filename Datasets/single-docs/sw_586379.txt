Club Penguin Island

Club Penguin Island is a Massively multiplayer online role-playing game. It was created by Disney Canada Inc. for iOS and Android smartphones. The game was released on March 29, 2017.

Club Penguin Island uses a three dimensional game engine rather than Adobe Flash Player like the original Club Penguin.

The game has received mostly negative reviews by critics and users.

The game was originally known as "Project: Super Secret" to the public. It was announced as "Club Penguin Island" on November 17, 2016.
