Mario &amp; Luigi: Superstar Saga

Mario & Luigi: Superstar Saga is a game for the Game Boy Advance. It is the third Mario RPG, after "" for the SNES and "Paper Mario" for the Nintendo 64. However, it is the first game out of three that stars both Mario and Luigi. The other two games starring both Mario and Luigi are "" and "". "Mario & Luigi: Superstar Saga" was released in North America on November 17, 2003 and in both Japan, Europe and Australia on November 21, 2003.

The Beanbean Kingdom ambassador comes to pay an unfriendly visit. They take Princess Peach's voice and then reveal they are the witch Cackletta and servant Fawful. Bowser hears about this when Mario beats him as first boss. Then Bowser helps out by bringing them to the Beanbean kingdom. But the Koopa Cruiser explodes sending them in Stardust Field. Their adventure really begins.
