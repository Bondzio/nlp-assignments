Robin Wright

Robin Gayle Wright (born April 8, 1966 in Dallas, Texas) is an American actress. Her roles include starring as Jenny in the Academy Award winning movie "Forrest Gump". She played in many other movies and television series. Wright was nominated for a Daytime Emmy Award for her acting on the soap "Santa Barbara".

Wright was marriage to actor Sean Penn from 1998 until 2010. They have 2 children.
