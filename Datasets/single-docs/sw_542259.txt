Romaldo Giurgola

Romaldo (Aldo) Giurgola AO (2 September 1920 â€“ 16 May 2016) was an Italian-Australian academic, architect, professor and author. Giurgola was born in Rome. He was a partner in the Philadelphia firm Mitchell/Giurgola Architects from 1958 until his death in 2016. He became an Australian citizen in 2000. He was known for designing the Parliament House in Canberra.

Giurgola died in Canberra, Australia on 16 May 2016, aged 95.
