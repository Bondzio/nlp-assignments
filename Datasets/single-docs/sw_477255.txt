Johanna Thydell

Johanna Thydell, born 14 November 1980 in Nässjö, Sweden is a Swedish wrtier. Her 2003 book "In the Ceiling the Stars Are Shining" was given the August Prize.

She was born in Nässjö. She grew up in Värnamo. After secondary school she attended a writer's course. She then got contact with writer Magnus Utvik. He assisting her to become a writer. She was a 2004 Sommar host. She studied at the writer's line course at Fridhems Folk High School in Svalöv. She also studied culture-courses focusing at literature and film at the Stockholm University.
