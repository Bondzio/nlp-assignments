Tonia Sotiropoulou

Tonia Sotiropoulou (born on 28 April 1987 in Glyfada, Athens, Greece) is a Greek actress and model. She left Greece to begin her international career when she was 21 years old. She is best known for being in the 2012 film "Skyfall". She also played in the 2014 film Hercules with Dwayne Johnson and Ian McShane.
