Arthur of the Britons

Arthur of the Britons is a British television show about the historical King Arthur. There were two series, released between 1972 and 1973. This King Arthur shows greatness by making peace between the two foremost peoples in the England of his era. The looks of King Arthur and his brother-in-arms Kai are similar to contemporary rockstars. "Arthur of the Britons" was broadcast repeatedly on many local ITV stations during the 1970s and 1980s.
