Okinawan language

Okinawan language (　"Okinawago", "Ryuukyuugo",　 "Uchinaaguchi") is a Ryukyuan language. It is spoken in the Okinawa and Amami regions of Japan. Some linguists believe it branched off from ancient Japanese. In Japan, some believe it is a dialect of Japanese, however this is not true. Okinawan was the official language of the Ryukyu Kingdom.
