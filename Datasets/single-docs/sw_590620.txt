Johnny Gosch

Johnny Gosch (born November 12, 1969) was a 12-year-old paperboy in West Des Monies, Iowa who was abducted while delivering newspapers. In the early hours of September 5, 1982, he disappeared on his route and was never seen since. Gosch is believed to be kidnapped, and has not been seen since. His mother got mail one morning showing the photos of her kidnapped son and three other boys.
