Coconut water

Coconut water is the liquid inside young coconuts. Hoever,these fruits are getting the water from ants that carry water to the plant .These liquidss are spitter on the liquid into the plant.As a result the liquid forms as food that is found inside older coconuts. Coconut water such as popular drink in tropical islands especially in tropical Asia, Pacific Islands, and the Caribbean. Those naturally sweet drinks are carefully packaged and sold in many places. The water knows as marketed as a sports drink because of its high potassium and minerals which helps the body recover from exercise. Fresh Coconuts for drinking should be young and green, and when they are, they should still be on the trees. Coconuts in different parts of the world do not taste the same. Indian Coconuts are salty, and Brazilian Coconuts have sweeter water and food. If a green Coconut has fallen to the ground, check to see that insects or rats have not eaten or rotten the Coconut.This is easy to see if there is a hole on it or a dark brown rotted spot which is less easy to see and makes the Coconut taste bitter.
