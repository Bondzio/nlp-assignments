Manuel Buendia Navarrete

Manuel Buendia Navarrete (born March 28, 1957 in Girona) is a skier from Spain. He has a disability: he is blind and is a type B2 skier. He raced at the 1992 Winter Paralympics in para-alpine skiing. He was the number 2 skier in the Super Giant. He raced at the 1994 Winter Paralympics in para-alpine skiing. He was the number 2 skier in the Super Giant, Giant Slalom and Slalom. He raced at the 1998 Winter Paralympics in para-alpine skiing.
