Pauline Wagner

Pauline Cynthia Wagner (August 18, 1910 â€“ May 2, 2014) was an American actress and glamour girl. She had minor roles in the 1930s. She made her debut in "King of Jazz" in 1930. 

Her first big role was in "College Lovers". She was Fay Wray's back-up in "King Kong" in 1933. She had small parts in "Lady Killer" and "Mr. Deeds Goes to Town". She worked for Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer and RKO. Her movie career ended in 1941. 

Wagner died in Glendale, California on May 2, 2014, aged 103.
