All for You (Janet Jackson song)

"All for You" is a song recorded by American singer Janet Jackson. It is from her album, also called "All for You". The song was released on March 13, 2001. It became her tenth number-one song in the United States. It also reached number one in Canada and the top three in France and the United Kingdom. It reached the top ten in several other countries. It finished as the number-three song of 2001 in the United States.

The song samples the 1980 song "The Glow of Love" by the soul group Change.

The song shows Jackson dancing in a train station and in a city with a group of dancers.
