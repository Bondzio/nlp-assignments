Kirby: Triple Deluxe

Kirby: Triple Deluxe is a "Kirby" video game for the Nintendo 3DS console. It is the 13th platform game in the series. It was developed by HAL Laboratory and published by Nintendo. It was released in Japan on January 11, 2014 and in North America on May 2, 2014, and will be released in Europe on May 16, 2014.

"Kirby: Triple Deluxe" is be a 2.5D (a type of game which plays like a 2D game, but has 3D graphics) platform game. Kirby is able to go into both the foreground and the background by using a special type of Warp Star, like in "Donkey Kong Country Returns".
