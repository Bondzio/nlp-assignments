Linas Kleiza

Linas Kleiza (born January 3, 1985 in Kaunas, Lithuania) is a Lithuanian basketball player who currently plays for the Toronto Raptors.

Kleiza played college basketball with the University of Missouri Tigers.

He also played with the Denver Nuggets from 2005 to 2009.

He plays for the Lithuanian national basketball team. 

He signed a four-year, $20 million dollar gross income offer sheet with the Toronto Rapors on July 7, 2010.
