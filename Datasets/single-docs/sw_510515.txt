Jurassic Park: The Ride (Universal Studios Hollywood)

Jurassic Park: The Ride is a water-based amusement ride. It is based on the Steven Spielberg movie "Jurassic Park" and Michael Crichton's novel. It is at Universal Studios Hollywood. The ride was researched and built while the movie was in its production phase.

The ride opened on June 21, 1996. Duplicates of the ride have since been built at Islands of Adventure and Universal Studios Japan. A river rapids ride version of the attraction, Jurassic Park Rapids Adventure, opened in Universal Studios Singapore in 2010.
