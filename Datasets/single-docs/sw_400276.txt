Ruben Delgado Martin

Ruben Delgado Martin (born Augsut 27, 1976 in Santa Cruz de Tenerife) is a track and field athlete from Spain. He has a disability: He is blind and a T12/B2 type athlete. He raced at the 1996 Summer Paralympics. He race and finished in third in 800 meter T12 race. He raced at the 2000 Summer Paralympics. He finished fifth in the 1,500 meter T12 race.
