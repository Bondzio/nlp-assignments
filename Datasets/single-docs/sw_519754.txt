Vivian Dandridge

Vivian Alferetta Dandridge (April 26, 1921 â€“ October 26, 1991) was an American singer, dancer and actress. She was the older sister of actress Dorothy Dandridge. She was a member of the Dandridge Sisters music group until it ended in 1940. She played Melisse in the movie "I Walked with a Zombie" (1943). She was in the 1940 movie "Irene".

Dandridge was born in Cleveland, Ohio. She died of a stroke at age 70 in Seattle, Washington.
