America's Most Wanted

America's Most Wanted is an American television program about wanted criminals that airs on Lifetime. The program is about catching people wanted for catching serious crimes. It aired on Fox from its debut until Fox canceled it on June 18, 2011. Six months later, Lifetime brought the show back. The creator and host is John Walsh.

On May 2, 2008, the program's website said it made its 1,000th capture.
