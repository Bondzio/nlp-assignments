Omar Graffigna

Omar Domingo Rubens Graffigna (born April 2, 1926) is a former Argentine Air Force officer. He served in the second military junta of the National Reorganization Process dictatorship. Alongside Reynaldo Bignone, he is one of the last surviving () members of the dictatorship. 

On 8 September 2016 he was sentenced to 25 years' imprisonment for crimes during the dictatorship.
