Dick Miller

Richard "Dick" Miller (born December 25, 1928) is an American character actor. He is known for his roles in "Gremlins", "", The Explorers, "Piranha", "The Howling", "A Bucket of Blood", "The Little Shop of Horrors", "Chopping Mall", "Night of the Creeps", "The Terminator", "The 'Burbs", "Pulp Fiction" and "Small Soldiers".

Miller was born in The Bronx, New York.
