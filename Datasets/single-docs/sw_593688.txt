West Creek Township, Lake County, Indiana

West Creek Township is one of eleven townships in Lake County, Indiana. When counted at 2010, its population was 6,826 people and it had 2,664 houses.

West Creek Township was founded in 1839. It took its name from the westernmost of the three nearby large streams.
