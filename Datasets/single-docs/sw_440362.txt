Alphons Egli

Alphons Egli (born 8 October 1924 - died 5 August 2016) was a Swiss politician. He was a member of the Swiss Federal Council (1983-1986).

He was elected to the Federal Council of Switzerland on 8 December 1982 and handed over office on 31 December 1986. He had connections to the Christian Democratic People's Party of Switzerland. He was also President of the Confederation in 1986.

Egli was a citizen of Entlebuch and Lucerne.
