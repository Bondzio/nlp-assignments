Beautiful Creatures (2013 movie)

Beautiful Creatures is a 2013 American romantic-fantasy drama set in South Carolina. It stars Emmy Rossum, Viola Davis and Emma Thompson. Thompson plays Mrs. Lincoln and Sarafine. "Beautiful Creatures" was released on February 14, 2013 for the United States. Jeremy Irons also stars. The film is based on the novel of the same name by Kami Garcia and Margaret Stohl.
