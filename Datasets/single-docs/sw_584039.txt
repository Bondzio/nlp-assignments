Curt Lowens

Curt Lowens (17 November 1925 â€“ 8 May 2017) was an American actor of the stage and in feature movies and television, as well as a Holocaust survivor and a rescuer who saved about 150 Jewish children during the Holocaust. He was known for his roles as Director Swift in "Werewolf in a Girls' Dormitory" (1962) and as a Soviet ambassador on "The A-Team", appearing from 1983 through 1987. He was also in "Angels & Demons".

Lowens died in 8 May 2017 in New York City at the age of 91.
