The Surgeon

The Surgeon is a 1995 movie originally called "Exquisite Tenderness". The Internet Movie Database gave it a 4.8 rating. It was directed by Carl Schenkel. It is rated R for restricted. It is a horror movie. Peter Boyle is in it. It is 100 minutes long. It was released in Spain in 1995. Some of the footage was shot in Canada. It made about 10,000,000 in profit. It also came out in the United Kingdom and Germany.
