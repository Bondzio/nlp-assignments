Siemens C651

The Siemens C651 train are a type of rolling stock. They can be found on the North South Line and East West Line in Singapore.

The Siemens C651 began service on 20 September 1994. Upgrades are being made between 2016 and 2018 to replace ageing parts and improve the appearance of the trains.

There are 19 trainsets with 114 cars in total.
