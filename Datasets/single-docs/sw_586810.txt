Gordon Brunton

Sir Gordon Charles Brunton (27 December 1921 â€“ 30 May 2017) was a British businessman, publisher, racehorse owner and breeder. He was born in London. 

Brunton served as Chief Executive of Thomson Newspapers & International Thomson Organisation Ltd (ITOL) Thomson Corporation from 1966-1984 during the Thomson period of global expansion. He retired from Thomson in 1985. Afterwards, he became chairman at Mercury Communications, Cable and Wireless Communications PLC, Racing Post, Bemrose PLC, NXT PLC, Galahad Gold and others.

Brunton died on 30 May 2017 in London at the age of 95.
