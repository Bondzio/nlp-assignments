Carla Bruni

Carla Bruni, now known by her name of use Carla Bruni-Sarkozy (born Carla Gilberta Bruni Tedeschi; 23 December 1967 in Turin, Italy) is an Italian-French singer, songwriter and former model. She married then President of the French Republic Nicolas Sarkozy in February 2008. They have one child.
