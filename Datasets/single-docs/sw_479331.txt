David Landau (journalist)

Landau was editor-in-chief of the Israeli newspaper "Haaretz" from 2004 to 2008. He was the founder and editor-in-chief of the paper's English edition from 1997 to 2004. After leaving "Haaretz" Landau became the Israel correspondent for "The Economist".
