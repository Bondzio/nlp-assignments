Thaanaa Serndha Koottam

Thaanaa Serndha Koottam () is an upcoming Tamil-language action-thriller film written and directed by Vignesh Shivan and produced by K. E. Gnanavel Raja.


The film is produced by K. E. Gnanavel Raja's Studio Green along with Suriya's 2D Entertainment and music by Anirudh, with whom he collaborated for "Naanum Rowdy Dhaan" (2015). Production began on 1 November 2016.
