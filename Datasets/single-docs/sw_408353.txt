Bessie Barriscale

Elizabeth Barry Scale (also known as Bessie Barriscale; September 30, 1884 â€“ June 30, 1965) was an American actress. She was especially popular in silent movies and on the stage. She began her movie career around 1913. Her movies included "The Woman Michael Married" in 1919 and "The Skirt" in 1921. She returned to stage with "Women Go On Forever".

Barriscale was born in New Jersey. She died in California at age 80.
