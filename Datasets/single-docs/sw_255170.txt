Arn Anderson

Martin Anthony Lunde (born September 20, 1958, in Rome, Georgia) better known by his ring name, Arn Anderson, is a retired American professional wrestler that is best known for wrestling for NWA and WCW under the ring name Arn Anderson. He is a one-time WWF Tag Team Championship, three-time WCW World Tag Team Championship and two-time WCW World Television Championship.

His was nicknamed ""The Enforcer".
