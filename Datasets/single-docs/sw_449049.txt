Norma Pons

Norma Delia Orizi (18 August 1942 – 29 April 2014) known by her stage name Norma Pons, was an Argentine actress and vedette. She was known for her roles in "El hombre del año", "Crimen en el hotel alojamiento", "Adiós Sui Generis", and in "El pozo". Pons was born in Rosario, Sante Fe, Argentina. 

Pons died on 29 April 2014 from a heart attack in Buenos Aires, Argentina, aged 70.
