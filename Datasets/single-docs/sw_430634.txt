Strood

Strood is a town in Medway, North Kent.

It has a retail park which has the following stores: 

In 2016 the B&Q store at Strood Retail Park was demolished, and new shops were built which include M&S Foodhall, Maplin, B&M Bargains, Starbucks and a Gym .

It has a railway station, Strood railway station.

It also has a leisure centre, Strood Leisure Centre.

Strood includes several named areas, notably Earl Estate and Marlowe Park.
