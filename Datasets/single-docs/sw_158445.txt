St. Donatus, Iowa

St. Donatus is a city of Iowa in the United States.

The city of St. Donatus is named after Saint Donatus of Muenstereifel. It used to be called Tetes de Morts, which is the name of a creek that flows through the town.

The geographic coordinates of St. Donatus are (42.360590, -90.538790).

St. Donatus is in Jackson County, Iowa.
