Cartoon Network (Latin America)

Cartoon Network Latin America (, ) is a Latin American television channel it is the Latin version of Cartoon Network USA. It primarily airs cartoons and animated programming, marketed towards children and teens. Operated in Latin America by Turner Broadcasting System Latin America, it began broadcasting on April 30, 1993 as the first children-oriented cable channel available in all of Latin America.
