WWF SmackDown! 2: Know Your Role

WWF SmackDown! 2: Know Your Role is a professional wrestling video game developed by Yuke's and published by THQ. The game was known as Exciting Pro Wrestling 2 in Japan. The Japanese version was published by Yuke's.

It was released on November 21, 2000 in North America, December 1, 2000 in Europe and on January 25, 2001 in Japan. The game was the second game in the WWF SmackDown! video game series. It was the sequel to WWF SmackDown! and was followed by WWF SmackDown! Just Bring It.
