This Is How We Do

"This is How We Do" is a dance-pop ballad from American singer and songwriter Katy Perry. It comes off her fourth studio album "Prism" (2013).

The song was released on August 11, 2014. It was the fifth and final single from the album. The remix has the rapper Riff Raff rapping on it.

Commercially, the song hit the Top 10 in Canada and Israel. It also hit the Top 30 in the United States, Sweden, Venezuela, Finland and Scotland.

The song is about Perry's hangout routine with her friends. The video has Perry getting her nails painted and doing karaoke with her friends.
