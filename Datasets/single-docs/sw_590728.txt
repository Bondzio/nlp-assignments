Maisie Williams

Margaret Constance "Maisie" Williams (born 15 April 1997) is an English actress. She was born in Bristol, England. Williams has 2 older brothers and one older sister: James, Ted and Beth. Her mother, Hilary Pitt (now Frances), was a former university course administrator, Williams grew up in Clutton, Somerset. and attended Clutton Primary School and Norton Hill School in Midsomer Norton, before moving to Bath Dance College to study Performing Arts. Her acting career began in 2011. She has performed in many movies and television programs. These include "Game of Thrones", "Doctor Who", "Gold", "The Falling" and "The Secret of Crickley Hall". Williams got worldwide recognition for "Game of Thrones" as the character Arya Stark.
