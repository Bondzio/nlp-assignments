Ashish Seth

Ashish Seth was on born 7 January 1979 in Lucknow. He is an astrological data collector, who collects birth information of notable people. He collects the information by telephonic conversation, searching notable blogs, newspapers, and emailing notable people.
Seth, studied in St. Francis' College, Lucknow and moved to Mumbai in 2002, to study Masters of Technology in Textile Chemistry from Institute of Chemical Technology Mumbai. He started contacting Bollywood celebrites on phone, from 2008 to get their birth details, to learn astrology and study their birth charts. In his collection, he also uses the data available on internet in form of books, blogs, and newspapers.
Some of the prominent people who shared their birth details on phone to him are actors and musicians
