Yo no creo en los hombres

Yo no creo en los hombres is a Mexican telenovela produced by Giselle González for Televisa. It is based on "Yo no creo en los hombres" (1991), produced in México.

Alejandro Camacho, Adriana Louvier and Gabriel Soto star in the series. Flavio Medina, Azela Robinson and Rosa María Bianchi also star.
