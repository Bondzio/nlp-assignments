James Franco

James Edward Franco (born April 19, 1978) is an American movie, television, stage, voice actor, movie producer, screenwriter, and movie director who is known for his roles in "127 Hours", "Rise of the Planet of the Apes", "Your Highness", "Spider-Man", "Spider-Man 2", "Spider-Man 3", "Oz the Great and Powerful", and "The Interview". He won an Golden Globe Award in 2001.

Franco was born on April 19, 1978 in Palo Alto, California. He studied at the Palo Alto High School and at University of California, Los Angeles.
