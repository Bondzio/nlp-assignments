Lower Silesian Voivodeship

Lower Silesian Voivodeship is one of the 16 Voivodeships of Poland. It can be found in the south-west Poland and in west Silesia (called Lower Silesia). The capital city is Wrocław. 

It base on 4 former voivodeships: wrocławskie, legnickie, wałbrzyskie and jeleniogórskie. Some parts of this area was a territories, which before 1999 belonged to contemporary Greater Poland Voivodeship.
