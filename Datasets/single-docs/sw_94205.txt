Treptower Tollensewinkel

Treptower Tollensewinkel was an "Amt" in the former district of Demmin. Since 4 September 2011, it is an Amt in the Mecklenburgische Seenplatte Rural District. Both districts were/are in Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania, Germany.

The seat (capital city) of the "Amt" is in Altentreptow.
