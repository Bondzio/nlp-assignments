Doomsday (comics)

Doomsday is a fictional super villain appearing in comics by DC Comics. He is a Superman villain. Doomsday was first shown in 1992. He made his first full appearance in "" that same year.

The character was created by writer-artist Dan Jurgens, who wanted to create a villain who could be a physical challenge for Superman. IGN's list of the Top 100 Comic Book Villains of All Time ranked Doomsday as #46. He is best known as the character who "killed" Superman.

The character appears in the 2016 movie "", voiced and performed by Robin Atkin Downes through motion-capture.
