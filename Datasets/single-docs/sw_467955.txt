Ravaillacz

Ravaillacz is a superquartet from Sweden. They participated at Melodifestivalen 2013 with the charting song ""En riktig jävla schlager"". The song ended up 10th. The group consists of Tommy Körberg, Claes Malmberg, Johan Rabaeus and Mats Ronander.

The group also appeared at Allsång på Skansen on 6 August 2013.
