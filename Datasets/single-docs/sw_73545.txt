The Powerpuff Girls

The Powerpuff Girls is a American children's animated television series. It airs on Cartoon Network in 1998-2005, 2009, 2012-2014. It is about three girls named Blossom, Bubbles, and Buttercup. They are in kindergarten and have superpowers. The show was created by Craig McCracken and produced by Hanna-Barbera. A reboot was aired on Cartoon Network in April 2016.
