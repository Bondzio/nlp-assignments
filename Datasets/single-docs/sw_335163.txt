Cullman, Alabama

Cullman is a city in Cullman County, Alabama. Cullman is along Interstate 65, about north of Birmingham, and about south of Huntsville. According to the 2010 census, it has 14,775 people.
Cullman is the county seat of Cullman County, Alabama.

The Cullman City School System operates five schools: Cullman Primary School (Pre-K - First Grade),<br> East Elementary (Second Grade - Sixth Grade),<br> West Elementary (Second Grade - Sixth Grade),<br> John G. Cullman Middle School (Seventh and Eighth Grades),<br> and Cullman High School (Ninth Grade - Twelfth Grade). 

Other schools in Cullman include the Saint Bernard Preparatory School, a Benedictine boarding school (Ninth Grade - Twelfth Grade),<br> Saint Bernard Middle School (Seventh and Eighth Grade),<br> Sacred Heart Elementary School (Pre-K - Sixth Grade),<br> Saint Paul's Lutheran School (Pre-K - Sixth Grade),<br> and Cullman Christian School (Pre-K - Twelfth Grade).



Cullman is in the TV broadcasting areas of both Birmingham, Alabama, and Huntsville, Alabama.

Also, there is one low-power broadcasting station in Cullman: WCQT-LP TV-27
