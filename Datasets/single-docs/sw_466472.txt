She also appeared in movies such as "Django Unchained" (2012) and "". Upham was also had roles in the television shows "Big Love" and "Auf Wiedersehen, Pet".

Upham was born in Kalispell, Montana and grew up in Auburn, Washington. She was Native American and apart of the Blackfoot Confederacy of Montana.

Upham went missing on October 5, 2014, and, on October 16, she was found dead at the bottom of a cliff in a wooded area near Auburn. She was 32.
