Swaziland at the Olympics

Swaziland was first at the Olympic Games in 1972. They missed the next two games but returned for the 1984 games in Los Angeles. They have been in all games since then. They were in the 1992 Winter Olympics.

They have been represented by the Swaziland Olympic and Commonwealth Games Association since 1972.

Swaziland has never won a medal.
