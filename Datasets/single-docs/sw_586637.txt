Sangamithra (movie)

Sangamithra is an upcoming two-part multilingual epic historical drama film written and directed by Sundar C. The film features Jayam Ravi and Arya in the lead roles.The film is being made in Tamil and dubbed in Telugu and Hindi. The film is made in the cost 300 crores. It is said to most expensive film after Baahubali 2: The Conclusion which is made in the budget of 250 crores. The film is produced by Hema Rukmani, N. Ramaswamy, and Murali Ramaswamy of Sri Thenandal Films. The film's music is composed by A.R. Rahman. The cinematography is handled by Aseem Mishra. The sound designer is Resul Pookuty. The film is expected to be released in late 2018 and the second part will be released sometime in 2019.
