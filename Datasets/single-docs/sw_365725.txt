Jack Betts

Jack Richard Nelson Betts (born April 11, 1929) or Hunt Powers is an American movie, television, stage, voice, radio actor, comedian, and activist. He is known for his roles in "One Damned Day at Dawn Django Meets Sartana", "A Barrel Full of Dollars", "Sugar Colt", "Mockingbird Don't Sing", "The Bloody Brood", and in "Spider-Man"

Betts was born on April 11, 1929 in Miami, Florida. He studied at the Actors Studio.
