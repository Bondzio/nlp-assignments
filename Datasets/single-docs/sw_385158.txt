Olivia Colman

Olivia Colman (born Sarah Caroline Colman on 30 January 1974) is a British actress. 

Colman was born and raised in Norwich, Norfolk and lives in south London.

She has played many supporting roles in television comedy, including Sophie Chapman in "Peep Show". Her movie roles include Hannah in "Tyrannosaur" and Carol Thatcher in "The Iron Lady".
