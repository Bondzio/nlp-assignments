Dhoby Ghaut MRT Station

Dhoby Ghaut Station (NS24/NE6/CC1) is a Mass Rapid Transit station in Singapore. It is an interchange station along North South MRT Line, North East MRT Line and Circle Line.

Dhoby Ghaut is the first triple-line interchange station in Singapore. It is the third deepest MRT station (after Bras Basah then Promenade) in Singapore. The deepest point is at 28 metres below ground level.
