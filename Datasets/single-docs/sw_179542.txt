The Greens - The Green Alternative

The Greens - The Green Alternative (German: "Die Grünen - Die Grüne Alternative", shortened to GRÜNE) is an Austrian political party. Its leader is Eva Glawischnig and it is founded 1993.

The party is a member of the European Green Party and its ideology is Center-Left. 
The main ideology is green politics that aims to create an ecologically sustainable society.
