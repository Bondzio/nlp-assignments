Dan McLaughlin

Dan McLaughlin born March 18, 1974 is a sportscaster. He currently calls games for the St. Louis Cardinals and college basketball games for the Missouri Tigers basketball, ESPNU, and Fox Sports Net. He also has called regional college football games for ESPN. McLaughlin was a play-by-play announcer for the St. Louis Blues and was a regional NFL play-by-play announcer for Fox in 2006, some involving with the St. Louis Rams. He also calls occasional Cardinals games on Fox.
