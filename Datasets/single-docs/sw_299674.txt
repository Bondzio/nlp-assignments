Susan Raab

Susan Raab (1969â€”November 27, 2006) was an American theatre actress and producer.

For seven years, Susan Raab worked as an associate producer for L.A. Theatre Works nationally syndicated radio theater series "The Play's the Thing", which airs weekly in the Bay Area on KPFA.

She was married to Oscar-winning director Eric Simonson since 2004 until her death in 2006. They had a one child.

Raab died after her 8-month battle with breast cancer at the age 37 in Marin County. She was buried in St. Anselm Church in Ross.
