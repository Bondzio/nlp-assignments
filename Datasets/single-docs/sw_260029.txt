Chuck Arnason

Ernest Charles "Chuck" Arnason (born July 15, 1951 in Ashern, Manitoba) is a Canadian retired professional ice hockey right wing who played 401 games over 8 seasons in the National Hockey League for the Montreal Canadiens, Atlanta Flames, Pittsburgh Penguins, Kansas City Scouts, Cleveland Barons, Colorado Rockies, Minnesota North Stars and Washington Capitals. He is the Father of current KHL`er (Kontinental Hockey League-Dinamo Riga) Tyler Arnason.
