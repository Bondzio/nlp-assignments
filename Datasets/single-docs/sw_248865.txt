Sami Khedira

Sami Khedira (; born 4 April 1987) is a German football player. He plays for JUVENTUS and Germany national team. Khedira moved from VfB Stuttgart to Real Madrid in 2010. He is known for his aerial ability, his ability to recover the ball and quickly join the team attack with his powerful shooting.

Khedira was born in Stuttgart to a German mother and Tunisian father.
