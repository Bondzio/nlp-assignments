Valentino Zeichen

Valentino Zeichen (24 March 1938 â€“ 5 July 2016), was an Italian poet and writer. He was born in Fiume, Italy. Zeichen's poetry has been praised for its ability to quickly hook the reader. His works include humor. A literary award named after him, the "Premio Zeichen", is held every December in Rome. Zeichen himself was the only member of the jury.

Zeichen died on 5 July 2016 in Rome, Italy, aged 78.
