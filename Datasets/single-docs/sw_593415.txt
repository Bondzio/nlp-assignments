Arrondissement of Vierzon

The arrondissement of Vierzon is an arrondissement of France, in the Cher department, Centre-Val de Loire region. Its capital, and subprefecture of the department, is the city of Vierzon.

When the Cher department was created on 1800, the "arrondissement" of Vierzon was not part of that original department. The "arrondissement" was created in 1984.

The "arrondissement" of Vierzon is in the northwest of the deparment. It is bordered to the north by the Loiret department, to the east by the Bourges "arrondissement", to the south by the Indre-et-Loire department and to the west by the Loir-et-Cher department.
