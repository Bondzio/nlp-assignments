The SpongeBob SquarePants Movie

The SpongeBob SquarePants Movie is a 2004 movie based on Nickelodeon's "SpongeBob SquarePants" television series.

SpongeBob and Patrick head for Shell City to get King Neptune's crown and save Mr. Krabs.

On February 28, 2012, a sequel to "The SpongeBob SquarePants Movie" was announced to be in production, and was released on February 13, 2015.
