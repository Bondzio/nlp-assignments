Chris Kelly

Chris Kelly (born November 11, 1980 in Toronto, Ontario) is a Canadian professional ice hockey forward that currently plays for the Boston Bruins of the National Hockey League (NHL). He was drafted by the Ottawa Senators with the 94th overall pick in the 1999 NHL Entry Draft. Kelly played in the OHL for 4 seasons with the London Knights and 1 season with the Sudbury Wolves. He also played hockey formerly with the Ottawa Senators for 7 seasons. He won the Stanley Cup with the Bruins on June 15, 2011 when the Bruins defeated the Vancouver Canucks in game 7 by a score of 4-0.
