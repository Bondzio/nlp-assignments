Abu Dhabi

Abu Dhabi (, "ʼAbū Ẓaby") is the capital city of the United Arab Emirates. It is in the emirate of Abu Dhabi. The city is on a T-shaped island going into the Persian Gulf from the central western coast. The city had a population of 921,000 in 2013. One of the many main attractions in Abu Dhabi is Ferrari World and Sheik Zayed mosque.
