Jorge Nuñez Piris

Jorge Nuñez Piris (born Jue 18, 1972 in Madrid) is a track and field athlete from Spain. He has a disability: He is blind and a T12/B2 type athlete. He raced at the 1996 Summer Paralympics. He finished first in the 4 X 100 meter T11-T13 race. He finished third in the 100 meter T12 race.
