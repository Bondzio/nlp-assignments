La Tracalera

"La Tracalera" () is a song by American singer Selena. The song was first recorded in 1985 for "The New Girl in Town". It was re-recorded in 1990 for "Ven Conmigo". The original version of the song called "El Tracalero" () was recorded by Selena's father Abraham Quintanilla Jr. Abraham had his own band called "Los Dinos" from the 1960s-1970s. Anselmo Sanchez Chavez covered the song in 1996.
