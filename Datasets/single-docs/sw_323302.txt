Vibe (magazine)

Vibe is a music magazine that is published in the United States. "Vibe" was founded by Quincy Jones and focuses on rhythm and blues and hip-hop music singers. "Vibe" is published bi-monthly from New York City. As of 2007, "Vibe" has a circulation of 800,000. The "Vibe" Awards is aired annually, which began in 2003, on the UPN channel.
