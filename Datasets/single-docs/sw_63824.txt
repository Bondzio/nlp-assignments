Ricky Martin

Enrico Martinez del Morales (born on December 24, 1971), stage name Ricky Martin, is a Grammy Award and Latin Grammy Award-winning Puerto Rican-American pop singer, dancer, musician, recording artist and actor. He was a member of the Latin boy band Menudo. From 1991 he has worked as a solo act. He has sold over 55 million albums around the world. He has 21 Top 10 songs on the US Latin Charts, including eight which made Number One.
