Vin Diesel

Vin Diesel (born Mark Sinclair Vincent; July 18, 1967) is an American actor, writer, director, producer and voice talent. His acting includes movies, television and video games. Diesel is known for his roles in the movies "The Fast and the Furious" series, "The Chronicles of Riddick" trilogy, and in "XXX".

Diesel was born in New York City, New York. He studied at Hunter College. He is partners with Paloma Jimenez.
