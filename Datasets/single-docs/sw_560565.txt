Didier Guillemot

Didier Guillemot, born in 1960, is a French doctor, specialized in infection. He is President of the "Université de Versailles Saint-Quentin-en-Yvelines" since May 2016.

Graduate from the Paris Diderot University in innovation and public health, he starts his career at Inserm in 1994. He moved to Institut Pasteur in 2001.
He worked on the HIV.
On April 2016, he is elected President of the "Université de Versailles Saint-Quentin-en-Yvelines", succeeding Jean-Luc Vayssière.
