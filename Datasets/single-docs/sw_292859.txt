Stamford, Lincolnshire

Stamford is a town in the county of Lincolnshire, England. It is about to the north of London. It has a long history, and has many interesting old buildings. The old town has been classed as an outstanding area of architectural or historic interest of national importance. There are two private boarding schools, Stamford School and Stamford High School. In 2013 a group of parents worried about education in Stamford started a campaign to make a free school there.
