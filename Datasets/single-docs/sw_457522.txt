Kuala Lumpur International Airport

Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA) is and a Southeast Asian airport. It was built at US$3.5 billion in Sepang district of Selangor. It is located approximately from Kuala Lumpur and serves the Greater Klang Valley conurbation.

It is the major hub of Malaysia Airlines, MASkargo, AirAsia, AirAsia X, Malindo Air and Department of Civil Aviation (DCA).
