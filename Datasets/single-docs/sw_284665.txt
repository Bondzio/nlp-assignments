Honda Civic

The Honda Civic is a subcompact and compact car that is built by the Honda company. The Civic has had 9 generations. The Honda Civic has been built and sold since 1972. The 8th and 9th generation Civics have a digital speedometer. The Civic LX is available with an automatic or manual transmission. the Civic EX is only available with an automatic transmission. The top-of-the-line Civic is called the Civic Si, and is only available with a manual transmission.
