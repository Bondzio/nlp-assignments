Scott Walker (singer)

Scott Walker (born Noel Scott Engel; January 9, 1943) is an American singer and record producer. He was born in Hamilton, Ohio. He became famous as the lead singer of the pop group The Walker Brothers in the 1960s. After The Walkers Brothers broke up Walker released albums on his own. Walker is currently signed to 4AD Records. Since 1965 he has lived in the United Kingdom. Walker is an only child. On February 25, 2009, there was a tribute concert to Walker's music in Los Angeles.
