The Imitation Game

The Imitation Game is a 2014 British biographical thriller drama movie. The movie was first shown at a movie festival on August 29, 2014. It was released on November 28, 2014. It is about Alan Turing, a computer scientist who was prosecuted for his homosexuality. Benedict Cumberbatch plays Turing. Keira Knightley plays Joan Clarke. The reviews of the movie were positive. Rotten Tomatoes gave it an audience rating of 94%.
