Charlie Brooker

Charlie Brooker (born 3 March 1971) is a British comedian, journalist, television presenter, writer and producer. He was born in Reading, Berkshire and grew up in Oxfordshire. He is widely seen as a very funny man, but can be serious like in the TV programme "Black Mirror" which he wrote. He is married to "Blue Peter" presenter Konnie Huq. Sometimes Brooker appears panel shows such as "Have I Got News For You". He presents his own programmes "Screenwipe" and "You Have Been Watching".
