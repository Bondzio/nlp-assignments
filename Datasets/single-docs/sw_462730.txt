Gisèle Casadesus

Gisèle Casadesus (born 14 June 1914) is a French actress. She was known for her roles in "Du Guesclin", "Verdict", "The Children of the Marshland", "What Love May Bring", and in "My Afternoons with Marguerite".

She is an honorary member of the Sociétaires of the Comédie-Française, Grand Officer of the Legion of Honor, Officer of the Ordre des Arts et des Lettres, and "Grand-Croix" of the National Order of Merit. Her career has lasted for nearly 80 years.
