Megamind

Megamind is a 2010 animated, science fiction-comedy movie that was produced by Lara Breay and Denise Nolan Cascino. Ben Stiller and Stuart Cornfeld were executive producers. The movie was directed by Tom McGrath. The movie was released in Russia on October 28, 2010 and on November 5, 2010 in North America. The movie received positive reviews with a 72% on Rotten Tomatoes which is "fresh" and has a 64 rating out of 100 on Metacritic. There is also a video game based on the movie with the same name.
