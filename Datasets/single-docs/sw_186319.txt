Kurt Busch

Kurt Thomas Busch (born August 4, 1978) is a NASCAR Sprint cup Race car driver from Las Vegas, Nevada. He drives the No. 41 Chevrolet for Stewart Haas Racing. Busch has raced for Roush Fenway Racing, Penske Racing, and Phoenix Racing. He has won one NASCAR Sprint Cup Championship, which he won in 2004. In 2014, Busch raced in the Indianapolis 500, where he finished in sixth place. The Indianapolis 500 was Kurt Busch's first try at racing in the IndyCar Series.
