Sodium periodate

Sodium periodate is a chemical compound. Its chemical formula can be NaIO (sodium metaperiodate) or NaIO (sodium orthoiodate). It has sodium and periodate ions in it. 

Sodium periodate is a colorless solid. It reacts with acids to make periodic acid. It breaks down when heated to make sodium iodate and oxygen gas. It is a strong oxidizing agent.

It can be made by oxidation of sodium iodide with sodium hypochlorite in a nitric acid solution.

Sodium periodate can oxidize cellulose. It is also used as a biodegradable chemical in the human body. It is also used in organic chemistry reactions.
