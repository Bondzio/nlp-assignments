Steven Yeun

Steven Yeun (born December 21, 1983) is a Korean-American actor. He is most well known for his role as "Glenn" in the AMC original series "The Walking Dead". Yeun was born in South Korea and grew up in Troy, Michigan. Along with "The Walking Dead", he has acted in multiple movies and television series, such as "The Big Bang Theory" and "The Lemons Show".
