Alan J. Pakula

Alan Jay Pakula (April 7, 1928 â€“ November 19, 1998) was an American movie director, writer and producer. He was nominated for three Academy Awards: Best Picture for "To Kill a Mockingbird" (1962), Best Director for "All the President's Men" (1976) and Best Adapted Screenplay for "Sophie's Choice" (1982). He was also known for his movies "Klute" and "The Parallax View".

Pakula was killed in a car accident in Melville, New York, aged 70.
