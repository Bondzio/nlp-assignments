Pineapple Express (movie)

Pineapple Express is an 2008 American stoner action comedy movie that was produced by Judd Apatow and Shauna Robertson and was directed by David Gordon Green. The movie was released on August 6, 2008 in North America. The movie received positive reviews with a 68% rating on Rotten Tomatoes and a 64 out of 100 from Metacritic.
