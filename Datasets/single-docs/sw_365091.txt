Héctor Elizondo

Héctor Elizondo (born December 22, 1936) is an American movie, television, stage, and voice actor. He is known for his roles as Bernard Thompson in "Pretty Woman" and as Dr. Phillip Watters in "Chicago Hope". He won an Emmy Award in 1997. Recently, he has voiced Commissioner James Gordon in "The Lego Batman Movie" (2017). Since 2011, he has played Ed Alzate on the sitcom "Last Man Standing". 

Elizondo was born on December 22, 1936 in New York City, New York. He studied at High School of Performing Arts and at City College of New York. He has been married to Carolee Campbell since 1969. He has one child.
