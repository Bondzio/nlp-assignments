Bibi Ferreira

Bibi Ferreira (born Abigail Izquierdo Ferreira; 1 June 1922) is a Brazilian actress. n 1962, she tested for a part in the Brazilian production of "My Fair Lady". She then received leads in "Hello Dolly" and "Man of La Mancha".

In 2016, she appeared in New York City in “Bibi Times Four.”
