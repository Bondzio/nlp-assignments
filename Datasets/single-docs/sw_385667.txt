Bill Macy

Bill Macy (born Wolf Mavin Garber; May 18, 1922) is an American actor and comedian. He was in many comedy shows during the 1980s. He is best known for his role as Walter in "Maude".

Macy was born on May 18, 1922 in Revere, Massachusetts. He has been married to Samantha Harper since 1975.
