Emperor Junna

Before he became the monarch, this prince's personal name ("imina") was .

Junna had six Empresses and Imperial consorts and 13 Imperial sons and daughters. 

Junna was Crown Prince for 13 years.





According to the Imperial Household Agency, the mausoleum ("misasagi") of Junna is in Nishikyō-ku, Kyoto. The emperor is traditionally venerated at a memorial Shinto shrine at this location.

The years of Junna's reign are identified by more than one era name ("nengō").
