Lester Bird

Lester Bryant Bird (born February 21, 1938) was the second Prime Minister of Antigua and Barbuda from 1994 to 2004. He is also a well-known athlete. He was chairman of the Antigua Labour Party (ALP) from 1971 to 1983. He became Prime Minister previous Prime Minister resigned. The previous Prime Minister was his father, Vere Bird.

Bird was born in New York City on February 21, 1938. He went to the University of Michigan. He became a lawyer.
