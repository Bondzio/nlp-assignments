The Master of the Mississippi

"The Master of the Mississippi" is the second chapter of "The Life and Times of Scrooge McDuck".

Scrooge comes to America. Here he begins to work on his uncle Pothole McDuck's boat. Scrooge has his first adventure with the Beagle Boys finding a lost treasure. The Beagles are arrested by the police and Scrooge buys his uncle's boat. But the Beagles escape and destroy Scrooge's boat.
