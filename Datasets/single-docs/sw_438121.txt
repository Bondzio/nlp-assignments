Phil Lord and Chris Miller

Phil Lord and Chris Miller (Phil: born April 21, 1977 in Miami, Florida, Chris: born September 23, 1975 in Everett, Washington) are American movie and television writers, producers, directors, actors and former animators. They are known for directing "Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs", "21 Jump Street", "The Lego Movie", and "22 Jump Street".
