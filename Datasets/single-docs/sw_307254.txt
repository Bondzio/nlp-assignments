Andrea Libman

Andrea Eva Libman (born January 30, 1984 in Toronto, Ontario) is a Canadian voice actress, television writer and singer who is best known for voicing Chi-Chi in "Dragon Ball", Emmy in "Dragon Tales" and Pinkie Pie and Fluttershy in "". She also made on-screen appearances in the television shows "", "Susie Q", "The Lotus Eaters", and "Lyddie".
