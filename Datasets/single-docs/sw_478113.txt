Göingeflickorna

Göingeflickorna was a girl vocal trio from Sweden. It was active between 1955-1955. The members were sisters Sonja Martinsson (1934–2013), Barbro Svensson (born 1941) and Agnetha Gardelid (born 1945). Their major breakthrough came in 1961 with the song "Kära mor". Barbro left the trio in 1975. Sonja and Agnetha then continued appearing together.
