Jesús González Margaride

Jesus Gonzalez Margaride (born September 28, 1967 in A Pontenova) is a track and field athlete from Spain. He has a physical disability: He is a T36 type athlete. He raced at the 1996 Summer Paralympics. He race and finished in 7 in the 5,000 meter T34-T37 race. He raced at the 2000 Summer Paralympics in Sydney, Australia. He finished third in the 1,500 meter T36 race.
