Walter Mondale

Mondale was the Democratic Party nominee for president in 1984. He lost to Ronald Reagan.

Mondale was the first Vice President to have an office. He made the role of Vice President more important than it once was.

Mondale was hospitalized with influenza at the Mayo Clinic, in Rochester, Minnesota, in March 2015.
