Måns Zelmerlöw

Måns Petter Albert Sahlén Zelmerlöw (born 13 June 1986) is a Swedish singer. He participated in the talent singing competition "Idol 2005". He won "Let's Dance" 2006, both broadcasted on TV4. He won Melodifestivalen 2015. He represented Sweden in the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 in Vienna, Austria. In the Eurovision final Zelmerlöw won with 365 pointsahead of Russia and Italy. He will host the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 to be held in Stockholm along with Petra Mede.
