See You Again (Wiz Khalifa song)

"See You Again" is a song by American rapper Wiz Khalifa featuring the vocals and piano playing of American singer Charlie Puth. The song is featured in the 2015 action movie "Furious 7". It was created as a final tribute to Paul Walker, who died in a car crash in November 2013. 

The single was released on March 17, 2015 in the US, followed by an international release on April 3, and a UK release on April 12.
