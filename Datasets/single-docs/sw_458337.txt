Problem (Ariana Grande song)

"Problem" is a song that is recorded by American recording artist Ariana Grande, featuring guest vocals from Australian recording artist Iggy Azalea. The song was released by Republic Records on April 28, 2014. It is the first single from Ariana Grande's second studio album "My Everything" (2014). The song first appeared at number three on the "Billboard" Hot 100. It later peaked at number two on the chart. It sold 438,000 copies on its first week on sale. Lyrically, the song is about "the feeling of being absolutely terrified to re-approach a relationship that's gone sour -- but you want to more than anything."
