Hank Aaron

Henry Louis Aaron (born February 5, 1935) is a retired baseball player. He played baseball professionally for 22 years. He hit 755 home runs. Even though he has 755 home runs in his career, he never hit 50 or more in a season. He was selected for 25 All-Star games. He was put into the Baseball Hall of Fame in 1982. Hank Aaron was born in Mobile, Alabama.
