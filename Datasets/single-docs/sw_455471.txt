Natalie Tran

Natalie Tyler "Nat" Tran (born 24 July 1986) is an Australian vlogger, actress, comedian and writer. She is best known for her YouTube channel "communitychannel". 

Tran makes "videos that are a combination of monologue and sketches that focus on the humourous aspects of everyday life." As of June 2014, her channel has over 1.5 million subscribers. She has a signature sign-off that is "Your Mum Rates Me." She made her first movie role was as Helen in "Goddess" (2013).

Tran was born in Sydney, New South Wales. Her parents are from Vietnam.
