Nathan Lane

Nathan Lane (born Joseph Lane, February 3, 1956) is an American actor and writer who is known for his roles in "Mousehunt" and in "The Lion King". He won an Tony Award, an Screen Actors Guild Award, an Emmy Award, and an Drama Desk Award.

Lane was born on February 3, 1956 in Jersey City, New Jersey. He studied at St. Peter's Preparatory High School. He is openly gay.
