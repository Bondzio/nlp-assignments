Meredith Vieira

Meredith Vieira (born December 30, 1953) is an American journalist, television personality and a game show host. She hosted the syndicated version of "Who Wants to Be a Millionaire". She hosted the show from 2002 to 2013. She was a co-host on the ABC talk show "The View" from 1997 to 2006. She left Millionaire in 2013 due to other commitments and projects.
