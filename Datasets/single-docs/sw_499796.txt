Borderlands 2

Borderlands 2 is a first-person shooter video game that was developed by Gearbox Software and published by 2K Games. It is the sequel to the 2009 video game Borderlands. The game follows the story of "Vault Hunters" on the planet Pandora, as one of four classes: "Gunzerker", "Assassin", "Siren" or "Commando".
