Craig Ferguson

Craig Ferguson (born 17 May 1962 in Glasgow, Scotland) is an Scottish-American late night television host, comedian and actor. He hosted the The Late Late Show with Craig Ferguson on CBS. He also hosted Shark Week in 2010 on the Discovery Channel. He became an American citizen on February 1, 2008.

He currently hosts Celebrity Name Game on American Broadcasting Company. He also hosts Join or Die on History (TV channel).
