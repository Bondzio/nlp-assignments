Brandi Love

Brandi Love, stage name of "Tracey Lynn Livermore", (born on March 29, 1973) is an American model and pornographic actress. She is the co-owner and Chief Financial Officer of No Rivals Media, a pornography company. She is also the creator of parentsinadult.com, an organization dedicated to parents working in the sex industry.

She was born in Raleigh, North Carolina.
