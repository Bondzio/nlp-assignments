Lède

The Lède is a river of south-western France that flows through the Lot-et-Garonne department, in the region of Nouvelle-Aquitaine. It is a right tributary of the Lot river.

The Lède has a length of about , and a drainage basin with an area of approximately .

Its average yearly discharge is at Casseneuil where the Lède joins the Lot. The largest discharges are during winter, with in February.
