Eric Newby

Eric Newby (1919-2006) was an English writer and traveler.

He traveled to many parts of Europe, South Asia and Central Asia. is best know for his famous and very enjoyable books like "The last grain of rice" (1956), "A Short Walk in the Hindu Kush" (1958), "Slowly down the Ganges" (1966), "Love and War in the Appenines" (1971), "A small place in Italy" (1994) and others.
