John Tortorella

John Robert Tortorella (born June 24, 1958 in Boston, Massachusetts) is an American ice hockey coach. 

He coached the Tampa Bay Lightning and won both the Jack Adams Award and the Stanley Cup, with the Lightning, in 2004. He most recently coached the New York Rangers from February 23, 2009 to May 29, 2013. He was fired by the Rangers after a disappointing playoff run.
