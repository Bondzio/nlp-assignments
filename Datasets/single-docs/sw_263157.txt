Man of the match

Man of the Match is an award given to one player from each FIFA World Cup match (game). This award was created and first awarded for the 2010 FIFA World Cup tournament. The award is sponsored by Budweiser, a large world-wide beer company.

The winner of the award is decided by a fan vote. The fan vote during the match. Voting closed when the match is over. The Budweiser Man of the Match trophy will be awarded to the winning player at the end of the game.
