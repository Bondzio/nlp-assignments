Biz Markie

Marcel Theo Hall (born April 8, 1965 in Savage, Maryland) is an American rapper, beatboxer, DJ, record producer, comedian, actor and reality television personality who is better known by his stage name, Biz Markie. 

He is probably best known for his 1989 song, Just a Friend.

Biz Markie is honored as being the "Clown Prince of Hip Hop".
