Reverend Lovejoy

Reverend Timothy Lovejoy is a fictional character on the long running TV show The Simpsons. His voice is performed by Harry Shearer. He is the reverend of The First Church of Springfield.

Reverend Lovejoy was named after Lovejoy St. in Portland, Oregon, the hometown of Simpsons creator Matt Groening.

Lovejoy is the pastor of the First Church in Springfield.
