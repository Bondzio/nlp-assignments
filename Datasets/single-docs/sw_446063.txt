General Butt Naked

Joshua Milton Blahyi (born September 30, 1971), better known as General Butt Naked, is an Evangelical Christian preacher. 

Blahyi is a member of the Sarpo tribe in Liberia. Blahyi has said that when he was 11 years old he became a tribal priest.

In 2011 a documentary film called "The Redemption of General Butt Naked" was released. Matt Stone and Trey Parker based the character "Butt Fucking Naked" in their musical The Book of Mormon on General Butt Naked.
