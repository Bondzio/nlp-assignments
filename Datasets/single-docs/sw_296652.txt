Mikhail Yakubov

Mikhail Yakubov (born February 16, 1982 in Barnaul, Russian SFSR) is a Russian professional ice hockey centre. He currently plays for the Severstal Cherepovets of the Kontinental Hockey League. Yakubov also played in the NHL for a total of 3 seasons; 2 with the Chicago Blackhawks and 1 with the Florida Panthers. He was drafted by the Chicago Blackhawks with the 10th overall pick in the 2000 NHL Draft.
