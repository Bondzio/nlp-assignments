Nicholas Hoult

Nicholas Caradoc Hoult (born 7 December 1989) is an English actor and model. He is best known for appearing in movies such as "About a Boy" (2002), "A Single Man" (2009), "" (2011), "Warm Bodies" (2013) and "Jack the Giant Slayer" (2013).

Hoult was born in Wokingham, Berkshire. He is in a relationship with American actress Jennifer Lawrence. They have appeared in the "X-Men" movies together.
