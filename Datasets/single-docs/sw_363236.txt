Abhijeet Bhattacharya

Abhijeet Bhattacharya (born 30 October 1958) is an Indian singer. He has won a Filmfare Award for the song "Main Koi Aisa Geet Gaaon" from "Yes Boss" in 1997.

He started his singing career in 1985. He became more popular in the 90s and early 2000s when he contributed to movies like "Baaghi", "Main Khiladi Tu Anari", "Yeh Dillagi", "Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge", "Yes Boss", "Phir Bhi Dil Hai Hindustani", "Dhadkan", "Chalte Chalte", "Main Hoon Na".
