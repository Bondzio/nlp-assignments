Yaacov Zilberman

Yaacov Zilberman (born May 26, 1954 in the Soviet Union) is an Israeli and former Soviet chess player. He became a grandmaster in 1998. 

Zilberman participated in the 30th Chess Olympiad at Manila in 1992.
