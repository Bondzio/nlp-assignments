Brother Bear 2

Brother Bear 2 is a 2006 Disney animated movie, released direct-to-video on August 29, 2006, and the sequel and second and final to Disney's animated feature movie "Brother Bear". Melissa Etheridge contributed wrote the movie's three songs. In the movie, the adventures of bear brothers Kenai and Koda continue. While the first movie dealt with Kenai's relationship with Koda, this one is more about his bond with a young human, Nita.


"Brother Bear 2" did surprisingly better in critical response than "Brother Bear 1", with a higher rating of 58% in Rotten Tomatoes, indicating mixed-to-positive reviews. The consensus is "the story seems lacking along with some annoying plot holes, but "Brother Bear 2" is a worth-watching fun family film that will entertain kids and adults along the way".

It is unknown when a "Brother Bear 3" will be released.
