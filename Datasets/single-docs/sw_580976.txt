Nabi Tajima

Tajima became the oldest living person in Japan on 27 September 2015, upon the death of a 115-year-old anonymous woman who was living in Tokyo.

Tajima was born in Araki in the westernmost part of Kikaijima Island.

She lives in Kikai. She has 9 children (7 sons and 2 daughters). 

As of September 2011, she had 28 grandchildren, 56 great-grandchildren and 35 great-great-grandchildren. As of September 2015, she had over 140 descendants, including great-great-great grandchildren.
