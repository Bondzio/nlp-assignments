Genevieve Cortese

Genevieve Cortese (also Genevieve Padalecki; born January 8, 1981) is an American actress. She works in movies and television. She played Kris Furillo on the television series "Wildfire". She played Ruby in the show "Supernatural". She played Veronica in the 2012 movie "Hated".

Cortese was born in Danville, California.
