Lila Tretikov

Lila Tretikov (; born January 25, 1978) is the current executive director of the Wikimedia Foundation, since 2014. She is a specialist in software engineering. 

Tretikov was born in the former Soviet Union in 1978. She emigrated to America in 1994. She studied art and computer science at the University of California, Berkeley.

In May 2014, Tretikov was selected as the future executive director of Wikimedia Foundation, replacing Sue Gardner and took up the post on June 1, 2014.
