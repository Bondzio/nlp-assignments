Arlene Golonka

Arlene Golonka (born January 23, 1936) is an American actress who works mostly on television. She is known for playing bubbly and eccentric blondes. She played Millie Swanson in "The Andy Griffith Show". She also did "The Rockford Files", "Murder, She Wrote" and "All in the Family".

Golonka was born in Chicago.
