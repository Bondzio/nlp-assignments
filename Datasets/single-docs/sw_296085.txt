Mojang

Mojang AB is a Swedish company made by Markus Persson, who is popular for making "Minecraft". It started in May 2009. It is currently the company that created and updates "Minecraft" but also develops other games.

On September 15 2014, Microsoft bought Mojang for 2.5 billion dollars. After the deal was made, Markus Persson announced that he would be leaving Mojang.
