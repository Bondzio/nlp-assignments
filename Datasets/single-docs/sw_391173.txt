Jon Lovitz

Jonathan M. "Jon" Lovitz (born July 21, 1957) is an American stand-up comedian, actor, writer, singer and comedian. He was known for his sketches on "Saturday Night Live" from 1985 though 1990. He was also known for his guest-star role as Steve on "Friends". He won two Emmy Awards for his writing on comedy series and an Screen Actors Guild Award for his acting in a crew. 

Lovitz was born on July 21, 1957 in Los Angeles, California to a Romanian-Hungarian-Russian-Jewish family. He studied at the University of California at Irvine.
