Umeå BBK

Umeå BBK is a basketball club in Umeå, Sweden. The representative teams play under the name "Udominate Basket". The club was established in 2012 out of the KFUM Umeå women's basketball section "Umeå Comets". The women's team debuted in Basketligan dam during the 2012-2013 season. They ended up fifth and reached the semifinals. They lost there to the Norrköping Dolphins. The club also plays in the BWBL.

During the 2013-2014 season the men's team qualified for the Swedish Basketball League.
