Ben Powers

Alton "Ben" Powers (July 5, 1950 – April 6, 2015) was an American actor. He was known for his role as Thelma Evans' husband, Keith Anderson, during the sixth and final season of the 1970s TV show "Good Times" (1978–1979). During the 1970s, he was a cast member of the TV comedy series, "Laugh-In".
