Vätterstads IK

Vätterstads IK was an ice hockey club in Jönköping-Huskvarna in Sweden. The club was established in-mid 1967 whenIK Stefa and Vättersnäs IF merged. The club played its home games in Rosenlundshallen. The club played in the Swedish second division during the seasons of 1967-1968 and 1968-1969

In 1971 the club merged with Husqvarna IF's ice hockey section. This led to the establishment of HV71.
