Estrella Morente

Estrella de la Aurora Morente Carbonell, (born August 14, 1980 in Las Gabias, Granada, Andalusia) is a Spanish flamenco singer. She is the daughter of flamenco singer Enrique Morente and dancer Aurora Carbonell.

She has performed with her father since age seven. She released her first album in 2001, "Mi Cante Y Un Poema" (My Songs and A Poem). This was followed the same year by "Calle del Aire". It was well received by critics and flamenco fans. She released her third album, "Mujeres" (Women) in 2006, which her father produced.
