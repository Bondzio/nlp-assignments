This Is What the Truth Feels Like

This Is What the Truth Feels Like is the third studio album by American recording artist Gwen Stefani. It was released on March 18, 2016 by Interscope Records, and is her first solo album in ten years.

"This Is What the Truth Feels Like" has received favorable reviews from music critics.

On April 18, 2016; Stefani announced that she will tour with American rapper Eve to promote the album, the tour was mainly around America and Canada and started at Mansfield, Massachusetts on July 12, 2016.
