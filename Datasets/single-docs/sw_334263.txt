Centro Federal de Educação Tecnológica de Minas Gerais

Federal Center of Technological Education of Minas Gerais ("Centro Federal de Educação Tecnológica de Minas Gerais", short Cefet-MG) is a public university of the Brazilian state of Minas Gerais. Established in 1909, it is the second largest university in Minas Gerais as well as one the country's most prestigious universities.
