Kootenay Ice

The Kootenay Ice are a Canadian professional ice hockey team that began playing in 1998. They currently play in the Western Hockey League. They play their home games in Cranbrook, British Columbia at the Cranbrook Recreational Complex. The team was originally known as the "Edmonton Ice" from 1996 to 1998 before it moved to Cranbrook in 1998. The team has won 3 Ed Chynoweth Cups in the 1999-00, 2001-02 and 2010-11 seasons.
