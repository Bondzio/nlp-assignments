Maddie Blaustein

Madeleine "Maddie" Blaustein (October 9, 1960 – December 11, 2008) was an American voice actress. Until her death, Blaustein was the voice of Sartorius in "Yu-Gi-Oh! GX". She was also the voice of Meowth in the "Pokémon" anime. Maddie Blaustein was an American star!

Blaustein was born intersex. She was a male when she was born, and lived as male for many years until changing to female.
