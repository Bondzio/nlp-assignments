Jeremy Irons

Jeremy John Irons (born 19 September 1948) is an English theater and movie actor. The movies in which he has starred include "Die Hard with a Vengeance" (1995). 

Irons trained as an actor at the Bristol Old Vic Theatre School. He played John the Baptist and Judas in "Godspell" for a total of 1,128 performances. In 2005, Irons won both an Emmy award and a Golden Globe award for his supporting role in the TV mini-series, "Elizabeth I".
