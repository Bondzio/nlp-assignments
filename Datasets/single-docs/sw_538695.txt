Jurong East MRT Station

Jurong East Station (NS1/EW24) is an MRT in Singapore. It is an interchange station along North South MRT Line and East West MRT Line. The station is a terminus for the North South MRT Line.


Formerly the Code was: W9 (1988 - 2001)
