Olga Feliú

Olga Feliú Segovia (20 August 1932 – 25 June 2017) was a Chilean politician and lawyer. She was appointed to the Senate in 1990 by the Supreme Court in 1990 and served until 1998, when she was replaced by Enrique Silva Cimma. She was born in Santiago de Chile.

Feliú died on 25 June 2017 in Santiago de Chile from a long-illness at the age of 84.
