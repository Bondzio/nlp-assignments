Metuchen, New Jersey

Metuchen ( ) is a borough in Middlesex County, New Jersey, United States

Metuchen was created by the New Jersey Legislature on March 20, 1900. The land was part of Raritan Township (now known as Edison).

According to the United States Census Bureau, the borough had a total area of 2.766 square miles (7.166 km). That included 2.764 square miles (7.160 km) of land and 0.002 square miles (0.006 km) of water (0.09%).
