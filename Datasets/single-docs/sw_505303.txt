Russell M. Nelson

Russell Marion Nelson (born September 9, 1924) is a member of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS Church). He is also a known cardiothoracic surgeon. Currently, he is the third most senior and the oldest living apostle among the ranks of the church. In July 3, 2015, following the death of President Boyd K. Packer, Nelson became President of the Quorum.
