Short I

Short I (Й, й) is a letter in the Cyrillic alphabet. It sounds like [j]. It is an И with a breve on top.

It is used in the Russian alphabet, where it is called "И краткое" ("I kratkoye" or "short I"), in the Ukrainian alphabet, where it is called "Йот", (Yot) or "Ий" ("Yi", sounded like [j]), in the Bulgarian alphabet, where it is called "И кратко" ("I kratko" or "short I"), and in the Belarusian alphabet.

In Serbian, Macedonian and Azeri, Ј is used instead of Й.
