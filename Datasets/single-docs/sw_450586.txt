Pál Orosz

Pal Orosz (January 25, 1934 – 12 May 2014) was a Hungarian Olympic football coach. He played soccer during the 1950s, but soon became the coach of the Hungarian National Football Team during the 1960 Summer Olympics. They won the bronze medal during at the Olympics in Rome. 

Orosz died in Sophia, Hungary from cancer, aged 80.
