Simi

Simisola Bolatito Ogunleye (born ), also known as Simi, is a Nigerian singer and songwriter best known for her hit singles "Tiff" and "Jamb Question". She is signed to X3M Music since 2014

Simi was born in Surulere, Lagos State. She graduated from Covenant University where she majored in Mass Communication.

In 2006, Simi released her first project titled "Ogaju", a gospel music studio album that was entirely produced by Samklef.

In 2014, Simi released an EP tited "Restless", a 5-track cover of popular international songs which earned her a record deal with X3M Music in April 2014.
