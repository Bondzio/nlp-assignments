Volkswagen Golf Cabriolet

The Volkswagen Golf Cabriolet is the cabriolet version of the Volkswagen Golf, introduced in 1979. The second generation were introduced in 1993, the third in 1998.

The engines for the Cabriolet are almost the same as for the regular Golf. 

The Golf Cabriolet was discontinued in 2002, and succeeded by the Volkswagen New Beetle Cabriolet and the Volkswagen Eos.
