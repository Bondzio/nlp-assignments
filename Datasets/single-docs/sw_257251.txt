Story of the Year

Story of the Year is an American alternative rock band. They formed in 1995 in St. Louis. The members of the group are Dan Marsal, the singer, Ryan Phillips, who plays the Guitar, Philip Sneed, who plays the guitar, Adam Russell, who plays the Bass guitar and Josh Wills, who plays the Drums. The band's original name was 67 North but was then changed to Big Blue Monkey and was again changed to Story of the Year.
