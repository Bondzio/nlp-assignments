Abel Avila Rodriguez (born November 9, 1986 in Manresa) is a track and field athlete from Spain. He has a disability: he is blind and is B3/T13 type athlete. He raced at the 2000 Summer Paralympics. He finished second in the 800 meter T12 race. He raced at the 2004 Summer Paralympics. He finished first in the 800 meter T12 race. He raced at the 2008 Summer Paralympics. He raced at the 2012 Summer Paralympics. He finished second in the 1,500 meter race.
