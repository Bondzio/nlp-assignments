Mike Adamle

Michael David Adamle (born October 4, 1949; pronounced ) is a sports personality and former National Football League player. 

In college he became a Big Ten Most Valuable Player. He is still thought of as one of the great players for Northwestern University. 

After a successful career with the National Football League, Adamle began working for NBC Sports. He then worked for a variety of programs. He was host of "American Gladiators" and worked for World Wrestling Entertainment. 

He has worked for WMAQ-TV in Chicago since 2004. 

In February 2017, Adamle was diagnosed with dementia.
