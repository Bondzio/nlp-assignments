Marla Maples

Marla Maples (born October 27, 1963 near Dalton, Georgia) is an American movie actress and television personality. She is best known for her marriage to the businessman and President Donald Trump. She is a distant cousin of the actress Heather Locklear. She divorced Pres. Donald Trump, being his first wife. To learn more about Donald Trump, search in the search box, Donald Trump.
