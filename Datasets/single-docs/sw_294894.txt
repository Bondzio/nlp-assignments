Red huckleberry

Red huckleberry (Vaccinium parvifolium) is a species of "Vaccinium". It grows in North America. There, it is often found in forests from southeastern Alaska and British Columbia, through western Washington and Oregon to central California. In the Oregon Coast Range, it is the most-seen "Vaccinium".
