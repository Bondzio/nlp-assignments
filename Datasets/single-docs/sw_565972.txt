Ciarán Hinds

Ciarán Hinds ( ; born 9 February 1953) is an Irish movie, television and stage actor. 

He is known for his roles in "Road to Perdition", "The Sum of All Fears", "The Phantom of the Opera", "Munich", "There Will Be Blood", "Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows – Part 2", "The Woman in Black", "Tinker Tailor Soldier Spy", "" and "Frozen". He has been cast as Steppenwolf, in the 2017 movie "Justice League".
