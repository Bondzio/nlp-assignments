Super League Greece

Super League Greece (Greek: Ελληνική Σούπερ Λιγκ) is the top division football league in Greece. The current version was formed on 16 July 2006 and replaced the "Alpha Ethniki". The first championship was won by Aris Thessaloniki. The club which has won the most championships is Olympiacos with 43 titles, next is Panathinaikos with 20 titles and third is AEK Athens with 11 titles. The league currently has 16 teams.
