Arrondissement of Clamecy

The arrondissement of Clamecy is an arrondissement of France. It is part of the Nièvre "département" in the Bourgogne-Franche-Comté region. Its capital is the city of Clamecy.

When the Nièvre department was created on 17 February 1800, the "arrondissement" of Clamecy was part of that original department.

The "arrondissement" of Clamecy is bordered to the north and northeast by the Yonne (Burgundy) department, to the southeast by the "arrondissement" of Château-Chinon (Ville), to the southwest by the "arrondissement" of Nevers and to the west by the "arrondissement" of Cosne-Cours-sur-Loire.
