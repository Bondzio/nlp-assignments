Joel Quenneville

Joel Norman Quenneville (born September 15, 1958 in Windsor, Ontario) is a retired Canadian ice hockey player and the current head coach for the Chicago Blackhawks of the National Hockey League (NHL). When he was an assistant coach for the Colorado Avalanche in 1996, he won the Stanley Cup. He became head coach of the Blackhawks in 2008, replacing Denis Savard.
