Googoosh

Faegheh Atashin (, , born on 5 May 1950 in Tehran), known by her stage name Googoosh (, ), is an Iranian singer and actress. She is considered one of most iconic female pop-singers in Middle Eastern and Central Asian countries. 

Googoosh has been married three times. She has a son, Kambiz. She currently lives in Los Angeles.
