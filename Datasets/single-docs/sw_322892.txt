Rick Jones (voice actor)

Richard "Rick" Jones is an American director, writer, content developer, voice actor, and comedian. He was the voice of Fu-Fu in "Sagwa, the Chinese Siamese Cat".

Since 1983, he's worked with Fox, Disney, Universal, Hanna-Barbera, Alphanim, Cinar and Nelvana. Jones has been nominated for a Gemini Award twice in 1988 and 2003.

He also was known for being the voice of Slax in "X-DuckX", and Razmo in "Ratz"

He was born Richard Jones.
