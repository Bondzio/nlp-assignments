Irgun

The Irgun (), (full title: Irgun Zevai Leumi) (from , "The National Military Organization in the Land of Israel"), was a Zionist paramilitary group in Palestine between 1931 and 1948.

Irgun is best known was the bombing of the King David Hotel in Jerusalem in 1946.

In the 1940s, Irgun was labelled as a terrorist organization.
