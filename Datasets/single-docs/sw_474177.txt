Gustaf Åkerhielm

Friherre Johan "Gustaf" Nils Samuel "Åkerhielm" of Margaretelund, born 24 July 1833, dead 2 1900, was a Swedish politician, baron and landowner. He was a member of the Swedish parliament twice, first from 1859 to 1866 and then again from 1870 to 1900. He was also Swedien's Minister of Finance from 1874 to 1875, and Minister for Foreign Affairs in 1889. Also, he served as Swedish Prime Minister from 1889 to 1891.

He was married to Ulrika Gyldenstolpe in 1860. Together with her, he had three children.
