Stijn Streuvels

Stijn Streuvels (3 October 1871, Heule, Kortrijk - 15 August 1969, Ingooigem) was a Belgian writer and translator.

His real name was Frank Lateur.

When he was young, Streuvels worked as a baker. 

The Flemish writer Guido Gezelle was his uncle. Gezelle inspired him to become himself a writer.

His house in Ingooigem is now a Streuvels museum.

Two times he got a nomination for the Nobel Prize in Literature.


Streuvels' books have been translated and published in Afrikaans, Czech, Danish, English, French, German, Latvian, Malay, Serbian, Slovak, Spanish.
