Khojali Rayon

Khojali (also called Khojaly, Khodjaly and Hojaly) is a raion in Azerbaijan. It is under the control of armed forces of the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic. Its capital is Khojali. The region was once a site of prehistoric Khojaly-Gadabay culture. It was the place of the Khojaly Massacre in February 1992.
