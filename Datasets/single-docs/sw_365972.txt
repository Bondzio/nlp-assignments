Jayma Mays

Jayma Suzette Mays (born Jamia Suzette Mays, July 16, 1979) is an American movie, television, stage, voice actress and singer. She is known for her roles as Emma Pillsbury in "Glee", as Charlie in "Ugly Betty", as Amy Anderson in "", and as Grace Winslow in "The Smurfs" and in "Smurfs 2".

Mays was born on July 16, 1979 in Bristol, Tennessee. She studied at Grundy High School, at Southwest Virginia Community College, and at Radford University. She was married to Adam Campbell since 2007.
