Hans Kraay Sr.

Hans Kraay Sr. (born Johan Hendrik Kraay; 14 October 1936) is a former Dutch footballer and manager. He played as a defender. He played for DOS, Feyenoord and the Netherlands national football team. He also managed Ajax, AZ Alkmaar, Sparta Rotterdam and Feyenoord. 

Kraay was born in Utrecht. His son, Hans Kraay Jr. (born 1959), was also a footballer and manager.
