HaBayit HaYehudi

HaBayit HaYehudi, Hebrew: הבית היהודי , meaning "The Jewish Home" (often called like that), is an Israeli political party.

Its ideology is (far) right-wing conservative liberal, nationalist, Modern Orthodox Jewish, and Religious Zionist.

Political leader is Naftali Bennett.

HaBayit HaYehudi was founded in 2008 as a result of a merger of three parties but in fact it is the successor of Mafdal (National Religious Party).

Number of seats in the 20th Knesset: 8 (-4).

Participating in the Fourth Netanyahu Government.
