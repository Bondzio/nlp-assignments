Luděk Bukač

Luděk Bukač, born 8 April 1935 in Ústí nad Labem, Czechoslovakia is a Czechoslovakian, later Czech, ice hockey player and manager. He played for CLTK Praha and HC Sparta Praha. He coached HC Sparta Praha, HC Kosice and HC Ceske Budejovice. He managed the Czechoslovak, Austrian, German and Czech national teams. With the Czech Republic, he won the 1996 IIHF World Championship.
