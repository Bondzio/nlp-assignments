Dermot Mulroney

Dermot Mulroney (born October 31, 1963) is an American actor, musician and director. He has been acting since the 1980s. He was born in Alexandria, Virginia.

He is known for his roles in the movies "Young Guns", "Where the Day Takes You", "My Best Friend's Wedding", "The Last Outlaw", "About Schmidt", and "". Mulroney played the main villain of NBC's "Crisis", Francis Gibson.
