Joan Carreras i Goicoechea

Joan Carreras i Goicoechea (Barcelona, 1962) is a Catalan journalist, screenwriter and writer. 

He is the son of Joan Carreras i Martí, and is a professor in the Faculty of Communication at Blanquerna. He is a digital journalism teacher there. He worked for ten years at the broadcasting company Corporació Catalana de Mitjans Audiovisuals, and he was the director of the Canal 33. He was also the manager of the program "Info-K". At television channel 324.cat he has tried out new formats and digital narratives. He has also worked for many newspapers, as "Avui", "Diari de Barcelona" and "El Temps". 

By 2014, he received the Premi Sant Jordi de novel·la for his novel "L'àguila negra" (The black eagle).
