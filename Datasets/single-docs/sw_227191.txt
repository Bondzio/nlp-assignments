Darrell Waltrip

Darrell Lee Waltrip (born February 5, 1947) is a retired American racecar driver. Waltrip won three Nascar Sprint cup championships and 84 races, including the 1989 Daytona 500. He is now part of the broadcast team for Fox Sports. Waltrips younger brother, Michael Waltrip owns and races for his own team in NASCAR. In June of 2011 Waltrip was given a spot in the Nascar Hall of Fame. He is a part of the Hall of Fame class of 2012.
