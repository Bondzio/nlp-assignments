Richard Arvine Overton

Richard Arvine Overton (born May 11, 1906) is an American supercentenarian. He is the oldest verified surviving American World War II veteran. He was born in Bastrop County, Texas.

He lives in Austin, Texas. When he was 82 years old, his wife, Wilma, died in 1988. In November 2015, Overton was hospitalized for pneumonia at the age of 109 in Austin. On May 11, 2016, Overton became a supercentenarian.
