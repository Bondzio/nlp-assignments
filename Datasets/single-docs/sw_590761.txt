Philippine Association for Language Teaching

The Philippine Association for Language Teaching is an academic and professional association that helps teachers of English as a Foreign Language (EFL). It is often called PALT for short. Its offices are at the College of Education of the University of the Philippines in Quezon City. 

PALT started in 1960.

PALT has a convention every year with papers, keynote speakers, workshops, and social events. Its first international convention was in 2001.
