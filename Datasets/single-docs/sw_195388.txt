Rod and Todd Flanders

Rod and Todd Flanders are two characters in the television show "The Simpsons". They are the neighbours of the Simpson family. They are brothers. Their parents are Ned and Maude Flanders. Rod is the elder of the two; he is twelve years old, while Todd is ten. The Flanders are all devout Christians.

Rod and Todd first appeared in the episode "Dead Putting Society". They have appeared in more than 100 episodes since then. The two brothers are both disliked by Bart and Lisa, in the same way that Ned Flanders is hated by Homer.
