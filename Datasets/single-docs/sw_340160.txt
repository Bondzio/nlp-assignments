Elizabeth Banks

Elizabeth Banks (born February 10 1974) is an American television and movie actress. She was born in Pittsfield, Massachusetts. Her movies include the "Spider-Man" franchise, 2008's comedy "Zack and Miri Make a Porno", "Invincible", "The Hunger Games", and in "The Lego Movie". Banks is a Democrat. She attended the 2012 Democratic National Convention to support President Obama's re-election.
