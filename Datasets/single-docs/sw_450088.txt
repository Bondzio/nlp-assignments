Juan Ponce Enrile

Juan Ponce Enrile, later Juanito Ponce (born Juanito Furagganan on 14 February 1924), is a Filipino politician. He served as Justice Secretary and then Defense Minister. He was Senate President from November 2008 until his resignation on June 5, 2013.

Enrile was born in Gonzaga, Cagayan.
