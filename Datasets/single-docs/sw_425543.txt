Remember the Time

"Remember the Time" is a song by Michael Jackson. It was released as the second single from his album "Dangerous" on January 14, 1992. It was written by Teddy Riley, Jackson, and Bernard Belle. When the single was released Jackson dedicated the song with love to Diana Ross. The music video is nine minutes long. John Singleton directed it. In 2006 the song was released again. It reached number 2 in the Spanish chart.
