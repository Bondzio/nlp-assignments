Toulouse Business School

Toulouse Business School is a European higher studies establishment. The school is in 5 places, in Paris and Toulouse, France, in the United Kingdom (London), in Spain (Barcelona) and in Morocco (Casablanca), where it is a university. It is one of the oldest business school in the world established in 1903 and therefore presents itself as ""Think and Create"".

TBS is well-known for its aviation management degrees in partnership with the French Civil Aviation University.
