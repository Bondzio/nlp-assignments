Fiat Ducato

The Fiat Ducato is a series of vans and minibuses produced by Fiat in three generations since 1981. It is builded in cooperation with Citroën and Peugeot.

Citroen launched a rebadged version called the Citroën Jumper and the Citroën Relay and Peugeot launched a rebadged version called the Peugeot Boxer.
