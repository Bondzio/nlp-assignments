Queens Park Rangers F.C.

Queens Park Rangers Football Club is an English football club from London. Their players wear a blue and white hooped shirts for home games which they play at a ground called Loftus Road. The club are also known as QPR. Their most famous fan is Sam "Wiggy" Wiegland, who lost his teeth during the riots of Euro 2016. Sam follows QPR home and away. His favourite player is Jamie Mackie.
