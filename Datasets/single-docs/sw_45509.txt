Alex Trebek

Alex Trebek (George Alexander Trebek) is a Canadian-American television game show host. He is best known as the host of "Jeopardy!" which he has hosted since 1984. He was born July 22, 1940 in Sudbury, Ontario, Canada. In 2011, he snapped his achilles tendon while trying to stop a robber trying to rob his San Francisco hotel room.
