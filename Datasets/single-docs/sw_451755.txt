Brian Drummond

Brian Drummond (born August 10, 1969 in Salmon Arm, British Columbia) is a Canadian voice actor and former stage actor. He is best known for voicing the roles of Vegeta, Yajirobe, and Vegito in the Ocean Studios English dub of the anime series "Dragon Ball Z". He has also voiced dubs for Yuna Roma Seiran in "Mobile Suit Gundam SEED Destiny" as well as Ryuk in "Death Note". He currently voices Maurice, Billy, Boss Ember and El Diablos Nacho in Slugterra.
