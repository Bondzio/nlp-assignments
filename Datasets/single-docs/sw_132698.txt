Wii Sports Resort

Wii Sports Resort (also known as Wii Sports 2) is a sports video game for the Wii. It is a sequel to "Wii Sports" and was released in Summer 2009. The game is part of a series of games sometimes referred to as the Wii Series. The game comes bundled with the MotionPlus device and another Wii Remote Jacket, both of which are also sold separately. The game is set in beach resort called Wuhu Island.
