Liam Gallagher

William John Paul "Liam" Gallagher (born 21 September 1972) is an English musician and songwriter.

Gallagher is best known as a singer in the rock band Oasis. The band split up in 2009. Gallagher formed a new band called Beady Eye. Former Oasis members Gem Archer, Andy Bell and Chris Sharrock were also in the new band. Beady Eye disbanded in 2014. 

In 2010 Gallagher was selected as "best frontman" by reader's of Q magazine.
