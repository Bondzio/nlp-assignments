Jesus and Mary School

Jesus and Mary School is a Lebanese nonprofit Roman Catholic school in Rabweh, Lebanon, a northern suburb of Beirut. The school was started in 1965 by Elias Farah, the Archbishop of Antelias and run by the Sisters of Jesus and Mary. It provides education for students from kindergarten to the 12th grade. in 2002, Maronite Archdiocese of Antelias became the owner of the school.
