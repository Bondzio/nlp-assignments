Sanjo Station (Kyoto)


Limited Express trains and K-Limited Express trains arrive at and depart from Tracks (Platforms) 1 and 3.


Sanjō Keihan Station (, "Sanjō Keihan Eki") is a subway station on Kyoto's Tozai Line. It intersects with the Keihan Electric Railway's Keihan Main Line. It is connected to the adjacent Keihan Sanjō Station. The station is underneath Sanjō-dori, just east of the Kamo River and Kyoto's Kawaramachi shopping district.
