Rip Taylor

Charles Elmer "Rip" Taylor, Jr. (born January 13, 1934) is an American movie, television, stage, voice, radio actor, activist, entertainer, and comedian who is known for his role in "Indecent Proposal", "", "", "Thing Are Tough All Over Again", "Jackass", and in "Alex & Emma".

Taylor was born on January 13, 1934 in Washington, D.C.. He was inspired by Groucho Marx, Charlie Chaplin, Redd Foxx, and Mickey Rooney. He has inspired Dana Snyder.
