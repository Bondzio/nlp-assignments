Frank Glieber

Frank John Glieber (April 5, 1934 - May 1, 1985) was an American sportscaster. He called college football games for CBS Radio, the Dallas Cowboys, Cleveland Browns on CBS, and the Texas Rangers. While working for CBS, Glieber called National Football League, National Basketball Association, college football, college basketball, soccer, tennis, bowling, and golf. He died of a heart attack in May of 1985.
