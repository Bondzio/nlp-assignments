Ed Wood (movie)

Ed Wood is a 1994 American comedy-drama movie directed and produced by Tim Burton, and starring Johnny Depp. The movie is about the movie director Ed Wood. The movie also stars Martin Landau, Sarah Jessica Parker, Patricia Arquette, Jeffrey Jones, Lisa Marie, George Steele and Bill Murray. It won two Academy Awards: Best Supporting Actor for Landau and Best Makeup for Rick Baker (who designed Landau's prosthetic makeup). Roger Ebert gave the movie four stars out of four.
