White House Press Secretary

The White House Press Secretary is an important job at the White House. They act as a spokesperson for the United States Government and the President, senior executives, and policies. Sarah Huckabee Sanders is the current White House Press Secretary.

 Did not brief the press after being wounded in the Reagan assassination attempt. "De facto" Press Secretary (as White House Deputy Press Secretary). Did not brief the press while Stephanopoulos was Communications Director. First female Press Secretary. "De facto" Press Secretary (as White House Communications Director).
