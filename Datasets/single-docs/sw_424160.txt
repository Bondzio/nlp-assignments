Calaisa

Calaisa is a quartet from the town of Malmö in Sweden. It consists of the systers, Caisa and Lisa Troedsson and the sisters Malin and Anna Törnquist. The band participated at Melodifestivalen 2008 with the song "If I Could", which was knocked out during the Karlskrona competition on 1 March 2008.

Calaisa has released several albums.
