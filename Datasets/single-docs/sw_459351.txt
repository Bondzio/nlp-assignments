Kyle Chandler

Kyle Martin Chandler (born September 17, 1965) is an American actor. He is best known for his role as Deputy Jackson Lamb in the movie "Super 8" and as Hamilton Jordan in the movie "Argo". In television shows, Chandler is known for his roles in "Early Edition" as Gary Hobson, "Grey's Anatomy" as Dylan Young, and as Coach Eric Taylor in "Friday Night Lights". He has won the Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actor in a Drama Series in 2011.
