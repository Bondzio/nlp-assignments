Geena Davis

Virginia Elizabeth "Geena" Davis (born January 21, 1956) is an Academy Award-, Golden Globe-winning and Emmy-nominated American actress, film producer, writer, former fashion model. Geena is also a member of Mensa International.

Since 1982 until 1983 Geena was married to Richard Emmolo.

Since 1987 until 1990 she was married to Jeff Goldblum.

Since 1993 until 1998 she was married to Renny Harlin.

Since September 1, 2001 she married to Reza Jarrahy. They has three children, daughter Alizeh Keshvar Davis Jarrahy (born on April 10, 2002) and twin sons Kian William Davis Jarrahy and Kaiis Steven Davis Jerrahy (born on May 6, 2004).
