Bonnie

Bonnie is a given name. It is usually used for females. Some males also have the name. It comes from the Scottish word "bonnie", which means "pretty" or "attractive". There are several celebrities with the name, including Bonnie Franklin and Bonnie Raitt.

In 1986 and 1998, hurricanes with the name "Bonnie" struck certain parts of the United States. The 1998 storm caused between $700 million and $1 billion in damage in North Carolina. The name is still being used on Atlantic hurricanes.
