Vinny Griffin

Vinny Griffin is a fictional character in the television comedy show "Family Guy". Vinny is replaced with Brian after his death in the episode "Life of Brian". On the episode "Christmas Guy", Stewie meets another version of himself and steals his time pad. He goes back in time to save Brian, causing Stewie not to meet Vinny. Vinny has an Italian accent and is kind to the Griffins. He last appeared in "Christmas Guy". He was voiced by Tony Sirico.
