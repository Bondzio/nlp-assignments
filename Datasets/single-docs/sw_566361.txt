Smurfs: The Lost Village

Smurfs: The Lost Village is an upcoming 2016 American 3D computer-animated adventure-comedy movie. It is directed by Kelly Asbury. It is based on The Smurfs comic book series. The movie will come out in Summer 2016. 


Smurfette finds a creature in the Forbidden Forest who leaves a mysterious map. With Brainy, Clumsy and Hefty she goes on a mission to find the lost village.

It is set for release on August 5, 2016, and is previously set for release on May 20, 2016.
