Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh () is a state in the Republic of India. About 75,727,000 people lived there as reported in census of 2001. The eastern boundary is a coastline along the Bay of Bengal. The state has an area of . It is bigger than Burkina Faso but smaller than Ecuador. In traditional Indian geography it falls under the South Indian zone.

The capital city is Hyderabad.
Andhra Pradesh has a gross domestic product (GDP) of 123,560 (in millions of US dollars). That makes it number 3 in India in terms of GDP, after Maharashtra and Uttar Pradesh. It holds a position in the provinces of the world with more than a billion dollars in GDP. Andhra Pradesh is also called "Rice Bowl of India".The latitude and longitude is 17.3660°N and 78.4760°E respectively.
