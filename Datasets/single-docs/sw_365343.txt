Brad Garrett

Bradley "Brad" Garrett (born Bradley H. Gerstenfeld; April 14, 1960) is an American stand-up comedian, actor, voice actor and professional poker player. He is known for his role as Robert Barone in "Everybody Love Raymond" and for his movie roles in "Stuart Little 2", "Finding Nemo", "Garfield", "The Amateurs", "The Pacifier", "Night at the Museum", "Tangled", "A Bugs Life", "Ratatouille", and "Hoodwinked Too! Hood vs. Evil". He has won two Emmy Awards.

Garrett was born on April 14, 1960 in Woodland Hills, California. Garrett was married to Jill Diven from 1999 until they divorced in 2006. They had two children.
