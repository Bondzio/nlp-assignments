Guitar Hero: Aerosmith

Guitar Hero: Aerosmith is a rhythm game made by Neversoft with other help from Activision. It is part of the "Guitar Hero" franchise. The game was released in North America on June 29 2008, and two days earlier in Europe.

Some of the songs featured in the game include "Combination", "Kings and Queens", "Train Kept A Rollin" and "Mama Kin".
