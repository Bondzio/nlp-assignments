Bill Elliott

William Clyde Elliott (born October 8, 1955) is a part-time American racecar driver. He drives in the Sprint cup series. He won the 1988 Sprint cup, and was inducted into the Motorsports Hall of Fame of America in 2007. In 2014, Elliot was voted into the NASCAR Hall of Fame. He will be inducted into the Hall of Fame in January of 2015.
