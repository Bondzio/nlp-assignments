Jared Harris

Jared Francis Harris (born 24 August 1961) is an English-Irish-Welsh actor who is known for his television role as Lane Pryce in "Mad Men" and as Ulysses S. Grant in Steven Spielberg's 2012 movie "Lincoln". He won an Sitges Film Festival in 1999. His father was late Irish actor Richard Harris.

Harris was born in London, England to an Irish father, actor Richard Harris and to Welsh socialite, Elizabeth Rees-Williams. He studied at Downside School and at Duke University. Harris was married to Emilia Fox from 2005 until they divorced in 2010. Since 2011, he has been partners with Allegra Riggio.
