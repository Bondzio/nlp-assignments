Gold (color)

Gold or golden is a yellowish orange color, that is a bit like the color of the metal gold.

The web color "gold" (shown at right) is sometimes referred to as golden in order to distinguish it from the color "metallic gold". The use of "gold" as a color term in traditional usage is more often applied to the color "gold (metallic)" (shown below). 
The first recorded use of "golden" as a color name in English was in the year 1300 to refer to the element gold and in 1423 to refer to blonde hair.
