Ruth Davidson

Ruth Davidson (born 10 November 1978) is a Scottish politician. She is leader of the Scottish Conservative and Unionist Party. She is the youngest ever party leader in the country.

Davidson was elected as a Member of the Scottish Parliament in May 2011. She became leader of the Scottish Conservative Party on 4 November 2011. 

Before being an MSP she worked as a journalist. She was in the Territorial Army for three years. She also worked as a Sunday School teacher.

Davidson was born in Edinburgh. She lives in Partick, Glasgow. She is a lesbian.
