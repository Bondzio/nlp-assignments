Mike McCormack

Claude George Michael "Mike" McCormack (born December 14, 1921) is a retired American politician. He served as U.S. Representative from the State of Washington's Fourth Congressional District from 1971 to 1981. He is a Democrat.

McCormack was born in Baltimore, Ohio. He studied at the University of Toledo and at Washington State University. 

In 1980, at age 59, in the midst of the "Reagan landslide", McCormack unsuccessfully sought a sixth term against Sid Morrison.
