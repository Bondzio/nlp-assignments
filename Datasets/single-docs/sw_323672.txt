Holly Valance

Holly Valance (born Holly Rachel Vukadinovic on 11 May 1983) is an Australian-British actress, singer and model. She is a dual citizen of Australia and the United Kingdom. She was born in Melbourne to an English immigrant mother and Serbian immigrant father. She began her career in 1999 as Felicity Scully on Australian soap opera "Neighbours". She began her singing career in 2002. 

Valance was a contestant in the 2011 series of "Strictly Come Dancing".
