Deborah Kerr

Deborah Kerr CBE (30 September 1921 - 16 October 2007) was a British actress. She was born Deborah Jane Kerr-Trimmer in Glasgow, Scotland. She won a Golden Globe Award for Best Actress for her starring role as Anna Leonowens in "The King and I". 

Kerr died of Parkinson's disease in Botesdale, Suffolk.
