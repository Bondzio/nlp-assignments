Garfield: A Tail of Two Kitties

Garfield: A Tail of Two Kitties is an 2006 animated comedy movie that was produced by John Davis, Michele Imperato and Brian Manis and was directed by Tim Hill. "Garfield: A Tail of Two Kitties" was released on June 16, 2006 in North America. The movie received negative reviews with a 11% rating on Rotten Tomatoes and a 37 out of 100 from Metacritic.
