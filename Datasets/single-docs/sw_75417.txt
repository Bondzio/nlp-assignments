Pervez Musharraf

General (retired) Pervez Musharraf () (born August 11, 1943) was a former military dictator and the tenth President of Pakistan. Previously, he was Chief Executive of Pakistan as well as former Chief of Army Staff of the Pakistan Army and he took over power in Pakistan in 1999, by one day removing the then elected government of Mr Nawaz Sharif. He has charged with multiple murders in April 2013 and was given bail in October 2013. Then he was arrested again, and his trial is still going on in Pakistan.
