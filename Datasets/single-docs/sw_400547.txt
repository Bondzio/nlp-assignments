Antonio Garcia Martinez (born December 24, 1956 in Sevilla) is a cyclist from Spain. He has a disability: He is LC3 type cyclist. He is a chemical engineer. People in his family are cyclists. This is why he started cycling. He competed at the 1996 Summer Paralympics. He competed at the 2000 Summer Paralympics. He competed at the 2004 Summer Paralympics. He finished first in the Combined Road (Pursuit / Time Trial) LC3 race. He competed at the 2008 Summer Paralympics.
