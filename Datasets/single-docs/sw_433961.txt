Anne Jeffreys

Anne Jeffreys (born January 26, 1923) is an American actress and singer. She was known for her works in "Topper", "Street Scene", and the musicals "Kiss Me, Kate" (1948) and the musical "Three Wishes for Jamie" (1952). She replaced Patricia Morison in the musical "Kiss Me, Kate".

Jeffreys was born Annie Carmichael in Goldsboro, North Carolina on January 26, 1923.
