Goodnight Goodnight

Goodnight Goodnight is the fifth single by pop-rock band Maroon 5 on the album "It Won't Be Soon Before Long". The song was released August 2008. The song is also the 8th track off the album. 

The music video was releasd August 21st, 2008. The video deals with a "before and after" sequence of a relationship between Levine and his girlfriend, Bailey. In the "before" part she was beautiful and in the "after" one they set apart, but he is still in love with her.
