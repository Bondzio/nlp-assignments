Dave Pasch

Dave Pasch (b. 1972) is an American sportscaster. Pasch works for ESPN. He calls play-by-play of college football, college basketball, NBA, WNBA, and Arena football games. He has also been the radio voice for the Arizona Cardinals of the NFL since 2002, teaming with Ron Wolfley. In 2007, Pasch called the Little League World Series.
