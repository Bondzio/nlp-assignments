Alfredo Alcón

Alfredo Félix Alcón (; 3 March 1930 – 11 April 2014) was an Argentine theatre and movie actor. Alcón born in Buenos Aires. He worked in more than 50 movies. His first movie was, "El amor nunca muere" ("Love Never Dies"), in 1955.

He died in Buenos Aires at the age of 84 from a respiratory illness.
