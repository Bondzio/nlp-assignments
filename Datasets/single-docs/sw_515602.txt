Bruce Djite

Bruce José Djité (born 25 March 1987) is an Australian soccer player. He currently plays for Adelaide United of the A-League and the national team as a striker.

Djite was born in Washington, D.C., United States. His father is from the Ivory Coast and his mother is from Togo. He moved to Sydney, New South Wales when he was three years old. Djite speaks French and English.
