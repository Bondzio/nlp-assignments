Connie Sawyer

Connie Sawyer (born November 27, 1912), is an American actress. She was born in Pueblo, Colorado and grew up in Oakland, California.

Sawyer is known for her roles in "The Mary Tyler Moore Show", "The Rockford Files", "Hawaii Five-O", "Dynasty", "When Harry Met Sally...", "Murder, She Wrote", "Home Improvement", "Seinfeld", "Boy Meets World", "Will & Grace", "That '70s Show", "8 Simple Rules", "ER", "How I Met Your Mother" and "The Office".
