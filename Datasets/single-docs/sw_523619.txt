Overcome

Overcome is the debut studio album by British singer Alexandra Burke, released 19 October 2009 on Syco Music. It was released a year after Burke won the British talent show, "The X Factor". It was also re-released in 2010 with a new artwork and three extra songs.

"Overcome" has received mainly positive reviews.
