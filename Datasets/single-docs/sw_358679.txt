Parupalli Kashyap

Parupalli Kashyap () (born 8 September 1986), also known as Kashyap Parupalli, is an Indian athlete. He is best known as a badminton player who competed in the Summer Olympic Games.

Parupalli trained at Hyderabad.

In the 2012 Summer Olympics at London, Parupalli reached quarter-finals in the badminton tournament.
