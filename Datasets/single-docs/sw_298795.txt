Central African Republic at the Olympics

Central African Republic at the Olympics is a history which includes 9 games in 8 countries and 40+ athletes. Since 1964, athletes of the Central African Republic have been part of the "Olympic Movement".

The International Olympic Committee's official abbreviation for the Central African Republic is CAF.

The Central African Republic has been in every Summer Olympic Games held between the1984 Summer Olympics and the 2012 Summer Olympics. A team from the Central African Republic first competed in the 1968 Summer Olympics.

The country has not won an Olympic medal. No athletes from the Central African Republic have been at any Winter Olympic Games.
