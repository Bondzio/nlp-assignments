Lizzy Pattinson

Elizabeth "Lizzy" Pattinson is an English singer and songwriter.

Lizzy has a sister, Victoria and brother, actor Robert Pattinson.

Lizzy has performed with the UK dance act Aurora and German duo Milk & Sugar. She was lead vocalist for them on Milk & Sugar's No. 1 "Billboard" Hot Dance Club Play track "Let The Sun Shine". She has had three Top 20 hits in the UK.

In 2008 Lizzy recorded background vocals for the movie "Twilight". She is heard on the track "Who Are They?" by Carter Burwell.
