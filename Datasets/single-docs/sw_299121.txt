Liberia at the Olympics

Liberia at the Olympics is a history which began in 1956. 

The International Olympic Committee's official abbreviation for Liberia is LBR.

Teams from Liberia has been to every Summer Olympic Games held since 1956, except for 1968, 1976 and 1992. The country has never won an Olympic medal. No athletes from Liberia have been in any Winter Olympic Games.

The National Olympic Committee for Liberia was formed in 1954. It was recognized by the International Olympic Committee in 1955.
