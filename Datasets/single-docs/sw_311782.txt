Charles Napier

Charles L. Napier (April 12, 1936 – October 5, 2011) was an American actor.

Napier was born on April 12, 1936, near Scottsville, Kentucky, Napier moved to Hollywood after graduating from Western Kentucky University with a degree in art. 

His first acting position in Hollywood was on an episode of "". Napier later acted as military figures villains, such as playing Marshal Murdock in the movie "". 

Napier died on October 5, 2011, in a hospital in Bakersfield, California from a blood clot, aged 75.
