Madeleine Sherwood (November 13, 1922 â€“ April 23, 2016) was a Canadian actress. She was known for her roles as Reverend Mother Placido in "The Flying Nun". She also appeared in "Cat on a Hot Tin Roof" and "Sweet Bird of Youth". Sherwood was once blacklisted as a communist during the 1960s, even though she wasn't.

Sherwood died on April 23, 2016 at her childhood home in Lac Cornu, Quebec at the age of 93.
