Oya Aydoğan

Oya Aydoğan (10 February 1957 – 15 May 2016) was a Turkish actress, model and television presenter. She was the winner of the Ses Magazine Movie Actress Contest in 1976. Her career began in 1976 with the movie "Deli Şahin". She was born in Erzincan, Turkey.

She was a Alevi Islam. She retired in 2015.

She suffered an aortic aneurysm on 7 May 2016, before the Mother's Day. She died on 15 May 2016 in a hospital in Istanbul, aged 59.
