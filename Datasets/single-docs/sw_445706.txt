James McAvoy

James McAvoy ( ; born 21 April 1979) is a Scottish actor. He is best known for his roles in movies such as "" (2005), "The Last King of Scotland" (2006), "Becoming Jane" (2007), "Atonement" (2007), "Wanted" (2008) and "" (2011).

McAvoy was born in Port Glasgow and raised in Drumchapel, Glasgow. He was raised as a Roman Catholic. He has been married to English actress Anne-Marie Duff since 2006. They have a son named Brendan (born 2010).
