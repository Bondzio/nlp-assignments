Peter Kelamis

Peter Kelamis (born December 11, 1967) is an Australian-Canadian actor, voice actor and comedian. He was born in Sydney and raised in Vancouver. He is best known for playing Dr. Adam Brody in "Stargate Universe", Goku in the Ocean Group English dub of the animated series "Dragon Ball Z" and Rolf from "Ed, Edd n Eddy".

Kelamis has been married to Canadian actress Alannah Stewartt since 2004.
