Lloyd Bochner

Lloyd Wolfe Bochner (July 29, 1924 â€“ October 29, 2005) was a Canadian movie, television, stage, and voice actor. He is known for his television role as Chief Inspector Neil Campbell in "Hong Kong" and as the voice of Gotham City's Mayor Hamilton Hill in "" and in "The New Batman Adventures". He won an ACTRA Award in 2004.

Bochner was born on July 29, 1924 in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Bochner was married to Ruth Roher until his death in 2005. Bochner died on October 29, 2005 in Santa Monica, California from cancer, aged 81.
