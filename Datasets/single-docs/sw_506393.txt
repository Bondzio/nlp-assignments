Alex Morgan

Alexandra Patricia Morgan (born 2 July 1989 in San Dimas, California, United States) is an American football forward. She currently plays for the Orlando Pride in the National Women's Soccer League, the top tier of women's soccer in the U.S. She is also a member of the United States women's national soccer team (USWNT). Morgan was the first pick in the 2011 FIFA Women's World Cup. She was an Olympic Gold medalist in the 2012 Summer Olympics, which was the USWNT's 4th Olympic Gold medal. She also played in the 2015 FIFA Women's World Cup, and her home country, the United States, won their third World Cup. She is known for being one of the greatest American forwards of all time.
