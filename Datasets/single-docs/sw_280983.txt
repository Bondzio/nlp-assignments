Shad Gaspard

Shad Chad Javier Romane Chittick Gaspard (born January 13, 1981 in Brooklyn, New York) is an American professional wrestler. He is best known for wrestling for World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). He teamed with JTG and they formed the tag team, Cryme Tyme. Gaspard is currently pursuing an acting career which he stated in an interview for Right After Wrestling.
