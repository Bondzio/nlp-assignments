Samanda

Samanda (Amanda Louise Marchant and Samantha Joanne Marchant), born 26 June 1988, were a British female duo. They formed in the UK in 2007 after exiting Big Brother in 2nd place behind Brian Belo. 

They have had 1 single with Barbie Girl which charted at 26 in the United Kingdom and 45 in the Republic of Ireland. They also had a top 200 hit with Honey Love. They are part of the Sony BMG record label.
