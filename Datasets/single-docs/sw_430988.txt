Kathryn Hahn

Kathryn Hahn (born 23 July 1973) is an American actress. She played Lily for six years in the television program "Crossing Jordan" on NBC. She played a hippie in the sitcom "Our Idiot Brother", also on NBC. She also played Karen in the movie "Wanderlust".

Hahn was born in Westchester, Illinois, near Chicago. She was raised near Cleveland, Ohio.
