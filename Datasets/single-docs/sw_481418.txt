Kingdom of Navarre

The Kingodom of Navarre was a small kingdom that existed from 841 to 1820. The kingdom was located roughly in the Basque Country on both sides of the Pyrenees mountains. In 1512, it was split into a Spanish part, and a French part. In 1620, the French part, called Lower Navarre was merged with France. The Spanish part existed until 1820. The capital of the kingdom was Pamplona.
