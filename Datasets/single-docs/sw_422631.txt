WWE TLC: Tables, Ladders &amp; Chairs

TLC: Tables, Ladders & Chairs is a yearly pay-per-view event from WWE. The matches during the TLC: Tables, Ladders & Chairs pay-per-views usually center around Tables, Ladders, and Chairs matches. TLC: Tables, Ladders & Chairs replaced Armageddon in WWE's December slot of their pay-per-view calendar.
