Anders Larsson

Anders Larsson, born 3 October 1961 in Karlstad, Sweden is a Swedish former cross-country skier. During his career, he represented Bondsjöhöjdens IK in club competitions.

He also participated at the 1984 Olympic Winter Games in Sarajevo. There, he ended up 39th in the 50 kilometers race. He also won Vasaloppet in 1987. In 1982 he was awarded the Sixten Jernberg Award.
