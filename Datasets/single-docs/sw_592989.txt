Magnus Böcker

Magnus Böcker (August 31, 1961 – July 27, 2017) was a Swedish businessman. He was born in Stockholm, Sweden. He was the Executive Chairman of Blibros Capital Partners, an investment company based in Singapore and Stockholm. He was also Chairman and Co-Founder of Tryb.

Böcker died at his home in Singapore of cancer on July 27, 2017 at the age of 55.
