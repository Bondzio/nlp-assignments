Ricky Jay

Richard Jay Potash (born 1948) is an American stage magician, actor, and writer. He is known for his card tricks, card throwing, memory feats, and stage patter. He acted in many movies including "Boogie Nights", "The Prestige", "Heist", and "Magnolia".

Jay was born in 1948 in Brooklyn, New York to a Jewish family. He is married to Chrisann Verges.
