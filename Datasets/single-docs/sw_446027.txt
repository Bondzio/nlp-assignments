Michael Prinz von Preußen

Michael Prinz von Preußen (22 March 1940 – 3 April 2014) or Michael, Prince of Prussia, was a German writer and royalty. He was the son of Louis Ferdinand, Prince of Prussia. He was the godfather of Georg Friedrich, Prince of Prussia. He studied in Freiburg. He wrote many biographies about his family members. He was born in Bermen.

Michael died on 3 April 2014 from an illness, aged 74.
