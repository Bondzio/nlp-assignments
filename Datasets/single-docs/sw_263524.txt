Vladimir Malakhov

Vladimir Malakhov (born 27 November 1980) is a chess grandmaster from Russia. 

He was a member of the gold-medal-winning Russian team at the 2009 World Team Chess Championship in Bursa.

Malakhov's career choices are interesting. Qualified as a nuclear physicist, he used to be an amateur chess player. Now he's a professional chess player who works part-time as a nuclear physicist.
