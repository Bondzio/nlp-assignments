Emile Roemer

Emile Gerardus Maria Roemer (born August 24, 1962 in Boxmeer) is a Dutch politician of the Socialist Party (SP). He has been political leader since 2010. 

He has been a member of the Second Chamber since 2006 and parliamentary leader since 2010. Previously he was an alderman of Boxmeer, and a member of the party board.

Roemer is a Roman Catholic and a former teacher.
