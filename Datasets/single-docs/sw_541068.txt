Abel Fernandez

Abel Gonzalez Fernandez (July 14, 1930 – May 3, 2016) was an American actor. He was known for his role as Federal Agent William "Bill" Youngfellow on the 1959 – 1963 ABC Television series "The Untouchables".

Fernandez died on May 3, 2016 from lung cancer at a hospital in Whittier, California, aged 85.
