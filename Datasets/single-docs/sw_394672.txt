Turpan

Turpan also referred to Turfan or Tulufan in Chinese, is an oasis county-level city in Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region of China. 

Historically, Turpan is an important ancient stop on the Silk Road in China. Turpan means 'the lowest place' in Uygur Language and 'the fertile land' in Turki. Now the city is a tourist spot with unique landscape, abundant historical relics and fertile farmland.
