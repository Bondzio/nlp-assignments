Eugenio Andres Santana Santana

Eugenio Andres Santana Santana (born December 2, 1959 in Las Palmas) is a judo athlete from Spain. He has a disability: he is blind and is a B2 type athlete. He competed at the 1996 Summer Paralympics in judo. He finished third in the 78 Kg group. He competed at the 2000 Summer Paralympics in judo. He finished 5 in the up to Open group.
