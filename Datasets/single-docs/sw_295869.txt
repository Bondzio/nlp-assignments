Neymar

Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior, known as Neymar or Neymar JR (born on 5 February 1972 in Mogi das Cruzes, Brazil), is a Brazilian footballer that can play on either side of the field as a Winger or he can play as a Striker. He plays for the Spanish team FC Barcelona, of La Liga, the top division of football in Spain. He plays for the Brazil national football team at international level.

Neymar joined Santos in 2003 and on March 7 2009, he made his debut for Santos at 17 years of age. He scored his first goal against Mogi Mirim, a week after his debut. On April 15 2010, Neymar scored five goals for Santos, beating Guarani 8-1 in the Brazilian Cup. Later, the club would win the cup. Neymar got red carded for wearing a mask in his celebration.

After Neymar's performances for Brazil's U-17 team at the 2009 U-17 World Cup, on July 26 2010, Neymar was selected in the squad for the Brazil team by new head coach Mano Menezes for a friendly match against USA team on August 10 2010. He scored on his debut, in a 2–0 win for Brazil. 
On March 27 2011, he scored twice in a 2–0 win against Scotland at the Emirates Stadium.

In the summer of 2013, Neymar was bought by the Spanish club FC Barcelona for 57 million euros.
