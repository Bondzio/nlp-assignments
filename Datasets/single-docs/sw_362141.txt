Chelsea Handler

Chelsea Handler (born February 25 1975) is an American writer, stand up comedian and actress. She has performed in many movies and television programs. She is the host of "Chelsea Lately". She was born near Livingston, New Jersey.

Handler was the host of the 2010 MTV Video Music Awards. 

"Are You There, Chelsea?" was an NBC sitcom. It was based on Handler's 2008 best-selling book "Are You There, Vodka? It's Me, Chelsea". The program aired from January 11 to March 28, 2012. On May 11, 2012, the network canceled it. Laura Prepon played the main character, "Chelsea Newman," a cocktail waitress. Handler was "Chelsea's" born-again Christian sister, "Sloane".
