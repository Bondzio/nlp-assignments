Home Alone: The Holiday Heist

Home Alone: The Holiday Heist (or Home Alone 5) is an American children's criminal comedy television movie. It is set in Maine. It is the fifth movie in the successful "Home Alone" series. It stars Malcolm McDowell, Debi Mazar, Eddie Steeples and Christian Martyn. The movie was first shown on ABC Family on November 25, 2012. Like "Home Alone 3", it does not focus on any of the characters from the first two films. Home Alone 4: Taking Back The House has some of the main characters from the first two films but all roles have different actors.
