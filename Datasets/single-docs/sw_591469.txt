Clara Ponsatí i Obiols

Clara Ponsatí i Obiols (born in 1957 in Barcelona) is a Catalan economist. She was appointed Councillor of Education of the Generalitat of Catalonia by President Carles Puigdemont the 14th of july of 2017.

Ponsatí was born in Barcelona in 1957. She was the daughter of Josep Maria Ponsatí i Capdevila and Montserrat Obiols i Germà. After studying in the Faculty of Ecofirstics of the UB. In 1988 she got the terminal degree from the Department of Economcs, of the University of Minnesota, (Minneapolis, Minnesota, USA), where she remained several years as a teacher.

Of liberal trend, Ponsatí directed the Institute of Economic Analysis of the CSIC (Consejo Superior de Investigaciones Científicas) between 2006 and 2012, where she was a teacher as well. She has been a visiting teacher in the universities of Toronto, San Diego and Georgetown. The year 2013 the Spanish government exercised a veto against her, after she exposed her opinion about the independence of Catalonia in different mediae.
