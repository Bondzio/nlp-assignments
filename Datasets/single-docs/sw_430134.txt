Millie Jackson

Mildred "Millie" Jackson (born July 15, 1944) is an American R&B singer and songwriter. She released her first album in 1972. In 1974 Jackson was nominated for a Grammy Award. In 2000 she sung on some songs for the soundtrack of a French movie called "Comme un aimant". In 2008 she played Claudette in a movie called "Soul Men". 

Jackson has a daughter called Keisha Jackson. She is also a singer.
