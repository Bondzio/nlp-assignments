Sodium selenate

Sodium selenate is a chemical compound. Its chemical formula is NaSeO. It contains sodium and selenate ions.

Sodium selenate is a white solid. It is a strong oxidizing agent. It dissolves in water easily.

Sodium selenate is made by reacting sodium hydroxide with selenic acid.

Sodium selenate is used as a mineral supplement.

Sodium selenate is very toxic in amounts any more than a very tiny amount.
