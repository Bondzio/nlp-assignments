Amber Heard

Amber Laura Heard (born April 22, 1986 in Austin, Texas) is an American actress. 

She made her movie debut in the 2004 sports-drama movie Friday Night Lights. She had a recurring role on the television show, Hidden Palms as Greta Matthews. Heard came out at GLAAD's 25th anniversary event. From 2008 to 2011, she was in a relationship with photographer Tasya van Ree. She began a relationship with Johnny Depp in 2012. The couple became engaged in December 2013. They married in February 2015. In May 2016, she filed for divorce.
