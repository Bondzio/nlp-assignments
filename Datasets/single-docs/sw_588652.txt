Tuomas Gerdt

Kaiho Tuomas Albin Gerdt (born 28 May 1922) is a Knight of the Mannerheim Cross. Gerdt, serving as a junior runner officer in the infantry regiment 7, was awarded the Mannerheim Cross on 8 September 1942. At that time he held the rank of a Sergeant.
