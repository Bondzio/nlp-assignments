Richie Rich (comics)

Richie Rich is a fictional character created by Alfred Harvey and Warren Kremer, and published by Harvey Comics in the 1950s. Richie Rich is called "the poor little rich boy", or also "the richest kid in the world". He appeared in comic book titles such as "Richie Rich Millions" (1961) and "Richie Rich Dollars & Cents" (1963).
