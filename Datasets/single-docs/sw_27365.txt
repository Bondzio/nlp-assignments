Sheryl Crow

Sheryl Crow (born February 11, 1962) is an American singer-songwriter. She can also play guitar and bass guitar.

Many of her songs have charted and are still popular. They include "The First Cut Is the Deepest", "Soak Up the Sun", "If It Makes You Happy" and "All I Wanna Do".

Crow has never married. She has two adopted sons, Wyatt Steven Crow (born on May 11, 2007) and Levi James Crow (born on April 30, 2010).

She had breast cancer in 2006.
