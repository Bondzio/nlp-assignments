Warren G

Warren Griffin III (born November 10, 1970 in Long Beach, California) is an American rapper and DJ and Producer better known by his stage name, Warren G. 

Warren G has been nominated for two awards including Grammy Award for "Best Rap Performance By A Duo Or Group" for his song "Regulate" with Nate Dogg and was also nominated for a Grammy Award for "Best Rap Solo Performance" for his song, "This D.J." in 1995.
