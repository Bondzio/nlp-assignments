Dana Snyder

Dana Snyder (born November 14, 1973) is an American voice artist and comedian. He is best known for voicing Master Shake in the "Aqua Teen Hunger Force" franchise and Alistair in "Open Season 3". 

Snyder was born in Allentown, Pennsylvania. But he grew up in Las Vegas, Nevada. He said Don Rickles, Rip Taylor, and Phil Silvers were his childhood influences. He graduated from Webster University, Missouri in 1996.
