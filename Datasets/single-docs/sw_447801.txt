Game of Thrones

Game of Thrones is an American fantasy drama television series. It is based on George R. R. Martin's book series "A Song of Ice and Fire". The first episode was shown on April 17, 2011 on HBO. There have been six seasons so far. It is very popular around the world. The first season of "Game of Thrones" was nominated for thirteen of the 2011 Emmy Awards, including Outstanding Drama Series. It won two. The series focuses on some kings and queens in two continents called Westeros and Essos. Many of the successors to the throne fight for power. The most prominent kingdom is the Lannister family. This royal family is very rich because they live near gold mines. Some people criticized the series for the twincestual scenes and having too much nudity.
