José Manuel Fernández Barranquero

Jose Manuel Fernandez Barranquero (born March 19, 1975 in Málaga) is a track and field athlete from Spain. He has a physical disability: He a T46 type athlete. He raced at the 1992 Summer Paralympics in Barcelona. He raced at the 1996 Summer Paralympics in Atlanta, Georgia, United States. He raced at the 2000 Summer Paralympics in Sydney, Australia. He finished second in the 4 X 400 meter T42-T46 race and finished third in the 800 meter T46 race.
