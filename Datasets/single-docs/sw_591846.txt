Beisfjord massacre

The Beisfjord massacre () was a massacre on 18 July 1942 in Beisfjord, Norway of 288 political prisoners who were killed at the concentration camp in Beisfjord: ["Beisfjord camp number 1"] "Lager I Beisfjord"

The massacre was ordered a few days earlier by the "Reichskommissar" for Norway Josef Terboven.
