Don King

Donald "Don" King (born August 20, 1931) is an American boxing promoter. He has promoted many famous boxers including Mike Tyson, George Foreman, Muhammad Ali, and Evander Holyfield.

King was born on August 20, 1931 in Cleveland, Ohio.On December 7,1941. The iron plant burst killed his father. He dropped out from Kent State University. King was married to Henrietta King from 1959 until her death in 2010. They had three children.
