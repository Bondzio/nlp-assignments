Shakib Al Hasan

Shakib Al Hasan () (born 24 March 1987) is a Bangladeshi international cricketer. He is an all-rounder. He bats left-handed in the middle order. He is a slow left-arm orthodox bowler. He played for Bangladesh at under-19 level, at Youth level at 2005-2006. He made his One Day International (ODI) debut against Zimbabwe in August 2006. He made his test debut the May 2007 against India.

Shakib Al Hasan is also a businessman. His company name is Fiesta Event Management Limited.
