Piotr Bikont

Piotr Bikont (12 May 1955 – 27 June 2017) was a Polish journalist, publicist, culinary critic and a theatre director. He was born in Łódź, Poland.

Bikont translated Art Spiegelman's "Maus" to Polish. He was a member of editorial staff of the talking magazine "Gadający Pies" based in Kraków. He was the author of a book "Jewish Cooking According to Balbina Przepiórko".

Bikont died on 27 June 2017 in a car accident near Warsaw, aged 62.
