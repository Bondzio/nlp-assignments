Zuzana Stevulova

Zuzana Števulová (born 1984) is a Slovakia lawyer and lecturer. She helps migrants. She was given an International Women of Courage Award.

Števulová was born in Slovakia in 1984. She is lecturer at the University of Trnava.

She had campaigned for migrants arriving in Europe, She was the first Slovak to be given an International Women of Courage Award in Washington DC. She has supported the rights of the migrants in her country.
