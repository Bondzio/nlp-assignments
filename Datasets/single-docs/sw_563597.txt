Bernardo Bertolucci

Bernardo Bertolucci (; born 16 March 1940) is an Italian director and screenwriter. His most well known works are "The Conformist", "Last Tango in Paris", "1900", "The Last Emperor", "The Sheltering Sky", "Stealing Beauty" and "The Dreamers". Since 1979, he has been married to screenwriter Clare Peploe.
