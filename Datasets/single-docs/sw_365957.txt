List of The Amazing World of Gumball episodes

"The Amazing World of Gumball" is a cartoon comedy series. It was created by Ben Bocquelet for the Cartoon Network.

Since its debut on May 3, 2011, more than 160 episodes of the series have been broadcast. Its fifth season began on September 1, 2016.

Cartoon Network has renewed the series for a sixth season. On September 6, 2016, series creator Ben Bocquelet said that season six of the show will be the final season.
