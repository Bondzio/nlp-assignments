Arrondissement of Château-Chinon (Ville)

The arrondissement of Château-Chinon (Ville), or simply arrondissement of Château-Chinon, is an arrondissement of France. It is part of the Nièvre "département" in the Bourgogne-Franche-Comtéregion. Its capital is the city of Château-Chinon (Ville).

When the Nièvre department was created on 17 February 1800, the "arrondissement" of Château-Chinon (Ville) was part of that original department.

The "arrondissement" of Château-Chinon (Ville) is bordered to the north by the "arrondissement" of Clamecy, to the northeast by the Côte-d'Or (Burgundy) department, to the east and south by the Saône-et-Loire (Burgundy) department and to the west by the "arrondissement" of Nevers.
