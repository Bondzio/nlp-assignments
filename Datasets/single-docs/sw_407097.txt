Eva Ngui Nchama

Eva Ngui Nchama (born on June 9, 1985 in Malabo, Equatorial Guinea) is a athlete from Spain. She has a disability: she cannot see and is a T12/B2 type athlete. She competed in the 2004 Summer Paralympics in Athens, Greece in the women's 100 metres - T12 event. She competed at the 2008 Summer Paralympics in Beijing, China and finished third in the women's 100 metres - T12 event and thir in the women's 200 metres - T12 event. She raced at the 2012 Summer Paralympics and was the 14th runner to finish.

In 2012, she had a €18,000 athlete scholarship with a €3,000 reserve and a €2,500 coaching scholarship.
