64 (number)

Sixty-four is a number. It comes between sixty-three and sixty-five, and is an even number. It is divisible by 1, 2, 4, 8, 16, 32, and 64. 64 is the 8th square number after 1, 4, 9, 16, 25, 36, and 49. It is the fourth cube after 1, 8, and 27. There are sixty-four 1x1 squares on a chess, checkers and draughts board. It is also a power of two.
