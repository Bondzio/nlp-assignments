Stan Mikita

Stan Mikita (born Stanislav Guoth; May 20 1940) is a Slovak-born Canadian retired professional ice hockey centre. He played a career total of 22 years in the National Hockey League (NHL). He played his entire NHL career with the Chicago Black Hawks. He is often regarded as the best centre of the 1960s.

He won the Stanley Cup with the Chicago Black Hawks in 1961. He was inducted into the Hockey Hall of Fame in 1983. He was also inducted into the Slovak Hockey Hall of Fame in 2002.

He currently works as an good will ambassador for the Chicago Blackhawks.
