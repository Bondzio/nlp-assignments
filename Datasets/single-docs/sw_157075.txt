Lesparre-Médoc

Lesparre-Médoc () is a commune in the Gironde department in Nouvelle-Aquitaine region in southwestern France. It is one of the five sub-prefectures of the department.

Lesparre-Médoc is on the Médoc peninsula on left side of the estuary of the Garonne river. The city is at about north of Bordeaux.
