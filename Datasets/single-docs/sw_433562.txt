Ronan Farrow

Ronan Farrow (born Satchel Ronan O'Sullivan Farrow; December 19, 1987) is an American activist. He is the son of actress Mia Farrow. It is not known who his father is - it may be filmmaker Woody Allen or singer Frank Sinatra. He was born in New York City. He has written for "The Guardian", "The Wall Street Journal", and other newspapers. His book, "Pandora’s Box: How American Military Aid Creates America’s Enemies", will be published in 2015.
