Redange (canton)

Redange () is a canton in the west of Luxembourg. Its capital is Redange, also called as Redange-sur-Attert.

On 1 January 1979, the "communes" of Arsdorf, Bigonville, Folschette and Perlé were merged to make the new "commune" of Rambrouch. The number of communes in the canton changed from 13 to 10.
