Bea Wain

Bea Wain (born April 30, 1917) is an American Big Band-era singer. She was born in New York City, New York. She led the vocal group "Bea and the Bachelors" (with Al Rinker, Ken Lane, and John Smedberg) and the V8 (seven boys and a girl) on the Fred Waring show.
