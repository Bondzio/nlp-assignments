Tomas Piñas Bermudez

Tomas Piñas Bermudez (born July 8, 1982 in Granada) is a table tennis athlete from Spain. He has a physical disability: he is class 3 player and competes while using a wheelchair. In 2012, he lived in Ogijares, Granada. He played table tennis at the 2004 Summer Paralympics, 2008 Summer Paralympics, 2012 Summer Paralympics and the 2012 Summer Paralympics. In 2008, he finished third in the Class 3 singles table tennis game..
