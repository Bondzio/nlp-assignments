Edmond Classen

Classen appeared on many movies and over 50 television shows since his career began in 1975. His first role was in "Amsterdam 700". His last role was in the 2012 movie "Black Out".

Classen was born on May 16, 1938 in Arnhem, Netherlands. His daughter, Kiki Classen, is also a Dutch actress. Classen died on January 27, 2014 in his home in Arnhem, Netherlands from heart failure, aged 75.
