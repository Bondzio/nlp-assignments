SpongeBob SquarePants (character)

SpongeBob SquarePants is one of the main characters in the Nickelodeon show "SpongeBob SquarePants". He is a sea sponge who loves his job as a fry cook at the Krusty Krab. SpongeBob lives in a pineapple with his pet snail Gary.

SpongeBob first appeared on the May 1, 1999 television pilot of "SpongeBob SquarePants"
