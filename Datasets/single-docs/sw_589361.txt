Gustavo Pareja

Gustavo Pareja Cisneros (born 10 January 1947) is a Ecuadorian politician. He was born in Otavalo, Ecuador. He served as Governor of the Imbabura Province from 1997 through 2009. In December 2014, he was elected Mayor of Otavalo. 

Pareja was born in Otavalo. He studied at Central University of Ecuador.
