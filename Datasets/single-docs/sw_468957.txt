Arnaud Gauthier

Arnaud Gauthier is a music composer and movie producer. Gauthier has worked as a music producer for the Showtime series "The Borgias". He has also composed music for two albums in 2008 and 2009. Gauthier's latest movie is called Hacker's Game. The movie completed production in May 2014. It is going to be put out in 2015.
