Bleeding Love

"Bleeding Love" is a song by Leona Lewis. It was released as her official second single on October 29, 2007 from her first album "Spirit". The song is written and produced by Ryan Tedder. 

"Bleeding Love" is said to be like a classic Prince record, or Whitney Houston's "My Love Is Your Love".

Lewis had performed the song on the live show of the fourth series of "The X Factor".
