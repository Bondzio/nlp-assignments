William Duborgh Jensen

William Duborgh Jensen (10 May 1935 â€“ 24 May 2017) was a Norwegian fashion and costume designer. He was Norway's first "haute couturier" with his own collection in 1958. He later worked in television and movie as a costume designer.

He was appointed as a Knight, First Class of the Order of St. Olav in 2006 for his efforts in fashion design.

William died on 24 May 2017 in Oslo at the age of 82.
