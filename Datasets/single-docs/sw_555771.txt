Beromünster

Beromünster is a municipality in the district of Sursee in the canton of Lucerne in Switzerland.
On 1 January 2004 the former municipalities of Schwarzenbach merged into the municipality of Beromünster.

On 1 January 2009 the former municipalities of Gunzwil merged into the municipality of Beromünster.

On 1 January 2013 the former municipalities of Neudorf merged into the municipality of Beromünster.
