Michèle Morgan

Michèle Morgan (; 29 February 1920 – 20 December 2016) was a French movie actress.

Morgan was born Simone Renée Roussel in Neuilly-sur-Seine, Hauts-de-Seine, a wealthy western suburb of Paris.

Morgan was known for her roles in "Port of Shadows", "Passage to Marseille" (alongside Humphrey Bogart), "Stormy Waters", "Fabiola", "The Proud and the Beautiful", and in "The Grand Maneuver".

Morgan died on 20 December 2016 at her home in Paris, aged 96.
