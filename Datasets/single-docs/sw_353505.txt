Mariah Carey (album)

Mariah Carey is the debut studio album by American singer-songwriter Mariah Carey. It was released on June 12, 1990 on Colombia Records. It reached number one on the "Billboard" 200 chart. The album has sold over 15 million copies worldwide. Her four singles "Vision of Love", "Love Takes Time", "Someday" and "I Don't Wanna Cry" all reached number one in the US.
