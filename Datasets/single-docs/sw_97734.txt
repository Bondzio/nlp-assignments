Tekken 2

Tekken 2 is a fighting video game made by Namco. It was released in arcades in 1995 and released around the world for the PlayStation in 1996. 

Tekken 2 has 25 playable characters. All of the characters from the original game return in this game (except for Jack, which was replaced by Jack-2).
