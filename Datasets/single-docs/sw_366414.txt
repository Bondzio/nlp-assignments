Emma (wrestler)

Tenille Dashwood (born 1 March 1989) is an Australian professional wrestler and model. She is better known by her ring name Tenille Tayla. She works for the WWE. Dashwood currently wrestles in its developmental territory NXT Wrestling under the ring name Emma.

On July 2, 2014, Emma was released by WWE after she was arrested for sixth-degree larceny. She was reinstated by WWE two hours later.

On June 30, 2014, before Monday Night Raw aired, Dashwood was arrested in Hartford, Connecticut for sixth-degree larceny. Dashwood's lawyer said that she had forgotten to pay for an iPad case, when she was using the self-checkout machine at a Walmart. After she appeared at a community court on July 1, she was ordered to do one day of community service and complete an online course.
