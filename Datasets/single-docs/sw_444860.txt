Cheste

Cheste (Valencian: "Xest") is a city in Spain. It is in the Valencian Community. It is west of Valencia and about east of Madrid. It covers . In 2008, 8,127 people lived there according to the National Institute of Statistics census.

Cheste's main industry is agriculture, specifically the vineyards. Cheste is also known for the MotoGP's Circuit Ricardo Tormo, built in 1999.
