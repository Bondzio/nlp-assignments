José José

José Rómulo Sosa Ortiz (born 17 February 1948), known by his stage name José José, is a Mexican singer-songwriter, musician and occasional actor. His 1983 album "Secretos" has sold over 7 million units. With a large number of international hits, he received several Grammy nominations and recognition worldwide.

In March 2017, José announced that he was diagnosed with pancreatic cancer.
