Geneviève Castrée

Geneviève Castrée Elverum (1981 – 9 July 2016) was a Canadian comics artist, illustrator, and musician. She was from Loretteville, Quebec. She once recorded under the name Woelv and later switched to Ô PAON.

Castrée died on 9 July 2016 from pancreatic cancer in Anacortes, Washington, aged 34.
