Billy Gunn

Monty Kip Sopp (born November 11, 1963 in Orlando, Florida) is an American professional wrestler. He is currently signed to the WWE where he competes under the ring name Billy Gunn. He also works as a trainer for WWE's developmental territory NXT. He has also wrestled for Total Nonstop Action.

Gunn was also known for being a tag team wrestler and was a part of The Smokin' Gunns with on-screen brother Bart Gunn, the New Age Outlaws with Road Dogg, Billy and Chuck with Chuck and D-Generation X.
