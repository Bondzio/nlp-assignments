Whiplash (2014 movie)

Whiplash is a 2014 American drama movie set in New York City. It is about a jazz student and his teacher. It was written and directed by Damien Chazelle. It stars Miles Teller, J. K. Simmons, and Paul Reiser. Filming of the movie took less than a month. Whiplash was released on October 10, 2014 with very positive reviews.
