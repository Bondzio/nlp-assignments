Bilawal Shah Noorani

Syed Bilawal Shah Noorani () was a Mystic Saint and Tabi‘in. Shah Noorani was the descendants of Ali Ibn Abi Talib. Noorani was of the Ahl al-Bayt (member of household of Muhammad).Noorani is considered to be a patron saint of Pakistan.

Noorani's Shrine is popuplar in Balochistan, Pakistan After Hinglaj Mata Mandir.
Noorani Baba Shrine is Located in a narrow valley between the mountains of Khuzdar District, Balochistan

Shah Noorani's annual "Urs" (death anniversary celebration), held on the 10th Ramadan – the ninth month of the Muslim lunar calendar, brings more than half a million pilgrims from all over Pakistan.

Khuzdar in Balochistan province of Pakistan on 13 November 2016 in a bombing at the shrine of Syed Bilawal Shah Noorani killed 54 people.
