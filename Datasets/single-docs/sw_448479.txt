DJ Rashad

Rashad Harden (October 9, 1979 â€“ April 26, 2014), better known by his stage name DJ Rashad, was an American electronic musician, record producer and DJ. He started his career in the early 1990s. He was best known for his work in the footwork genre. He worked with Hyperdub Records and artists such as DJ Spinn. He was born in Hammond, Indiana and was raised in Calumet City, Illinois.

DJ Rashad died from complications relating to a blood clot in his leg on the afternoon of April 26, 2014 in Chicago, Illinois. He was 35.
