Agdash Rayon

Agdash is a raion in central Azerbaijan. Agdash raion has Leki settlement and 72 villages. This raion has a good economic and geographic location. Turyan River and Kura River go through the raion. This area is surrounded by Tugay forests along Kura river bank, and Turyanchay State Preservation to the north.

Agdash city was started in 16th century. It is the capital.
