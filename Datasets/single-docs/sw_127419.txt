Radio:ACTIVE

Radio:Active (typeset as Radio:ACTIVE) is the fourth album by British pop rock band McFly. It is the band's first album with their new, self created label, Super Records after they left Island Records. The first single from the album is "One for the Radio".

On 24 June 2008, McFly said that they would give "Radio:Active" free with copies of "The Mail on Sunday" on 20 July 2008. They will release a deluxe edition of the album in 22 September 2008. They released a second single on 15 September 2008 called "Lies" which is also on the album. The deluxe edition includes four extra songs, a bonus DVD and a 32-page booklet and was released on 22 September 2008.
