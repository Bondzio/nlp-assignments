Ice Age: Dawn of the Dinosaurs

Ice Age 3: Dawn of the Dinosaurs (or Ice Age 3), is a computer animated movie. It was released on July 1, 2009. It is the third movie in the "Ice Age" series. The movie was produced by Blue Sky Studios. It was distributed by 20th Century Fox. The movie was also released on RealD anywhere available.

A sequel, "Ice Age 4: Continental Drift", was released in 2012. "Ice Age: Collision Course" will be released in 2016.

Queen Latifah recorded and performed a version of the song "Walk the Dinosaur" with John Leguizamo.
