Uhm Ji-won

Uhm Ji-won (born December 25, 1977) is a South Korean actress. She was known for her roles in "The Scarlett Letter", "Traces of Love", "The Good, the Bad, and the Weird", "Like You Know It All", and in "Hope".

Uhm married architect Oh Young-wook on May 27, 2014 at the Shilla Hotel in Seoul.
