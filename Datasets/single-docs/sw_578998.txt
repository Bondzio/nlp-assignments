Richard Bustillo

Richard S Bustillo (January 28, 1942 â€“ March 30, 2017) was a American martial arts instructor. He was a student of the late Bruce Lee and an authority on Jeet Kune Do Concepts and Filipino Martial Arts.

Bustillo was born to a Filipino family at a YMCA center in Oahu, Hawaii. 

Bustillo taught Jeet Kune Do, Filipino martial arts, Boxing, Muay Thai, wrestling, mixed martial arts and other arts at his Torrance, California school, the IMB Academy. 

Bustillo died on March 30, 2017 in Torrance, California, aged 75.
