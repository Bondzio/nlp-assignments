Gwangaeto the Great

Gwanggaeto the Great (, , 374 - 413) was an ancient Korean monarch. He was the 19th King of Goguryo and reigned from 393 to 413 CE. During his reign the Goguryeo territory widened with the conquests of SukSin, Mohe, Khitan, Buyeo. His era name was "Yuungrak" (, ).

King Gwanggaeto died from a sickness in the year 413.
