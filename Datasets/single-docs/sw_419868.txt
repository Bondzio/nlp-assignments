Arrondissement of Dijon

The arrondissement of Dijon is an arrondissement of France, in the Côte-d'Or department, Bourgogne-Franche-Comté region. Its capital, and the Prefecture of the department, is the city of Dijon.

Dijon was one the "arrondissement" of Côte-d'Or when the "arrondissements" were created on 17 February 1800.

It is in the northeast of the department and has an area of . It is the "arrondissement" of the Côte-d'Or department where more people lives, with 373,574 inhabitants and a density of inhabitants/km².

The "arrondissement" of Dijon is bordered to the north by the Haute-Marne department, to the northeast by the Haute-Saône department, to the southeast by the Jura department, to the south by the "arrondissement" of Beaune and to the west by the "arrondissement" of Montbard.
