Ashwin Sundar

Ashwin Sundar (27 July 1985 – 18 March 2017) was an Indian race driver. He was most active during 2005 till recent years winning several national titles in Go-Kart, Single Seater Formula Cars, Two Wheelers and Saloon cars.

He was born on 27 July 1985 at Chennai, Tamil Nadu. He was killed in a car accident in Chennai, India on 18 March 2017, aged 31.
