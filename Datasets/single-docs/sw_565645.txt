Sutton Common railway station

Sutton Common station opened in 1930. The only train that operates at this station is Thameslink. This station is in fare zone 4.

There are two platforms at this station. Platform 1 is for trains towards Luton via Wimbledon station. Platform 2 is for trains towards Sutton (Surrey).
