Sodium phosphide

Sodium phosphide is a chemical compound. It contains sodium and phosphide ions. Its chemical formula is NaP.

Sodium phosphide is a black solid. It is ionic. There are several other sodium phosphides that do not have the formula NaP. It reacts with acids to make phosphine gas. It is a reducing agent.

It can be made by reacting liquid sodium with phosphorus(V) chloride. It can also be made by reacting sodium with phosphorus. 

It is used to make semiconductors. It can also be used as a catalyst. 

Sodium phosphide is flammable, making toxic gases when burned. It also makes toxic flammable gases when reacted with acids.
