F. Murray Abraham

F. Murray Abraham (born October 24, 1939) is an American actor. He won the Academy Award for Best Actor for his role as Antonio Salieri in "Amadeus" (1984). He is also known for his roles on "The Name of the Rose" (1986), "Finding Forrester" (2000), "Inside Llewyn Davis" (2013), "All the President's Men" (1976) and "Scarface" (1983).
