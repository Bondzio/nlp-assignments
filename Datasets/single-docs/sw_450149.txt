Valentina Cortese

Valentina Cortese (born 1 January 1923) is an Italian actress. She was known for her roles in "The Glass Mountain", "Thieves' Highway", "The House on Telegraph Hill", and in "The Barefoot Contessa". She was a BAFTA Award in 1973 and was nominated for an Academy Award in 1975 for her role in "Night for Day".
