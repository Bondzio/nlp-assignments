Angry Grandpa

Charles Marvin "Charlie" Green Jr. (born October 16, 1950), better known as Angry Grandpa (AGP), is an American Internet personality. He is a former fireman. With his son, Michael, he has a YouTube channel, "TheAngryGrandpaShow", that has over three million subscribers.

Angry Grandpa was born in Charleston, South Carolina. He has bipolar disorder and skin cancer.
