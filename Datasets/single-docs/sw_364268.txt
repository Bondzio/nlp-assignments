Jim Hill

James Webster "Jim" Hill (born October 21, 1946 in San Antonio, Texas) is a retired American football defensive back. He played in the National Football League. He is now a Los Angeles-based sportscaster. He is a Studio Host for the both the LA Dodgers and LA Lakers games.
