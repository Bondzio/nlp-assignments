Hideaway

Hideaway is a melodramatic horror movie from 1995. It is about a man who dies during a car accident. However, this man is revived two hours afterwards. He begins having frightening visions. He thinks he may have become connected to a serial killer. Jeff Goldblum plays the accident victim, Alicia Silverstone plays Regina Harrison and Christine Lahti plays Lindsey.

The movie was released March 3, 1995 in the United States. Reviews were mixed to very negative. It made nearly US$3 million below its budget of $15 million.
