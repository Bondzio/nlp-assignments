Neutral Milk Hotel

Neutral Milk Hotel is an American indie rock band. They started in 1989. They were formed by singer, songwriter, and guitarist Jeff Mangum. He was the only member at first. Then Robert Schneide joined the band for its first album, "On Avery Island". It was released on Merge Records. After "On Avery Island" was released, Julian Koster, Scott Spillane and Jeremy Barnes joined the band. They then released an album called "In the Aeroplane Over the Sea" in 1998. Pitchfork scored their second album 10/10 when it was released again in 2005. In 2009, Amazon.com's editors put "In the Aeroplane Over the Sea" at number two on their list of "The 100 Greatest Indie Rock Albums of All Time".
On December 11, 2011, the band released their first release since 1998. It was an EP called "Ferris Wheel on Fire".

They are going to perform at the 2014 Primavera Sound Festival in Barcelona.

In "Will Grayson, Will Grayson", a 2010 novel by John Green and David Levithan, Will Grayson 1 is a Neutral Milk Hotel fan.

"Oh Comely" magazine is a named after the song of the same name on "In the Aeroplane Over the Sea".
