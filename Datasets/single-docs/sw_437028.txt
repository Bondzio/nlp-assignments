Chevrolet Celebrity

The Chevrolet Celebrity was a mid sized automobile. It was made for years 1982 to 1990. The Celebrity was best selling for 1986 in North America. The vehicle lasted one generation.

The sedan was stopped for 1989. The station wagon was last produced for 1990. Chevrolet replaced the sedan with the Lumina, and the station was succeeded by Chevrolet Lumina APV (a minivan).
