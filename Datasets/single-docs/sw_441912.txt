Graham Norton

Graham William Walker (born 4 April 1963), better known by his stage name Graham Norton, is an Irish actor, comedian and television presenter. He is best known for hosting "So Graham Norton" (1998–2002), "V Graham Norton" (2002–03), "The Graham Norton Effect" (2004), "Graham Norton's Bigger Picture" (2004–05) and "The Graham Norton Show" (2007–present).

Norton was born into a Protestant family in Clondalkin, Dublin though he was raised in Bandon, County Cork. He is openly gay.
