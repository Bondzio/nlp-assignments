Chris Parnell

Thomas Christopher "Chris" Parnell (born February 5, 1967) is an American actor, comedian and singer. He is best known as a cast member on NBC's "Saturday Night Live" from 1998 until 2006 and for his role as Dr. Leo Spaceman on NBC's Emmy Award-winning comedy series "30 Rock". He has appeared in movies such as "Down with Love" (2003), "" (2004), "21 Jump Street" (2012), "Hotel Transylvania" (2012), "" (2013).

Parnell was born in Memphis, Tennessee to a Southern Baptist family.
