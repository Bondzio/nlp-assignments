Aldo Ciccolini

Aldo Ciccolini (, 15 August 1925 – 1 February 2015), was an Italian-French pianist. He was born in Naples, Italy. 

He taught at the Conservatoire de Paris from 1970 to 1988. His students included Akiko Ebi, Géry Moutier, Jean-Yves Thibaudet, Artur Pizarro, Nicholas Angelich, André Sayasov and Jean-Luc Kandyoti.

Ciccolini died at the age of 89 on 1 February 2015 at his home in Paris.
