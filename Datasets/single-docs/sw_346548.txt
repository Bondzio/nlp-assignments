Marc Sellam

Marc Sellam, born the 3th of March 1949, is a French businessman and President of a private education group in France. He is the founder of IONIS Education Group.

Graduate from "École spéciale de mécanique et électricité" (1974) and from "Institut des études supérieures des techniques des organisations", Marc Sellam has done a career in telecommunications industry before joining higher education. He first founds the "Institut supérieur européen de gestion group" in 1980 before created IONIS Education Group. He is still in 2012 the current President of the group.
