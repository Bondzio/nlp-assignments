Lje

The Cyrillic letter lj (Љ, љ) was similar to Л and Ь and active member of words that participate in: palatal lateral , a sound similar (but not equal) to the palatalized alveolar lateral represented by the digraph ЛЬ . It is used in Serbian and Macedonian. It was created by Vuk Karadžić and it is same to the Latin digraph in Serbian language and Croatian language.
