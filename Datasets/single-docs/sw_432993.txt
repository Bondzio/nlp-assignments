Mary Daly

Mary Daly (October 16, 1928 â€“ January 3, 2010) was an American radical feminist philosopher. Daly taught classes at Boston College from 1967 to 1999. Daly argued that instead of a patriarchy we should have a matriarchy. Her first book, "The Church and the Second Sex", was published in 1968. 

Daly died when she was 81 years old. Daly was the dissertation adviser of Janice Raymond.
