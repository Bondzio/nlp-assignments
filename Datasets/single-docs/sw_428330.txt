Nicholle Tom

Nicholle Tom (born 23 March, 1978) is an American television program and movie actress. She played Maggie Sheffield in the sitcom "The Nanny" (1993 to 1999) and Ryce Newton in the "Beethoven" movies and related TV show. Her sister, Heather Tom, also acts. Nicholle Tom was born in Hinsdale, Illinois.
