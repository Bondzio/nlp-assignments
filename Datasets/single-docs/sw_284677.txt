Andy Panda

'Andrew "Andy" Panda' is a fictional panda. He was created by Walter Lantz for Universal Studios in the 1930s -1940s from 1939-1949.
His girlfriend was Miranda Panda. He introduced Woody Woodpecker in 1940's Knock Knock. He was one of Walter Lantz's staples (the others being Woody Woodpecker, Oswald Rabbit, Chilly Willy). His last appearance was in 1949's "Scrappy Birthday". In comics he was often paired with Charlie Chicken.
