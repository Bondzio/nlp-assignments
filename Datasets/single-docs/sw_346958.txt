Spock

Spock is a character in "Star Trek". He is often called Mr. Spock by others. He has been played by Leonard Nimoy and by Zachary Quinto. Jacob Kogan played Spock when he was a child in the 2009 movie "Star Trek". Spock is half Vulcan and half human.

Spock first appeared in "" and he was in many of the Star Trek movies. He also appeared in two episodes of "". 

Spock's signature gesture was the Vulcan salute.
