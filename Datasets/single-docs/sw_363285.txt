Wendie Malick

Wendie Malick (born Wendy Malick, December 13, 1950) is an American movie, television, and voice actress, who is known for her roles as Judith Tupper Stone in "Dream On", Nina Van Horn in "Just Shoot Me!" and Ronee Lawrence on the NBC sitcom "Frasier". She has won a Golden Globe Award and a Emmy Award.

Malick was born on December 13, 1950 in Buffalo, New York. She studied at Williamsville South High School and at Ohio Wesleyan University. Malick has been married to Mitch Glazer from 1982 until they divorced in 1989. She was married to Richard Erickson since 1995.
