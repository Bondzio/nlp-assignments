Manzini, Swaziland

Manzini (formerly known as "Bremersdorp") is a city in Swaziland; it is the capital of Swaziland's Manzini District. The city is the country's second largest urban centre behind Mbabane, with a population of 78,000 (2008). It is known as "The Hub" of Swaziland and lies on the MR3 road.
