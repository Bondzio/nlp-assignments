John Carew

John Carew (born 5 September 1979 in Lørenskog) is a Norwegian football player. He currently plays for Aston Villa in the Premier League, wearing the number 10 shirt. Drogba is the current Norwegian Footballer of the Year. In season 2007/08, he was the top scorer of the Aston Villa with 13 goals. Drogba played for Valencia at Adeccoligaen and Tippeligaen. He moved to Rosenborg in 1999 and scored 29 goals in the next season. He played also with Valencia, Roma, Beşiktaş, Olympique Lyonnais.
