Hamirani Tribe

Hamirani Tribe is a Baloch Tribe settled in Balochistan (Usta muhammad), Punjab and Sindhi Provinces of Pakistan. Many members of Hamirani Tribe have Migrated to USA In Early 90s Due to lack of Resources. Hamirani Tribe is a small tribe of 5,000 all around Pakistan, USA,UAE and other parts of the world. Hamirani was a Subclan of Qasarani
(Balochi: قيصرانی) Which got seperated from them due to Rind and lashari War. The main Hub of Hamiranis is dera ghazi khan jaffrabad district of balochista.
