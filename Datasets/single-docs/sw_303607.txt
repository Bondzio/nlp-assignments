Melissa McCarthy

Melissa McCarthy (born August 26, 1970) is an American movie and television actress and comedienne. She is well known for her role as Sookie St. James on The WB television show "Gilmore Girls" and "Dena" on "Samantha Who?". She currently stars in the CBS sitcom "Mike & Molly".

She also starred in the movies Bridesmaids and Identity Thief.
