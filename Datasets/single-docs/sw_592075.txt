Ariyalur taluk

Ariyalur taluk is a taluk of Ariyalur district of the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. The headquarters of the taluk is the town of Ariyalur.

According to the 2011 census, the taluk of Ariyalur had a population of 255749 with 126931 males and 128818 females. There were 1015 women for every 1000 men. The taluk had a literacy rate of 64.54. Child population in the age group below 6 was 13812 Males and 12795 Females.
