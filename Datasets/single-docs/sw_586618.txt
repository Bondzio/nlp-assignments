Ngwanaka Okae

Ngwanaka O Kae? is a 1984 South African Sepedi Drama that was popular in those times. It deals specifically with the issue of stealing a child(son) of a young couple called Mathlodi(wife) and Rateka(husband). The couple lives with Mathlodi's aunt in a rural township of Pretoria called Sohanguvhe. the couple originally hails from a province called Limpopo, the then Northen Transvaal Province.

Their neighbour woman steals their child and vanishes with it but finally caught
