Checkers (restaurant)

Checkers Drive In Restaurants is one of the largest chains of double drive-thru restsurants in the United States. It became a private company in June 2006 by merging with Taxi Holdings.

The company operates Checkers and Rally's restaurants. Checkers was founded in 1985. Rally's was founded in 1986.

They are called two different names in South and Midwest. They serve Cajun French fries and burgers.
