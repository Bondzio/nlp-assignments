Arrondissement of Millau

The arrondissement of Millau is an arrondissement of France. It is part of the Aveyron "département" in the Occitanie region. Its capital, and subprefecture of the department, is the city of Millau.

When the "arrondissements" were created in 1800, Millau was one of them in the Aveyron department.

It is the southeastern part of the "arrondissements" of the department, with an area of , the second in area of the department, and a population of 70,995 inhabitants and a population density of inhabitants/km².

The "arrondissement" is bordered to the north and west by the "arrondissement" of Rodez, to the northeast by the Lozère department, to the east by the Gard department, to the south by the Hérault department and to the southwest by the Tarn department.

After the reorganisation of the cantons in France, cantons are not subdivisions of the "arrondissements" so they could have "communes" that belong to different "arrondissements".
