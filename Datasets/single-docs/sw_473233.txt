Tom Adams (actor)

Tom Adams (9 March 1938 - 11 December 2014) was an English actor. Adams was known for his roles as Dai Nimmo in "The Great Escape" and as Commander Vorshak in "Doctor Who".

Adams died on 11 December 2014 at the age of 76 at Wexham Park Hospital in Slough, Berkshire from cancer.
