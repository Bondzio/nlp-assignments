Hanahoi

Hanahoi (Society of One) was one of South Korea's power elite from 1979-93. The group monopolized strategic military posts and produced major political leaders, including two presidents: Chun Doo-hwan and Roh Tae-woo.[https://books.google.com/books?id=efZ8AgAAQBAJ&pg=PA186&lpg=PA186&dq=Hanahoi+military+korea&source=bl&ots=gUOi_sWG3M&sig=sz8KrgmGRK_o2GHmttaYxIpZgBQ&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwir7qGW5ejTAhUpw4MKHWX6AtQQ6AEIMTAC#v=onepage&q=Hanahoi&f=false [1<nowiki>]</nowiki>]
