Ken Gorgal

Kenneth Robert Gorgal (February 13, 1929 â€“ May 8, 2016) was an American football player. He was born in Peru, Illinois. He played in the National Football League for the Cleveland Browns, the Chicago Bears and the Green Bay Packers in the 1950s. He played college football at Purdue University.

After retiring from football, Gorgal went back to Chicago. He later held a seat on the Chicago Board of Trade, a major exchange.

Gorgal died on May 8, 2016 in Vero Beach, Florida at the age of 87.
