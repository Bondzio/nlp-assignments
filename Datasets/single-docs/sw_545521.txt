Fraubrunnen

Fraubrunnen is a municipality in the administrative district of Bern-Mittelland in the canton of Berne in Switzerland.

On the 1 January 2014 the former municipalities of Büren zum Hof, Etzelkofen, Grafenried, Limpach, Mülchi, Schalunen and Zauggenried merged into the municipality of Fraubrunnen.
