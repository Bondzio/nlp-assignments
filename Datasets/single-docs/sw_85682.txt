William H. Macy

William Hall Macy, Jr. (born March 13, 1950) is an Emmy Award-winning and Academy Award-nominated American actor. He was in the movie "Fargo". He is also a teacher and director in theatre, movie and television. Macy has described his screen persona as "sort of a Middle American, WASPy, Lutheran kind of guy... Everyman". He is now known for his award-winning role as Frank Gallagher in the television series "Shameless".
