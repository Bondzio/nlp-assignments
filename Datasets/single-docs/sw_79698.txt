Broward County, Florida

Broward County is a county in the U.S. state of Florida. As of 2000, 1,623,018 people lived there. This makes it the second most populated county in the state. According to 2006 U.S. Census estimates, 1,787,636 people lived there. Its county seat is Fort Lauderdale, Florida.

Broward County is part of the "Miami metropolitan area", which was home to an estimated 6,012,331 people at the 2015 census.
