Community (TV series)

Community is an American comedy series on NBC. "Community" first aired on September 17, 2009, at 9:30 PM (EST). However, it was moved to 8:00 PM (EST) by its fourth episode. 

The show is about the lives of students at a community college in the fictional city of Greendale, Colorado. 

"Community"'s sixth and final season concluded on June 2, 2015.
