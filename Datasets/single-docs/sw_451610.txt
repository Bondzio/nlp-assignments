Brooke White

Brooke White (born June 2, 1983) is an American indie pop singer and songwriter. She was the fifth place finalist on the 7th season of "American Idol". Her first album, "Songs from the Attic", was released in 2005. Her first post-idol record, "High Hopes & Heartbreak", was released in 2009.

White was born in Phoenix, Arizona. She was raised in Mesa, Arizona.
