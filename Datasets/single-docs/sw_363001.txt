Bob Rathbun

Bob Rathbun (born November 25, 1954 in Wakefield, Rhode Island) is an American sportscaster. He currently is the voice of the Atlanta Hawks of the NBA and the Atlanta Dream of the WNBA. He was the voice of the Atlanta Braves for many years. He calls college football and basketball for FSN South and college basketball games for Jefferson-Pilot/Raycom Sports. Rathbun also called games for the Washington Bulletts, Baltimore Orioles, Tidewater Tides, Detroit Tigers, and Richmond Braves.
