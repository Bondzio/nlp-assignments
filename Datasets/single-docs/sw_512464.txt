This Used to Be My Playground

"This Used to Be My Playground" is a soft rock ballad from American singer Madonna. The song is about memories of childhood dreams. The song was featured in the credits of the movie "A League of Their Own" in 1992. It is, however, not on the soundtrack album. 

The song spent one week at #1 on "Billboard" Hot 100 in summer 1992.

The longest version of the song is just over six minutes in length.
