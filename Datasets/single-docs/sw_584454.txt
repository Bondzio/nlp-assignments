Chu Ke-liang

Chu Ke-liang (; 5 December 194615 May 2017) was a Taiwanese, comedian, actor, television show host and singer. He was known for his "over-the-top appearance" with unusual clothing and hair styles and his coarse humor. He was born in Zuoying, Kaohsiung, Taiwan. He was known for his roles in "The New Legend of Shaolin", "David Loman", and in "The Wonderful Wedding".

Chu died in Taipei, Taiwan from liver failure caused by colorectal cancer on 15 May 2017 at the age of 70.
