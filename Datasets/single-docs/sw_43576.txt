Belinda (entertainer)

Belinda Peregrin Schüll (born August 15, 1989 in Madrid, Spain) is a Spanish-born Mexican singer and actress. She starred in Disney Channel's movie "". She sang "A La Nanita Nana" and "Why Wait" in the movie. For a music video, she sang "Dance with Me" with Drew Seeley. She has a disc called "Utopia" and her song "Ni Freud Ni Tu Mamá". She has been acting since she was 5 or 6 years old.
