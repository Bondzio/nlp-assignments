Damage (1992 movie)

Damage is a 1992 British melodramatic thriller. It was directed by Louis Malle. It is based on the novel "Damage" by Josephine Hart. Juliette Binoche is in the cast. "Damage" was originally rated NC-17, though was edited downwards to an R rating. The plot is about a politician who falls in love with his son's girlfriend.

Miranda Richardson was nominated for an Academy Award and won a BAFTA in the category of Best Supporting Actress.
