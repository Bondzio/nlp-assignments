Miss USA

The Miss USA pageant is a beauty contest. It has been held every year since 1952. Winners compete in the Miss Universe pageant. The Miss Universe Organization operates both pageants. It also operates Miss Teen USA. 

On June 8, 2014, Nia Sanchez became Miss USA 2014. 

It used to be owned by billionaire businessman and television personality Donald Trump who is currently the 45th President of the United States since 2017.
