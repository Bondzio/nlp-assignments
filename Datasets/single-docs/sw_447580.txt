Derek Cooper

Derek MacDonald Cooper (25 May 1925 – 18 April 2014) was a British journalist. He wrote about food, wine and whisky. He worked with "The Listener", "The Observer", "Home & Garden" and "Saga Magazine". 

Cooper created the idea of BBC Radio 4’s weekly program "The Food Programme". He was born in London, England.

Cooper died from Parkinson's disease in London, aged 88.
