Puss in Boots (2011 movie)

Puss In Boots is a 2011 animated movie. It is a spin-off prequel in the "Shrek" series of animated movies. It follows the character Puss In Boots, who first appeared in "Shrek 2". It is about his adventures before appearing in "Shrek 2". A sequel titled Puss In Boots 2: 9 Lives And 40 Thieves will be released on November 2, 2018.

The movie was directed by Chris Miller, produced by Guillermo del Toro, and written by Brian Lynch. It stars Antonio Banderas as Puss in Boots, Zach Galifianakis as Humpty Dumpty, Salma Hayek as Kitty Softpaws, and Billy Bob Thornton and Amy Sedaris as Jack and Jill. Like the "Shrek" movies, this movie is a parody of fairy tales.

"Puss in Boots" was released on DVD, Blu-ray and Blu-ray 3D on February 24, 2012. The movie has a short animation called "Puss in Boots: The Three Diablos". 7.2 million home entertainment units have been sold worldwide.

Another featured extra short is "Klepto Kitty"; a three-minute profile of Dusty the Klepto Kitty, a notorious cat in California who steals items from neighbors' yards. Some of it was filmed on a night vision kitty-cam, hung around Dusty's neck by the Animal Planet network for their own documentary.
