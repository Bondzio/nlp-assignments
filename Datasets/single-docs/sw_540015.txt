Gordon Peters

Gordon Peter Wilkinson, known professionally as Gordon Peters (born 29 November 1926 in Durham) is a British television actor. 

He starred in a BBC TV comedy series in 1973 called "The Gordon Peters Show". He is known for his roles in "Hi-de-Hi!", "Dad's Army", "Are You Being Served?", "Grace & Favour", "One Foot in the Grave", "Never the Twain", "Little Britain", "Keeping Up Appearances" and "HBO's Tales from the Crypt".
