Al Feldstein

Albert B. Feldstein (October 24, 1925 â€“ April 29, 2014) was an American writer, editor, and artist. Feldstein is best known for his work at EC Comics. He worked, from 1956 to 1985, as the editor of the satirical magazine "Mad". Since retiring from "Mad", Feldstein has worked on American paintings of Western wildlife.

Feldstein died on April 29, 2014 at his home near Livingston, Montana. No cause of death was released.
