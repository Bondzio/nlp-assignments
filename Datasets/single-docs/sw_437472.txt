Armie Hammer

Armand Douglas "Armie" Hammer (born August 28, 1986) is an American actor. He is best known for his roles as the Winklevoss twins in the 2010 movie "The Social Network", The Lone Ranger in the 2013 movie "The Lone Ranger" and as Jackson Storm in the 2017 Pixar movie "Cars 3".

Hammer was born in Los Angeles, California and is of Russian Jewish, Russian, English, Scotch-Irish, and Cherokee origin. He took courses while at the University of California, Los Angeles, before he started acting in 2004. He has been married to television host and actress Elizabeth Chambers since May 10, 2010. They live together in Los Angeles.
