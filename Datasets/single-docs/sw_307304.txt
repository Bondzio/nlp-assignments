Four Christmases

Four Christmases is an 2008 American romantic comedy movie that was produced by Gary Barber, Roger Birnbaum, Jonathan Glickman, Vince Vaughn and Reese Witherspoon and was directed by Seth Gordon. "Four Christmases" was released on November 26, 2008 in North America. The movie received negative reviews with a 25% rating on Rotten Tomatoes and a 41 out of 100 from Metacritic.
