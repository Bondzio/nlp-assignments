Ted 2

Ted 2 is a 2015 American comedy movie. It was directed, produced, and co-written by Seth MacFarlane. It is the sequel to the 2012 film "Ted". Mark Wahlberg, MacFarlane, and Mila Kunis will reprise their roles, with new roles played by Amanda Seyfried, Morgan Freeman, and Richard Schiff.

Filming for this movie started on July 28, 2014. It is scheduled to be released on June 26, 2015.
