Gal Gadot

Gal Gadot ( ; ; born 30 April 1985) is an Israeli actress and model. Gadot won Miss Israel 2004. She played Gisele Harabo in the 2009 movie "Fast and Furious". Gadot also played Wonder Woman in "" in 2016. She will appear in her own Wonder Woman movie simply titled "Wonder Woman" (2017). She will appear again as Wonder Woman in "Justice League" (2017). In 2013, she was ranked as the second highest earning fashion model in Israel. She is the face of Gucci's Bamboo perfume.

Gadot was born in Israel to Jewish parents. She was born and grew up in Rosh HaAyin, Israel. In Hebrew, her first name means "wave". Her last name means "riverbanks". Her parents were born in Israel.
