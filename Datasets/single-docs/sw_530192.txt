Karolína Kurková

Karolína Kurková (born 28 February 1984), is a Czech model. She is best known as a former Victoria's Secret Angel.

Kurková was discovered by Miuccia Prada in 1999. She became the face of Louis Vuitton in 2001, Chanel in 2003. In 2002, Kurková was the youngest model to be elected the Model of the Year at the VH1/Vogue Fashion Awards. After walking for Victoria's Secret since 2001, she was an Angel from 2005 to 2009. In 2002 and 2006, Kurková wore the Fantasy Bra, a multimillion dollar bra, during the Victoria's Secret Fashion Show. In 2008, E! named her the sexiest woman of the year.
