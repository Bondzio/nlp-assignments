Juan Manuel Lebrero Morillo

Juan Manuel Lebrero Morillo (born October 22, 1969 in Sevilla) is a track and field athlete from Spain. He has a physical disability: He is a T44 type athlete. He competed the 1996 Summer Paralympics in the shot put. He was the third best person in the shot put. He finished 9th in the javelin and he finished 5th in the discus.
