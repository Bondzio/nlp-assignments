Dan Hamhuis

Daniel Hamhuis (born December 13, 1982 in Smithers, British Columbia, Canada) is a Canadian professional ice hockey defencemen. He currently plays for the Vancouver Canucks of the National Hockey League (NHL).

Hamhuis formerly played for the Nashville Predators for 6 seasons.

Hamhuis played in the WHL for the Prince George Cougars for 4 seasons and played for the Milwaukee Admirals in the AHL.
