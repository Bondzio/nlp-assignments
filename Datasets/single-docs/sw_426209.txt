Evan Almighty

Evan Almighty is a 2007 American comedy movie and a spin-off sequel to "Bruce Almighty". It stars Steve Carell, Morgan Freeman, Molly Shannon, John Goodman, Wanda Sykes, and Jonah Hill. 

It is about congressman, Evan Baxter, trying to save many animals before a massive flood wipes-out an entire forest after he was sent by God. It was released on June 22, 2007 to negative reviews.
