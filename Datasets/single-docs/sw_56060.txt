Philipp Lahm

Philipp Lahm (born 11 November 1983, in Munich, Germany) is a German football player. He plays as a defender for Bayern Munich.

From 2003 until 2005 he played for Stuttgart. He played with the Germany national team from 2004 to 2014. He is the captain for his club, and the former captain for Germany.
