Tyrese Gibson

Tyrese Gibson (December 30 1978) is an American R&B singer, rapper, actor and model. He has acted in many movies and television series. These include "Baby Boy" (2001), "2 Fast 2 Furious" and the "Transformers" movie series. In 2000, Gibson won the American Music Award for "Favorite New R&B/Soul Artist". He has also been nominated for two Grammy Awards.
