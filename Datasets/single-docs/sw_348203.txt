Wide Sargasso Sea

Wide Sargasso Sea is an erotic drama movie of 1993 in which Rachel Ward, Karina Lombard and Naomi Watts star. "Wide Sargasso Sea" is rated NC-17 because of sexual content. It is based on the 1966 Jean Rhys book of the same name.

The movie is about love and lust in 1840s Jamaica, shortly after Britain freed the slaves in its Caribbean colonies. 

The movie received mixed reviews from critics. Roger Ebert gave the movie three and a half stars.
