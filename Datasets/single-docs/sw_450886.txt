Riding in Cars with Boys

Riding in Cars with Boys is a 2001 American biographical drama movie. It is based on Beverly Donofrio's autobiography. The movie is set in the years from 1961 to 1985. Drew Barrymore plays Beverly. Brittany Murphy plays Fay. Maggie Gyllenhaal plays Amelia.

The movie received mixed reviews. It was released in October 2001.
