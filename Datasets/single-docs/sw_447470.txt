Adam Rose

Raymond Leppan (born 20 July 1979) is an South African professional wrestler. He currently works for the WWE where he competes in their developmental territory NXT Wrestling. He competes under the ring name Adam Rose and is also known by his former ring name Leo Kruger.

On the January 30 tapings of "NXT", Leppan debuted under the Adam Rose name and had the gimmick of an eccentric rock star.
