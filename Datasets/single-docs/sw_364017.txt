Michael Peña

Michael Anthony Peña (born January 13, 1976) is an American movie, television, and voice actor, and scientologist. He is best known for his roles in movies such as "Crash", "World Trade Center", "Shooter", "Observe and Report", "30 Minutes or Less", "Tower Heist", and "End of Watch". 

Peña was born on January 13, 1976 in Chicago, Illinois. He is married to Brie Shaffer. They have a son, Roman.
