Hand in My Pocket

"Hand in My Pocket" is a rock song by Alanis Morissette. It was from her third studio album "Jagged Little Pill". The album peaked at number one in Canada. In the United States it hit number one on the U.S. Billboard 200 albums chart. The song was released as a single on 31 October 1995 in the United States. It became her second song to hit number one on the Billboard Modern Rock Tracks chart. A music video in black and white was released for the single showing Morissette at a festival driving her car.
