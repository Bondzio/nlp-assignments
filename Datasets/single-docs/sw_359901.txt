Lee Lyang

Lee Lyang (, 1519 - 1583) was a politician and philosopher during the Korean Joseon Dynasty.

Lee was a member of the royal family of the Joseon Dynasty. He was a descendant of Prince Hyoryeongs and uncle of Queen Insun, wife of Myeongjong of Joseon. In 1546 he passed to Saengwon and Chinsa exam and passed to Imperial examination in 1552.
