Barcelona Metro

The Barcelona Metro (Catalan and Spanish: "Metro de Barcelona"), part of the public transportation system of Barcelona, Spain is an extensive network of electrified railways that run underground in central Barcelona and above ground into the city's suburbs. It has 10 lines, which together are 112 km (133 mi) long. There are 152 stations. In 2015, it will have 11 lines, 240 km and 296 stations.
