Mihnea Motoc

Mihnea Motoc (born 11 November 1966, in Bucharest) is the current ambassador of Romania to the European Union.

Motoc has been a Romanian diplomat since 1990. Since then most of the time he spent in post around the world. In 2003, he joined the United Nations as a permanent representative of Romania. In 2008 he went to Brussels to be a permanent representative of Romania to the European Union.
