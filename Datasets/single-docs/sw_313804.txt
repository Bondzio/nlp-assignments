Wonderful Christmastime

"Wonderful Christmastime" is Christmas song which was first recorded by Paul McCartney and released in 1979. The single did not chart on "Billboard" Hot 100 in the United States. It did, however, reach #6 on United Kingdom music charts. This single is played during the Christmas season worldwide with strong popularity. Other singers, including Kelly Rowland of Destiny's Child, Hilary Duff and Demi Lovato have covered this song throughout the years.
