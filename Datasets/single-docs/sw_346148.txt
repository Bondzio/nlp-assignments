Elliott Belgrave

Elliot Belgrave, CHB, QC (born March 16, 1931 ), is a Barbadian politician and retired High Court Judge. He is the Acting Governor-General of Barbados since 1 November 2011. Belgrave graduated from the universities of Cambridge (MA) and London (LLB). He is a former Director of Public Prosecutions in Barbados. He was Judge of the Supreme Court and Court of Appeal of Barbados. He is a Queen's Counsel. Belgrave was awarded the Companion of Honour of Barbados for his contribution to the legal system in Barbados.
