Something to Talk About (movie)

Something to Talk About is a 1995 American romantic comedy-drama movie. Julia Roberts plays Grace. Dennis Quaid plays her husband Eddie, who is having an affair with another woman. Robert Duvall plays her father Wyly. Kyra Sedgwick plays Emma. Gena Rowlands plays Georgia.

The movie got mixed reviews from the critics. The title comes from a Bonnie Raitt song of the same name.
