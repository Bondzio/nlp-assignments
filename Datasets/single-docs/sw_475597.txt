Star Wars: The Clone Wars (movie)

Star Wars: The Clone Wars is a 2008 feature length computer animated movie. It has the same title as the by Lucasfilm and Lucasfilm Animation and released by Warner Bros.. The movie takes place as the start of the television series. It was released in August 2008.
The movie takes place three months after the battle of Geonosis and outbreak of the Clone Wars. Anakin Skywalker must take on a Padawan called "Ahsoka Tano". They must save the hulett from the "Confederacy Systems of Independence" with his master Obi-Wan Kenobi.
