Cox Habbema

Cornelia "Cox" Habbema (21 March 1944 â€“ 18 April 2016) was a Dutch movie and television actress. From 1968 to 1984 she played in movies for the East-German filmproduction DEFA. She was known for her roles in "To Grab the Ring" (1968), "Eolomea" (1972), "The Incorrigible Barbara" (1977), "Apprehension" (1982) and "A Question of Silence" (1982). She was born in Amsterdam, Noord-Holland, Netherlands.

Habbema died of cancer in Amsterdam on 18 April 2016, aged 72.
