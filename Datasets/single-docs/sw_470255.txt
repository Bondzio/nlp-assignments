Bengt Hennings

Bengt Hennings is a dansband from Länghem in Sweden. The band was established in 1967. By the late 2000s and the early 2010s, the band scored album chart successes in Sweden.

The band participated at Dansbandskampen 2008. The band made it further from the first programme together with Scotts, before getting knocked out by Larz-Kristerz in the second programme. The band was then revoted into the finals. The band's wind section won a Guldklaven Award in 2008 in the year "wind musicians of the year". The band has also participated in the documentary film Får jag lov - till den sista dansen?.
