Lyubka Rondova

Lyubka Vasileva Rondova, Ljubka Rondova or Lubka Rondova () (24 September 1936 â€“ 15 March 2016) was a Bulgarian folk singer. She best known for performing and recording many of the traditional song from Macedonia as a folklore region. She was born in Sidirochori, Greece to Bulgarian parents. She was awarded the highest Bulgarian order Stara planina, First Degree in 2002.

Rondova died in Sofia, Bulgaria at the age of 79.
