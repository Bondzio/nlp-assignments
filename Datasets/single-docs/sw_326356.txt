Iwate Museum of Art

The or IMA is a prefectural art museum in northern Honshu. It is at Morioka in Iwate Prefecture.

The museum acquires, conserves, researches, communicates and exhibits a collection of art created by local artists such as Yorozu Tetsugoro, Matsumoto Shunsuke, and Funakoshi Yasutake.
