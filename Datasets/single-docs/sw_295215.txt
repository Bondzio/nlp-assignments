Sineu

"Sineu" is a mountainous municipality in central Majorca, one of the Balearic Islands, Spain.
Sineu is situated in the middle of mallorca´s island. Sineu has a population of more than 3000 residents.
Sineu is the capital of spring fairs. Sineu Fair is the oldest on the island and has more visitors than any other fair in Mallorca.Sineu’s weekly market takes place on Wednesdays. It is quite an event. It is the only livestock market of Mallorca.Nowadays the market is part of the visit to Sineu and many visitors come to see the market.
