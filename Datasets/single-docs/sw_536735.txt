Sher e Bangla Medical College

Sher-e-Bangla Medical College (SBMC), is a government medical school in Bangladesh, established in 1968. It is located in Barisal.

Construction began on the college on 6 November 1964, at that time it was known as Barisal Medical College.

There are three hostels for male students and two for the female students. Both male and female intern doctors have each one hostel for them.
