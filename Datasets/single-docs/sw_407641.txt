Ailee

Amy Lee, better known by her stage name Ailee, is a Korean-American singer. She was born in Denver. She grew up in New Jersey. She released her first single, "Heaven", on February 8, 2012.

On September 13, 2011, Ailee along with Wheesung held a performance on the MBC's Chuseok special episode of "Singer and Trainee". Ailee's stage presence was immediately felt by the audience, who was amazed that she was like a professional singer, even though she had not yet officially debuted.
