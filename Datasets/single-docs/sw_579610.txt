Montana, Switzerland

Montana is a former municipality in the district of Sierre in the canton of Valais in Switzerland.

It was one of the six municipalities that form the ski resort Crans-Montana (together with Icogne, Lens, Randogne, Mollens and Chermignon).

On 1 January 2017 the former municipalities of Montana, Chermignon, Mollens and Randogne merged into the new municipality of Crans-Montana.
