Sheikh-ul-Bandi

Sheikhul Bandi is one of the 51 Union Council (sub-divisions) of Abbottabad District in the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan. It is in the valley toward the north-west of the Sarban Hills. It is located at 34°8'60N, 73°13'60E 
Most people of Sheikhul Bandi are the Jadoons. The main language is Hindko although Urdu is also spoken and understood.

The Union Council is subdivided into the following areas: Cantonment, Qasba Abbottabad, Sheikhulbandi and Rakh Civil Military.
