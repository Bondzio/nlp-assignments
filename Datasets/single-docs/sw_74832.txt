Las Terrenas, Samaná

Las Terrenas is a Dominican municipality in the Samaná province, on the north coast of the peninsula of Samaná.

The name Las Terrenas comes from the French "La terrienne" (the "lady that owns land").

The municipality had, in , a total population of 18,829: 9,776 men and 9,053 women. The urban population was of the total population.

Las Terrenas has a total area of , about of the total area of the province of Samaná. The city is an elevation of above sea level, and at to the northwest of Samaná.

Las Terrenas does not have any municipal district.

The municipality of Las Terrenas has the Atlantic Ocean to the north, the municipality of Sánchez to the west and south, and the municipality of Samaná to the east.

The main economic activities of the municipality are the same as the province: tourism, agriculture and fishing.
