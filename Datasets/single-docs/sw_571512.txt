Piet Keizer

He made 365 league appearances for Ajax. He also played 34 games for the Netherlands national football team, scoring eleven goals. He retired in 1974 after an argument with Hans Kraay Sr., then-manager of Ajax.

Kiezer was born in Amsterdam, North Holland. He married Jenny Hoorman in 1967. They had a son.

Keizer died from lung cancer on 10 February 2017, at the age of 73.
