Christian Bale

Christian Charles Philip Bale (born January 30, 1974) is an English actor. He is from Haverfordwest in Pembrokeshire. Bale is best known for playing the part of Batman in Christopher Nolan's "The Dark Knight Trilogy". He began in a movie of Steven Spielberg Empire of the Sun at the age of 13. He also played in "American Psycho" and "The Machinist". He is married to Sandra Blažić since 2000 and they have one child together.
