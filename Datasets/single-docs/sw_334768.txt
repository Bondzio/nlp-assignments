Mary Elizabeth Winstead

Mary Elizabeth Winstead (born November 28, 1984) is an American motion picture and television actress. She played Jessica in the soap opera "Passions". She also played Lucy McClane in "Live Free or Die Hard". She also played the scream queen in many horror pictures, including "Black Christmas" and "Final Destination 3".

Winstead was born in Rocky Mount, North Carolina. She was raised in Utah.
