Ryan Newman

Ryan Joseph Newman (born December 8, 1977) is an American racecar driver who drives the No. 31 Caterpillar Chevrolet SS for Richard Childress Racing. Newman used to drive the #39 U.S. Army/Haas Automation Chevrolet Impala for Stewart Haas Racing, and the #12 Alltel Dodge for Team Penske. Newman won the 2008 Daytona 500.
