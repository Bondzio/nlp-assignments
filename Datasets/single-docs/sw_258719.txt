Nicktoons Nitro

Nicktoons Nitro is a video game which is a sequel to "Nicktoons Racing" and "Nicktoons Winners Cup Racing". It features characters from the TV shows: "SpongeBob SquarePants", "Danny Phantom", "", "The Fairly OddParents", "Invader Zim", "", "Back at the Barnyard", "The Mighty B!", "", "Kappa Mikey", "Dora the Explorer", "Go, Diego, Go!", "Wow! Wow! Wubbzy!", and "Ni Hao, Kai-Lan". "Maggie and the Ferocious Beast , and My Life as a Teenage Robot," The game features "Mario Kart" like gameplay. The game was released in 2009 as an arcade game by developer Raw Thrills.
