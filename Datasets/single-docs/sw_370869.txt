Chris Pratt

Christopher "Chris" Michael Pratt (born June 21, 1979) is an American movie, television, stage, voice actor, and producer. He is known for his television roles as Harold Brighton "Bright" Abbott in the series "Everwood," Ché in "The O.C." (2003), and Andy Dwyer and "Parks and Recreation" (2009), a role which garnered him a Critics' Choice nomination for Best Supporting Actor in a Comedy Series in 2013. He also was the voice of Emmet in the 2014 animated film "The Lego Movie".

Pratt was born in Virginia, Minnesota on June 21, 1979. He was raised in Lake Stevens, Washington. He has been married to Anna Faris since 2009. Their son, Jack Pratt, was born August 25, 2012. In 2017, Pratt and Faris legally separated.
