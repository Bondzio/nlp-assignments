Frank Oz

Frank Oz (born Richard Frank Oznowicz on May 25, 1944) is an American puppeteer and filmmaker. He is known for his work on "The Muppets", "Star Wars", and "Sesame Street". He also directed some popular films, such as "Little Shop of Horrors" and "Dirty Rotten Scoundrels". His most popular puppets are Yoda and Cookie Monster.
