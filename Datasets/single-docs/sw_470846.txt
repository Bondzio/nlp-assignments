Isidoor Teirlinck

Isidoor Teirlinck (2 January 1851, He was the father of the writer Herman Teirlinck.

He was famous for his books on legends and folklore, especially folklore of plants. His books "Flora Diabolica" and "Flora Magic"a are still classic books in their field. He worked for years as a school teacher. He taught mathematics and physics at normal schools in Brussels.


With his brother in law, Rijmons Stijns, he wrote a famous novel Arm Vlaanderen. (1884)
