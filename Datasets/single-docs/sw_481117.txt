Bob Simon

Bob Simon (May 29, 1941 â€“ February 11, 2015) was an American television journalist. He was an award winning CBS News television correspondent. He was known for his appearances in "60 Minutes".

On February 11, 2015, Simon was killed in a car crash. He was a passenger in a livery cab that collided with another car and then crashed into a middle barrier of the West Side Highway in Manhattan. Simon went into cardiac arrest and was taken to St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital, where he died.
