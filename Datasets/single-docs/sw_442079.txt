Barbro Kollberg

Barbro Anna Elisabet Hildestrand (née Kollberg; 27 December 1917 – 6 March 2014) was a Swedish actress. She is best known for her roles in "Hem från Babylon" (1941), "It Rains on Our Love" (1946), "A Life for the Taking" (1995) and "As It Is in Heaven" (2004). She was born in Eskilstuna, Södermanland County.

Kollberg died on 6 March 2014 in Stockholm. She was 96 years old.
