Artemisia (movie)

Artemisia is a 1997 French and Italian erotic and biographical movie. It is about Artemisia Gentileschi, a 17th-century Italian painter. She is as talented as her father, who is also a painter. At that time in Italy, women were not allowed to paint humans in the nude. Artemisia, though, is extremely determined.

The movie got very positive reviews. The MPAA originally gave "Artemisia" an NC-17 rating. On appeal, the rating was changed to R.
