Reykjavík

Reykjavík is the capital city of the island country of Iceland. It is also the largest city in that country. The population of Reykjavík is over 117,000 people. There is a geothermal bath, both natural and unnatural in appearance. It is situated in the capital and people relax in this hot spring during the cooler months. Björk, an Icelandic singer, is from Reykjavik.
