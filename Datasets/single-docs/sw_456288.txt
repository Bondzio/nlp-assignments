Vincenzo Irolli

Vincenzo Irolli was an Italian painter. He was born on September 30, 1860 in Naples, He died on November 27, 1949 in Naples.

Trained at the Academy of Fine Arts in Naples, followed by the current painting of Domenico Morelli, characterized by a vibrant use of color. Pursued its production resented the style of Antonio Mancini portraying subjects in the Neapolitan folklore.

His talent easy, flashy, brought him great success with the public.
The artist has produced over 5,000 works.

He participated in the "Biennale of Art" in Venice in 1922 and in 1924.
