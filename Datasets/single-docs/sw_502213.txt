Russi Taylor

Russell "Russi" Taylor (born May 4, 1944) is an American voice actress. She is best known for being the voice of Disney's Minnie Mouse since 1986. She also voices numerous other Disney characters. Taylor is the widow of the late voice actor Wayne Allwine. He was the third voice of Mickey Mouse from 1991 until his death in 2009. In 2008 she received a Disney Legends award.
