Costa-Gavras

Costa-Gavras (short for Konstantinos Gavras - Κωνσταντίνος Γαβράς; born 12 February 1933) is a Greek-French director, screenwriter, and producer. He is known for movies "Missing" (1982), "Hannah K." (1983), "Betrayed" (1988), "Music Box" (1989), "Mad City" (1997) and "Amen." (2002).
