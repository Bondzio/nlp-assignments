Ford Focus (North America)

The Ford Focus is a compact vehicle made by the Ford Motor Company. The North American version was introduced in 1999 for the 2000 model year. It was then facelifted in April 2004 for the 2005 model year, a new version came in late 2007 for the 2008 model year and was then redesigned in early 2011 for the 2012 model year, the current generation is the same that's sold worldwide.
