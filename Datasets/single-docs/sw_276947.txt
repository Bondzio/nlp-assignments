Nintendo DSi XL

The Nintendo DSi XL, (Nintendo DSi LL in Japan) is a game console made and manufactured by Nintendo. It is a bigger version of the Nintendo DSi. It has bigger screens than the DS, DS Lite or regular DSi. It was first sold in Japan on 21 November 2009, Europe on 5 March 2010, Australia on 15 March 2010, and North America on 28 March 2010. The DSi XL comes with several titles with the console including Flipnote Studio, , and Photo Clock.
