Denis Norden

Denis Mostyn Norden, CBE (born 6 February 1922) is a retired English comedy writer and television presenter. He was known for presenting the radio programme "My Word!".

He also presented "My Music"."Looks Familiar" and blooper shows "It'll Be Alright on the Night" and "Laughter File". He retired in 2006.

Norden was born in Mare Street, Hackney, London on 6 February 1922. He studied at City of London School. Norden married Avril Rosen in 1943. They have two children.
