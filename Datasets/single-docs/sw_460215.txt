Get Smart (movie)

Get Smart is a 2008 American spy-fi action movie directed by Peter Segal and produced by Charles Roven and Andrew Lazar. It stars Steve Carell, Anne Hathaway, Dwayne Johnson, Alan Arkin, Bill Murray, Terence Stamp, and James Caan. It was composed by Trevor Rabin. It was released on June 20, 2008.
