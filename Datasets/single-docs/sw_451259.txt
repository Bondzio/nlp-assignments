Toby Kebbell

Tobias Alistair P. "Toby" Kebbell (born 9 July 1982) is an English actor. He is known for his roles in the movies "Dead Man's Shoes", "", "The Sorcerer's Apprentice", "War Horse", and "Wrath of the Titans". 

Kebbell won the Best Supporting Actor Award at the British Independent Film Awards for "Control".
