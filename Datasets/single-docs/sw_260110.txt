Tomáš Kaberle

Tomáš Kaberle (born March 2, 1978 in Rakovník, Czechoslovakia) is a professional Czech ice hockey defenceman that currently plays for the Montreal Canadiens of the National Hockey League (NHL). He also played for the Toronto Maple Leafs from 1998 to 2011, the Boston Bruins in 2011 and the Carolina Hurricanes in 2011. He was drafted by the Toronto Maple Leafs with the 204th overall pick in the 2003 NHL Draft. He played in the Czech Extraliga for the HC Kladno where he played four seasons. He won the Stanley Cup with the Bruins on June 15, 2011 when the Bruins defeated the Vancouver Canucks in game 7 by a score of 4-0.
