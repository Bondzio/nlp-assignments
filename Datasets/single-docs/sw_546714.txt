Samia al-Amoudi

Samia al-Amoudi (Arabic language: سامية العمودي ) is a Saudi Arabian doctor. In 2007 she received the International Women of Courage Award.

In 2006 al-Amoudi learned she had breast cancer. She fought the cancer and broke the silence about this women's health problem.

Al-Amoudi is an Associate Professor at King Abdulaziz University in Jeddah and a consultant obstetrician gynecologist. She is also the head of the Women's Health Empowerment Scientific chair for women's health rights. 

In 2013 King Abdullah named al-Amouid to the board of directors of the Union for International Cancer Control in Geneva, Switzerland.


Al-Amoudi wrote several books.
