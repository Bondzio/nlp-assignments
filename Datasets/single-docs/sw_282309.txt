Gucci Mane

Radric Davis (born February 2, 1980 in Birmingham, Alabama), known by his stage name, Gucci Mane, is an American rapper.

Gucci Mane is currently signed to 1017 Brick Squad Records.

Gucci Mane has a large tattoo of an ice cream cone tattooed onto his face which he got after he was released from the hospital.
