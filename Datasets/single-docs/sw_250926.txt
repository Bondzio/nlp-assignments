Kate Cary

Kate Cary (born 4 November 1967 in England) is one of the authors who write the "Warriors" book series using the name Erin Hunter. She works with Cherith Baldry, Victoria Holmes, and Tui Sutherland while writing these books. As well, she has written books on her own, such as "Bloodline" and "Reckoning".

Kate Cary owns three cats, which she revealed on the Warriors website. She also has a son, and has lived in Scotland, but now lives in England.
