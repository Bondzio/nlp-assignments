José Greci

José Greci (10 January 1941 – 1 June 2017) was an Italian movie, television and stage actress. She made her movie debut in 1959, playing the Virgin Mary in William Wyler's "Ben-Hur".

Greci studied at Silvio D'Amico National Academy of Dramatic Art.

Greci died in Rome, Italy on 1 June 2017, aged 76.
