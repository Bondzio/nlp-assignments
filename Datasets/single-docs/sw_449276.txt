Power Rangers In Space

Power Rangers In Space is an American television series and the sixth season of the "Power Rangers" franchise. It is based on the Super Sentai series "Denji Sentai Megaranger". It is the sequel to Power Rangers Turbo.

The turbo rangers head for space and meet andros. He brings them the astro morphers and they become the power rangers in space. They battle the evil Astronema and her monsters.

A sequel called Power Rangers Lost Galaxy started in 1999.
