Harry Leslie Smith

Harry Leslie Smith (born 25 February 1923) is a British writer and political commentator. He served in the RAF during the Second World War, later moving to Canada. 

After retiring, Smith began writing his memoirs and about the social history of Great Britain, during his youth and in the 21st century. He has written five books about Britain during the Great Depression, the Second World War, and postwar austerity. 

Smith writes for "The Guardian" newspaper, "The New Statesman", "The Daily Mirror", "International Business Times" and the "Morning Star", and has made a number of public appearances in the UK (including the 2014 Labour Party conference and during the 2015 General Election and the 2016 EU Referendum) and Canada (in his 2015 Stand up For Progress National Tour).
