Shawn Ashmore

Shawn Robert Ashmore (born October 7, 1979) is a Canadian movie and television actor. He is best known for his roles as Jake Berenson in the television series "Animorphs", Agent Mike Weston in the television drama series "The Following", and Bobby Drake / Iceman in the "X-Men" movie series. He is the identical twin brother of actor Aaron Ashmore.
