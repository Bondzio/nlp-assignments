HighBeam Research

HighBeam Research is a business which has a search engine and fulltext online archive. It is owned by Cengage Learning (through its Gale subsidiary) for newspapers, magazines, academic journals, newswires, trade magazines and encyclopedias in English. It is headquartered in Chicago, Illinois, United States.

HighBeam has access to over 6,556 publications. However, only fulltext access is provided. No complete issues of papers are included.
