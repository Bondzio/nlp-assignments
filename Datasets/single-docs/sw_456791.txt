Eddie Calvo

Eddie Baza Calvo (born August 29, 1961) is an American Guamanian politician. He is the Governor of the United States territory of Guam since 2011. He is a member of the Republican Party. His father, Paul McDonald Calvo, also served as governor of Guam from 1979 through 1983.

Calvo was a five-term Senator within the Legislature of Guam. He became the Governor of Guam, having defeated Democrat Carl Gutierrez in the 2010 gubernatorial election.
