Kim Chaek

Kim Chaek(Korea:김책;金策, 14 August 1903 - January 31 1951) was one North Korean resistance activists and one Military leader, politicians, poet. realname was Kim Hong-gye(김홍계 金洪啓). first Industry Minister of DPRK, September 2 1948 to January 31 1951. he was Military leader of North Korea, first time to Korean Wars.

child years, his family was romove to Manchu, after 1926, he was Jointed to communist organization. In 1925, he was study to Dongheung Middle school, but In 1927, dropped out of Middle School. 1930's he was activity to communism military organization, in Soviet Union. In September 2, 1948 he was appointed to Industry Minister of DPRK, until his death. In January 31, 1951 He was died in battle to Korean war, in Pyongyang. his death cause of myocardial infarction and gas poisonings.
