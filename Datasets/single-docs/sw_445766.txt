Thomas Haden Church

Thomas Haden Church (born Thomas Richard McMillen; June 17, 1960) is an American actor, movie director and screenwriter. He first became known when co-starring in the 1990s sitcom "Wings". He is best known for his movie appearances including in his Academy Award nominated role in "Sideways" (2004) and his role as the Sandman in "Spider-Man 3" (2007). He directed his first, "Rolling Kansas", in 2003.

Church was born in Woodland, California. He has a daughter, Cody (born 2004), with actress Mia Zottoli.
