Ed Gillespie

Edward Walter "Ed" Gillespie (born August 1, 1961) is an American Republican political strategist. He served as the 61st Chairman of the Republican National Committee and Counselor to the President in the George W. Bush administration. 

Gillespie, along with Democrat Jack Quinn, founded Quinn Gillespie & Associates, a bipartisan lobbying firm. In January 2014, Gillespie announced he was running for Virginia's U.S. Senate seat currently held by Mark Warner in the 2014 election, and on June 7, 2014, he received the Republican nomination.. In October 2015, Gillespie announced his plans to run for Governor of Virginia in 2017
