I Will Always Love You

"I Will Always Love You" is a song by American singer-songwriter Dolly Parton. It is a country music song. It was released on June 6, 1974. It was the second single from Parton's thirteenth solo album, "Jolene" (1974). Whitney Houston recorded a version of the song for the movie "The Bodyguard" in 1992. That version is one of the best selling singles of all time.
