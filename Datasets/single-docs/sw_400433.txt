Julio Requena Hernandez

Julio Requena Hernandez (born September 9, 1969 in Leon) is a track and field athlete from Spain. He has a disability: He is blind and a T11/B1 type athlete. He raced at the 1992 Summer Paralympics. He finished first in the 100 meter B1 race. He raced at the 1996 Summer Paralympics. He finished first in the 4 X 100 meter T11-T13 race, the 100 meter T11 race and the 200 meter T11 race. He raced at the 2000 Summer Paralympics. He finished third in the 4 X 100 meter T11-T13 race and the 200 meter T11 race.
