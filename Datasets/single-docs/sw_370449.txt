Old Christian Cemetery Abbottabad

The Old Christian Cemetery, Abbottabad (also known as the OCC, or the "Gora Qabristan", White-people's cemetery), is a cemetery situated in Abbottabad, Pakistan. It dates from British Indian colonial times. 

The cemetery was established in 1853 when James Abbott founded Abbottabad. It was later attached to St Luke's Church, Abbottabad when it was completed in 1864.
