Ratchet &amp; Clank (movie)

Ratchet & Clank is a 3D computer-animated science fiction action comedy movie. It was directed by Jericca Cleland and Kevin Munroe. The movie is based on the platforming video game series of the same name. The movie was originally scheduled to be released in 2015. The movie was released on April 29, 2016.

The movie was produced at Rainmaker Entertainment's Vancouver studio in Canada. It was executive produced by Michael Hefferon, the President of Rainmaker Entertainment.

At the 2014 Electronic Entertainment Expo on June 9, 2014, the first trailer for the movie was revealed during Sony's press conference.
