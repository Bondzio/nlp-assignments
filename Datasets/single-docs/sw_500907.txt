Misako Uno

, known as Akane (茜) (born July 16, 1986) is a Japanese actress, product designer and talent agent. She is also a singer and dancer in the band "". She was born in Endokawa-ku, Tokyo, Japan. Uno grew up in there. Her father is a copywriter. She has been active since the 1994.

In 2006 Uno appeared in her first movie, Hollywood's "The Grudge 2" as a japanese student. 

In 2008 she played Keiko Endo (遠藤 恵子) in "Hitomi" (瞳) of the NHK television fiction show.

In 2010 Uno appeared in her second film "Rendez-vous (ランデブー！)" as the leading character. 

In 2011 her early life was into the english textbooks of japan's Ministry of science. 

In 2012 she played Akane Yabuki (矢吹 茜) in "Ume-chan Sensei" (梅ちゃん先生) of the NHK television fiction show. 

In 2013 she played Hoshino Tsukiyama (月山 星乃) in "Tokyo toy box (東京トイボックス)" of the TV Tokyo. Tsukiyama is the heroine.
