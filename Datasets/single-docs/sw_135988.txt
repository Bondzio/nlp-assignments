Stormbreaker

Stormbreaker is a book by British writer Anthony Horowitz. It is the first book in the "Alex Rider" series. It was released on 4 September 2000 in the United Kingdom and on 21 May 2001 in the United States. A movie based on the book was released on 21 July 2006. It starred Alex Pettyfer as the book's main character, Alex Rider.
