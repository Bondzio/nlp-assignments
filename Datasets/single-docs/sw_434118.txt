Erotic thriller

An erotic thriller is a form of the thriller genre that involves sexuality. Many people liked this from around 1984-85 until the early 1990s. After that, not as many people liked it. It is, however, still popular.

An erotic thriller can be a book, a movie, or a television program. Examples of erotic thriller movies are "Color of Night", "Single White Female" (1992), "Body of Evidence" and "Crash" (1996).
