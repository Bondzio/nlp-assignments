Matisyahu

Matthew Paul Miller (born June 30, 1979), better known by his Hebrew name and stage name Matisyahu, is a Jewish, Hasidic, rapper. His more famous songs are ""King Without a Crown"", ""Jerusalem"", and ""One Day"". He has collaborated with Akon, The Crystal Method, and Infected Mushroom. He has 3 studio albums, as well as 2 live albums, 2 remix albums, and 2 DVDs with live concerts.
