Henryk Vogelfanger

Henryk Vogelfanger (1904 - 1990), was a Polish actor. He worked and lived in Lviv until World War Two when he joined Polish underground army, Armia Krajowa. Together with the legendary actor, radio personality Kazimierz Wajda, he was a star of the radio and theater comedy show "Szczepko and Tonko". This was widely popular in Poland and other countries with over six million regular listeners, 1932-1939. The show ended when World War Two started. The team made three family movies, adventures of Szczepko & Tomko.
