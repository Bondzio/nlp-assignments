Ice Climbers

The Ice Climbers are a series of video games produced and developed by Nintendo. The series revolves around Popo and Nana, also known as the "Ice Climbers". They have been produced from 1985 and 2007. The plot of the game was to recover stolen vegetables from a giant condor on 32 ice-covered mountains. Popo and Nana have also appeared in the Super Smash Bros. series.
