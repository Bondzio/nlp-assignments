Fast &amp; Furious 7

Fast & Furious 7 is a 2015 American action-thriller movie. It is the seventh film in "The Fast and the Furious" media franchise series. Jordana Brewster plays Mia. Michelle Rodriguez plays Letty Ortiz. Vin Diesel plays Dominic Toretto. Paul Walker plays Brian O'Conner. Jason Statham plays Deckard Shaw.

The movie was released on April 3, 2015.
