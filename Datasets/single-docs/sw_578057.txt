Etienne Vermeersch

Etienne Vermeersch (born May 2, 1934 in Bruges) is a Belgian philosopher, activist and former academic. 

He studied classics and philosophy at Ghent University.

Being a Jesuit, he turned atheist at the age of 25.

Vermeersch was vice rector at Ghent University, and is considered an important critic in Belgian society.

He was co-founder of the Belgian skeptical organization of SKEPP.
