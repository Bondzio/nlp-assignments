Laurie Brokenshire

Commodore Laurence Phillip Brokenshire CBE, RN (20 October 1952 â€“ 4 August 2017) was a British navy officer and magician. He was born in Plymouth, Devon. He studied at the University of Exeter. His career developed as a submariner and later as a senior Royal Navy officer. He retired as officer in 2003. Laurie was accepted into the Inner Magic Circle, and became an occasional professional / semi-professional magic performer.

Brockenshire died of brain cancer on 4 August 2017 in Stubbington, Hampshire at the age of 64.
