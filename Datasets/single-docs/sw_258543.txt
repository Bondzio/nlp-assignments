Noel Gallagher

Noel Gallagher (born 29 May 1967) is an English musician and singer-songwriter. 

Gallagher was the former lead guitarist and main songwriter for the band Oasis. He left the band in 2009. 

In 2011 he released his first solo album "Noel Gallagher's High Flying Birds". The band's second album "Chasing Yesterday" was released 2 March 2015.
