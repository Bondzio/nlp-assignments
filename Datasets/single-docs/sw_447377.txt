Division of Hasluck

The Division of Hasluck is an electoral division of the Australian House of Representatives, in Western Australia.

It was set up in 2000, for the 2001 federal election. It is named after Sir Paul Hasluck, the Governor-General of Australia from 1969 to 1974, and his wife, Alexandra Hasluck, an author.
