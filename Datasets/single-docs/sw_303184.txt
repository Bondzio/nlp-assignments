I Wanna Go

"I Wanna Go" is a 2011 single by Britney Spears. The song is third from her seventh studio album "Femme Fatale". The song was released on June 13, 2011. The song was an instant success. It reached the top of the Billboard Hot 100 a day after debuting. This resulted in a flash mob in Times Square. She also shut down Times Square for an unexpected performance. The song has currently been downloaded 1 million times on iTunes.
