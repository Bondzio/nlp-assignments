Ugrić

Ugrić is a right tributary of the Ugar river in Central Bosnia. It is about in length, and its source is Vlašić's Mountain slopes. It is an essential body of water for surrounding wildlife.

Ugrić springs from the fountain of the same named Osmanovac stream; altitude 940 m, in the region of the elevation Krčevine. Above Krčevina regional there is communications Skender Vakuf – Turbe – Travnik, which marks a watershed between the basins of Ugar and Vrbanja rivers. It runs to the southwest, between Ćelehovsko hill (909 m) and by Barišića strane (Glavica, 897 m).
