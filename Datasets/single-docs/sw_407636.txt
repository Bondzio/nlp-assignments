Abbie Cornish

Abbie Cornish (born August 7, 1982) is an Australian actress who has performed in some notable movies. Cornish was born in New South Wales, and grew up on a farm.

Her movies include "Somersault", "Candy" and "Bright Star". She played Emma Matisse in "One Perfect Day". She plays Ellen Murphy in the 2014 remake of RoboCop.
