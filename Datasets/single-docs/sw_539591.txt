Ray Fisher (actor)

Ray Fisher (born 1988) is an American movie, television and stage actor. He is best known for his roles in the comedy "The Good, the Bad and the Confused". Ray played Muhammad Ali in Will Power's Fetch Clay Make Man in 2013. He is mainly known for playing Victor Stone / Cyborg in the DC Extended Universe such as in "" and in the upcoming "Justice League". Sometime in 2020, Fisher will be in a Cyborg movie.
