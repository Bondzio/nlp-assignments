Jane Powell

Jane Powell (born Suzanne Lorraine Bruce on April 1, 1929) is an American actress. She played the title character in "A Date with Judy" in 1948. She also played Tess Morgan in "Three Daring Daughters" (also 1948).

Powell was born in Portland, Oregon. She was married to actor Dickie Moore from 1988 until his death in 2015.
