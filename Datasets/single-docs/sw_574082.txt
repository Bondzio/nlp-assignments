Frits Soetekouw

Frits Soetekouw (born 16 June 1938) is a former Dutch footballer. He was born in Amsterdam, North Holland.

Soetekouw played as a defender at club level between 1961 and 1971. He played for De Volewijckers, Heracles, Ajax, Eindhoven and DWS. He was captained of Ajax for a short time..
