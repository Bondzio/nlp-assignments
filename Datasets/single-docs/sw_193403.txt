Beat It

"Beat It" is a popular song written and performed by singer Michael Jackson. It appears on his sixth album "Thriller". It was released as the album's third single. There is also a video that goes with the song. The song features a guitar solo by Eddie Van Halen. He chose to do the solo without getting paid. It got the Grammy Awards for Record of the Year and Best Male Rock Vocal Performance in 1984.
