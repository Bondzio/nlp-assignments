Ivanka Trump

Ivanka Trump (born October 30, 1981) is an American businesswoman, writer, heiress, and former model. Trump is serving as Assistant to the President since March 29, 2017. She is the daughter of U.S President Donald Trump and Czech model Ivana Trump. She works at The Trump Organization with the company's real estate and hotel management. She also has several other business deals. 

On October 25, 2009, Trump married businessman Jared Kushner.
