Carmen Salinas

Carmen Salinas Lozano (born October 5, 1933) is an Mexican movie and television actress. She is thought to be one of the first actress' in Mexico. She has appeared in over 100 movies.

She is known for her roles in "María Mercedes", "Hombre en llamas", "La rose de Guadalupe", "La familia P. Luche", "Mujeres Asesinas 2", and in "Parodiando". Salinas is known for her movie role in "Abrázame muy fuerte".

Salinas was born on October 5, 1933 in Torreón, Coahuila, Mexico.
