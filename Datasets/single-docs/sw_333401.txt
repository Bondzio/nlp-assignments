Lauren Holly

Lauren Holly (born October 28, 1963, Bristol, Pennsylvania) is an American television and movie actress. She played Maxine Stewart in the CBS television show "Picket Fences". She played Mary Swanson in the 1994 movie "Dumb and Dumber". She also did many other TV and movie performances. She was married to actor Jim Carrey between 1996 and 1997.
