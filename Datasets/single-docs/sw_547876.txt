Oberdiessbach

Oberdiessbach is a municipality in the administrative district of Bern-Mittelland in the canton of Berne in Switzerland.

On 1 January 2010 the former municipalities of Aeschlen bei Oberdiessbach merged into the municipality of Oberdiessbach and On 1 January 2014 the former municipalities of Bleiken bei Oberdiessbach merged into the municipality of Oberdiessbach.
