Emilee Wallace

Emilee Wallace (born September 19, 1989) is an American actress.

Wallace began her acting career in 2005. Wallace was born in Oklahoma City and raised in Edmond, Oklahoma. Her brother, BJ (born 1991), is also an actor. She went to California State University (CSU) and now lives in Los Angeles, California.
