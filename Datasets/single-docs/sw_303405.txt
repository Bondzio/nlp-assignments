Jared Leto

Jared Joseph Leto (born December 26, 1971) is an American actor and musician. he is known for acting in movies such as "Fight Club"; "Girl, Interrupted"; "Panic Room"; "American Psycho"; "Alexander"; "Requiem for a Dream"; "Lord of War"," Prefontaine", "Mr. Nobody" and "Suicide Squad".

He started a rock band called Thirty Seconds to Mars in 1998 with his brother Shannon Leto. Leto is the singer. The band has released four albums.
