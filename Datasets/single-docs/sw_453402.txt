Steven Bochco

Steven Ronald Bochco (born December 16, 1943) is a American television producer and writer. He created a number of popular television hits including "Hill Street Blues", "L.A. Law", "Doogie Howser, M.D.", and "NYPD Blue", as well as some notable flops such as "Cop Rock". He won an Emmy Award for his writing in 1981 and in 1987.
