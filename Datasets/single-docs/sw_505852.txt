Alan M. Lovelace

Alan Mathieson Lovelace (born September 4, 1929) is an American civil servant. He was the Deputy Administrator of NASA from July 2, 1976 to July 10, 1981.

Lovelace was born in St. Petersburg, Florida. He graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Chemistry from the University of Florida in Gainesville in 1951. He received a Master of Science in Organic Chemistry in 1952. He became a Doctor of Philosophy in 1954, also in Organic Chemistry.

In June 1981, Lovelace was presented with the Presidential Citizens Medal by President Ronald Reagan.
