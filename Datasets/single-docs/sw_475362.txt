Power Rangers Wild Force

Power Rangers Wild Force is the tenth season of the Power Rangers American television series. It aired from February to November 2002. The first half was aired on Fox Kids. After the Disney purchase Of Fox Family, the second half aired on ABC Kids. This was the last Power Rangers to be filmed in the US. Disney moved filming to New Zealand. It is based off the Super Sentai series, Hyakujuu Sentai Gaoranger. Ironically, Gaoranger celebrated 25 years of Sentai while Wild Force celebrated 10 years of Power Rangers. The show is best known for one of the best Power Rangers specials to date, "Forever Red".
