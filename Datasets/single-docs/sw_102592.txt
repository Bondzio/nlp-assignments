Honda S2000

The Honda S2000 is a roadster created by the car company Honda. It was first on sale in April 1999. It is the successor (new model) of the S800. Production of the S2000 ended in 2009. 

AP1
The S2000 was introduced in 1999 as a 2000 model.

AP2
In 2004, the new S2000 was introduced.
