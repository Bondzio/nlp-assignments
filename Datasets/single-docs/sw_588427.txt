Antonio Medellín

Antonio Medellín (15 April 1942 – 18 June 2017) was a Mexican actor. He was known for his roles in the telenovelas "Muchachitas" and "Rubí". In movies, he was known for his role in the 1982 drama Mexican movie "Aquel famoso Remington". He was born in Mexico City, Mexico. His career lasted from 1962 until his death in 2017.

Medellín died on 18 June 2017 at a hospital in Mexico City at the age of 75.
