Thiago Alcántara

Thiago Alcántara do Nascimento (born 11 April 1991 in Bari, Italy) is an Italian-born Spanish footballer, that currently plays for FC Barcelona. He also made the most goals in the Audi Cup 2011, making all two winning goals against Bayern Munich, and a goal against Internacional.

His dad is former Brazilian player Mazinho, his mom was a former volleyball player, and his younger brother plays for FC Barcelona B.
