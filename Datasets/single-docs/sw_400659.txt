Rafael Moreno López

Rafael Moreno Lopez (born August 25, 1966 in Málaga) is a judo athlete from Spain. He has a disability: he is blind and is a B2 type athlete. He competed at the 1996 Summer Paralympics in judo. He finished 6 in the + 95 Kg group. He competed at the 2000 Summer Paralympics in judo. He finished second in the Open group. He competed at the 2004 Summer Paralympics in judo. He competed at the 2008 Summer Paralympics in judo.
