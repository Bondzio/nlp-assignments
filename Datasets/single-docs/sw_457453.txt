Tuva-Lisa Johansson

Tuva-Lisa is a series of children's/youth novels by Swedish writers Anders Jacobsson and Sören Olsson, about 12 years old Swedish girl "Tuva-Lisa Johansson", soon 13. Her parents work with theater, and she has written theater stageplays on her own, and wants to become an actress. Her friend is Jessica.

Among the stageplays written by her are "Ett vemodigt tillstånd av tråkighet", depicting an old man reflecting on his life as a vegitables-dealer.
