Bumrungrad Hospital

Bumrungrad hospital (BH). 
is an internationally accredited hospital in Bangkok, Thailand. It was started on September 17, 1980. The hospital is the largest private hospital in Southeast Asia. Bumrungrad got the first hospital awarded Thailand Hospital Accreditation. Bumrungrad has more than 1.2 million patients each year. There are more than 3,000 employees. The hospital has the special staff who take care of patients from other countries.

“We provide world class healthcare with care and compassion.”
