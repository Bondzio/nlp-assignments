Super Sentai

Super Sentai is a Japanese "tokusatsu" media franchise that has been on the air since 1975. The franchise has had subsequent versions over the years. The seasons are adapted for an American franchise called "Mighty Morphin Power Rangers". The current Sentai, Doubutsu Sentai Zyuohger, debuted in February 2016. The seasons air on the TV-Asahi Tenlevision network.
