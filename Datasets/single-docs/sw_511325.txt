Uhaa Nation

Sesugh Uhaa (born August 25, 1987) is an American professional wrestler. He is better known by his ring name Uhaa Nation. He is currently signed to the WWE and works in their developmental territory NXT under the ring name Apollo Crews.

Uhaa is best known for his work in Dragon Gate USA, as well as the Japanese promotion Dragon Gate. During his time in Dragon Gate, he won the Open the Twin Gate Championship with BxB Hulk. He was also named the Best Newcomer by Dragon Gate USA in 2011.

In October 2014, Uhaa entered a WWE tryout camp and he was offered a developmental contract. On April 6, 2015, he reported to the WWE Performance Center, the home of their NXT. In a press release on April 13, the company announced that Uhaa was a member of their new NXT recruits.

Uhaa made his first televised appearance on the May 6 episode of NXT and signed his NXT contract during a segment with William Regal. On August 5, it was announced that Uhaa would make his televised NXT debut on August 22 at the "" event. It was also announced that his new ring name would be Apollo Crews.
