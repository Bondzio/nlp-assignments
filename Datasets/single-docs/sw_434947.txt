Horrible Bosses 2

Horrible Bosses 2 is an American comedy movie. It is the sequel to 2011's "Horrible Bosses". It stars Jason Bateman, Jason Sudeikis, Charlie Day, Kevin Spacey, Jennifer Aniston, Chris Pine, Jamie Foxx, and Christoph Waltz. The movie was released on November 26, 2014. It was produced by New Line Cinema and distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures.
