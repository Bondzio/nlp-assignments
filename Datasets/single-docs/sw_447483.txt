Blanche Barton

Blanche Barton (born Sharon Densley; October 1, 1961 in San Diego, California) is an American religious leader. She is the Magistra Templi Rex of the Church of Satan. Satanists address her as "Magistra Barton". 

Barton became the High Priestess after Anton LaVey died on October 29, 1997. She held the position of High Priestess until April 30, 2002. Barton has written two books: "The Church of Satan: A History of the World's Most Notorious Religion" and "The Secret Life of a Satanist: The Authorized Biography of Anton LaVey".
