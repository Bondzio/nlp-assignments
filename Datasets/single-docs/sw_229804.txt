Drew McIntyre

Drew Galloway (born June 6, 1985 in Ayr, Scotland), is a Scottish professional wrestler, better know by his ring name Drew McIntyre. He is currently signed to WWE working in their developmental territory NXT, where he is the current NXT Champion. During his previous run in the WWE, he won the WWE Intercontinental Championship one time and the WWE Tag Team Championship one time with Cody Rhodes. McIntyre married former WWE diva Taryn Terrell (known by the ring name Tiffany) in May 2011 but they divorced on May 24, 2011.

On June 12, 2014, McIntyre was released from his WWE contract.

In April 2017, he returned to WWE in their developmental territory NXT. He is the current NXT Champion.
