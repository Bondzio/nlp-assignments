Shawn Horcoff

Shawn Horcoff (born September 17, 1978 in Trail, British Columbia, Canada) is a Canadian professional ice hockey centre. He currently plays for the Edmonton Oilers of the National Hockey League (NHL) and is the current captain of the team.
