Amancio Escapa Aparicio

Amancio Escapa Aparicio (30 March 1938 – 5 May 2017) was an Roman Catholic bishop. He was born in León, Spain. Escapa Aparici was ordained to the priesthood in 1962. He served as titular bishop of Cenae and as auxiliary bishop of the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of Santo Domingo from 1996 until 2016.

Aparicio died on 5 May 2017 in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic at the age of 79.
