Javier Bardem

Javier Ángel Encinas Bardem (born 1 March 1969) is a Spanish movie, television, stage, and voice actor who is known for his roles in "Skyfall", "No Country for Old Men", "Biutiful", "Vicky Cristina Barcelona", "Before Night Falls", "Jamón, jamón" and in "". He has won an Golden Globe Award, an BAFTA Award, and an Academy Award.

Bardem was born on 1 March 1969 in Las Palmas of the Canary Islands. He has been married to Penélope Cruz since 2010. They have two children.
