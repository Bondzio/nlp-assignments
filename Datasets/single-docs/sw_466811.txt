Efua Dorkenoo

Efua Dorkenoo, OBE (6 September 1949 â€“ October 2014) was a Ghanaian campaigner against female genital mutilation (FGM). She was Programme Director of '' and Senior FGM Advisor for Equality Now, an international human rights organisation. 

She was one of the founders in 1983 of the Foundation for Women's Health, Research and Development (FORWARD). It is a British charity that supports women who have experienced FGM. Dorkenoo is the author of "Cutting the Rose: Female Genital Mutilation" (1996). 

Dorkenoo died of cancer at the age of 65 in London.
