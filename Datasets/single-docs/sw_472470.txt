Arrondissement of Avallon

The arrondissement of Avallon is an arrondissement of France. It is part of the Yonne "département" in the Bourgogne-Franche-Comté region. Its capital is the city of Avallon.

When the Yonne department was created on 17 February 1800, the "arrondissement" of Avallon was part of that original department.

The "arrondissement" of Avallon is bordered to the north by the Aube (Champagne-Ardenne) department, to the east by the Côte-d'Or (Burgundy) department, to the south by the Nièvre (Burgundy) department and to the west by the "arrondissement" of Auxerre.
