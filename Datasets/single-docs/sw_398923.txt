Hipolito Gonzalez Calvo (born November 10, 1976 in Barcelona) is a goalball athlete from Spain. He has a disability: he is blind. He is a B2 type goalball player. He played goalball at the 1996 Summer Paralympics. His team was third. He played goalball at the 2000 Summer Paralympics. His team was third.
