Jon Snow

Jon Snow (born 28 September 1947) is a British journalist and television presenter. He was born in Ardingly, Sussex. He went to Ardingly College. In 2006 he started presenting "Channel 4 News". 

In March 2010 he married Precious Lunga.

His father was George D'Oyly Snow. His grandfather was Thomas D'Oyly Snow. His cousin is Peter Snow.
