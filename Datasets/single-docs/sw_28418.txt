Gillingham F.C.

Gillingham Football Club is an English football club. They are based in the town of Gillingham, Kent. They are currently in the Football League One. They play home games at Priestfield Stadium. Gillingham are managed by Justin Edinbourgh and the chairman is Paul Scally. The club captain, Michael Flynn, plays in central midfield. they finished their last season (2015-16) in 9th.
