HV71

HV71 is an ice hockey club that plays its home games in the Kinnarps Arena in the town of Jönköping in Sweden. The club was established on 24 May 1971 when the two local clubs of Husqvarna IF and Vätterstads IK merged their ice hockey sections.

HV71 won the Swedish national championship in 1995, 2004, 2008, 2010 and 2017. They also lost the finals of 2009.
