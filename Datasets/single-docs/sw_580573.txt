Todd Helton

Todd Lynn Helton (born August 20, 1973) is a former baseball player. He was a first baseman for the Colorado Rockies from 1997 to 2013. His uniform number, 17, was retired by the Rockies in 2014. Helton had a batting average of .316 during his career and he hit 369 home runs. Helton also played football, and he was a quarterback at the University of Tennessee.
