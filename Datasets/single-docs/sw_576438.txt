Golden God

Golden God is the first EP made by Angolan rapper, Pedro Fernando. It was released on December 19, 2016. It was produced and mastered by DJ Nonó, Pedro Fernando, Márcio Dallas. The album Golden God (EP) was released on February 19, 2017 for digital sale on iTunes, and other digital platforms. The extended play was given 5.0 out of 5 stars by Discogs.
