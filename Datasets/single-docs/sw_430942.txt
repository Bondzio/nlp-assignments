Florian Klein

Florian Klein (born 17 November 1986) is an Austrian football player. He plays for FC Red Bull Salzburg in the Austrian Bundesliga as a defender.

Klein started in September 1994 in the youth team for LASK Linz. He was promoted to the senior team in July 2004 . After 163 games in 14 years for LASK he signed with Austria Wien on 30 April 2009. In 2012 he went to FC Red Bull Salzburg.

In July 2014 Klein moved to VfB Stuttgart.

Florian Klein played for the Under-19 and Under-21 team of Austria. He also played 17 matches for the A-team.
