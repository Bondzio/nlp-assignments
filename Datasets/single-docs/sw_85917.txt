Claro, Ticino

Claro is a former municipality of the district Riviera in the canton of Ticino in Switzerland.

On 2 April 2017 the former municipalities of Claro, Camorino, Giubiasco, Gnosca, Gorduno, Gudo, Moleno, Monte Carasso, Pianezzo, Preonzo, Sant'Antonio and Sementina merged into the municipality of Bellinzona.
