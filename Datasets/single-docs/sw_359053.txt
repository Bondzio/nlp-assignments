Greg Kinnear

Gregory "Greg" Kinnear (born June 17, 1963) is an American movie, voice, and television actor. He first became famous in 1991. He has appeared in more than 20 movies, and was nominated for an Academy Award for his role in "As Good as It Gets". His career began in 1988.
He has appeared in other notable films including "Sabrina", "You've Got Mail", "Nurse Betty", "We Were Soldiers", "Little Miss Sunshine", "Invincible", and "Green Zone". Notable TV roles include "Friends" and his Emmy Award nominated roles for "Soup", "The Kennedys", and "Modern Family".

Kinnear was born in June 17, 1963 in Logansport, Indiana to Suzanne and Edward Kinnear. Kinnear married Helen Labdon since 1999.
