The Last Airbender (video game)

The Last Airbender is a video game for Wii and Nintendo DS. It is based on the film of the same name. It was released on 29 June 2010 in USA. It was released in Europe on 6 August 2010. It will be released in Australia on September 2010. Like the previous games, it was developed by THQ Studio Australia and published by THQ.
