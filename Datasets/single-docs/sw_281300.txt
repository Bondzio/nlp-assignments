Patric Hörnqvist

Patric Gösta Hörnqvist (born January 1, 1987) is a Swedish professional ice hockey right winger. He currently plays for the Pittsburgh Penguins of the National Hockey League (NHL). He has also played for the Nashville Predators of the NHL and Djurgårdens IF of the SEL.

Hörnqvist was selected 230th overall in the 2005 NHL Entry Draft by the Nashville Predators. On June 28, 2014, the Predators traded Hörnqvist along with Nick Spaling to the Pittsburgh Penguins in exchange for James Neal.
