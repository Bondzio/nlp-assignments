Rudolph Wanderone

Rudolph Wanderone (January 19, 1913 â€“ January 15, 1996) was an American professional pool player. He never won a world championship. His first nickname was "Brooklyn Fats" and then "New York Fats." Wanderone named himself "Minnesota Fats" after the character in the movie "The Hustler". Wanderone was the host of a national television show, "Celebrity Billiards," during the 1960s. He is a member of the Billiard Congress of America Hall of Fame.
