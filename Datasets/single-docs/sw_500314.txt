James Wan

James Wan (born 27 February 1977) is an Australian movie producer, screenwriter and movie director, He is best known for directing the horror movie "Saw" (2004) and creating Billy the puppet. He has also directed "Dead Silence" (2007), "Death Sentence" (2007), "Insidious" (2010), "The Conjuring" (2013), "" (2013), "Furious 7" (2015) and "The Conjuring 2" (2016). He and Leigh Whannell also served as executive producers for "Saw II", "Saw III", "Saw IV", "Saw V", "Saw VI", "Saw 3D: The Final Chapter", "" and "". He will direct the 2018 DC Extended Universe-Warner Bros. movie "Aquaman".

Wan was born in Kuching, Sarawak, Malaysia to parents of Chinese ancestry. He and his family moved to Perth, Western Australia when he was seven, and later Melbourne, Victoria.
