Loftus Versfeld Stadium

Loftus Versfeld Stadium - Tshwane/Pretoria
Loftus Versfeld Stadium is a sports stadium in Pretoria, South Africa. 

It is mostly used for Rugby games.

The stadium co-hosted the 2010 FIFA World Cup football matches. Is one of the 10 stadiums where World Cup matches were played.
