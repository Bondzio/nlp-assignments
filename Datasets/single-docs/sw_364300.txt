Asking Alexandria

Asking Alexandria are a British metalcore band from York, North Yorkshire. Asking Alexandria have five members in the band; the current band members are Ben Bruce, James Cassells, Danny Worsnop, Cameron Liddell and Sam Bettley.

They released their first album "Stand Up and Scream" on September 15, 2009. Their newest album "Reckless & Relentless" was released on April 5, 2011. They will release their third studio album in 2013.

In 2012, Asking Alexandria received an 11th Annual Independent Music Award for the best metal/hardcore album for "Reckless & Relentless".
