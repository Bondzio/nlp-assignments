Hubert Minnis

Hubert Alexander Minnis (born 17 April 1954) is a Bahamian physician and politician. He is the 4th and current Prime Minister of the Bahamas since May 2017. Minnis is the leader of the Free National Movement, the present majority party, and the Member of Parliament for the New Providence constituency of Killarney. First elected to the legislature in the 2007 election, he succeeded Hubert Ingraham as party leader following the party's defeat in the 2012 election.

Minnis became Prime Minister following the victory of his party in the general election held on 10 May 2017 defeating the incumbent Perry Christie.
