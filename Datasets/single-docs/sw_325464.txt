Machine Gun Kelly (rapper)

Richard Colson Baker (born April 22, 1990), better known by his stage names MGK and Machine Gun Kelly, is an American rapper. He is currently signed to Diddy's Bad Boy Records and Interscope Records. He was also named the "Hottest Breakthrough MC of 2011" by MTV on December 14, 2011. His song "Invincible" featuring singer Ester Dean is the official theme song of WrestleMania XXVIII.
