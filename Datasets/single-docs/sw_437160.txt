Tamina Snuka

Sarona Reiher (born January 10, 1978) better known by her ring name Tamina Snuka, is an American professional wrestler. She is curretly wrestling for the WWE. She is the daughter of WWE Hall of Famer Jimmy "Superfly" Snuka.

On the May 24, 2010 episode of "Raw", Reiher debuted along with Jimmy and Jey Uso. On the September 28, 2013 episode of "SmackDown", she began accompanying WWE Divas champion AJ Lee to the ring. She currently teams with her and acts as her bodyguard.
