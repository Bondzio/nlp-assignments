Alexei Kosygin

Alexei Nikolayevich Kosygin (, "Aleksej Nikolajevič Kosygin"; 21 February 1904 – 18 December 1980) was a Russian man who was Premier of the Soviet Union. He was a friend of the previous Prime Minister, Nikita Khrushchev. He and Leonid Brezhnev led the country together until he died.
