'N Sync

'N Sync, sometimes called *NSYNC was an American pop boy band formed in Orlando, Florida in 1995. The five members of the group were Lance Bass, JC Chasez, Joey Fatone, Chris Kirkpatrick, and Justin Timberlake. Since the band broke up, Timberlake and Chasez have released solo albums.



<nowiki>'N Sync's</nowiki> album "No Strings Attached" holds the record for the most copies of an album sold in its first week after being released. The album sold 1.1 million copies its first day and 2.4 million in the first week.
