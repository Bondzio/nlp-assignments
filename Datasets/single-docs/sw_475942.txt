Philippe Honoré

Philippe Honoré (; November 25, 1941 – January 7, 2015), known by the pen-name Honoré, was a French cartoonist. He was born in Vichy, France. Honoré worked with the newspaper company Charlie Hebdo during most of his career. He was a staff cartoonist there.

Honoré died on January 7, 2015, during the January 2015 shooting attack on the "Charlie Hebdo" newspaper office.
