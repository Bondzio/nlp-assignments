Mario Super Sluggers

Mario Super Sluggers, known in Japan as , is a Mario sports game for the Wii. It was a sequel to Mario Superstar Baseball. It released in Japan and North America, but was never released in Europe. It was later released in Australia. 

The game got average to good reviews. The game got a 69/100 from Metacritic, and a 7.4/10 from IGN.
