Liz Phair

Elizabeth Clark "Liz" Phair (born April 17, 1967) is an American singer-songwriter and a guitarist. 

Her career began in the early 1990s. She began by self-releasing audio cassettes using the nickname of Girly Sound. Her 1993 debut studio album "Exile in Guyville" was released with critical acclaim. She has sold nearly three million records worldwide.

Phair was born in New Haven, Connecticut. She was adopted and grew up in Chicago suburb Winnetka, Illinois.
