Aghjabadi Rayon

Aghjabadi is a raion in central Azerbaijan. The capital is Aghjabadi. Aghjabadi raion was started in 1930. In 1963 it was ended and connected to Agdam Raion. In 1965, it became separate again. There are 46 settlements and 1 town in the district. There were 122,649 people in 2010.
