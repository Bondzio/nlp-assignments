Mitchell Ryan

Mitchell Ryan (born January 11, 1934) or Mitch Ryan is an American actor. He is known for his roles as Edward Montgomery in "Dharma & Greg" and as Dr. Terence Wynn in "".

Ryan was born on January 11, 1934 in Cincinnati, Ohio. He was raised in Louisville, Kentucky. He was married to Lynda Morse from 1972 until they divorced. They had three children.
