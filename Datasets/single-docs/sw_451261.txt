George Givot

George Givot (February 18, 1903 - June 7, 1984) was an actor in movies, television and radio. His strength was speaking in several ethnic dialects. One famous role he played was the voice of Tony in "Lady and the Tramp".

Givot was born on February 18, 1903 in Omaha, Nebraska. He died on June 7, 1984 in Palm Springs, California at age 81.
