Gerrit Holdijk

Gerrit Holdijk (17 November 1944 â€“ 30 November 2015) was a Dutch jurist and politician. He was born in Uddel. He was a member of the Reformed Political Party (SGP). He was a member of the States-Provincial of Gelderland from 1987 to 2011. He was also a member of the Senate from 1986 to 1987 and again from 1991 to 2015. 

Holdijk died in Uddel from lung cancer, aged 71.
