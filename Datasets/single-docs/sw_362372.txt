George Hamilton (actor)

George Stevens Hamilton (born August 12, 1939) is an American movie, television, stage, and voice actor. He has appeared in some comedy movies such as in; "Love at First Bite", "Zorro", and "Gay Blade". He has won a Saturn Award (1979).

Hamilton was born in Memphis, Tennessee on August 12, 1939. Hamilton was married to Alana Stewart from 1972 until they divorced in 1973. Hamilton was partners with Kinberly Blackford from 1997 until they split up in 1999. With Stewart, he has a son and with Blackford, he has a child.
