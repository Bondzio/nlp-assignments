Traci Lords

Traci Elizabeth Lords (born Nora Louise Kuzma; May 7, 1968) is an American writer, director, musician and actress. Most of her movies are B-movies. She played "Carl's Date" in the movie "Serial Mom". She also played Racquel in "Blade".

Lords first became known for being in pornographic movies before she was an adult.

Lords was born in Steubenville, Ohio.
