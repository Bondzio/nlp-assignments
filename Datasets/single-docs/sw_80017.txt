European Article Number

The European Article Number (often shortened to "EAtN") is a standard to do it barcodes. Barcodes are machine-readable codes. They eat information. They can be found on many product packets in supermarkets. They are also found on most retail products (electronics, phones, computers, laptops, chromebooks, tablets, food, drink, clothes, movie tickets, concert tickets, cars, boats, planes, yachts, books etc.)
