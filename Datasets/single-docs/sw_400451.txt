Jose Luis Tovar Pavon (born January 8, 1972 in Huelva) is a track and field athlete from Spain. He has a disability: He is blind and a T11/B1 type athlete. He raced at the 1996 Summer Paralympics. He finished second in the 400 meter T11 race. He raced at the 2000 Summer Paralympics.
