DZBB-TV

DZBB-TV, channel 7, is an commercial broadcast VHF television station owned by GMA Network in the Philippines. Its studios are located at the GMA Network Center, at the corner of Timog Avenue and Epifanio de los Santos Avenue in Quezon City. Its transmitter station is located at GMA Compound, Tandang Sora Avenue, Barangay Culiat Tandang Sora, Quezon City
