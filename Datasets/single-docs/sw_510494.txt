Saint Petersburg Stadium

The Saint Petersburg Stadium Russian: Стадион Санкт-Петербург) is an association football stadium in Saint Petersburg, Russia that will be completed in 2016. The stadium will have a capacity of 66,881 people, and it costs $1.1 billion to $1.4 billion.

It will eventually be the home of FC Zenit St. Petersburg, and it will be used in the 2017 FIFA Confederations Cup, 2018 FIFA World Cup and the UEFA Euro 2020.
