Paris Saint-Germain F.C.

Paris Saint-Germain F.C., also abbreviated as PSG, is a football club in France. It is based in Paris, the capital city and most populated city of France. They have been champions of Ligue 1 6 times (1985/1986, 1993/1994) including each of the last four seasons (2012/13, 2013/14, 2014/15 and 2015/16). They have been champions of Ligue 2 once (1970/1971). They play their home games at the Parc des Princes.
