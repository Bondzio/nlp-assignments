Diana Rigg

Diana Elizabeth Rigg DBE (born 20 July 1938) is an English actress. Rigg played Emma Peel in the television thriller "The Avengers" (1965-68). She played Helena in a "Midsummer Night's Dream" and Arlene Marshall in "Evil under the Sun". She has also performed on stage.

Rigg was born in Doncaster, West Riding of Yorkshire, England. Rigg was made a Commander of the Order of the British Empire (CBE) in 1988. She was made a Dame Commander of the Order of the British Empire (DBE) in 1994.
