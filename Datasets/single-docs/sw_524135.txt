Kylo Ren

Kylo Ren (born Ben Solo) is a fictional character in the "Star Wars" franchise. He was introduced in the 2015 movie "". He was portrayed by Adam Driver. 

Kylo Ren is the pseudonym of Ben Solo, the son of original "Star Wars" trilogy characters Han Solo who he later killed and Leia Organa. Though trained by his uncle Luke Skywalker as a Jedi, he has been seduced to the dark side of the Force, and aspires to be as powerful as his grandfather, Darth Vader who he praised and thought was the king of evil not the one who killed the emperor.
