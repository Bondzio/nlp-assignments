Cerezo Osaka

The club was founded in 1957.



Yanmar Diesel S.C. had reserve team ; Yanmar Club from 1976 to 1979.

The team has some notable achievements.

In 2010, Adriano Ferreira Martins made four goals in one game against Jubilo Iwata.

In 2011, Ryuji Bando scored three goals in one game against Sanfrecce Hiroshima; and he also scored three time in a game against Shimizu S-Pulse.
