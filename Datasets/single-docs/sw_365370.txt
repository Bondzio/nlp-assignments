Chris O'Donnell

Christopher Eugene "Chris" O'Donnell (born June 26, 1970) is an American movie, television, stage, and voice actor. He is known for his roles in "", "Batman & Robin", "Batman Forever", "Scent of a Woman", and in "Vertical Limit".

O'Donnell was born on June 26, 1970 in Winnetka, Illinois. He has been married to Caroline Fentress since 1997. They have five children.
