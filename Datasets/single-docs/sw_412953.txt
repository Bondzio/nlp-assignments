Margaret Pellegrini

Margaret Pellegrini (September 23, 1923 â€“ August 7, 2013) was an American actress. She was one of the last surviving munchkins from the 1939 movie "The Wizard of Oz". Pellegrini was one of only two surviving female munchkins from the movie.

Pellegrini was born on September 23, 1923 in Tuscumbia, Alabama. She was married to Willie Pellegrini until his death. They had two children. Pellegrini died on August 7, 2013 in Glendale, Arizona from a stroke, aged 89.
