Serena Williams

Serena Jameka Williams (born September 26, 1981) is an American professional tennis player who is currently ranked No. 1 in women's singles tennis. Williams has won 23 Grand Slam singles titles. She is the younger sister of another former world no. 1 professional female tennis player, Venus Williams.

The Williams sisters grew up in Los Angeles and currently live together in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida, United States. Both are Jehovah's Witnesses.
