Kalki Avatar aur Muhammad Sahib

Kalki Avatar aur Muhammad Sahib () is a book written in 1969. It was written by Ved Prakash Upaddhay. It was published by Saraswat Vedant Prakash Sanggha. It is written in Hindi.

The book mainly attempts to argue that the Islamic prophet Muhammad was Kalki. Kalki is the last avatar of Vishnu according to Hindu scriptures and Puranas.   

The author is Hindu and a scholar of Sanskrit writing.
