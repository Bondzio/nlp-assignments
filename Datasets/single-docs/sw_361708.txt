Eugene Levy

Eugene Levy (born December 17, 1946) is a Canadian movie, television, stage, voice actor, and comedian. He is known for his role in the all eight of the "American Pie" series of movies. He appeared in the 2012 movie "Madea's Witness Protection". He has won a Emmy Award, a Grammy Award, a Gemini Award, and a Satellite Award. He will appear in the 2015 Pixar movie "Finding Dory".

Levy was born in Hamilton, Ontario, Canada on December 17, 1946. and studied at McMaster University. He has been married to Deborah Divine since 1977. They have two children.
