John Aniston

John Aniston (born Yannis Anastasakis; July 24, 1933) is a Greek-American television and movie actor. He is the father of actress Jennifer Aniston. He is best known for his role as Victor Kiriakis in "Days of our Lives". That started in 1985. Anisto's career lasted 50 years. He has appeared in over 100 television programs and movies. 

Aniston was born in Chania, Crete, Greece to Stella Jones and to Antonios Anastasakis. Aniston went to Pennsylvania State University. 

Aniston was married to Nancy Dow from 1965 until they divorced in 1980. Aniston has been married to Sherry Rooney since 1984. With Dow, Aniston had 1 daughter, Jennifer Aniston. With Rooney he had a son, Alexander Rooney.
