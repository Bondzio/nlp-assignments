Leonard Lopate

Leonard Lopate (born September 23, 1940) is an American talk show host of the public radio talk show "The Leonard Lopate Show", broadcast on WNYC. He first broadcasted on WKCR, the college radio station of Columbia University—where his brother Phillip was a student—then later at WBAI, before ultimately moving to WNYC.

Lopate's talk show airs on WNYC from noon to 2 pm every weekday. Segments of the show are available as podcasts found on iTunes and on the station's website.
