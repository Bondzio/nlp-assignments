Arrondissement of Nevers

The arrondissement of Nevers is an arrondissement of France. It is part of the Nièvre "département" in the Bourgogne-Franche-Comté region. Its capital, and prefecture of the department, is the city of Nevers.

When the Nièvre department was created on 17 February 1800, the "arrondissement" of Nevers was part of that original department.

The "arrondissement" of Nevers is bordered to the north by the "arrondissement" of Cosne-Cours-sur-Loire, to the northeast by the "arrondissement" of Clamecy, to the east by the "arrondissement" of Château-Chinon (Ville), to the south by the Allier (Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes) department and to the west by the Cher (Centre-Val de Loire) department.
