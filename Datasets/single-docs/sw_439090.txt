Home Alone 4

Home Alone 4 (also known as Home Alone 4: Taking Back the House) is an American family television movie. It first aired on ABC on November 3, 2002. Home Alone 4 was directed by Rod Daniel.

The film has some of the main characters from the first two films, but all of the roles are played by different actors. The story is in Chicago but the movie was actually filmed in South Africa. 

The film was not popular. A planned TV series based on the movie was cancelled because of the poor reviews. It's an intrequel to the first two films.
