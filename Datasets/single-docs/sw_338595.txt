Hrithik Roshan

Hrithik Roshan (born 10 January 1974) is an Indian Bollywood actor. Roshan had appeared as a child actor in many movies in the 1980s. He made his movie debut in "Kaho Naa... Pyaar Hai" in 2000. His performance in the movie was widely appreciated and earned him a Filmfare Awards for Best Actor and Best Male Debut. He followed it with leading roles in "Fiza" and "Mission Kashmir" and had a male supporting role in the blockbuster movie "Kabhi Khushi Kabhie Gham..." (2001), which was India's highest-grossing movie in the overseas market at that time.
