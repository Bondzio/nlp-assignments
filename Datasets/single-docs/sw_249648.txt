J.P. Manoux

Jean-Paul Christophe Manoux (born June 8, 1969) is an American actor. He is best known for his role in the Disney Channel series "Phil of the Future" as Curtis/Vice Principal. He is also famous for playing Kuzco in "The Emperor's New School".

Manoux was also a contestant on "Jeopardy!", "Family Feud" and "Wheel of Fortune".

He has authored and read two pieces for NPR's "All Things Considered". Back in 2000, he provided commentary on the Screen Actors Guild strike. He was a striking actor and refused to do work.
