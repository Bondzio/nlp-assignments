Unstoppable (2010 movie)

Unstoppable is a 2010 American action thriller movie. It was produced by Tony Scott, Ridley Scott, Julie Yorn, Mimi Rogers, Eric McLeod and Alex Young and was directed by Tony Scott. It was final film directed by Tony Scott before his death in 2012. The movie was released on November 12, 2010. The movie received positive reviews with a 86% on Rotten Tomatoes which is certified fresh and is also rated as a 69% by Metacritic.
