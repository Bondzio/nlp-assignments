Bambi, A Life in the Woods

Bambi, A Life in the Woods () is a novel by Austrian author Felix Salten. It was published in 1923. The story is about a young Roe Deer named Bambi, and his life as he grows up.

The book was made into a film called "Bambi" by Walt Disney Productions. The film is famous and received a midquel titled "Bambi II" in 2006. In the Disney films, Bambi was made a White-tailed deer.
