Lodewijk Asscher

Lodewijk Frans Asscher (born September 27, 1974 in Amsterdam) is a Dutch politician of the Labour Party (PvdA). He has been political leader since 2016. 

He has been a government minister and also deputy prime minister in the Second Rutte cabinet since 2012. Previously he was an alderman of Amsterdam. As an alderman he was fighting sexual slavery and criminal companies.

Asscher studied law at the University of Amsterdam (including a PhD) and is a former academic at the same university. He is partly of Jewish descent.
