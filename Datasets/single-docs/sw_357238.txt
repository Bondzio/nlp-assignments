Estelle Harris

Estelle Harris (born Estelle Nussbaum April 4, 1928 in New York City, New York) is a comedian, actress, voice actress, and television actress. She is best known for her role as Estelle Costanza in "Seinfield". To younger audiences she is known for her role as Mrs. Potato Head in "Toy Story 2", and in "Toy Story 3". She is also known as Muriel on "The Suite Life of Zach and Cody". 

Harris was born on April 4, 1928 in New York City, New York to Jewish-American parents, Issac Nussbaum and to Anna Nussbaum
. Her career began in 1977 in the movie "Looking Up", since then she had appeared in; "Out to Sea", "Toy Story 2", Tarzan II", and "Toy Story 3".

Harris is currently married to Sy Harris and they have 3 children together. Harris currently lives in Los Angeles, California. Estelle and her husband Sy, escaped serious injuries when their car blew a tire and flipped twice on September 20, 2001.
