Love?

Love? is the seventh studio album by Jennifer Lopez. It was released on April 29, 2011 by Island Records. It was the only album that Lopez released on that label. This was Lopez' third consecutive album to have all singles reach number one on the "Billboard" Hot Dance Club Songs. That made eleven number ones for her. Lopez also worked with many entertainers including Pitbull, Lil Wayne, Lady Gaga, and The-Dream.

The album's first single was "On the Floor". Pitbull sings on it. The song reached number three on the "Billboard" Hot 100. It reached number one on more than eighteen international charts.
