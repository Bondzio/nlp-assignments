Juice (movie)

Juice is a 1992 American crime drama movie. It is about four young African-American men in Harlem who are being having trouble with police and gangs every day. They begin as friends, but the friendship goes bad after a robbery. One of the men shoots several others, killing two of them near a building, including his friend Raheem. The murderer later dies from falling off a building.

The movie got very positive reviews. It was filmed and completed in late 1991.
