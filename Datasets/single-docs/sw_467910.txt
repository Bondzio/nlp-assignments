Bob Geigel

Robert "Bob" Geigel (October 1, 1924 â€“ October 30, 2014) was a former American professional wrestling promoter and also a former professional wrestler. 

Geigel ran the NWA Central States promotion between 1963 and 1986, until it was bought out by Jim Crockett Promotions. He was also president of the National Wrestling Alliance for most of the years 1978 to 1987.

Geigel died on October 30, 2014, after a long battle with Alzheimer's disease in Kansas City, Missouri, aged 90.
