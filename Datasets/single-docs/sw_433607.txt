Mishon Ratliff

Mishon Ratliff (born February 3, 1993), also simply called Mishon, is an American singer, dancer and actor. Ratliff is best known for his role as Taylor "Tay" Sutton on the ABC Family television series "Lincoln Heights". He has appeared on music television broadcast such as "Soul Train", "America's Most Talented Kid", "The Mo'Nique Show" and "106 & Park". As well as being an actor, Mishon is a musician who plays the keyboard and both acoustic and electric guitar.
