Ryan Reynolds

Ryan Rodney Reynolds (born October 23, 1976) is a Canadian movie, television, stage, and voice actor who is known for his roles as Hal Jordan/Green Lantern in "Green Lantern" and as Wade Wilson/Deadpool in "" and "Deadpool". He is also known for his roles in "National Lampoon's Van Wilder", "Just Friends", "Definitely", "Maybe", "The Proposal", "The Change-Up", and "Safe House".

Reynolds was born on October 23, 1976 in Vancouver, British Columbia. He studied at Kitsilano Secondary School and at Kwantlen Polytechnic University. Reynolds was married to Scarlett Johansson from 2008 to 2011. He married Blake Lively in 2012.
