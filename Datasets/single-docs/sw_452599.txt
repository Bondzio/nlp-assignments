White Dog

White Dog is a 1982 American drama movie. It is about a white dog that is trained to attack black people. Burl Ives plays Carruthers. Kristy McNichol plays Julie Sawyer.

The movie was very controversial for its racial themes. Paramount Pictures didn't release it for a long time because of concern for negative press. It was finally released uncut in the United States in December 2008.

The movie received very good reviews from critics.
