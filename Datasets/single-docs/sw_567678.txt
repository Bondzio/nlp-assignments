Steinschartenkopf

The Steinschartenkopf is a high mountain in the Allgäu Alps. It is north of the Hohes Licht, in Tyrol, Austria. It is the sixth tallest mountain in the Allgäu Alps. The popular Heilbronn Way ("Heilbronner Weg") runs over the summit. It is the highest point on the Heilbronn Way.
