Gyula Gömbös

Gyula Gömbös de Jákfa (26 December 1886 – 6 October 1936) was a Hungarian military officer and politician. He served as Prime Minister of Hungary from 1 October 1932 until his death on 6 October 1936. He was born in Murga, Austria-Hungary.

Gömbös died in Munich, Germany from testicular cancer, aged 49.
