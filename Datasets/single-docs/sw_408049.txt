Mildred Natwick

Mildred Natwick (June 19, 1905 â€“ October 25, 1994) was an American stage and movie actress. She played the mother of actress Jane Fonda in the movie "Barefoot in the Park". She acted in John Ford movies, including "She Wore a Yellow Ribbon" and "3 Godfathers", both of which got released in 1948. Her last role was in "Dangerous Liaisons" in 1988.

Natwick was born in Baltimore, Maryland. She died from cancer at age 89 in New York City.
