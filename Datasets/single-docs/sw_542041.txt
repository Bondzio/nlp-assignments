Jamala

Jamala born Susana Dzjamaladinova born 27 August 1983 is a Ukrainian singer that won the Eurovision Song Contest 2016 with the song "1944". 

On 14 May 2016, Jamala won the competition with 534 points.

Her song, "1944" is about the deportation of the Crimean Tatars in 1944. It is also about her great-grandmother, who lost her daughter while being deported to Central Asia.
