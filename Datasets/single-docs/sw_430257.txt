Chris Furrh

Chris Furrh (born 18 December 1974) is an American actor. He was born in San Marcos, Texas. He is most famous for starring as Jack in the 1990 movie version of "Lord of the Flies". After "Lord of the Flies", he played the role of Nick Bankston in the 1990 television movie "A Family for Joe". He played another castaway teenager in "Exile". Furrh has since retired from acting.
