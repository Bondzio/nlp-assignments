North American Soccer League

North American Soccer League (NASL) is a soccer league with teams from United States, Canada, and Puerto Rico. The league was founded in 2009 and began play in 2011. It is the second level of the soccer pyramid in both the U.S. and Canada, behind Major League Soccer. Puerto Rico has its own soccer league, but it plays at a level below the NASL.

The New York Cosmos are the most succesful club with 3 titles.
