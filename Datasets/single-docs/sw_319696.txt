Malta at the Olympics

Malta at the Olympics is a history which began in 1928. 

The International Olympic Committee's official abbreviation for Malta was MAT. It is now MLT.

Teams from Malta have been in 15 Summer Olympic Games and 1 Winter Olympic Games. 

As of 2014 no Maltese athlete has won an Olympic medal.

The Maltese Olympic Committee was formed in 1928 and recognized by the IOC in 1936.
