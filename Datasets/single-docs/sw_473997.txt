Talia Shire

Talia Shire (; born April 25, 1946) is an American actress. She was most known for her roles as Connie Corleone in "The Godfather" movies and Adrian Balboa in the "Rocky" series.

Shire is the sister of Francis Ford Coppola, the mother of actor Jason Schwartzman, and the aunt of actor Nicolas Cage.
