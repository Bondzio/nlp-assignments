Harry Lonsdale

Harold K. Lonsdale (January 19, 1932 â€“ November 11, 2014) was an American scientist, businessman, and former politician. A Democrat, he ran for United States Senate in the U.S. state of Oregon three times, losing twice in the primaries and once as the Democratic candidate, losing in the 1990 general election to incumbent Mark Hatfield. 

Lonsdale died in Indio, California from heart failure, aged 82.
