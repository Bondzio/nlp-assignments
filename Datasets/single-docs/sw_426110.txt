Mohammed Karim Lamrani

Mohammed Karim Lamrani (born 1 May 1919) was the Prime Minister of Morocco three times. Lamrani is one of the oldest former living world leaders who are still alive today. 

Lamrani was born in Fes on 1 May 1919.

Lamrani served as the Prime Minister three times: from 6 August 1971 to 2 November 1972, then from 30 November 1983 and 30 September 1986 and lastly from 11 August 1992 to 25 May 1994.
