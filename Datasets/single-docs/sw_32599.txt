Furniture

Furniture is the word that means all the things like chairs, tables and cupboards. 

In other words, furniture are all the things that are in the house and that people can use to sit, to lie on or that are supposed to contain smaller things like cloths or cups. Furniture is made of wood, particle boards, leather, screws etc.

Bedroom furniture is the objects in the bedroom. The bed is covered by sheets, a bedspread and maybe a blanket. There is also a pillow on the bed. Next to the bed is the nightstand. It is also called the night table. A lamp may be on the nightstand. The dresser has drawers for clothes. On top of a dresser may be a mirror.
