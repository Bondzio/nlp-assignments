Mesothelioma

Mesothelioma is a cancer of the "mesothelial" tissue, a membrane that surrounds several cavities inside of the human body, including the lungs. Although mesothelioma hs many possible causes, most cases can be traced to asbestos exposure.

Mesothelioma is a deadly form of cancer. It is different from asbestosis, another disease caused by asbestos. Mesothelioma has been the subject of many lawsuits.
