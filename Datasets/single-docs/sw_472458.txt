Something's Gotta Give

Something's Gotta Give is a 2003 American romantic comedy-drama movie. This movie is about a man and woman finding love later in life. Jack Nicholson plays Harry Sanborn. Diane Keaton plays Erica. Amanda Peet plays Marin. Frances McDormand plays Zoe. "Something's Gotta Give" was released on December 12, 2003. Critics generally gave it good reviews. It made over $124 million at the box office.
