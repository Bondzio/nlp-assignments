Chatham Waterfront bus station

Chatham Waterfront bus station is a bus station in Chatham, Kent. The bus station opened in October 2011. It replaced the town's previous Pentagon bus station.

The main operators at the bus station are Arriva Medway Towns and Nu-Venture.

The bus station has 19 stands. Buses run from the bus station around the Medway area and to parts of Kent. The bus station has a travel shop and toilets.
