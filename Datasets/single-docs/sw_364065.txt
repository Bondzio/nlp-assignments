Hamish Linklater

Hamish Linklater (born July 7, 1976) is an American actor and comedian, best known for playing Matthew in "The New Adventures of Old Christine".

Linklater was born and raised in Great Barrington, Massachusetts on July 7, 1976, to Scottish Kristin Linklater, Professor of Theatre and Chair of the Acting Division at Columbia University and a renowned teacher of vocal technique, and Jim Cormeny.
