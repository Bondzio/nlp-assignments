Lee Weaver

Lee Weaver (born April 10, 1930) is an American movie and television actor. He is known for his role in "O Brother, Where Art Thou?". He also appeared in "The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air".

Weaver was born on April 10, 1930 in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. He has been married to 
Ta-Tanisha since 1971. They have one child. Weaver now lives in The Bronx, New York.
