Shmuley Boteach

Shmuel "Shmuley" Boteach (born November 19, 1966) is an American Orthodox rabbi and writer. He hosted a reality television series called "Shalom in the Home" on the TLC network.

Boteach is married to Debbie Boteach. He became friends with Michael Jackson in the 1990s. He was Jackson's Rabbi for two years.
