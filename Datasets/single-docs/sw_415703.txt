Kylie (album)

Kylie is the first album by Australian singer Kylie Minogue, released on 4 July 1988. The album was produced by Stock, Aitken and Waterman, who also wrote nine of the ten songs. Mostly a dance-pop album, it also contains many bubblegum pop elements.

All songs written and produced by Mike Stock, Matt Aitken and Pete Waterman, except where noted.
