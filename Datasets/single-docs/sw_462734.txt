Adrianne Palicki

Adrianne Palicki (born May 6, 1983) is an American actress. She was born in Toledo, Ohio.

Palicki has acted in both television series and movies. She was in the television series "Friday Night Lights" (2006–2011) and "Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D." (2014–2016). She acted in "Legion" (2010), "Red Dawn" (2012), "" (2013), and "John Wick" (2014).
