Ian St John

Ian St John (born 7 June 1938) is a former Scottish footballer. He played for Scotland 21 times. He later became a manager and pundit. He was born in Motherwell, Lanarkshire, Scotland.

In 2008 he was inducted into the Scottish Football Hall of Fame.

In June 2014 he announced that he had undergone surgery for bladder cancer in April of that year, removing his bladder and prostate gland.
