"Siempre Hace Frio" () is a song by American singer Selena. It was released as the lead single from "Siempre Selena" (1996). It was released after Selena's murder. A music video was released, starring Jennifer Lopez as "Selena". She later had the role as Selena in the 1997 movie about Selena's life.
