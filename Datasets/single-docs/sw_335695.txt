Brodus Clay

George Murdoch (born February 21, 1980) is an American professional wrestler and actor. He is better known by his ring name, Brodus Clay. He is best known for his time working with the WWE. Before he became a wrestler he worked as a bodyguard for Snoop Dogg.

Murdoch took part as Brodus Clay in 4th season of "WWE NXT". In NXT, Clay was first helped by kayfabe couple Ted DiBiase and Maryse. They were replaced later by the duo Alberto del Rio and "Ricardo Rodriguez". Clay lost the final against Johnny Curtis in the latest NXT poll.

On 9 January, Clay made ​​his re-debut on WWE RAW Super Show, where he won his match against Curt Hawkins. That Friday on WWE SmackDown, he won his second match after just 13 seconds against Tyson Kidd. After that match he was better known as the "Funkasaurus from Planet Funk".

On June 12, 2014, Clay was released from his WWE contract.
