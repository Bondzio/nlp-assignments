Chloe

The name Chloe is a female given name. It is especially popular in United Kingdom. The name comes from the Greek word "khloē", one of many names of the Greek goddess Demeter. The name refers to young, green foliage. The name appears in New Testament. In Northern Ireland, "Chloe" was among the most popular names for babies between 1997 and 2002.

People named "Chloe" include Chloë Moretz, Chloë Sevigny and Khloé Kardashian. Fictional Chloes include Chloe Sweeney, played by Amanda Seyfried in the 2009 movie "Chloe".
