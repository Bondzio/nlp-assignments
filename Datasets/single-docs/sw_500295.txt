Orma Slack

Orma Slack (née Snider; February 19, 1903 – April 13, 2015) was a Canadian supercentenarian who, at the time of her death aged 112 years, 53 days, was the oldest living person in Canada since the death of Colombe Benoît-Leclerc on 14 January 2015.

Slack was born in Bracebridge, Ontario. Her husband, William George Slack, died in 1988. They had two children, Rosemary (–1991) and Ted ().

Slack died on April 13, 2015 in Belleville, Ontario, aged 112 years and 53 days.
