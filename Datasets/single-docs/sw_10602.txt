Lenny Kravitz

Leonard Albert "Lenny" Kravitz (born May 26, 1964) is an American rock singer, songwriter and musician. His mother, Roxie Roker (1929-1995), was an actress on "The Jeffersons" in the 1970s. Some of his biggest hit songs include "It Ain't Over Til It's Over", "Again", and "Fly Away". He sung on "(I Can't Make It) Another Day" Michael Jackson's 2010 album "Michael". He also wrote and produced the song.
