Red (2010 movie)

Red is a 2010 American action comedy movie directed by Robert Schwentke. It is based on the limited comic book series printed by DC Comics. 

It stars Bruce Willis, John Malkovich, Morgan Freeman, Helen Mirren, Mary-Louise Parker, Brian Cox, Julian McMahon, Richard Dreyfuss, Karl Urban, and Ernest Borgnine. It was released on October 5, 2010. A sequel, "Red 2", was released on July 19, 2013.
