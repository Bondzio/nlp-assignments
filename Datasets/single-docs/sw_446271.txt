Albert Brooks

Albert Lawrence Brooks (born Albert Lawrence Einstein; July 22, 1947) is an American actor, voice actor, writer, comedian, and director. He was known for his roles in "Modern Romance" (1981), "Lost in America" (1985), "Defending Your Life" (1991), "Finding Nemo" (2004), "The Simpsons Movie" (2007), and "Drive" (2011).

Brooks was born in Beverly Hills, California.
