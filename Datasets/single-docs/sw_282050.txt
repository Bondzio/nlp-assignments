Fran Drescher

Francine Joy "Fran" Drescher (born September 30, 1957 in Queens, New York) is an American television, movie, musical theatre and voice actress, television director and producer, comedian, screenwriter, model, health, LGBT rights and political activist. She is of Jewish descent.

Her acting career began around 1977 when she acted in the movie "Saturday Night Fever". She is best known for playing Fran Fine from the sitcom "The Nanny". 

Drescher wrote her memoirs and is a Universal Life Church minister.
