Burt Reynolds

Burt Reynolds (born February 11, 1936) is an American actor, director, and producer.

He has starred in many roles, such as "Dan August", "Deliverance", "The Longest Yard" with its 2005 remake, and "Smokey and the Bandit".

Reynolds was born in Waycross, Georgia. He studied at Florida State University. From 1962 through 1965, Reynolds was married to British actress Judy Carne. From 1988 through 1993, he was married to Loni Anderson.

In 1972, he posed nude in the April issue of "Cosmopolitan Magazine". Reynolds claims the nudie pic hurt his chances for receiving an Academy Award.
