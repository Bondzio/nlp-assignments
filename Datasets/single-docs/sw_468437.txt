Nina Timofeeva

Nina Vladimirovna Timofeeva (; 11 June 1935 - 3 November 2014) was a Russian ballet dancer. Timofeeva was born in Leningrad, Soviet Union. She was known for her performance in "Swan Lake" (1956, 1970), "Don Quixote" (1959), "The Tale of the Stone Flower" (1959), and in "Macbeth" (1980).

Timofeeva died in Moscow, Russia, aged 79.
