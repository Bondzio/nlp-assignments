Malverde

Jesus Malverde known simply as "Malverde" is a Mexican hip-hop singer. He got his name from the movie "Robin Hood" character of Mexican folklore. Malverde released his debut album, "Mi Palabra" ("My Word") in 2004. The album was certified gold (sales of 50,000 copies) by RIAA, it also peaked at number-one on the US Latin Regional Mexican Albums.
