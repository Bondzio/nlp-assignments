Neve Campbell

Neve Adrianne Campbell (born October 3, 1973) is a Canadian actress. She was born in Guelph, Ontario and is 5'7" (1.70 m) tall. She became famous after playing one of the main roles in 1990s television series "Party of Five". She has had leading roles in several Hollywood films, including Scream 1 - 4 and The Lion King 2: Simba's Pride.
