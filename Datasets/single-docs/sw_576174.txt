BandNews FM

Rádio BandNews FM is the first Brazilian all news FM radio network. It first started in 20 March 2005. It is owned by Grupo Bandeirantes de Comunicação.

The network started broadcasting at exactly midnight in 20 March 2005, after at 23:59, Rádio Cidade said goodbye to its listeners after 25 years and top 3 songs. The first on-air journalist was Carlos Nascimento.

Currently, it serves ten cities: Brasilia, Rio de Janeiro, Porto Alegre, Belo Horizonte, Curitiba, Fortaleza, Sao Paulo, Vitoria, Joao Pessoa and Salvador. Four flagship cities for the network are: Rio de Janeiro, Porto Alegre, Belo Horizonte and Sao Paulo.

Two former cities that used to have BandNews FM are Campinas in 106.7 FM and Ribeirao Preto in 96.7 FM. These two stations are both now Band FM.

BandNews FM is well-known for its slogan "Em 20 minutos, tudo pode mudar" (In 20 minutes, everything can happen), which refers to how a segment plays every 20 minutes. It says, this format is brought from 1010 WINS in New York City.

From 6:00 to 23:00, each station plays the regional programming (on this block: 11 minutes of national programs, 6 minutes of national programs, and 3 minutes left for anchors to comment about the news), with local tracks from 9:00 to 11:00 and 18:00 to 18:50. Live national announcers will be on the air overnight.

However, the network also occasionally broadcasts Brasilian football matches.
