Vanessa Paradis

Vanessa Paradis (born 22 December 1972) is a French singer, model and actress.

She became a child star at age of 14 with the great worldwide success of her single "Joe le taxi". Since 1991, she has been a spokesmodel for Chanel. She was in a relationship with American actor Johnny Depp from 1998 to 2011, and they had two children: Lily-Rose and Jack. She is also the older sister of Alysson Paradis, who is also an actress.
