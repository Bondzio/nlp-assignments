Jeff Flake

Jeff Flake (born December 31, 1962) is the junior United States Senator from Arizona. He is a member of the U.S. Republican Party and has been in Senate since January 3, 2013. He was in the United States House of Representatives from 2001 to 2013.

Flake was born on December 31, 1962 in Snowflake, Arizona. He graduated from the Brigham Young University. He is married to Cheryl. They have five children.
