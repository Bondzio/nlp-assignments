Bryan Henderson

Bryan Henderson is an American Wikipedia editor. He is known for changing "comprised of" on the English Wikipedia. On Wikipedia, he uses a user name "Giraffedata". In February 2015, his edits for changing "comprised of" became national news. Most media coverage liked his edits as "uniquely effort for correctness", while others criticized it as being wrong. By August 2010, Henderson removed all – around 18,000 – occurrences of the term. He has since monitors any edits using "comprised of" and removes them. As per Wikipedia logs, he has made over 47,000 edits to the English Wikipedia.
