Mike Myers

Michael John "Mike" Myers (born May 25, 1963) is a Canadian actor, comedian, screenwriter and movie producer. He was a comedian on "Saturday Night Live" from 1989 to 1995. He has acted in movies including: "Wayne's World", "Austin Powers", "", "Austin Powers in Goldmember", "Shrek" and "The Love Guru".

Myers was born to English parents and raised in Scarborough, Toronto, Ontario. He holds three citizenships: Canadian, British and American.
