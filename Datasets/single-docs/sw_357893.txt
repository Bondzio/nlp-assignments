Julianne Hough

Julianne Alexandra Hough (born July 20 1988) is an American dancer, country music singer and actress. She is best known for being a two-time champion on the ABC series "Dancing with the Stars". Her first album was released May 20, 2008. She acted in the 2011 remake of the movie "Footloose".

Hough is the second of five children, she has four sisters. All of her grandparents were professional dancers.
