Girl Meets World

Girl Meets World is an American coming of age sitcom. The show first aired on June 27, 2014 on Disney Channel. It followed the premiere of the Disney Channel Original Movie, "Zapped". "Girl Meets World" is a sequel to the sitcom "Boy Meets World". The original show aired on ABC in its TGIF block from 1993 to 2000. On August 6, 2014, "Girl Meets World" was renewed for a second season by Disney Channel.

The show follows Cory and Topanga Matthews' daughter Riley (Rowan Blanchard) and her best friend, Maya Hart (Sabrina Carpenter) as they go through the challenges of life as they start seventh grade. The show also features their classmates Lucas (Peyton Meyer) and Farkle (Corey Fogelmanis), and Riley's younger brother Auggie (August Maturo). The show is set in New York City where Cory and Topanga moved to in the "Boy Meets World" series finale.
