Princess of Xiaohe

Princess Xiaohe (), also known as Beauty of Xiaohe () is a female mummy discovered in 1934 by Swedish archaeologist Folke Bergman, at Xiaohe Tomb () (literally "Little River Tomb"), Luobu Po(罗布泊), Ruoqiang County(若羌县), Xinjiang. Because of its beauty, this mummy was also nicknamed Marlene Deitrich of the Desert, Sophia Loren of Xiaohe.

The mummy was discovered Tarim Basin, 60 km south of Konche-darja (孔雀河) at . 

The mummy was estimated to be between 20 and 40 years old at the time of death, and noteworthy for being more similar to an European than an Asian. The mummy's hair, tooth and eyelashes were perfectly preserved, has well-defined features and sharp nose, cavernous eyes. She was estimated to have lived between B.C 1900 and B.C 1613.
