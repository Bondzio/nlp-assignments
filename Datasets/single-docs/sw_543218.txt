Clash of Clans

Clash of Clans is a freemium mobile MMO strategy video game made by Supercell. The game was made in Finland. It was released for iOS platforms on 2 August, 2012, and on Google Play for Android on 7 October, 2013 It is an online multiplayer game. Players build a community (called a clan), train troops, and attack other players to earn gold, elixir and Dark Elixir. These can be used to build defenses that protect the player from attacks by other players attacks.
