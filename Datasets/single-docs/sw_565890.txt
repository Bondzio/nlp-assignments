Leah Bracknell

Leah Bracknell (born Alison Rosalind Bracknell 12 July 1964 in Westminster, London) is an English actress. She is best known for her role as Zoe Tate in "Emmerdale". She is also a teacher with the British School of Yoga and designs and produces her own line of jewelry.

In October 2016, Bracknell revealed that she was suffering from terminal lung cancer.
