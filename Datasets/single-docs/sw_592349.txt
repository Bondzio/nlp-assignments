Gil Coan

Gilbert Fitzgerald "Gil" Coan (born May 18, 1922) is a former professional baseball outfielder. He was born in Monroe, North Carolina.

Coan played in Major League Baseball (MLB) for the Washington Senators, Baltimore Orioles, Chicago White Sox and the New York Giants. He batted left-handed and threw right-handed. As of 2016, he is the oldest living former New York Giants player.
