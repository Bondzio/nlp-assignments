Haut-Bocage

Haut-Bocage is a commune in the Allier department of central France, in the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes region.

The town hall is in Maillet.

The Haut-Bocage was established on 1 January 2016 with the former communes of Maillet, Givarlais and Louroux-Hodement.

Haut-Bocage is in the western part of the Allier department, in the valley of the Cher river at to the north of Montluçon, the subprefecture of the "arrondissement".
