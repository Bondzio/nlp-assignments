Nicolas Noxon

Nicolas Lane Noxon (July 29, 1936 â€“ May 3, 2016) was an American documentary filmmaker. He worked in television programs about history, science and the natural world. Noxon produced television specials and series with ABC, David Wolper, Columbia Pictures Television, Metromedia, MGM, Survival Anglia, Time-Life and National Geographic Television. He was born in London, England to American parents. He was known for producing the documentary "Secrets of the Titanic".

Noxon died of pancreatic cancer on May 3, 2016 in Westlake Village, California, aged 79.
