2019 Africa Cup of Nations

The 2019 Africa Cup of Nations (also called AFCON 2019 and CAN 2019) will be the 32nd edition of the Africa Cup of Nations tournament. The tournament will be hosted by Cameroon. It will be held from 12 January to 3 February 2019.

Because they withdrew from the 2017 Africa Cup of Nations qualification, Chad is banned from the tournament.
