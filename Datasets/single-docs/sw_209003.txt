Whitey Bulger

James Joseph "Whitey" Bulger, Jr. (born September 3, 1929) is an American former mob boss that worked out of South Boston, Massachusetts. He was on the ten top most wanted fugitives in the United States from 1999 until 2011. His story was on "America's Most Wanted" on FOX. He was captured by police on 22 June 2011.
