Gambling addiction

Gambling addiction is when someone is obsessed with gambling despite the negative consequences. Gambling addictions often puts the person in risk of losing everything. People with this illness often contemplate suicide more than the general population. Many people use gambling as a way to get away from their terrible lives and to gain satisfaction.
