Malin Åkerman

Malin Maria Åkerman (born 12 May 1978) is a Swedish-Canadian actress. She acted in "27 Dresses" in 2008 and in "Watchmen" in 2009. She also does singing and modeling. She played Kate Harrison in the ABC comedy "Trophy Wife".

Åkerman was born in Stockholm. She moved to Canada at age two and was raised partly in Sweden and partly in Ontario.
