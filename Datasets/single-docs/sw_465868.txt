Punch-Out!! (series)

Punch-Out!! is a series of boxing games created by Nintendo. There are seven games in the "Punch-Out" series. 

The player controls Little Mac as he fights his way up the ranks in the World Video Boxing Association. He is helped by his trainer, Doc Luis. Doc Luis later got his own WiiWare game. Little Mac is a playable fighter in both Super Smash Bros. for Nintendo 3DS and Super Smash Bros. for Wii U.
