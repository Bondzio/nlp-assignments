José Manuel Rodríguez Ibañez

Jose Manuel Rodriguez Ibañez (born May 20, 1966 in Madrid) is a track and field athlete from Spain. He has a disability: He is blind and a T11/F11/B1 type athlete. He competed at the 1996 Summer Paralympics. He finished first in the triple jump and long jump. He finished second in the 100 meter race. He raced at the 2000 Summer Paralympics.
