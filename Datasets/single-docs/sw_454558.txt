Eden Hazard

Eden Hazard (Born 7 January 1991) is a Belgian footballer who plays for Chelsea F.C and Belgium national team. He usually plays as an attacking midfielder. He was born in La Louviere, Hainaut. He is widely regarded as one of the best players in his position and his skills are often compared with players like Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo.

Hazard won the player of the year for Chelsea in the 2013-2014 season and the 2014-15 season. He also was the Young Player of the Year in 2012-2013.
