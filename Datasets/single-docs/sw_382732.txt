Ron Jeremy

Ronald Jeremy "Ron" Hyatt (born March 12, 1953) or Ron Jeremy is an American pornographic actor, movie actor, businessman, and television personality. He is known for his pornographic movies and he was listed as one of the "Top 50 Best Porno Actors of All Time" as of 2007.

Jeremy was born on March 12, 1953 in Queens, New York City to a Jewish family. He was raised in New York City. He studied at the Queens College in New York. He was a teacher before he became a porn star.

Jeremy was a contestant in the second season of "The Surreal Life". 

On January 30, 2013, Jeremy went to the hospital in Los Angeles, California after he had an heart aneurysm. The next day he had a surgery. He recovered from the surgery on February 7. He left the hospital on February 19.
