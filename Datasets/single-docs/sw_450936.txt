Muppet Treasure Island

Muppet Treasure Island is a 1996 musical adventure comedy movie. It is based upon Robert Louie Stevenson's novel Treasure Island, and is about The Muppets going on a sea voyage and meeting pirates. It is the fifth feature movie to star The Muppets. Brian Henson (son of Jim Henson) directed.

The reviews of "Muppet Treasure Island" were mostly positive. The movie was a strong success at the box office.
