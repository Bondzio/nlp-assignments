Yolanda Hadid

Yolanda Hadid (n√©e van den Herik, previously Foster; born 11 January 1964) is a Dutch-American television personality, model, and interior designer. 

She was born and raised in Papendrecht, South Holland. She was married to Palestinian real-estate developer Mohamed Hadid from 1994 until they divorced in 2000. They had two daughters, models Gigi Hadid and Bella Hadid and a son, Anwar. She married Canadian musician David Foster in 2010. They are divorcing.

She was one of the leading personalities on the reality series "The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills". Foster has modelled in places such as Paris, Tokyo and Sydney for Eileen Ford.
