December Boys

December Boys is a 2007 Australian movie. It was directed by Rod Hardy and written by Marc Rosenberg. It was taken from the 1963 novel of the same name by Michael Noonan. It was released on 14 September 2007 in the UK and US and 20 September 2007 in Australia. "December Boys" is Daniel Radcliffe's first non-"Harry Potter" movie role since 2001's "The Tailor of Panama".
