King George V DLR station

King George V DLR station is a station on the Docklands Light Railway (DLR) which opened on 2 December 2005 and named for King George V Dock, nearby. Until January 2009, it served as a temporary terminus for the King George V branch of DLR but the line has since been extended under the River Thames to Woolwich Arsenal.

The station is in the London Borough of Newham and is located in Travelcard Zone 3.
