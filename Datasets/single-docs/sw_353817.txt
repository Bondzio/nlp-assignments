Tom Hiddleston

Thomas William "Tom" Hiddleston (born 9 February 1981) is an English actor. His most famous roles include Capt. James Nicholls in "War Horse" (2011), F. Scott Fitzgerald in "Midnight in Paris" (2011), Freddie Page in "The Deep Blue Sea" (2011), and Loki in "Thor" (2011) and "The Avengers" (2012). He will play Loki once again in "" (2013).
