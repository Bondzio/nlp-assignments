Raw Footage

Raw Footage is the eighth studio album by American rapper, Ice Cube. "Raw Footage" was released on August 19, 2008 through Lench Mob Records. The album featured the singles "Gangsta Rap Made Me Do It" and "Do Ya Thang". 

The album debuted at #1 on both the "Billboard" Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums and the Top Rap Albums Charts. It also debuted at #5 on the "Billboard" 200.
