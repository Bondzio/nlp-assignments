Wikimedia CEE Meeting

Wikimedia CEE Meeting ("from Wikimedia Central Eastern European Meeting") is the annual meeting of delegates of the Wikimedia movement from Eastern and Central Europe. It helds upon Wikimedia Foundation (WMF).

Wikimedia CEE-2012 was the first meeting. It took place in Belgrade, Serbia on October 13–14, 2012.

Wikimedia CEE-2013 took place in Modra, Slovakia on November 14-17 2013. It was co-organized by Wikimedia Slovakia and Education@Internet.

Wikimedia CEE-2014 took place in Kyiv, Ukraine on 19—21 December 2014. It was organized by Wikimedia Ukraine.
