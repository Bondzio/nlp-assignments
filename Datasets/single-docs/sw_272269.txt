Tin(IV) bromide

Tin(IV) bromide, also known as stannic bromide, is a chemical compound. Its chemical formula is SnBr. It contains tin and bromide ions. The tin is in its +4 oxidation state.

Tin(IV) bromide is a colorless solid. It melts easily. It is soluble in water. 

Tin(IV) bromide is made by reacting tin with bromine.
