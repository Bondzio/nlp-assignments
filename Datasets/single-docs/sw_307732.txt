The Marshall Mathers LP

The Marshall Mathers LP is the third studio album by American rapper, Eminem. "The Marshall Mathers LP" was released on May 23, 2000 and was produced by Dr. Dre, Mel-Man, Bass Brothers, Eminem and The 45 King. The album was given 5 out of 5 stars by Allmusic. "The Marshall Mathers LP 2", the sequel to "The Marshall Mathers LP", was released on November 5, 2013.
