Before Sunrise

Before Sunrise is a 1995 romantic drama movie. The movie stars Ethan Hawke and Julie Delpy. In the movie, a man named Jesse (Hawke) and a woman named Celine (Delpy) meet on a train and get off the train in Vienna. A romantic encounter begins from there. "Before Sunrise" was released January 27, 1995. It was very well liked by critics.

It was followed by sequels "Before Sunset" and "Before Midnight".
