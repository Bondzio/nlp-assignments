Arrondissement of Sens

The arrondissement of Sens is an arrondissement of France. It is part of the Yonne "département" in the Bourgogne-Franche-Comté region. Its capital is the city of Sens.

When the Yonne was created on 17 February 1800, the "arrondissement" of Sens was part of that original department.

The "arrondissement" of Sens is bordered to the northeast and east by the Aube (Champagne-Ardenne) department, to the south by the "arrondissement" of Auxerre, to the west by the Loiret (Centre-Val de Loire) department and to the northwest by the Seine-et-Marne (Île-de-France) department.
