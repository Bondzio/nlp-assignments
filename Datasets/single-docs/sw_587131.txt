Vincent Tshabalala

Vincent Vesele Tshabalala (16 March 1942 â€“ 3 June 2017) was a South African professional golfer. He was born in Johannesburg, South Africa. He was banned from the South African Tour for many years as a result of apartheid, Tshabalala played in the European Tour, and won the 1976 French Open. Tshabalala also did good in the European Seniors Tour and won two events in the South African Tour after the country lifted its racial restrictions.

Tshabalala died 3 June 2017 at the age of 75.
