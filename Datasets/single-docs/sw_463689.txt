Georges Danton

Georges Jacques Danton (26 October 1759 â€“ 5 April 1794) was a politician during the French Revolution. He was the first president of the committee of public safety. He started what is known today as the Reign of Terror. The Reign of Terror lasted from 1793 to 1794. During the Reign of Terror, Danton changed his mind: He said that continuing the Reign of Terror was a bad idea, but other people disagreed. Danton was charged with treason and executed on the guillotine.
