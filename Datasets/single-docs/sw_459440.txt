Who Do You Think You Are

"Who Do You Think You Are" is a 1997 song from Spice Girls. The song's lyrics are about superstar life and how anyone can get trapped in the world of fame. The song has a disco-style beat which resembles music of the late 1970s.

The song was a commercial and critical success. The voice of singer Melanie Chisholm was praised by pop music critics. It became their fourth number one song in Europe and the United Kingdom.
