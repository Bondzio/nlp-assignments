Virisila Buadromo

Virisila Buadromo is a human rights activist from Fiji. In 2008 she received the International Women of Courage Award. 

In 2001 she became the Executive Director of the Fiji Women’s Rights Movement. This organization helps women by changing laws and policies. 

After the Fiji government changed in December 2006, Buadromo had difficulty with the new government. Buadromo made the program larger by starting the Emerging Leaders Forum, a program for young women. the Emerging Leaders Forum is a one-year program. It works with Fiji’s family law.

Buadromo is also chairperson of the Fiji NGO Coalition on Human Rights.
