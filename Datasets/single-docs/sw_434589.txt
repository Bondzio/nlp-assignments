Binyavanga Wainaina

Binyavanga Wainaina (born 18 January 1971) is a Kenyan writer. In July 2002 he won the Caine Prize For African Writing for his short story "Discovering Home". He started "Kwani?" magazine. His first book, "One Day I Will Write About This Place", was published in 2011.

Wainaina was born in Nakuru.

In 2014 he tweeted that he is gay.
