Iñigo García Ruiz

Iñigo Garcia Ruiz (born July 3, 1974 in San Sebastian) is a track and field athlete from Spain. He has a disability: He is blind and a T12/B2 type athlete. He was at the 1996 Summer Paralympics and competed in the F12 shot put and discus. He finished 4 in the shot put and 8 in the discus. He was at the 2000 Summer Paralympics in Sydney, Australia and competed in the F12 shot put and discus. He finished 2 in the shot put and 7 in the discus. He was at the 2004 Summer Paralympics in Athens, Greece and competed in the F12 shot put and discus. He was 6 in the shoput and 9 in the discus.
