Anansa Sims

Anansa Sims Patterson (born in December 27, 1978 in New York) is an American plus-size model. She is the daughter of model Beverly Johnson and record producer Danny Sims.

Sims has been married to David Patterson since July 10, 2010. The couple have a daughter Ava Patterson (b. August 2011).

Their second child, David Bernard Patterson III, was born November 6, 2012.

Their third child, Dean Danny Benjamin Patterson, was born June 20, 2014.
