Thaila Ayala

Thaila Ayala Sales (born April 14, 1986 in Presidente Prudente) is a Brazilian actress and model.

She played Marcela, the protagonist of the 14th season of the teen soap "". In 2006 she posed nude for the magazine Trip. 

In 2007, she was photographed by Terry Richardson for the book, "Rio, Wonderful Town", released the same year. Subsequently the release actress and model, who appears in a photo with the breast showing and biting her lip, claimed to be sorry that she participated in the book.

In 2011, she said that posing nude was "rape".

On 19 November 2011 she married actor Paulo Vilhena.
