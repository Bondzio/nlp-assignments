Blake and the Aliens

Blake and the Aliens is a American TV show by Marathon Media and Nickelodeon. The series will premiere in April 2015 in the United States and in autumn 2015 in France.

The series is about Blake Meyers, an adventurous boy who is destined to one day become a Space Ranger who protects humanity from alien squirrels called "Squaliens". The alien squirrels are sent back in time to prevent Blake from ever becoming the adult hero
