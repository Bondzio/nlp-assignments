Pacific Heights (movie)

Pacific Heights is a melodramatic 1990 suspense drama movie. It is about apartment tenants who are at first welcomed, but later evicted and forced to leave the apartment. Michael Keaton, Melanie Griffith and Laurie Metcalf are in the cast. This movie was released on September 1990, and the reviews were mixed. However, the movie performed very well at the box office, earning back almost $45 million.
