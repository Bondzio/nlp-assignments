Nashua, Iowa

Nashua is a city in Iowa in the United States. As of the 2010 census, the city had 1,663 people. This was an increase of 45, or 2.8%, from the 1,618 people reported at the 2000 census. The famed Little Brown Church featured in the song "The Church in the Wildwood" is located in Nashua.
