Mark Kirk

Mark Steven Kirk (born September 15, 1959) is an American politician. He was elected to the United States Senate for Illinois in 2010. Before being a senator, Kirk was a United States Representative for Illinois from 2001 to 2010. 

On November 8, 2016, he was defeated in his bid for re-election to his U.S. Senate seat by Democrat Tammy Duckworth.
