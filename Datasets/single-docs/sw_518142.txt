Muse (Grace Jones album)

Muse is the third studio album by Jamaican recording artist Grace Jones. The album was released in September 4, 1979 by Island Records.

"Muse" was the last of Jones's disco albums that was recorded with producer Tom Moulton. Her first album was released 1977 with her debut "Portfolio". It was a mix of disco and R&B. After "Muse" Jones moved on to making reggae and electronica sounding albums in the 1980s.
