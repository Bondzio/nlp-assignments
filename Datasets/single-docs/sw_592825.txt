Montreux

Montreux () is a city and municipality of the district of Riviera-Pays-d'Enhaut in the canton of Vaud in Switzerland. In 2015, 26,433 people lived there.

In 1295, the Bishop of Sion sold the parish of Montreux to Girard of Oron.

In 1798, Napoleon liberated the region from the Bernese.
