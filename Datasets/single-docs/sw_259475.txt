Tony Orlando

Michael Anthony Orlando Cassavitis (born April 3 1944) better known as Tony Orlando is an American singer. He is best known for being in the singing group, Tony Orlando and Dawn. They were known for singing the songs, "Tie a Yellow Ribbon Round the Ole Oak Tree", "Knock Three Times", and "He Don't Love You (Like I Love You)".

Orlando is from Greek, Latino, and Puerto Rican descent.
