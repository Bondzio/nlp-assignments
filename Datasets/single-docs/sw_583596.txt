Bertschikon bei Attikon

Bertschikon bei Attikon (or Bertschikon) was a municipality in the district of Winterthur in the canton of Zurich in Switzerland.

On 1 January 2014 the former municipalities of Bertschikon bei Attikon merged into the municipality of Wiesendangen.
