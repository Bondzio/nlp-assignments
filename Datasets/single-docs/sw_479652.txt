We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together

"We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together" is a 2012 dance-pop, bubblegum pop, pop rock, pop song from Taylor Swift. The song is about Swift being frustrated at her ex-lover wanting to fix their relationship. The song was the lead single from her album "Red".

"Rolling Stone" named this song the second best song of 2012.

The song hit #1 on the "Billboard" Hot 100.
