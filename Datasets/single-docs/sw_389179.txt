Cousins (movie)

Cousins is a 1989 American romantic comedy movie. It was directed by Joel Schumacher. It is about step-cousins who have an affair. It is a remake of French movie Cousin Cousine. The movie stars Ted Danson as Larry, Sean Young as Tish and Isabella Rossellini as Maria Hardy. This movie was released in February 1989. It got mixed to high reviews from critics.
