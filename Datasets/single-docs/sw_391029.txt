Kindergarten Cop

Kindergarten Cop is a 1990 American criminal comedy movie. It is about an undercover cop pretending to be a teacher at an elementary school to find the wife of a drug dealer. Penelope Ann Miller and Arnold Schwarzenegger are in the cast. The movie was released in December 1990. It received mixed reviews by critics. However, earned over $200 million at the box office, making the movie very successful.
