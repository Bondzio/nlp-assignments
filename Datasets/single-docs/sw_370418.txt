Matthew Lillard

Matthew Lyn Lillard (born January 24, 1970) is an American movie, television, stage, voice actor, movie and television director and producer. He is known for his roles as Stu Macher in "Scream" and as Shaggy Rogers in all of the live-action "Scooby-Doo" movies and as his voice in the animated movies and in the 2011 animated series.

Lillard was born on January 24, 1970 in Lansing, Michigan. He studied at Foothill High School and at American Academy of Dramatic Arts. Lillard married Heather Helm since 2000. They have three children.
