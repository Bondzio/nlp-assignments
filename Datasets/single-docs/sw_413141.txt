Scott Wilson (actor)

Scott Wilson (born March 29, 1942) is an American actor of television and movies. He is known for his roles in "In the Heat of the Night", "In Cold Blood", "The Great Gatsby", "Dead Man Walking", "Pearl Harbor", and "Junebug". 

Wilson was born on March 29, 1942 in Atlanta, Georgia. He has been married to Heavenly Wilson since 1977.
