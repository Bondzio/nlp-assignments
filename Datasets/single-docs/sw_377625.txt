Opel Signum

The Opel Signum is a automobile produced by Opel. It was a more upmarket version of the Vectra that it was based on and it arrived in 2003 with a facelift in 2005 and was discontinued in 2008 with no replacement. It was sold in the UK as the Vauxhall Signum. 

It is a hatchback version of the Opel Vectra.
