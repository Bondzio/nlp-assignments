Michael Kiessou

Anacle Michael Ghounam Kiessou, better known by his stage name Michael Kiessou is a Cameroonian singer-songwriter and entertainer. He became popular with his song "Bennam". The song was on his debut EP, "Partage", released on April 2, 2014.
