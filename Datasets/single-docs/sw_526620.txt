Gino Marchetti

Gino John Marchetti (born January 2, 1927) is a former American professional football player in the National Football League and businessman. A defensive end, he played in 1952 for the Dallas Texans and from 1953 to 1966 for the Baltimore Colts.

Marchetti was born in Smithers, West Virginia. He was raised in Antioch, California. Marchetti studied at Modesto Junior College and at the University of San Francisco. 

In 2004, Marchetti was voted to the East-West Shrine Game Hall of Fame.
