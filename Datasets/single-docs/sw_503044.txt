Markiplier

Mark Edward Fischbach (born June 28, 1989), known by his YouTube username Markiplier, is an American internet personality, video game commentator, and charity fundraiser. He was born in O'ahu, Hawaii. 

Fischbach began his career in Cincinnati, Ohio. He now lives in Los Angeles, California. He studied Civil Engineering at the University of Cincinnati. He later changed to Biomedical Engineering. He then left college and started his YouTube career. 

As of May 2017, his YouTube channel has over 17 million subscribers, He is known for his Let's Play gaming style and for his video game commentary on horror or survival games.
