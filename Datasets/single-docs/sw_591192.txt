Wang Shouren

Wang Shouren（王守仁）’s courtesy name is Bo'an.His art name is Yangming.He was famous for his theory,but he was not only a philosopher,but also a statesman,a militarist,a litterateur.

"A lot of people admired him,such as,Sun Zhongshan,Liang Qichao,Lin Zexu,Mao Zedong"

He thought that Knowledge is action, action is knowledge(知行合一).His main idea is the extension of innate knowledge(致良知）
