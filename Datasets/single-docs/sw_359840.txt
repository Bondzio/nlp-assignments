June Foray

June Lucille Forer (September 18, 1917 â€“ July 26, 2017), better known as June Foray, was an American voice actress and actress. She was mostly known for making the voice of Rocky the Flying Squirrel and Granny in all of the Looney Tunes shows or movies. 

She was also the voice of Cindy Lou Who, Jokey Smurf, Lucifer, and Witch Hazel. Foray was awarded a Daytime Emmy Award, Annie Award, and has a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame for her voice work. 

A well known voice artist, Foray helped create the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature during her years as a ASIFA-Hollywood member.

Foray was born in Springfield, Massachusetts on September 18, 1917. She was educated at the Classical High School. She was married to Hobart Donovan from 1954 until his death in 1976, they had no children.

On July 26, 2017, Foray died at a hospital in Los Angeles of cardiac arrest. She was 99 years old.
