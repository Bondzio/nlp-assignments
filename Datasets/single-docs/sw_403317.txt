Aleix Suñé Segala

Aleix Suñé (born May 21, 1986 in Girona) is a skier from Spain. He helps skiers who cannot see by telling them where to go on a mountain. He skiied at the 2009 IPC Alpine Skiing World Championships with Gabriel Gorce Yepes. He skied at the 2010 Winter Paralympics with Andrés Boira.
