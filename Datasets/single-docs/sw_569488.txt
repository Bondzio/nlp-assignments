Time in Iran

Iran Standard Time (IRST), also called Iran Time (IRT), is a time zone used in Iran. The standard time is 3.5 hours ahead of Coordinated Universal Time (UTC+03:30). During its daylight saving time, it uses Iran Daylight Time (IRDT), which is 4.5 hours ahead of Coordinated Universal Time (UTC+04:30). Originally, from 2006 to 2007, there was no daylight savings in Iran, but was re-introduced on March 21, 2008.

Daylight saving time (DST) starts on March 21 or 22 (Farvardin) and ends on September 21-22 (Shahrivar).
