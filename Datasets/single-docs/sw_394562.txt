Chrissy Amphlett

Christine Joy "Chrissy" Amphlett (October 25, 1959 - April 21, 2013) was an Australian singer-songwriter who was the lead singer of the rock band Divinyls. She had many hits with the group and in solo. 

Amphlett was born in Geelong, Victoria, Australia. She died from breast cancer. She also had multiple sclerosis. She died in New York City at age 53.
