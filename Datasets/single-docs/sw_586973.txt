Dome-mae Chiyozaki Station

Dome-mae Chiyozaki Station(ドーム前千代崎駅, どーむまえちよざきえき) is a Railway station of Osaka Municipal Subway in Nishi-ku, Osaka, Japan. There is Dome-mae Station(ドーム前駅, どーむまええき) which Hanshin Electric Railway possess near Dome-mae Chiyozaki Station.

This is the nearest station to Kyocera Dome Osaka.
